## Workflow Layers

1. **Starting** (Pre-Revenue to First Client): foundation > niche > offer > acquisition > sales
2. **Building** ($0-10K/month): onboarding > operations > management
3. **Growing** ($10K-50K/month): acquisition (advanced) > sales (refined) > finance > team (first hires)
4. **Scaling** ($50K+/month): team (leadership) > finance (projections) > operations (matrix) > management (at scale)
