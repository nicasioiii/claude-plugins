# /agency-audit

## Purpose
Diagnose the user's current agency state and identify the highest-leverage next actions.

## Skills Required
Load in order: `agency-foundation`, `agency-finance`, `scaling-team`

## Step-by-Step Execution

### Step 1: Gather Current State
Ask the user (all at once to avoid back-and-forth):
1. What services does your agency provide?
2. What is your current monthly revenue (approximate)?
3. How many active clients do you have?
4. What is your team size (full-time + freelancers)?
5. What is your biggest bottleneck right now? (acquisition / fulfillment / finance / team / all of the above)
6. How long have you been running this agency?

### Step 2: Stage Diagnosis
Map to Shan Hanif's agency evolution stages:
- **Freelancer:** $0-10K/month, 1-5 clients, doing the work yourself
- **Side Hustle:** Small audience, ghost writing, coaching alongside
- **Branded Agency (freelancers):** One service, 5-10 remote workers, $10-20K/month
- **Branded Agency (team):** Multiple services, hired experts, long-term clients, up to $100K/month
- **Market-Leading Agency:** Industry leader, equity team, 2-year contracts, $15-50K+/month per client

### Step 3: Agency Triangle Assessment
Score each side of Dee Deng's Agency Triangle (1-5):
- **Acquisition:** How reliably can they generate new leads and close clients?
- **Fulfillment:** How consistently can they deliver results?
- **Finance:** Are they tracking revenue, costs, profit, and cash buffer?

Plus assess:
- **Systems/Processes:** Do they have SOPs, project management, documented workflows?
- **Leadership Clarity:** Do they have vision, values, goals, delegation?

### Step 4: Output
Deliver:
1. **Current Stage:** Name it clearly with the benchmarks they need to hit for the next stage
2. **Agency Triangle Score:** Visual scoring of all 3 sides + 2 connectors
3. **Top 3 Priorities:** Ordered by leverage -- what will move them forward fastest
4. **Skills to Focus On:** Map to specific agency-mastery skills they should read
5. **Red Flags:** Any warning signs (revenue concentration, no cash buffer, over-servicing, no SOPs)
6. **Next Milestone:** The specific revenue/client/team target to aim for in the next 90 days

### Step 5: Quick Wins
Suggest 1-2 things they can do THIS WEEK based on their stage and bottleneck.
