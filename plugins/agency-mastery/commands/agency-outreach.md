# /agency-outreach

## Purpose
Generate a personalized outreach sequence for a specific prospect.

## Skills Required
Load in order: `client-acquisition`, `niche-positioning`

## Step-by-Step Execution

### Step 1: Gather Prospect Details
Ask the user:
1. Prospect name and company name
2. Industry/niche
3. Specific problem you have identified (what are they doing wrong or missing?)
4. Something personal or noteworthy about them (recent post, product you bought, event)
5. Estimated deal size (monthly retainer you would charge)
6. Your service offering (what you would do for them)

### Step 2: Channel Recommendation
Based on deal size (Donvesh's framework):
- **$2K-$3K/month:** 1-2 channels (cold email + LinkedIn OR DMs)
- **$5K-$10K/month:** 2-3 channels (email + LinkedIn + video audit)
- **$10K+/month:** Omni-channel (email + LinkedIn + video audit + cold call + direct mail)

Based on target type:
- **B2B companies:** LinkedIn + cold email + cold calling
- **Local businesses:** Outbound calls (LinkedIn and cold email less effective)
- **High-value ($10K+):** Handwritten notes + direct mail + video audits + calls + LinkedIn

### Step 3: Generate Hot Outreach Email
Use Shan's 3-section template:
1. **Peak Interest:** State the problem found + mention something not obvious + personal connection
2. **Value:** Give the answer FOR FREE in the email -- specific, actionable insight
3. **About You + CTA:** Brief intro + end with a QUESTION (never "let me know")

### Step 4: Generate DM Message
Use the appropriate DM framework (Shan's 4 types):
- Framework 1 (Open Loop) for most situations
- Framework 2 (Example Work) if you have spec work ready
- Framework 3 (Lead Magnet) if you have a relevant resource
- Framework 4 (Conversation) for warm connections

### Step 5: Generate Follow-Up Sequence
- **Day 2:** Continuation of the same angle
- **Day 5:** NEW angle/message (compose fresh email with different hook)
- **Day 10:** Continuation of Day 5's angle

NEVER say: "Just following up" / "Did you get a chance" / "Just wanted to know if interested"

### Step 6: Suggest Value-Add
Based on deal size, recommend:
- **Under $5K:** Fake video audit (Donvesh's screenshot method) or lead magnet
- **$5K-$10K:** Real video audit (11-step script, under 4 minutes)
- **$10K+:** Spec work sample (Dee's sample platter) or direct mail gift

### Step 7: Output
Deliver complete outreach package:
1. Channel recommendation with rationale
2. Hot outreach email (ready to send)
3. DM message (ready to send)
4. Follow-up sequence (Day 2 / Day 5 / Day 10)
5. Value-add recommendation with instructions
