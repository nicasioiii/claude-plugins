# /agency-report

## Purpose
Structure a client report (weekly or monthly) that builds trust, demonstrates value, and makes the client look good to their stakeholders.

## Skills Required
Load in order:
- `skills/client-management/SKILL.md` and `skills/client-management/references/ref_client_management.md`
- `skills/agency-finance/SKILL.md` (for financial KPI context if monthly report)

## Step-by-Step Execution

### Step 1: Gather Report Context
Ask the user:
1. **Report type:** Weekly or monthly?
2. **Service type:** What do you deliver? (paid ads, content, email, SEO, lead gen, social media, etc.)
3. **Key metrics available:** What data do you have access to? (list specific metrics)
4. **Notable wins or challenges:** Anything significant that happened this period?
5. **Audience:** Who reads this report? (client founder, marketing manager, CMO, board)
6. **Client goals:** What targets were set for this period? (revenue, CPA, ROAS, leads, etc.)

### Step 2: Generate Report Structure

**If Weekly Report** (operational/tactical):
Generate a structured weekly update with these sections:
1. **This Week's Highlights:** 2-3 quick wins or progress items
2. **Deliverable Status:** Table of active items with status (On Track / At Risk / Blocked)
3. **Key Metrics Snapshot:** 3-5 most important numbers vs. target
4. **Next Week Plan:** What will be worked on, priorities
5. **Needs From Client:** Any approvals, assets, or decisions needed
6. **Next Meeting Agenda Items:** Topics for the upcoming call

**If Monthly Report** (Shan's 8-section template):
Generate the full monthly report structure from ref_client_management.md:
1. **Business Scorecard:** Key metrics table with This Month, Last Month, Change, Target, Status (Good/Fair/Low)
2. **Summary Performance Commentary:** 3-5 paragraph written analysis
3. **Combined Business Performance:** Trend charts (recommend: revenue, CPA, ROAS over last 6 months)
4. **Funnel Performance:** Conversion metrics at each stage (impressions -> clicks -> leads -> conversions)
5. **Primary Channel Performance:** Platform-specific metrics + screenshot of best-performing asset
6. **Secondary Channel Performance:** Supporting channels (social, email, content)
7. **Creative/Content Performance:** Top performers with why they worked
8. **Next Month:** Goals, operations actions (what/who/when table), strategic priorities

Customize sections to match the user's service type. For example:
- Paid ads: emphasize ROAS, CPA, ad creative performance
- Content/social: emphasize engagement, reach, follower growth, top posts
- Lead gen: emphasize leads generated, cost per lead, meeting booked rate, close rate
- SEO: emphasize rankings, organic traffic, keyword positions, backlinks

### Step 3: Commentary Template
Generate fill-in-the-blank commentary following Shan's 4-part structure:

1. **What happened** (factual): "[Month] was a [good/challenging/strong] month for [area], delivering [headline metric] which is [above/below/on] target by [X%]."
2. **Why it happened** (analysis): "This was driven by [specific cause -- new creative, budget change, seasonal trend, optimization]."
3. **What we are doing about it** (action): "[For positive:] We plan to scale this by [action]. [For negative:] To address this, we are [action] starting [date]."
4. **What we recommend** (strategic): "We recommend [specific next step] which we estimate would [projected impact]."

Include Shan's principle: always quantify the size of MISSED opportunity. Example: "If we had received the approved creative 2 weeks earlier, we estimate an additional $[X] in revenue based on current conversion rates."

### Step 4: Reporting Principles Checklist
Include these reminders with the output:
- [ ] Input metrics (actions taken) AND output metrics (results achieved) both included
- [ ] Compared vs. YTD average (excluding outlier months)
- [ ] Industry benchmarks cited where relevant
- [ ] Targets/forecasts set, then reported against
- [ ] Metrics translated to financial equivalents (e.g., "10% CTR improvement = $X additional revenue")
- [ ] Report formatted so client can forward/share to their stakeholders
- [ ] Missed opportunity quantified for any unimplemented recommendations
- [ ] Best-performing assets included with explanation of why they worked

### Step 5: Visualization Recommendations
For each section, suggest the right chart type:
- **Scorecard:** Summary table with color-coded status indicators
- **Trend data:** Line charts (revenue, CPA, ROAS over time)
- **Funnel:** Horizontal funnel diagram with conversion rates between stages
- **Comparisons:** Bar charts (this month vs. last month, vs. target)
- **Breakdown:** Pie/donut charts (spend by channel, revenue by source)
- **Top performers:** Screenshot/image with caption and metrics overlay

### Step 6: Output
Deliver complete report package:
1. **Report structure** with all section headings customized to service type
2. **Scorecard table template** with the user's specific metrics
3. **Commentary template** with fill-in-the-blank sections following the 4-part structure
4. **Visualization recommendations** per section
5. **Next-month goals format** (targets, actions, owners, deadlines)
6. **Shareability tip:** "Format this in Google Slides or a branded PDF so the client can forward it to their leadership team. Making your client look good to their boss is one of the strongest retention tools you have."
