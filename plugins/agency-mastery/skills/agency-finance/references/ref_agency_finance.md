# Agency Finance Reference

## Revenue Waterfall Diagram (Dee Deng)

```
REVENUE (Total client payments)
  - Operating Costs
    - Execution team salaries
    - Software/subscriptions (per client)
    - Contractor/freelancer costs
  - Overhead
    - CEO/leadership salary
    - Coaches/managers salary
    - Department heads
    - Office/workspace
  - Miscellaneous
    - Travel
    - Team events/lunches
    - Learning & development
    - Utilities/insurance
  = EBITDA
  - Interest
  - Tax
  - Depreciation
  - Amortization
  = CASH IN THE BANK
```

**Cost structure rule:** For every ~4-8 execution team members, you need one manager/coach. Start with 50/50 or 60/40 (managers also execute), transition to full coaching role as you can afford it.

## Full KPI Chain (Dee Deng)

### Acquisition KPIs
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing Spend | Total $ invested | Track monthly |
| Leads Captured | # from all channels | Track weekly |
| Cost Per Lead | Spend / Leads | Benchmark by channel |
| Discovery Calls Held | # calls conducted | Track weekly |
| Show Rate | Calls held / Calls booked | 70%+ |
| Proposals Sent | # proposals delivered | Track weekly |
| Proposals Signed | # and $ value | Track weekly |
| Conversion Rate | Signed / Proposals | 30%+ target |
| New Revenue | $ from new clients | Track monthly |
| Recurring Revenue | $ from existing clients | Track monthly |
| New Clients | # signed | Track monthly |
| Current Clients | Total active | Track monthly |
| Avg Revenue Per Client | Revenue / Clients | Track quarterly |

### Cost KPIs
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Execution Team Cost | Total team salaries + tools | Track monthly |
| Time Per Client | Hours allocated per client | Track weekly |
| Rev Gen vs Non-Rev Gen | Ratio of productive to admin time | 70%+ rev gen |
| **Profit Per Team** | (Team revenue - Team costs) / # teams | **THE KEY METRIC** |
| Overhead Cost | Non-client-facing costs | Track monthly |
| Net Profit | Revenue - All costs | 20-30% target |

## Revenue Concentration Risk Triggers (Dee Deng)

| Threshold | Action |
|---|---|
| Any client > 15% of revenue | Monitor closely |
| Any client > 20% of revenue | Strategy meeting to diversify |
| Any client > 30% of revenue | Active risk mitigation required |
| Any client > 50% of revenue | Emergency -- business is at existential risk |

### Mitigation Actions
- Increase acquisition investment to add new clients
- Avoid growing one client at expense of portfolio diversity
- Set internal policy: no single client above 20%
- If client naturally grows past threshold, do not reduce their service but intensify new business efforts

## Profit-Per-Team Calculation

```
Team Revenue = Sum of all client retainers for this team
Team Costs = Sum of all team member salaries + tools + allocated overhead
Profit Per Team = Team Revenue - Team Costs

Example:
  Team of 5 serving 8 clients
  Revenue: 8 clients x $5,000 avg = $40,000/month
  Team salaries: $22,000/month
  Tools/overhead allocation: $3,000/month
  Profit Per Team: $40,000 - $25,000 = $15,000/month (37.5% margin)
```

Compare across teams. If one team is significantly lower, investigate: over-servicing? under-priced clients? skill gaps?

## Acquisition Cost Framework (Donvesh)

### Rule: CAC < LTV (always)

| Deal Size | Acceptable CAC Range | Channels |
|---|---|---|
| $2-3K/month | $100-$500 | 1-2 lean channels |
| $5-10K/month | $500-$2,000 | 2-3 channels |
| $10K+/month | $2,000-$15,000 | Omni-channel + direct mail |
