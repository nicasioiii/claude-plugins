# Agency Foundation Reference

## GRA Diagnostic Grid (Dee Deng)

Print multiple copies. Each founder fills in independently. Score each cell: Green (80%+ correct), Yellow (20-80%), Red (<20%). Discuss as a group. Revisit quarterly.

### Leadership & Clarity
| Area | R1 (Basic) | R2 (Developing) | R3 (Established) | R4 (Advanced) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Financial endgame/BHAG | No clear goal | Vague revenue target | Specific 3-year financial target | Exit multiple/BHAG with timeline |
| Organizational design | No structure | Informal roles | Function chart exists | Matrix management with PSIU |
| Company vision | None | Founder has mental picture | Written vivid vision | Shared, reviewed quarterly |
| Core values | None | Informal/unspoken | Documented V1 | Used in hiring, reviews, recognition |
| 3-year strategic outlook | None | General direction | Written strategy with milestones | Reviewed quarterly with KPIs |
| Delegation | Founder does everything | Some tasks delegated | Functions delegated | Autonomous teams, founder coaches |

### Infrastructure
| Area | R1 | R2 | R3 | R4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Systems & processes | None/ad hoc | Some documented | Process library exists | RACINDA + regular audits |
| Recruitment & onboarding | Hire when desperate | JDs exist | Full hiring process | Culture fit + skills assessment |
| Learning & development | None | Informal mentoring | Knowledge base/LMS | Structured development pathways |
| Performance & reporting | No tracking | Basic metrics | KPI dashboards | Profit-per-team tracking |

### Sales & Marketing
| Area | R1 | R2 | R3 | R4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lead generation | None/random | 1-2 channels active | 4+ channels active | Full 6-channel strategy |
| Customer acquisition | Founder closes all deals | Basic sales process | Documented framework + CRM | Dedicated sales team/closers |

### Cash
| Area | R1 | R2 | R3 | R4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cash conversion cycle | Unknown | Tracked informally | Managed with projections | Optimized with buffer targets |
| Profit centers & drainers | Unknown | Identified | Actively managed | Concentration risk monitored |

## Vivid Vision Exercise Prompts (Dee Deng)

Set a timer for 30 minutes. NO censorship. Write freely about your life and business 3 years from now.

**Relationships:**
- Who is on your team? What are they like?
- How do you interact with clients?
- What does your relationship with co-founders/partners look like?

**Financial Security:**
- What is your personal income?
- What is the agency revenue?
- How much is in the bank as a buffer?
- Do you own equity worth $X?

**Team & Culture:**
- How big is the team?
- Where are they located?
- What does a team meeting feel like?
- What are the core values in action?

**Client Types:**
- Who are your dream clients?
- How much do they pay?
- How long do they stay?
- What results do you deliver?

**Lifestyle:**
- Where do you live and work?
- How many hours per week do you work?
- What do your mornings look like?
- What hobbies/interests do you pursue?

**Impact:**
- What is your agency known for?
- What speaking engagements or recognition have you received?
- How has your work changed your industry?

Revisit 2-3 days later for a second pass. Repeat every ~3 months.

## Service Selection Checklist (Shan Hanif)

All 4 must be YES to proceed:

- [ ] Service is based on your ACTUAL skills (not aspirational)
- [ ] You are genuinely interested and experienced as a consumer of this service
- [ ] There is clear market demand visible in the real world
- [ ] You can find competitors doing exactly what you want to do (proof of concept)

**If any are NO:** Do not proceed. Either develop the missing skill (2-3 month sprint) or choose a different service.

## Core Values Creation Checklist (Dee Deng)

Phase 1: Discovery
- [ ] Each founder brainstorms independently: "How we want to be" vs "How we do NOT want to be"
- [ ] Consolidate into shared list, eliminate duplicates

Phase 2: Refinement
- [ ] Reduce to 5-7 core values
- [ ] Write each in plain English (not corporate speak)
- [ ] Test: does each value guide a real decision? If not, cut it.

Phase 3: Deployment
- [ ] Communicate to team, gather feedback
- [ ] Build into interview process (interview for values alignment)
- [ ] Include in performance reviews
- [ ] Use peer recognition at team meetings to reinforce
- [ ] Review quarterly: are these still resonating?

## Agency Evolution Stage Benchmarks (Shan Hanif)

### Stage 1: Freelancer ($0-10K/month)
- Focus: Build skills, get 1-5 clients via hot outreach
- Team: Just you + maybe 1 VA
- Priority: Get first case study/testimonial (this is the real goal -- Donvesh)
- Timeline: 1-6 months

### Stage 2: Side Hustle (Variable)
- Focus: Test market, build small audience
- Team: Solo + VA
- Priority: Validate service-market fit
- Timeline: 3-12 months

### Stage 3: Branded Agency with Freelancers ($10-20K/month)
- Focus: One core service, systematize delivery
- Team: 5-10 remote freelancers
- Priority: Build SOPs, create repeatable process
- Timeline: 6-18 months

### Stage 4: Branded Agency with Team (up to $100K/month)
- Focus: Multiple services, hired experts, long-term clients
- Team: Full-time employees with equity potential
- Priority: Function chart, matrix management, financial controls
- Timeline: 1-3 years

### Stage 5: Market-Leading Agency ($15-50K/client/month)
- Focus: Industry leadership, 2-year contracts, premium positioning
- Team: 100+ people with equity
- Priority: Leadership development, concentration risk management, exit planning
- Timeline: 3-5+ years

## Agency Triangle Assessment Template (Dee Deng)

Score each area 1-5 (1 = nonexistent, 5 = excellent):

**Acquisition:** ___/5
- Can you reliably generate new leads?
- Do you have multiple channels active?
- What is your close rate?

**Fulfillment:** ___/5
- Can you consistently deliver results?
- Do clients stay long-term?
- Do you have SOPs and processes?

**Finance:** ___/5
- Do you know your profit margin?
- Do you have a cash buffer?
- Are you tracking KPIs?

**Systems/Processes:** ___/5
- Are workflows documented?
- Can new team members get up to speed quickly?
- Is project management in place?

**Leadership Clarity:** ___/5
- Do you have a vivid vision?
- Are core values defined and lived?
- Can you delegate effectively?

**Interpretation:**
- All 4-5: Ready to scale aggressively
- Mix of 3-4: Solidify weak areas before scaling
- Any area below 3: This is your bottleneck -- fix it first
- All below 3: Go back to CIGAR, start with Current State assessment
