## Onboarding Sequence (Shan Hanif)

6 steps in order:
1. **Internal KO Email** -- notify team about new client
2. **Client Onboarding Email** -- welcome client, propose KO meeting dates
3. **Client Onboarding Form** -- gather all needed info
4. **Internal KO Meeting** -- brief team before client meeting
5. **Client KO Call** -- introduce team, align on services, set expectations
6. **Follow-up Email** -- recap actions, deadlines, attach deck

## The First 30 Days Rule

**Shan Hanif:** The first 30 days sets the tone for the ENTIRE client relationship.

**Dee Deng:** "Disappointment = expectations minus reality." Manage expectations from day one.

**Donvesh:** Manually onboard first 3-5 clients before automating. After signing first client, STOP outreach for 45-60 days to focus on delivery.

## Internal KO Meeting Checklist (Shan)

- Client overview: industry, background, key stakeholders
- Relationship specifics: how you signed them, client personality
- Client goals: success metrics, timeframes
- Scope of work: deliverables, timelines, milestones
- SLAs: reporting frequency, meeting cadences
- Roles: each team member's tasks
- Timeline: deadlines, internal milestones
- Risks: key challenges, proactive mitigation
- Next steps: assign actions with deadlines

## KO Deck (12 Slides -- Shan Hanif)

1. Client name + date
2. Agenda
3. Core team (photos, names, titles)
4. Partnership -- services overview
5. Client's role/obligations
6. Communication & approvals
7. Communication cadence (daily/weekly/monthly)
8. Initial strategy outline
9. Deliverables needed from client
10. Timelines
11. Next steps
12. Closing

## Post-KO Actions (Shan)

- Set up client Slack channel + send invite
- Set up client folders internally
- Add weekly call to calendar
- Begin the work

## Client Dashboard (Donvesh)

Share with client for transparency:
1. KPIs -- results visible at all times
2. Roadmap -- dates and targets
3. Accounts & Credentials
4. Drive folders
5. Scripts in use
6. Task list

## First 30 Days Playbook

| Week | Focus | Actions |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Setup | Complete onboarding, gather assets/access, finalize strategy |
| 2 | Execute | First deliverables in progress, first check-in call |
| 3 | Deliver | First results or progress report, address friction |
| 4 | Review | Monthly report, strategy refinement, set month 2 goals |
