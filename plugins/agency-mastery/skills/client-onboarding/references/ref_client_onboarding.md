## Onboarding Form Fields

### Business Information
- Company name and website
- Industry/vertical
- Target market/customer description
- Current revenue (approximate)
- Key competitors (top 3)

### Goals & KPIs
- Primary goal for the engagement
- Key metrics to track
- Target numbers for 30/60/90 days
- Previous benchmarks (if available)

### Brand Assets
- Logo files (all formats)
- Brand guidelines (colors, fonts, tone)
- Existing creative assets

### Account Access (customize by service type)
- Platform logins
- Analytics access
- CMS/website access
- Social media accounts

### Stakeholders
- Primary point of contact (name, email, phone)
- Decision maker (if different)
- Preferred communication channel

## Internal KO Meeting Agenda

Duration: 30-45 minutes (internal team only)

1. Client overview: industry, background, stakeholders (5 min)
2. Relationship: how signed, rapport, personality (5 min)
3. Goals: success metrics, timeframes (5 min)
4. Scope: walk through agreement, deliverables (10 min)
5. SLAs: reporting, meetings, communication style (5 min)
6. Roles: each member's responsibilities (5 min)
7. Timeline: deadlines, milestones (5 min)
8. Risks: challenges, mitigation (3 min)
9. Next steps: actions with owners and deadlines (5 min)

## Sales-to-Delivery Handoff Checklist (Shan Hanif)

Critical transition point. Incomplete handoffs cause early churn.

### Before Internal KO Meeting
- [ ] Signed agreement uploaded to shared drive
- [ ] Sales call recording or notes accessible to delivery team
- [ ] Client personality notes: communication style, preferences, red flags
- [ ] Agreed fee, term, and any special conditions documented
- [ ] Client expectations and promised outcomes clearly written (avoid verbal-only promises)
- [ ] Decision-maker vs day-to-day contact identified

### During Internal KO Meeting
- [ ] Sales lead walks team through relationship context (how signed, rapport, personality)
- [ ] Delivery lead confirms understanding of scope and deliverables
- [ ] Team roles assigned with RACINDA clarity (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed, Not involved, Deciding, Assisting)
- [ ] Risks and known challenges flagged (e.g., difficult stakeholder, tight timeline, limited budget)
- [ ] First 30-day milestones reviewed and owners confirmed

### After Internal KO Meeting
- [ ] Sales lead introduces delivery lead to client via email or first call
- [ ] Sales lead remains available for first 2 weeks as relationship bridge
- [ ] Sales lead conducts handoff debrief with account lead at day 14

## Expanded First-30-Days Milestones (Shan Hanif + Donvesh)

### Week 1: Setup and Alignment
- Day 0: Contract signed, internal KO email sent, Slack channels created
- Day 1: Welcome email + onboarding form sent to client
- Day 2-3: Onboarding form returned, brand assets and account access gathered
- Day 3-5: Internal KO meeting held, roles and milestones confirmed
- Day 5-7: Client KO call completed, post-KO setup finalized
- Day 7: All project management boards live, first deliverables in progress

### Week 2: First Deliverables
- Day 7-10: Initial strategy or audit delivered for client review
- Day 10: First weekly status update sent (video call or written)
- Day 10-14: First round of deliverables completed and submitted for approval

### Week 3: Refinement and Feedback
- Day 14: Internal review -- identify gaps, alignment issues, or scope creep
- Day 14-21: Incorporate client feedback, refine deliverables
- Day 21: Second weekly update including early performance metrics if available

### Week 4: Month-End Review
- Day 21-28: Continue delivery cadence, finalize month-1 outputs
- Day 28-30: Month-end review meeting with client covering results, feedback, and month-2 goals
- Day 30: Internal retrospective -- what worked, what to improve, update SOPs if needed

## Manual Onboarding Rule (Donvesh)

- Manually onboard first 3-5 clients before building automation
- Get deep understanding of the process from direct experience
- Do not build systems before you understand them firsthand
- After first case study, life is 10x easier for acquiring more clients
