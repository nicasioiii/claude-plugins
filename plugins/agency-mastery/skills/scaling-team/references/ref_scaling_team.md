## Interview Checklist (Dee Deng)

### Stage 1: Application Screening
- [ ] Video intro submitted (shows effort)
- [ ] Resume reviewed for relevant experience
- [ ] Application task completed with quality
- [ ] Evidence of research into your agency

### Stage 2: Culture Fit Interview
Questions:
- "What can we do to be part of your personal growth journey?"
- "How do you handle disagreements with a manager?"
- "Describe your ideal work environment."
- "What are the values you look for in a company?"

### Stage 3: Skills Assessment
- Real-world scenario relevant to the role
- Time-boxed (24-48 hours)
- Example: Black Friday strategy for a watch brand (Dee)

### Stage 4: Tandem Interview
- 2 interviewers: one asks, one takes notes
- Reduces bias and catches what one person misses

### Stage 5: Offer
Have ready:
- [ ] KPIs for the role
- [ ] Responsibilities and accountabilities
- [ ] Development pathway
- [ ] Compensation and benefits

### Stage 6: Probation
- 30-day check-in: immediate fit assessment
- 60-day check-in: performance trajectory
- 90-day check-in: full evaluation, convert or exit

## SDR Commission Tiers (Donvesh)

| Tier | Meetings/Month | Commission |
|---|---|---|
| Base | 0-50 | 15% per booked meeting |
| Mid | 50-75 | 20% per booked meeting |
| High | 75-100 | 25% per booked meeting |

- Hire from Upwork at $10-$15/hour
- Hire EXPERIENCED SDRs to avoid micro-management
- Do not pay per hour alone -- commission drives performance

## Talent-Sales Staircase Diagram (Dee Deng)

```
Revenue
  ^
  |    /\      /\      /\
  |   /  \    /  \    /  \
  |  /    \  /    \  /    \
  | / OVER \ UNDER \ OVER  \
  |/  CAP   \/  CAP  \/  CAP \
  +---------------------------------> Time
       Hire    Hire    Hire

Each peak = over capacity (team stretched)
Each valley = under capacity (new hires, costs up)
Goal: minimize triangle size with better forecasting
```

## Equity/Vesting Structure (Dee Deng)

### Standard Vesting
- 4-year vesting period
- 1-year cliff (earn nothing if leave before year 1)
- Monthly or quarterly vesting after cliff

### Alternative: KPI-Based Equity
- Unlock equity tranches by hitting performance milestones
- Aligns incentives with business outcomes

### Voting Rights Warning
- 50/50 creates deadlock risk
- 3 founders enables majority votes
- Consider voting vs non-voting shares

### Shareholder Agreement Topics
- Profit distribution reviews (quarterly/annual)
- Work division and expectations
- Consensus protocols for major decisions
- Financial decision authority
- Hiring/firing authority
- Exit scenarios

## Co-Founder Alignment Checklist (Dee Deng)

- [ ] Life stage alignment (similar financial needs)
- [ ] Complementary (not overlapping) skill sets
- [ ] YIN to YANG competencies
- [ ] Shared values but different strengths
- [ ] "Can this person traverse the CIGAR gap with me?"
- [ ] Tested the working relationship before formalizing (Dee's dinner test)
- [ ] Set validation milestone (6 clients in 6 weeks)

## Metcalfe's Law -- Communication Lines (Dee Deng)

| Team Size | Communication Lines |
|---|---|
| 3 people | 3 lines |
| 4 people | 6 lines |
| 5 people | 10 lines |
| 10 people | 45 lines |
| 40 people | 780 lines |

Fastest way to coordinate = improve communication quality. This is why SOPs, meeting frameworks, and clear channels matter exponentially as you grow.
