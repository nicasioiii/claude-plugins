# /structure-cfo-call -- Monthly CFO Call Planner

I will help you plan a **structured monthly CFO review call** using the 3x3 method (3 reports, 3 sections). Answer these questions so I can build your agenda and talking points.

## Step 1: Business Context

1. **Who is the call with?**
   - Business owner (you are their outsourced CFO)
   - Internal leadership team
   - Board of directors
   - Investor or lender
   - Other (describe)

2. **What month/period are you reviewing?**

3. **How long is the call?** (30 min / 45 min / 60 min)

## Step 2: Financial Snapshot

4. **What is the revenue trend?** (Up / Down / Flat vs prior period)
5. **Any margin changes to highlight?** (Gross margin, operating margin shifts)
6. **Cash position:** Comfortable / Tight / Concerning?
7. **Budget vs Actuals:** On track / Over budget / Under budget?

## Step 3: Key Issues and Wins

8. **What are the top 1-2 wins this month?** (Revenue milestone, cost savings, new client, etc.)
9. **What are the top 1-2 concerns?** (Cash flow gap, margin erosion, overdue AR, missed target, etc.)
10. **Any decisions that need to be made on this call?** (Hiring, capital expenditure, pricing change, financing, etc.)

## Step 4: Audience Calibration

11. **Financial literacy of the audience?**
    - Expert (fluent in financials)
    - Intermediate (understands basics)
    - Non-financial (needs plain language)

12. **Are they a concrete or conceptual thinker?**
    - Concrete: wants specific numbers, checklists, action items
    - Conceptual: wants big picture, strategy, direction
    - Mixed / Unsure

---

## What I Will Produce

**3x3 Call Agenda**
- Section 1: Backward Look (what happened) -- review actuals, BvA, key variances
- Section 2: Current Position (where we stand) -- cash, AR/AP, working capital, KPIs
- Section 3: Forward Look (what is coming) -- forecast, decisions needed, risks and opportunities

**Three Essential Reports** (prep guidance)
- Cash Flow Snapshot: what to include, how to present
- Profitability Dashboard: key metrics to highlight
- KPI Summary: which 3-5 KPIs matter most for this audience

**Talking Points Script**
- Opening (R-D-R format: lead with the key recommendation)
- Data walkthrough (what to emphasize, what to skip)
- Decision items (how to frame each decision for buy-in)
- Closing (action items, owners, next steps)

**Audience Adaptation**
- Language adjustments for their literacy level
- Concrete vs conceptual framing
- Anticipated questions and prepared responses
- Objection handling using EQ techniques

**Time Allocation**
- Minute-by-minute breakdown fitting your call length
- Where to spend extra time vs where to move quickly
- How to handle the call going off-track

**Follow-Up Template**
- Post-call summary email structure
- Action items with owners and deadlines
- What to send in advance vs what to present live
