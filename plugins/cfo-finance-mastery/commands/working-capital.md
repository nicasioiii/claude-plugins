# /working-capital -- Cash Conversion Cycle Optimization

I will analyze your **working capital position** and build a plan to unlock cash trapped in your AR/AP/inventory cycle. Answer these questions so I can pinpoint the biggest opportunities.

## Step 1: Accounts Receivable (AR)

1. **What are your current payment terms?** (Net 15 / Net 30 / Net 60 / Other)
2. **What is your average days to collect?** (DSO -- approximate is fine)
3. **What percentage of invoices are past due?**
4. **How do you invoice?** (Manual / automated / recurring)
5. **Do you offer early-pay discounts?** (e.g., 2/10 Net 30)

## Step 2: Accounts Payable (AP)

6. **What are your typical vendor payment terms?**
7. **Do you pay early, on time, or late on average?**
8. **When did you last renegotiate vendor terms?**
9. **What is your largest recurring payable?** (Rent / inventory supplier / payroll / other)

## Step 3: Inventory (if applicable)

10. **Do you carry physical inventory?** (Yes / No -- skip to Step 4 if No)
11. **What is your approximate inventory value?**
12. **How many days of inventory do you hold on average?** (DIO)
13. **Do you have slow-moving or obsolete stock?**

## Step 4: Cash Flow Timing

14. **What is your payroll frequency?** (Weekly / biweekly / semi-monthly / monthly)
15. **How many employees?**
16. **Are there seasonal patterns in your cash flow?**

---

## What I Will Produce

**Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC) Analysis**
- Calculate DSO + DIO - DPO = CCC in days
- Benchmark against industry targets
- Identify which component has the most room for improvement

**AR Improvement Plan**
- Billing frequency optimization (28-day billing cycle analysis)
- Collection process recommendations
- Early-pay discount math (is 2/10 Net 30 worth offering?)
- Retainer or progress billing opportunities
- Factoring evaluation if applicable

**AP Optimization**
- Vendor term renegotiation strategy
- Payment timing to maximize DPO without damaging relationships
- Early-pay discount capture analysis (when the math favors paying early)

**Inventory Recommendations** (if applicable)
- ABC analysis framework for prioritizing inventory management
- JIT or consignment opportunities
- Slow-moving stock action plan

**Cash Flow Timing Plan**
- Payroll frequency impact analysis
- Monthly cash flow calendar showing inflows vs outflows
- Recommendations for smoothing cash gaps

**Dollar Impact Estimate**
- How many days you can reduce the CCC
- Approximate cash freed by each improvement
- Priority ranking by effort vs impact

**Share your answers above** and I will map your working capital cycle and build a plan to free up trapped cash.
