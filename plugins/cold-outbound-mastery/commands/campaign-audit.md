# Campaign Audit

Diagnose why a cold outbound campaign is underperforming. Analyze metrics, identify bottlenecks, and provide specific fixes. Covers all channels: email, LinkedIn, phone, DMs.

## Trigger

User runs `/campaign-audit` or asks about improving campaign performance, diagnosing low reply rates, or optimizing outbound metrics.
