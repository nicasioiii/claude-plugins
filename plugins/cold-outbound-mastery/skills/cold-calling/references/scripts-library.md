# Cold Calling Scripts Library

Complete word-for-word scripts for every situation: openers, pitches, closes, voicemails, and specialized scenarios.

---

## 1. OPENING STATEMENTS

### Basic Opening (Most Common)
```
"Hi [first name], it's [your name] from [company]. I reached out because
we specifically help [target audience] [achieve this outcome] using
[your method]. We recently helped [client company] [results], and I
thought we might be able to do something similar for you."
```

### Opening with Immediacy + Interest Hook
```
"Hi [first name], it's [your name] from [company]. Can I have 20 seconds
of your time to tell you why I'm calling, and at the end of that 20
seconds you can tell me whether or not we should be speaking?"
```

### Humorous/Different Opening
```
"Hi [first name], it's [your name] from [company]. Can I have 17.2
seconds? I reached out because..."
```

### Direct & Appreciative Opening
```
"Hi [first name], it's [your name] from [company]. I know this is a bit
of a cold call, so I appreciate you taking a moment. I reached out because
we work exclusively with [target audience]..."
```

## 2. PRIMARY CALL STRUCTURE (WORD-FOR-WORD)

### Complete First Call - Decision Maker

**Opening/Intro:**
```
"Hi [first name], it's [your name] from [company]. I appreciate you
taking a moment. I reached out because we specifically help [target
audience] [achieve outcome] using [your method]. We recently helped
[client in same industry] [concrete results], and I thought we might
be able to do something similar for you."
```

**Why You Reached Out (Extended):**
```
"I reached out to you because I saw you guys were a [local/industry]
[company type], and I'm from [location]. I saw the results you've
been getting for your clients recently, especially the [specific
success story]. That's incredible!

I came across your LinkedIn profile, saw you were a forward-thinking,
innovative person—someone I could see myself working with. More
importantly, I got excited because with the case studies you have,
I'm extremely confident I can generate some amazing results for you!"
```

**Identify Need (The Question):**
```
"Is [generating new leads and increasing revenue] a priority for you?"
```

**Qualify Them:**
```
"To make sure this would be a great fit for both of us, I wanted to
ask a few questions - is that okay?

- What [your service type] are you doing now or have done in the past,
  and what results did you get?
- What's currently working for you to drive new business?
- What does your sales process look like? Do you have a team?
- What's your average deal size?
- Do you have case studies?"
```

**What You Can Do For Them:**
```
"Great! So [first name], here's what I would do for you:

First, [Step 1 - specific action and why]
Then, [Step 2 - specific action and why]
Next, [Step 3 - specific action and why]

This is different from competitors because [your unique value]. Based
on what you've told me about your deal size and customer lifetime value,
you could expect [specific ROI estimate]."
```

**Call to Action:**
```
"[First name], could we arrange a time where I can either get you a
demo or we could arrange another time to talk that's convenient for you?"
```

**Get Contact Details:**
```
"I'll need your mobile number so I have a direct line in case the
number I have doesn't come through."

[They give number]

"And your email address - I'll send you a calendar invite."
```

**Confirm Decision-Making:**
```
"Let's say you love the strategy we come up with for you and you want
to work with us - who makes that decision?"

[If someone else: "Can we get [that person] on the call too?"]
```

## 3. CLOSING CALL SCRIPTS (Second Call - Full Close)

### Opening (Rapport)
```
"Hey [first name], it's [your name] from [company] - how are you?
It's nice to put a voice to the emails! Ha"
```

### Introduction (With Results)
```
"So a bit about me: I started [company] on [date] after working as
a freelancer for around 8 years. After generating some great results,
I decided to go out and get clients on my own instead of waiting for
them to come to me!

We've been getting some amazing results for our clients recently! Just
last month we helped [client name] generate an extra [revenue amount] -
that's a 25% increase on the previous month!

I'm confident we could do something similar for you, given the chance."
```

### Introduction (Early Stage - With Guarantee)
```
"So a bit about me: I started [company] on [date] after working as a
freelancer for around 8 years. After generating some great results,
I've decided to go out and get clients on my own instead of waiting
for them to come to me!

I am so confident in my ability to generate results that I promise
[your guarantee - e.g., 'I will generate [specific results] or work
for free until I do!']"
```

### Why You Reached Out (Detailed for Closing Call)
```
"I reached out to you because I saw you guys were a local agency. I'm
from [location] and saw the results you have been getting recently for
your clients, especially the [specific monetary result] you helped
generate for a manufacturing company in [location] - that's incredible!

I came across your LinkedIn profile, saw you were a forward thinking,
innovative person and someone I could see myself working with. More
importantly, I got excited because with the case studies you have,
I'm extremely confident I can generate some amazing results for you!

For example, for our first campaign, we might look to contact other
[industry] companies and explain how you recently helped [company]
generate [results] and see if we could do the same for them!"
```

### Questions to Ask (Qualify & Interview)
```
"To make sure this would be a great fit for both of us, I wanted to
ask some questions - is that okay?

1. What email marketing are you doing now or have done in the past,
   and what results did you get?
2. What is currently working for you to drive new business?
3. What does your sales process look like? Do you have a team?
4. What's your average deal size?
5. Do you have case studies?
6. What do you want from our service?"
```

### What You Can Do For Them (Detailed Process)

**Lead Gen Agency Example:**
```
"Great! So [first name], here's what I would do for you:

First, we need to get you a second domain - one where I would send
emails from. I had a look and saw that [domain name] was available on
Google for $[price]. We do this to make sure there is ZERO chance of
anything happening to your main domain.

Then we will add Google Workspace to the domain to create a personalised
email address, which looks more professional when contacting prospects.
Google also allows us to easily add DMARC, which gives us a boost in
deliverability to get straight in the inbox and avoid spam.

Because the domain is new, I will add it to an engagement group where
we send each other emails and unmark them as spam if that occurs. This
helps us ensure we get straight into the inbox.

Whilst the domain is being warmed up, I will start generating a list
of qualified leads for us to contact - in this case, manufacturing
companies within the UK - and find direct email addresses for
owners/decision makers.

Once we're ready to send, I will personalise EVERY email like the one
I sent to you. This takes time but is why we stand apart and generate
amazing results!

I will send emails in small batches, testing and tweaking as I go.
I'll send follow-ups to anyone who doesn't reply and forward all
replies to your email for you to arrange calls."
```

### The Close (State Price & Silence)

**Without Guarantee:**
```
"So [first name], I can do all of this for you for $[price] a month."

[THEN: BE QUIET, WAIT FOR RESPONSE, DON'T SPEAK]
```

**With Guarantee:**
```
"So [first name], I guarantee I can get you [specific results - e.g.,
'10 qualified sales calls a month'] or I will [your guarantee - e.g.,
'work for free until I do!'].

I can do all of this for you for $[price] a month."

[THEN: BE QUIET, WAIT FOR RESPONSE, DON'T SPEAK]
```

## 5. VOICEMAIL SCRIPTS

### Voicemail Tactic 1: The Vague Script
**For Local Businesses - First/Second Calls**
```
"Hi [name], it's [your name]. Can you give me a call on [number]?
Again, that's [number]. Thanks!"
```

### Voicemail Tactic 2: The Value Proposition Voicemail
**For Communicating Value Immediately**
```
"Hi [prospect name], it's [your name] from [company]. Is generating
new leads and increasing revenue a priority for you? We have just
helped [client company] generate an additional [specific results]
last month through personalised, cold email, LinkedIn and call
outreach! Could you give me a call please on [number] - again that's
[number]. Thank you!"
```

### Voicemail Tactic 3: The Follow-Up Voicemail (3rd+ Call)
```
"Hi [name], it's [your name] from [company]. I know I've called a
couple times - I wouldn't keep reaching out if I didn't think this
could be valuable for you. I wanted to [specific value point].

I'm around most of the week. Give me a call on [number] - again,
[number]. Looking forward to speaking with you!"
```

**CRITICAL:** Always leave your phone number TWICE. Prospects might miss it the first time.

## 7. APPOINTMENT SETTING CLOSE (For Client Calling)

Use this when calling on behalf of clients to set appointments with them.

```
"[First name], our [CEO/Founder/owner] [name] is available on
[day] at [time]. Would you have time then if I can schedule a
call for you both to see if we can help you with this further?"
```

**Why This Works:**
- Makes prospect feel important (getting the CEO)
- They must be important if the CEO is calling
- Creates perception of status
- Gets immediate agreement to second call

## 8. FOLLOW-UP SCRIPTS (When They Say "Send Information")

### Response to "Send Me Information"
```
"Sure, I can absolutely send that over. But before I do, can I just
ask - what information would be most useful for you? [Let them answer]

Actually, that way I can send you exactly what you need rather than
sending everything. But before we do that, can I ask - if the
information I send answers these questions, would you be open to a
quick 15-minute call next week to discuss how we can help?"
```

**Why This Works:**
- Gets commitment for a call after info
- Ensures you're sending what they actually need
- Prevents dead-end info sends

## 9. RESPONSE WHEN THEY ASK ABOUT PRICING (Before Qualifying)

```
"Well [first name], I'm not sure exactly what it is you actually need
yet. If you could outline what you need help with specifically, of
course I can give you a price.

What I'd actually do is outline all the problems based on the
information you give me, and then we can arrange a call where I can
come back with a specific proposal and pricing."
```

**Why This Works:**
- Doesn't give price until you've qualified
- Pushes them to talk about their problem
- Gets them to agree to follow-up call

## 11. RESPONSE TO "CALL ME BACK LATER"

```
"Absolutely! When would be a good time? [Get specific time]

I want to make sure I call at a good time when you're not busy. Is
[specific time/date] good for you?"
```

**Why This Works:**
- Gets specific time (not vague "later")
- Confirms they'll be available
- Increases chance of connection

## 14. TIPS FOR SCRIPT DELIVERY

1. **Don't sound robotic** - Practice until it sounds natural
2. **Use conversational pauses** - Not a rapid-fire delivery
3. **Emphasize key phrases** - "Specific", "similar situation", results
4. **Use their name 2-3 times per call** - Creates connection
5. **Let them talk** - Ask questions and listen (60% listening, 40% talking)
6. **Adapt based on responses** - Scripts are frameworks, not gospel
7. **Record yourself** - Practice on non-leads first
8. **Review what works** - Keep scripts that generate results
