## Success Checklist

Before launching any campaign, verify:

- [ ] Do you have a proven case study OR no-brainer offer?
- [ ] Is your offer hyper-specific (not generic)?
- [ ] Have you researched 15-30 min per prospect?
- [ ] Is your target list verified (UpLead or similar)?
- [ ] Email account warmed for 2-3 weeks?
- [ ] First lines personalized to each prospect?
- [ ] Email is 4-6 sentences maximum?
- [ ] Subject line A/B tested?
- [ ] Follow-ups scheduled (Days 3, 7, 14)?
- [ ] You've documented your offer and can explain it in 2 sentences?

If all YES: Launch it.
If any NO: Go back and strengthen before sending.

## Continuous Improvement

1. **Track everything:** Opens, responses, meetings booked
2. **Test one variable per 100 emails:** Subject line, offer angle, sending time
3. **Document wins:** Keep your own "vault" of successful conversations
4. **Iterate:** Use data to optimize, don't rely on intuition
5. **Study patterns:** What works in YOUR niche may differ slightly from benchmarks
