# Instagram DM Strategy

## Follower Range Targeting

| Follower Range | Results | Quality | Best For |
|---------------|---------|---------|----------|
| Below 500 | Lower quality prospects | Low | Skip |
| **500 - 4,000** | **8-10 calls/day** | **Good** | **Primary target range** |
| 4,000 - 10,000 | Moderate results | Good-High | Transitional |
| Above 10,000 | 1-3 calls/day | Highest conversion | Supplement (not primary) |

The 500-4K range is the sweet spot. These prospects are active, invested in growing, but not so big that they ignore DMs.

## Cold IG DM Process (7 Steps)

1. **Find lead** in spreadsheet
2. **Open their profile**, review quickly. If not a fit, mark "Not a Fit" and move on.
3. **Like a recent post** -- creates a notification, makes you visible
4. **Follow them** -- signals genuine interest
5. **Comment on a post:** "Got a question -- dropped you a DM" -- gets them to check DMs
6. **Send DM:** "Love the content you're pushing out! Cool vibe going on, excited to see more. Completely off base but saw you're a [their role]. Do you do one-on-ones or group coaching?"
7. **Get yes/no answer** (this is a bait question)
8. **Pivot to pitch:** "I'm helping [target] achieve [result]. Do you have open slots?"
9. **Propose call:** "Sounds good, could we run over this? [calendar link]"

### The Bait Question
"Do you do one-on-ones or group coaching?" is designed to get ANY answer. It doesn't matter what they say -- the answer lets you pivot to your pitch naturally.

## Volume & Benchmarks

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| New cold messages/day | 100 (across 3 IG accounts, ~33-50 per account) |
| Follow-ups/day | 50-100 |
| Total IG messages/day | ~150 |
| Calls proposed/day | At least 5 |
| Calls booked/day (sub-4K followers) | 1-3 |
| Peak performance (sub-4K range) | 10-12 calls/day |

Use multiple Instagram accounts to hit volume without triggering platform limits.

## Warm IG DM Openers

### New Followers
"Thanks for following! Curious what caught your eye about my page?"

### Story Engagers / Content Likers
"Thanks for engaging with my content! I noticed you're doing [something from their profile]. How long have you been doing that?"

### Personalized Example
"Thanks for the follow! I saw the 10X hat -- guess we both like Uncle Cardone. How long have you been following his stuff?"

**When getting an influx of new followers** (after shoutout, viral post): Change up your messages to avoid appearing automated.

## Instagram Profile Optimization

### 3 Pinned Posts (Critical)
1. **Who Am I** -- Personal story (carousel or photo, NOT reel)
2. **What We Do** -- Service/offer overview (carousel or photo)
3. **Client Results** -- Testimonials and proof (carousel or photo)

Format as carousels or photo posts, NOT reels. People checking your profile need to skim quickly.

### Bio Structure
- Clear statement of who you help and how
- Social proof metric (clients served, revenue generated)
- CTA to DM or link

### Bio Link Strategy
- Use URL Genie for deep links (opens in-app instead of Safari)
- Link to VSL or Facebook group (not just a plain URL)
- Deep links dramatically improve click-through since users stay in-app

## Content Strategy for IG DM Outreach

### Belief-Shifting Carousels
Outperformed reels for DM-based businesses. Topics:
- Why your model is better than alternatives
- Transformation stories
- MRR/revenue showcases
- Framework breakdowns

### Hashtags
Mixed results. Test with and without. Some niches benefit, others don't.

### Reels
Good for reach and new followers, but carousels convert better for DM-based businesses. Use reels to grow audience, carousels to convert.

## Instagram Account Safety

### Avoid Account Bans
- Don't cold message people from similar groups before they accept your follow request (especially in competitive niches: marketing, health, dating)
- Spread volume across multiple accounts (~33-50 messages per account)
- Mix warm and cold outreach on each account
- Don't send identical messages to many people in rapid succession

### Multiple Account Strategy
Run 3 Instagram accounts to hit 100 new cold messages/day:
- Each account handles 33-50 new outreach messages
- Plus 50-100 follow-ups distributed across accounts
- Keeps each account under platform limits

## Connecting Instagram to Other Channels

### Data Collection
Every DM conversation should lead to capturing:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number

Collect via opt-in funnels, lead magnets, or booking forms. This enables marketing through email and SMS in addition to DMs.

### Instagram to Facebook Group Funnel
- IG DM conversation warms them up
- Invite to Facebook group for deeper engagement
- Group membership questions capture data
- Group onboarding triggers call booking flow

### Instagram to Call
- Build rapport in DMs (Stages 1-3)
- Propose call when Level 2 pain identified (Stage 4)
- Send calendar link directly in DM
- Follow up with "Did you run into any problems finding a time slot that suits your personal agenda?"
