# Platform-Specific DM Outreach Strategies

## FACEBOOK DM OUTREACH

### Lead Hierarchy (Hottest to Coldest)

#### Tier 1: Facebook Group Members (With Survey Response)
**Warmest possible lead:** Already qualified intent through intake form

```
Initial Message:
Hey! Just accepted you into the [Group Name] group. Love the [GOAL] you have, how's that going?

[WAIT FOR RESPONSE]

If opted into help:
I also saw you wanted me to reach out to see how I may be able to help you with that.
Are you keen to connect on a quick call this week? :D

[Include Calendly Link]
```

---

#### Tier 2: Facebook Group Members (No Survey)
**Second warmest:** They joined but didn't fill out intake form

```
Message 1:
Hey NAME, thank you for joining my group! [Group Name]
I just released my free course over there.

As a thank you for joining, would you be against it if I sent it over to you?

[WAIT FOR RESPONSE]

Message 2:
Great, here you go: [LINK]

Just a heads up, this is literally what our setters and clients have used
to consistently book 3-5 calls/day on average.

Are you getting clients through DM setting already or are you looking to get started?

### Facebook Timing & Best Practices

- **Best Days:** Monday-Friday
- **Best Hours:** 8am-9pm
- **Avoid:** Weekends, late nights (after 9pm), early mornings (before 8am)
- **Message Spacing:** Don't send more than 5-10 messages per hour to different people
- **Tool:** Use GroupTrack Chrome extension for direct Messenger integration
- **Platform Link:** https://www.facebook.com/messages/t (use this, not app—more stable)

### Cold Leads (Inflect.com Scraping)

#### Lead Scraping Setup

```
Step 1: Go to Inflect.com → Tools → Instagram Scraper
Step 2: Set Filters:
  - Keywords in bio: "coach", "consultant", "advisor" (adjust for niche)
  - Follower count: 1-3k range (or test different ranges: 2-5k, 5-10k)
  - Country: Your target
  - Category: Entrepreneur, Coach, Consultant, etc.

Step 3: Download CSV files in batches (1k profiles per export)
Step 4: Merge all CSVs into one Google Sheet
Step 5: Create "DM" column with checkboxes to track outreach
Step 6: Create column for response status (No Response, Responded, Booked Call, etc.)
```

### Instagram Best Practices

- **Follow + Like Strategy:** Follow + Like 3-4 posts before sending first DM
- **Opener Formula:** "Completely off base but saw you're a [profession]—how long have you been doing that?"
- **Target Profile:** 2-10k followers = sweet spot (high engagement, owner-operator)
- **Personalization:** Don't ask "What do you do?" directly—read their bio first
- **Conversation Pace:** Ask easy questions that get short replies (momentum building)
- **Natural Transition:** Move to business naturally based on their answers
- **Timing:** Send messages 1-2 hours apart for conversational pacing
- **Avoid:** Account bans from mass messaging too fast; space out actions, personalize

- Engagers on your tweets
- Followers with relevant profiles
- Tweet/reply conversation participants

### Strategy

```

Hey! Saw your reply on [topic]—interesting take.

### Best Practices

- **Keep it native:** Don't hard-pitch immediately—engage first
- **Timeline advantage:** Twitter shows real-time interests (recent tweets = current focus)

## CROSS-PLATFORM PRINCIPLES

### Quality Scoring
1. **Story Engagement > Post Engagement**
   - Stories show higher intent (user watched entire story)
   - Prioritize story viewers/engagers over post likers
   - Result: Better response rates from story-based outreach

2. **Warm Leads Beat Cold by 5-10x**
   - Cold Facebook: 2-5% conversion to calls
   - Warm Facebook: 10-20% conversion to calls
   - Investment in warm audience = highest ROI

3. **Engagement Type Hierarchy** (Best to Worst)
   - Story responders (2-way engagement)
   - Survey respondents (self-qualified)
   - Action post commenters (high intent)
   - Story viewers (passive interest)
   - Post likers (lowest intent)
   - New followers (depends on source)

### Personalization at Scale
- **Minimum Personalization:** Mention one thing from their profile/post
- **Time Allocation:** Spend 2 minutes on first 10 custom messages, then templates
- **Scale Formula:** Personalize 20-30%, use templates 70%
- **Exception:** Never fully template the opener—always customize at least one line

### Account Safety
- **Avoid Bans:** Wait for friend request acceptance before messaging (Facebook)
- **Message Spacing:** Max 5-10 messages per hour to different people
- **Avoid Generic Spam:** Same message to 50+ people = account risk
- **Safe Practices:** Personalize, space actions, use legitimate openers
