## PAIN DISCOVERY TEMPLATES (Phase 2)

### Market Assumption Framework
```
Structure: [Observation] + [Industry Assumption] + [Question]

Template 1:
"I noticed you [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION]. Usually when people [COMMON SITUATION],
they struggle with [ASSUMED PAIN]. Is that something you're dealing with?"

Template 2:
"I saw you're pretty active on [PLATFORM] with [TYPE] of content. Most [PROFESSION]s
I know who post consistently are either getting tons of leads or not getting ANY.
Which camp are you in?"

Template 3:
"You seem like you've got the [SKILL] down. The one thing most people struggle with
in your position is [COMMON BOTTLENECK]. How are you handling that?"

Template 4:
"I noticed you mention [GOAL] a lot. Usually when people want that, the thing that
stops them is [SPECIFIC PROBLEM]. Are you running into that?"

Template 5:
"You've clearly built [ACHIEVEMENT]. What's the next big thing you want to accomplish?"
```

### Direct Pain Questions
```
What's the biggest focus for you right now to get to [THEIR GOAL]?

So what do you feel like you need in order to hit [THEIR GOAL]?

What do you think is holding you back from being at [THEIR GOAL] right now?

What are you currently trying in regards to [THEIR FOCUS AREA]?

If [PROBLEM] wasn't an issue and you had that part nailed, where would you want
your [METRIC] to be by the end of the year?

How are you currently getting most of your [DESIRED OUTCOME]?

In your opinion, what does your business need most right now?

When you think about [THEIR GOAL], what's the biggest gap between where you are
and where you want to be?
```

### If They Say "Things Are Going Great"
```
Sounds like things are going pretty well for you then!
Just out of curiosity, is there anything you'd like to improve on if you could?

[OR]

That's awesome. So when you think about the next 12 months, what's one area
you'd like to improve or optimize?

[OR]

Got it. I'm just curious—if you had unlimited [RESOURCE: time/money/knowledge],
what's the ONE thing you'd change about your business right now?
```

## CALL INVITATION TEMPLATES (Phase 3)

### Soft Invitation Format
```
[Recap what they told you] + [Your belief in solution] + [Soft invitation] + [CTA]

Template 1:
Based on what you've told me, I think we might actually be able to help you
with [THEIR SPECIFIC PROBLEM], as we've helped dozens of [PROFESSION]s in your exact situation.

How would you find it to hop on a quick chat to see if we could help in the first place?
No strings attached, just to see if it makes sense. [CALENDLY LINK]

Template 2:
So if I'm hearing you right, you're struggling with [SPECIFIC PROBLEM].
We've built a whole system around this, and it's worked for X number of people just like you.

Would you be open to a 15-minute gameplan call this week?
Just to see if we can help and go from there. [CALENDLY LINK]

Template 3:
Okay so the main thing is [THEIR PAIN], got it. Here's what I'm thinking—
a lot of coaches we work with had the same problem, and we've figured out a way to solve it.

How would you find it if I shared the exact framework we use with you over a quick chat?
[CALENDLY LINK]

Template 4:
For someone in your position trying to hit [GOAL] and dealing with [PAIN],
I think a quick strategy session could be really valuable. No pitch, just us looking at your situation
and seeing if we have something that could help. Sound good? [CALENDLY LINK]

Template 5:
Regardless if you are wanting to be a private client or not, I'd love to give you
some insights on [TOPIC]! Would you be open to do a 15 minute Gameplan chat this week?
[CALENDLY LINK]
```

## OBJECTION HANDLING SCRIPTS

### "I'm not sure if I can afford it right now"
```
Totally get it, that's super common. Here's the thing though—
most people I talk to in your position end up making the investment back 3-5x
within the first month just from implementing one of our frameworks.

That's why it makes sense to jump on a quick call—not to sell you, but to make sure
this is actually a fit before you commit any money. Sound fair? [LINK]

[OR - Payment plan angle]

I totally understand. Here's what most people do—we have a 30-day cash rule where
you can save up if you need to and implement the framework first. No pressure.

Want to just chat and see if this makes sense? [LINK]
```

### "I need to think about it"
```
Totally fair. Here's what I'd recommend—let me send you over [QUICK RESOURCE/FRAMEWORK]
that might help you think through whether this is the right move.

Give it a look and let me know your thoughts. We can chat more after. [LINK TO RESOURCE]

[OR - Shorter version]

Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think about?
Maybe I can clarify something right now that would help you decide.
```

### "I'm already working with someone on this"
```
Got it. How's that been going for you? Getting the results you want?

[LISTEN TO THEIR ANSWER]

Cool. The reason I ask is because a lot of people we work with were in the same boat,
and they found that adding what we do actually accelerated their results.

Not saying you need to switch—just worth exploring if we could complement what they're doing.
Quick call? [LINK]

[OR - Shorter version]

That's cool! How's that been working out? Getting the results you need?
If not, we might be able to help. Worth a quick chat? [LINK]
```

### "I don't have time right now"
```
I totally get it. Most people feel that way. Here's the thing—this is only 15-20 minutes,
and it could save you weeks of figuring this out on your own.

What if we scheduled it for when things calm down? When would that be? [LINK]

[OR - Permission-based]

Totally understand. When do things usually calm down for you?
Could we lock something in for then? [LINK]
```

### "I'm not interested"
```
No worries at all! Just curious—what specifically doesn't appeal to you?
Is it the topic itself or just the timing?

[LISTEN]

Fair enough. Feel free to reach out if anything changes. :)

[OR - If they didn't understand]

No problem! Just so you know, what we do is specifically help [PROFESSION]s with [PAIN].
But if it's not for you right now, no sweat. Let's stay in touch. [LINK TO RESOURCE]
```

### "I want to see some proof/results first"
```
Totally fair! Most people want to see if this actually works before committing.
That's exactly why the strategy call exists—we'll walk through case studies of
[PROFESSION]s we've helped with your exact situation.

Let me show you what other people in your position have achieved. [LINK]

[OR]

I've got some case studies and testimonials from people in your exact situation.
Want me to send those over first? Then if you're interested, we can hop on a quick call.
```

### "Can I just get the course/resource instead of a call?"
```
Great question! Here's why the call is important—your situation is unique.
The course gives you the framework, but the call is where we customize it for YOUR specific business.

Without understanding your exact situation, you might implement it wrong and get frustrated.
That's why the call comes first. Cool? [LINK]

[OR]

Love the enthusiasm! The thing is, most people need specific guidance for their situation.
That's what the call is for—15 minutes to map out exactly what YOU need to implement.
Then you'll have the resources. [LINK]
```
