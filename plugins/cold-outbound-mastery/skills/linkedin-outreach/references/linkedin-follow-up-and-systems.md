# LinkedIn Follow-Up & Systems

## The 2-Bucket Follow-Up System

Follow-ups are HIGH LEVERAGE: more formulaic, lots of copy-paste (just switch names). 20-50% of calls can come through follow-ups alone.

### Bucket 1: 1-6/10 Interest (Haven't Said They Want a Call)
**Strategy: THROW ROPE**
- Foreshadow value, add value, foreshadow introductions
- Give them reasons to reply = answer WIIFM
- Detach from the sale completely in follow-up messaging
- The frame you built in initial DMs carries over even without reply

### Bucket 2: 7-9/10 Interest (Said They'll Book But Haven't)
**Strategy: FRIENDLY CHECK-IN**
- Don't need to throw rope -- already built trust
- Simple nudge: "Hey [name], want to get something booked in with me? Got some time early this week."
- NEVER say "just bumping this up"
- NEVER use meme GIFs (Pablo Escobar, Kermit the Frog, etc.)

---

## Follow-Up Timing

| Situation | Wait Time |
|-----------|-----------|
| They said they'd book | 20-24 hours minimum (never within 24 hours) |
| Less warm prospects | 2-4 days between messages |
| Each subsequent unanswered follow-up | Increase gap: 3 days -> 4-5 days -> 1-2 weeks |

These are rules of thumb, not hard rules. Adapt to the conversation.

---

## When to Stop Following Up

- After 3-4 messages with no answer
- Make a LOGICAL decision, not an emotional one
- "My time is better spent focusing on other prospects that have shown more interest"
- Can re-engage after a few weeks or months
- NEVER stop based on shame or fear -- only stop based on logic

---

## Interest Level Is Malleable

A 7-9/10 prospect who doesn't book can slide to 5-6/10. If they drop:
- Switch from friendly check-in (Bucket 2) to throwing rope (Bucket 1)
- Constantly reassess where they are
- Don't assume interest stays fixed

## 11 Follow-Up Frameworks

1. **Foreshadow value** -- "Let me know what you're struggling with. I might have some free resources to send over, can make intros, etc."
2. **Add value** -- Send hyper-specific free training video or asset
3. **Voice message follow-up** (30-60 sec) -- Relay back to pain points, foreshadow value
4. **Foreshadow introduction** -- "My good friend [name] is amazing at [relevant skill]. I'd be down to make the intro."
5. **Friendly check-in + skill endorsement** -- "Want to get something booked? Just endorsed your top two skills by the way."
6. **Send relevant asset with context + social proof** -- Match the asset to their specific situation
7. **Breakup message** -- "Have you given up on [problem]?" / "Should I close this out?"
8. **Ask question about previously sent asset** -- "Did you get a chance to check out that training?"
9. **Asset + introduction foreshadow combo** -- Combine frameworks 2 and 4
10. **Reference their recent content/post** -- Shows you're paying attention, not just following up robotically
11. **Endorse skills + like recent posts** -- Accompaniment action that shows genuine engagement

## Follow-Up Across Modalities

Always follow up via the most intimate channel available:
1. **Text message** -- Most intimate (use when you have phone number)
2. **DMs** -- Primary channel
3. **Voice message** -- High impact, conveys personality
4. **Email** -- Useful for LinkedIn-to-email bridge

## Connection Request Strategy

### Always Send Empty Requests
Never include a note with connection requests. Empty requests consistently outperform noted ones.

### Automation Guidelines (Dripify)
- Dripify is 100% optional
- ONLY use for automating connection requests, NEVER DMs
- If not getting 40%+ acceptance rate with Dripify, switch to manual
- Manual takes 10-15 min/day but gets 10-20% better acceptance rate
- Send requests Mon-Fri, 8 AM - 1 PM in prospect's timezone

### Targeting Best Practices
- Separate searches by geography to send during business hours in prospect's timezone
- For companies 1-10: Target Founder/CEO
- For companies 11-50: Founder/CEO + CMO/Head of Marketing
- For companies 51-200+: Send to 3-7 people per company

## Sales Systems & Call Booking

### Pre-Call System
1. **Calendly/Calendar** -- Questionnaire to collect info + phone number; limit availability to appear busy
2. **Pre-Call Drip** -- Automated email sequence before the call
3. **Day-of Text** -- "Hey [NAME] it's [YOUR NAME] from [COMPANY]. Talk to you soon. [TIME]" (optional: include Zoom link). Boosts show-up rate by 10%+.

### Post-Call System
4. **Post-Call Drips** -- Combine automated with manual follow-ups (LinkedIn DM, text, email)
   - "Hot" Drip: Shorter intervals between emails (days)
   - "Follow-Up" Drip: Longer intervals (weeks)
   - Automate transition from Hot to Follow-Up drip

### Unqualified Call Handling
5. Review calls booked. If unqualified (financially, niche, competence), send automated cancellation via CRM automation. Don't waste time on unqualified calls.

### Sales Call Doc
6. Create new copy for each prospect with their name. Ask: "Is it OK if I write some notes down while we talk?" (shows genuine care).

### Results Database (The Cheat Code)
Build a database of ALL results:
- Client's situation and pain points
- Their service and niche
- How you helped, time to results, the results
- Visual proof (testimonials, screenshots, videos)

Use to match prospects with relevant case studies in DMs. "The cheat code of all cheat codes to booking sales calls on LinkedIn."

### Sales Assets (Minimum 2-3)
- Free training videos
- Lead magnets (PDFs, guides)
- One-off course modules
- Book recommendations
- Create once, distribute infinitely
- Make sure they're relevant to specific prospect
- Use as excuse to naturally provide social proof

## Content Strategy for Outbound Support

### Posting Cadence
- Minimum: 2 posts/week
- Recommended: 3-5 posts/week
- Can repurpose old content after 3 months

### Content Engagement Strategy
- 10-20 comments/day on other people's content
- Engage with influencers -- they'll start commenting on yours
- Set up engagement groups (WhatsApp, 10-15 creators in adjacent niches)
- When commenting: mention the poster, add value, sneakily insert social proof

### Lead Magnets
- Comment-to-get format: "Comment [keyword] to get this resource"
- Use client results in lead magnets
- Follow-up sequence: Send lead magnet -> ask pain-priming questions
- Start conversations with dozens/hundreds of prospects simultaneously

### Content Rules
- Get to the point quickly
- Hooks must be specific and captivating
- CTAs to comment/book in maximum 20% of posts
- No blocky paragraphs -- must be easy to skim
- Use parentheses, quotes, names, and numbers for scannability
