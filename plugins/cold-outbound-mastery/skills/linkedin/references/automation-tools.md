# LinkedIn Automation Tools: Setup & Configuration

Complete guide to automating LinkedIn outreach (connection requests, DMs monitoring, CRM integration) without losing authenticity. Focus on tools that enhance your system, not replace it.

---

## Core Principle

**Automate what's repetitive. Hand-craft what's personal.**

- ✅ Automate: Connection request scheduling/sending, LinkedIn monitoring, profile growth
- ✅ Automate: CRM data entry, outreach logging, follow-up reminders
- ❌ Never automate: DMs (always handwritten, unique), first-line copy (see first-lines skill)

---

## Primary Tool: Dripify (LinkedIn Connection Automation)

### What Dripify Does

Automates the sending of LinkedIn connection requests on schedule, at optimal times, with consistent daily volume.

- Schedule requests for specific times (8 AM-1 PM in prospect's timezone)
- Send 40-50+ requests/day automatically
- Track acceptance rates and responses
- Integrate with CRM tools

### When to Use Dripify

**Use Dripify when**:
- Targeting 40-50+ requests/day consistently
- Want optimal timing (8 AM prospect local time)
- Have a validated target list (100-500+ prospects)
- Need to maintain volume without manual effort

**Don't use Dripify if**:
- Just starting out (under 20 requests/day)
- Still experimenting with targeting
- Need flexibility to personalize requests
- Don't have enough budget ($50-150/month)

### Dripify Setup Guide

**Step 1: Create Your Target List**

Option A - Upload CSV:
1. Create list of LinkedIn profile URLs (copy from Sales Navigator or LinkedIn Search)
2. Format as CSV: `URL, First Name, Company, Title`
3. Upload to Dripify

Option B - Connect Sales Navigator:
1. Create saved search in Sales Navigator
2. Connect Dripify account
3. Dripify syncs prospects automatically

Option C - Manual entry:
1. Go to LinkedIn Search
2. Find prospects
3. Dripify auto-populates as you browse

**Step 2: Configure Sending Parameters**

1. **Daily Volume**: Set to 40-50 requests/day
2. **Sending Window**: 8 AM - 1 PM
3. **Timezone Handling**: Set to prospect's local timezone
4. **Days**: Monday-Thursday only
5. **Frequency**: Spread throughout sending window (not all at once)

**Step 3: Message Configuration**

- **Include message?** No (skip this - your profile does the work)
- Connection request only, no text message
- Let profile quality drive acceptances

**Step 4: Start Campaign**

1. Review list (100-200 prospects minimum to start)
2. Set daily limit (40-50)
3. Launch campaign
4. Monitor acceptance rate daily

**Step 5: Monitor & Adjust**

**Daily checklist**:
- [ ] 40-50 requests sent
- [ ] Acceptance rate tracking
- [ ] No LinkedIn warnings/restrictions
- [ ] Response volume tracking

**Weekly review**:
- Total requests sent: 200-250
- Total acceptances: 80-100+ (target 40-50%)
- Acceptance rate trending up/down
- Any LinkedIn warnings

**If acceptance rate drops below 30%**:
- Pause campaign
- Review profile (too salesy? weak social proof?)
- Review targeting (too broad? wrong industry?)
- Adjust and restart

### Dripify Pricing & Discount

- **Standard**: $50-100/month for up to 50 requests/day
- **Advanced**: $100-150/month with CRM integration
- **Ty Frankel Discount**: Visit dripify.io/#a_aid=frankel for potential discount

### Dripify Best Practices

1. **Don't oversend**: 40-50/day is sustainable. 100+/day kills acceptance rate.
2. **Combine with manual**: Use Dripify for volume, manual requests for strategic VIPs.
3. **Quality targeting first**: Garbage in = garbage out. Refine target list first.
4. **Don't automate DMs**: Never use Dripify to send DMs. Always hand-craft.
5. **Monitor constantly**: Check daily for LinkedIn warnings or acceptance drops.
6. **Rotate lists**: Use 3-5 different prospect lists to avoid overdoing any one segment.

## Secondary Tool: Sales Navigator

### What Sales Navigator Does

Premium LinkedIn targeting tool that lets you find and filter prospects with advanced criteria (engagement, growth stage, job changes, etc.).

### Sales Navigator Setup

**Step 1: Subscribe**
- Go to LinkedIn.com/sales/navigator
- Subscribe to Sales Navigator ($99/month minimum)
- Link to your main LinkedIn account

**Step 2: Create Saved Searches**

Create 3-5 saved searches for different ICPs:

**Search #1: Agency Owners (Example)**
- Titles: CEO, Founder, Agency Owner
- Company size: 11-50, 51-200
- Location: USA
- Industry: Marketing, Advertising
- Posted in last: 30 days
- Save as "Agencies - 11-200"

**Search #2: SaaS Founders**
- Titles: CEO, Founder, CTO
- Company size: 1-50
- Industry: Software, SaaS
- Location: USA, Canada
- Posted in last: 30 days
- Save as "SaaS Founders"

**Search #3: Services (Consultants, Coaches)**
- Titles: Founder, CEO, Consultant, Coach
- Location: Worldwide
- Industry: Consulting, Coaching, Professional Services
- Posted in last: 30 days
- Save as "Coaches & Consultants"

**Step 3: Add to Accounts**

"Accounts" feature shows company-level insights:
- How many employees match your target title
- Company growth stage
- Hiring activity
- Revenue/funding

Use to find pockets of opportunity (companies actively hiring = growing = need your service).

**Step 4: Lead Recommendations**

Sales Navigator suggests leads based on:
- Your activity (who you engaged with)
- Popular in your network
- Similar to leads you've engaged with
- New connections matching your criteria

Review weekly for high-quality suggestions.

### Sales Navigator Best Practices

1. **Save multiple searches**: Don't rely on one. Test 3-5 different angles.
2. **Sort by engagement**: "Posted in last 30 days" shows active users.
3. **Use account insights**: Not just individual targeting—look at company trends.
4. **Combine with Dripify**: Search in Sales Navigator, export list, feed to Dripify.
5. **Review recommendations**: Sales Navigator's suggestions are often high-quality.
6. **Update searches regularly**: People change jobs. Re-run searches monthly.

## CRM Integration: Tracking & Following Up

**Goal**: Automatically log connections, track conversations, set follow-up reminders.

### Option 1: Close.io (Recommended for Sales)

**Best for**: Sales-focused teams, detailed conversation tracking

**Integration with LinkedIn/Dripify**:
- Add Dripify accepted connections to Close automatically
- Log DM conversations in Close
- Set follow-up reminders
- Track call bookings

**Setup**:
1. Create Close account (freemium available)
2. Connect LinkedIn via Close app
3. Set up "Prospect" custom fields (Acceptance date, DM response, Call booked, etc.)
4. Create pipelines: "Connected," "Responded," "Call Booked," "Client"
5. Log DMs and manually update status

**Cost**: $29-129/month (depending on features)

### Option 2: Pipedrive (Recommended for Simplicity)

**Best for**: Simple sales pipeline, visual management

**Integration**:
- Manual import of accepted connections
- Track each person through pipeline
- Set automation triggers (auto-follow-up after 3 days, etc.)
- Email/LinkedIn integration

**Setup**:
1. Create Pipedrive account
2. Set up custom pipeline: "Connection Requested" → "Connected" → "Engaged" → "Call Booked" → "Client"
3. Add fields: Connection date, DM status, Follow-up date, Call date
4. Import connections weekly

**Cost**: $14-99/month

### Option 3: Notion (DIY, Free/Cheap)

**Best for**: Solo practitioners, maximum flexibility, free/cheap

**Setup Template**:
```
Database: LinkedIn Prospects

Fields:
- Name (text)
- Title (text)
- Company (text)
- LinkedIn URL (URL)
- Connection Date (date)
- DM Sent (checkbox)
- DM Response (text)
- Call Booked (checkbox)
- Call Date (date)
- Status (select: "To Reach Out", "Connected", "Engaged", "Call Booked", "Client", "Archive")
- Notes (text)
```

**Workflow**:
1. Copy LinkedIn profile info into Notion after sending connection request
2. Update "DM Sent" when you send first message
3. Track responses and follow-ups
4. Move to "Call Booked" when scheduled
5. Archive when finished

**Cost**: Free or $10/month

```

1. Open Dripify → confirm 40-50 requests sent ✓

3. DM each new acceptance (handwritten, unique)

Monthly (Strategic):
1. Review metrics:
   - Requests sent: X

   - DM response rate: X%
   - Calls booked: X
2. Adjust targeting based on best-performing ICPs
3. Refresh Sales Navigator searches
4. Update Dripify prospect lists
```

## Critical Rules for Automation

1. **Never automate personalization**: Every DM is handwritten, unique to that person.

2. **Never automate first-touch value**: Your opening line, compliment, or question should always be personal.

3. **Always monitor**: Check daily for LinkedIn warnings, acceptance rate drops, or spam reports.

4. **Manual beats automated**: A small number of handwritten, targeted messages > large number of automated generic messages.

5. **Profile quality first**: Automate once your profile is strong (2+ recs, 2+ posts/week, 500+ connections).

6. **Test small**: Run 100-200 requests manually first. Once you find what works, automate.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

**Issue**: Acceptance rate drops from 45% to 25% after 2 weeks
- **Cause**: LinkedIn limits, or targeting became too broad
- **Fix**: Pause Dripify for 3-5 days, review and refine target list, restart with quality over quantity

**Issue**: Dripify requests aren't sending (stuck in queue)
- **Cause**: LinkedIn API limits, account warnings, or app sync issue
- **Fix**: Check LinkedIn for warnings/restrictions. Disconnect/reconnect Dripify. Restart campaign.

**Issue**: No one responding to DMs even though acceptance rate is good
- **Cause**: DM quality is poor, or you're not sending to real prospects
- **Fix**: Review DM templates (see dm-templates.md). Make sure you're qualifying prospects (check their profile before DM).

**Issue**: Getting "unusual activity" warnings from LinkedIn
- **Cause**: Too many requests too fast (100+/day), or Dripify scheduling wrong
- **Fix**: Reduce to 40-50/day. Make sure Dripify is scheduling throughout the day, not all at once.
