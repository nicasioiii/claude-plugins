## What This Plugin Does

This plugin teaches professional copywriting methodology based on proven frameworks from top copywriters (Todd Brown, Ryan Deiss, Arman Assadi, Adil Amarsi, Sean Vosler, and others).

It covers **copywriting as a system** — not random tips, but step-by-step frameworks for understanding customers, discovering benefits, positioning offers, and structuring pages.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation Skills (Currently Complete)

### 1. Customer Avatar & Audience Research
**Folder:** `skills/customer-avatar-research/`

Learn how to understand your customer deeply before writing a single word.

- Three-layer research framework (demographics → frustrations → aspirations)
- Before-After grid (most valuable exercise for copywriters)
- Audience awareness lifecycle (5 stages from unaware to most-aware)
- Customer interview guide (structured 11-question framework)
- Community research method (extract language from Reddit, Facebook groups)

**Start here if:** You don't know your customer well enough to write compelling copy.

---

### 2. Benefit Discovery & Problem Translation
**Folder:** `skills/benefit-discovery/`

Convert product features into benefits that emotionally resonate.

- Three-layer benefit model (functional → dimensionalized → emotional)
- FAB Chart (Features-Advantages-Benefits template with examples)
- Sensory language guide (activate senses in benefit descriptions)
- Faux benefit detector (avoid benefits that sound good but don't sell)
- Contrast principle (make benefits powerful by showing before/after)

**Start here if:** Your copy focuses on features instead of transformation.

---

### 3. Big Idea & Unique Positioning
**Folder:** `skills/big-idea-positioning/`

Develop the intellectual and emotional concept behind your offer.

- Three components of a Big Idea (interesting, compelling, unique)
- Mechanism interrogation (understand your unique HOW)
- Primary promise formulation (make vague promises specific)
- 5 deconstructed Big Ideas (Slack, Dollar Shave Club, Peloton, Basecamp, Twitch)
- Offer solution framework (problem + promise + mechanism)

**Start here if:** Your positioning feels generic or you struggle to differentiate.

---

### 4. Page Structure & Template Selection
**Folder:** `skills/page-structure-templates/`

Choose and structure the right page type for your goal.

- 5 core page types (digital, physical, service, opt-in, homepage)
- Page type decision tree (choose the right structure)
- 84% rule (above-fold strategy proven to work)
- 7 essential page elements (every page needs these)
- Digital product page template (complete with all sections and copy examples)

**Start here if:** You're building a landing page or not sure what page type to use.
