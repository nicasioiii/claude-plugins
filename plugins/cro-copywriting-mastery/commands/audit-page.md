# Audit Page

This command performs a comprehensive conversion rate optimization audit of any page. You provide the URL or copy, and I'll score each section and identify specific improvements.

## What You'll Get

- Section-by-section audit (headline, copy, structure, CTA, etc.)
- Numerical score for each section (0-100)
- Overall page score (0-100)
- Specific, actionable improvement recommendations
- Priority ranking (quick wins vs. major revisions)
- Testing recommendations

## Questions I'll Ask

1. **Do you have a live URL or should I audit pasted copy?**
   - URL or paste the page content

2. **What type of page is this?**
   - Landing page, sales page, product page, email, ad

3. **What's the conversion goal?**
   - Purchase, signup, demo request, contact, download

4. **Do you know the current conversion rate?**
   - Optional but helps me set realistic benchmarks

## My Process

I'll reference these skills to audit thoroughly:
- **testing-audit-optimization** — Full CRO audit methodology
- **page-structure-templates** — Check structure against best practices
- **headline-hook-engine** — Evaluate headline effectiveness
- **call-to-action-mastery** — Assess CTA clarity and urgency

## Output Format

Your audit will include:

1. **Headline & Hook** — Score + feedback + rewrite suggestion
2. **Opening Copy** — Hooks reader? Clear promise?
3. **Page Structure** — Logical flow? Proper section order?
4. **Benefit Communication** — Clear, ranked, compelling?
5. **Social Proof** — Credibility signals present?
6. **Objection Handling** — Addresses top 3 concerns?
7. **Call-to-Action** — Clear, urgent, easy to find?
8. **Visual/Layout** — Scannability, white space, hierarchy
9. **Mobile Experience** — (If applicable)
10. **Overall Page Score** — 0-100

Final section includes:
- Top 3 quick wins (highest impact, easiest to fix)
- Major structural improvements needed
- Testing roadmap
