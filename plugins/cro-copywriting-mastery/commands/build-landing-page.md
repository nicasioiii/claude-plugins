# Build Landing Page

This command guides you through creating a complete landing page from research through finished copy. You'll work through audience research, positioning, structure, and copy in a logical flow.

## What You'll Get

- Complete landing page copy organized by section
- Research-backed positioning and Big Idea
- Optimized headline with hook
- Clear benefit hierarchy
- Compelling CTA aligned with page goal

## Questions I'll Ask

1. **What type of business/product are you building this for?**
   - Example: SaaS tool, digital course, physical product, service

2. **Who is your ideal audience?**
   - Job title, pain points, goals, experience level

3. **What is the main offer on this page?**
   - Product name, price (if public), key benefit

4. **What's your audience's awareness level?**
   - Unaware of problem, aware but no solution, solution-aware, product-aware

5. **What's the primary goal of this page?**
   - Email signup, product purchase, demo request, consultation booking

## My Process

I'll reference these skills to build your page:
- **customer-avatar-research** — Validate audience targeting
- **benefit-discovery** — Identify top 3-5 benefits
- **big-idea-positioning** — Create compelling positioning
- **page-structure-templates** — Apply proven section structure
- **headline-hook-engine** — Generate high-converting headline
- **copy-flow-organization** — Organize sections for flow
- **call-to-action-mastery** — Craft your CTA

## Output Format

Your landing page will be organized as:

1. **Hero Section** — Headline, subheading, benefit callout
2. **Problem/Desire Section** — What your audience wants
3. **Solution Section** — How you solve it (Big Idea)
4. **Benefits Section** — Ranked benefits with proof
5. **Social Proof** — Trust elements
6. **CTA Section** — Primary conversion point
7. **FAQ** — Top objections addressed
