# Write Headline Command

## What This Command Does

Generates 20+ headline variations organized by formula type, scored against the 6 U's, with specific recommendations for your awareness stage and page type.

---

## Step 1: Gather Context

Before generating headlines, I need:

1. **What is the product/offer?** (Brief description)
2. **Who is the target reader?** (Their role, situation, or identity)
3. **What is the primary benefit or transformation?** (The one big result)
4. **What is the unique mechanism?** (If you have one -- the named method/system/process)
5. **What awareness stage is the audience?** (Unaware / Problem Aware / Solution Aware / Product Aware / Most Aware)
6. **Where will this headline appear?** (Landing page, ad, email subject line, sales page, homepage)
7. **Any specific proof points?** (Numbers, results, timeframes, authority figures)

---

## Step 2: Generate 20+ Headlines by Category

I will generate headlines across these categories, prioritized by your awareness stage:

### For Unaware/Problem Aware audiences:
- Story/Curiosity headlines (4-5 variations)
- Question headlines (3-4 variations)
- Two-part headlines (2-3 variations)

### For Solution Aware audiences:
- How-to headlines (4-5 variations)
- Transformation headlines (3-4 variations)
- PMS formula headlines (2-3 variations)

### For Product Aware audiences:
- Benefit/Result headlines (4-5 variations)
- Objection-busting headlines (3-4 variations)
- Social proof headlines (2-3 variations)

### For Most Aware audiences:
- Command headlines (3-4 variations)
- News/Announcement headlines (3-4 variations)
- Direct offer headlines (3-4 variations)

### Bonus (all stages):
- Clarity/Kernel sentences (2-3 variations)
- The 60-Second Sales Hook compressed to headline format (1-2 variations)

---

## Step 3: Score Top 5 Against the 6 U's

For the top 5 headlines, I score each against:

| U | Definition | Score (0-2) |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| Urgent | Creates time pressure or immediacy | |
| Useful | Provides clear value | |
| Unique | Feels different from everything else | |
| Ultra-specific | Uses precise numbers, dates, amounts | |
| Ultra-relevant | Directly relatable to the reader | |
| Curiosity-arousing | Makes the reader need to know more | |

**Target:** 4 of 6 U's at score 1 or 2.

---

## Step 4: Recommend Top 3 with Rationale

I will select the top 3 headlines and explain:
- Why this headline will work for your awareness stage
- Which formula it uses
- How to test it (what to pair it against)
- Suggested subheadline to pair with it

---

## Step 5: Subheadline Recommendations

For each top headline, I provide a subheadline that:
- Expands the headline with specificity
- Uses future pacing language ("imagine," "picture this," "stop [pain], start [desire]")
- Adds the mechanism or proof point not included in the headline
- Keeps to 15-25 words

---

## Step 6: Copy Massaging (Optional)

If requested, I will take the winning headline through the copy massaging process:

1. Start with the base headline
2. + Intrigue (add unexpected element)
3. + Specificity (add numbers, timeframes)
4. + Proof (add authority or result)
5. + Mechanism name (add your named method)
6. + Positioning (add contrast or "don't trust" qualifier)

Each step produces a progressively stronger headline.
