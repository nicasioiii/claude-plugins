# Write Sales Page

This command builds a complete long-form sales page designed to overcome objections, establish positioning, and drive conversions. You'll get a full page from headline to close.

## What You'll Get

- Full sales page copy (typically 2,000-4,000 words)
- Big Idea positioning statement
- Story-driven narrative structure
- Objection handling integrated throughout
- Strong close with clear CTA

## Questions I'll Ask

1. **What are you selling?**
   - Product/service name and basic description

2. **What's the price point?**
   - Helps me understand objection types and positioning

3. **Who is your target audience?**
   - Job title, industry, pain points, budget authority

4. **What's your unique mechanism?**
   - How you're different, your proprietary process or method

5. **What are the top 3 objections your audience has?**
   - Cost, time, skepticism, comparison to alternatives, etc.

## My Process

I'll reference these skills to build your sales page:
- **big-idea-positioning** — Create unique, defensible positioning
- **copy-flow-organization** — Structure the page for persuasion
- **story-narrative-copywriting** — Weave a compelling narrative
- **objection-psychology-framework** — Address objections strategically

## Output Format

Your sales page will include:

1. **Hero** — Headline, subheading, benefit statement
2. **Hook/Story** — Opening narrative to capture attention
3. **Problem/Desire** — Deep dive into the problem
4. **Positioning** — Big Idea and how you're different
5. **How It Works** — Your mechanism explained
6. **Benefits** — Specific outcomes with proof
7. **Objection Handling** — Addressing common hesitations
8. **Urgency/Scarcity** — Why act now (if applicable)
9. **Close** — Strong CTA with guarantee
