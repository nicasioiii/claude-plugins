# Write Story

This command helps you craft compelling, persuasive stories for your copy. Stories are the most powerful way to communicate benefits and build belief. You'll get a complete story written and structured.

## What You'll Get

- Complete story (800-1,500 words depending on type)
- Story structured using 12-point framework
- Clear connection between story and your offer
- Proof of transformation or benefit
- Emotional hooks to drive reader investment

## Questions I'll Ask

1. **What type of story are you telling?**
   - Origin/founder story, customer case study, personal transformation, before-and-after testimonial, struggle-and-solution narrative

2. **What's the core context or situation?**
   - Where does the story start? What was the problem or challenge?

3. **Who should the audience connect with?**
   - Is this about the customer or you/your founder? Who is the protagonist?

4. **What's the outcome or transformation?**
   - Where does the story end? What changed?

## My Process

I'll reference the **story-narrative-copywriting** skill to construct your story using the proven 12-point framework:

1. Relatable character/situation setup
2. Specific problem or challenge
3. Emotional hook (stakes, desire, fear)
4. Wrong turns or failed attempts
5. Discovery or insight
6. Skepticism or doubt
7. First small win
8. Expanded effort
9. Major breakthrough
10. Transformation or result
11. New normal or possibility
12. Bridge to your solution/offer

## Output Format

Your story will be presented as:

```
[COMPLETE STORY TEXT]

─────────────────────────────────────
STRUCTURE BREAKDOWN:
- Story type and why it works
- Key emotional beats
- Where to place in sales/marketing context
- How to connect story to offer
- Suggested modifications for different audiences
```
