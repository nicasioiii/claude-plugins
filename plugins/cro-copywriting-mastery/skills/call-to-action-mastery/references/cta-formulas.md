# CTA Copy Formulas & Templates

## The Anatomy of a Powerful CTA

A call-to-action is more than a button. It's:
1. **The button/link text** (what the button says)
2. **The surrounding context** (the copy around it)
3. **The visual prominence** (how it stands out)
4. **The clarity of outcome** (what happens when clicked)

This guide focuses on the copy (items 1-2), which directly impacts conversion.

---

## Core CTA Formula

**The Basic Structure:**

[ACTION VERB] + [SPECIFIC BENEFIT/OUTCOME] + [URGENCY/SCARCITY] (optional)

**Examples:**

- "Get Your Free Report" ✓ (action + benefit)
- "Claim Your Spot Before We Hit Capacity" ✓ (action + benefit + urgency)
- "Start Your 60-Day Trial" ✓ (action + benefit + timeframe)
- "Submit" ✗ (no benefit, no clarity)
- "Click Here" ✗ (no benefit, no action clarity)

3. "Discover [Benefit]"

4. "Start [Action]"

   - "Start Your 30-Day Challenge"

5. "Access [Benefit]"

   - "Access Exclusive Content"
   - "Access Your Account"

6. "Download [Specific Item]"
   - "Download The Free Guide"
   - "Download The Template"
   - "Download Your Checklist"

### FORMULA #2: ACTION + BENEFIT + URGENCY

**Structure:** [VERB] [BENEFIT] + [URGENCY]

**Best For:** Conversions where creating urgency increases conversion

**Urgency Types:**

**Time-Based:**
- "Today"
- "This Week"
- "Before [Deadline]"
- "Right Now"

**Scarcity-Based:**
- "Before Spots Fill"
- "While [Limited Resource] Lasts"
- "Before Price Increases"
- "1 of 3 Remaining"

**Examples:**

1. Time Urgency:
   - "Get Your Free Guide Today"
   - "Start Your Trial Before Friday"
   - "Register Now for Tomorrow's Webinar"
   - "Claim Your Discount Today Only"

2. Scarcity Urgency:
   - "Claim Your Spot Before We Hit 100"
   - "Get Early Bird Pricing (3 Spots Left)"
   - "Access the System Before Price Increase"
   - "Reserve Your Spot Now"

3. Consequence Urgency:
   - "Stop Wasting Time—Register Now"
   - "Don't Miss Out—Book Your Consultation"
   - "Lock In Founding Member Pricing"

## Strongest CTA Formulas (By Research)

### #1: Benefit-First CTA

**Formula:** [ACTION] + [SPECIFIC BENEFIT] + [TIMEFRAME/GUARANTEE]

**Why It Works:** Immediate clarity on what they get and when

**Example:**
- "Get Instant Access to the Course (60-Day Guarantee)"
- "Claim Your Free Trial (No Credit Card Required)"
- "Download Your Custom Score in 60 Seconds"

**Conversion Boost:** +15-25% vs. generic CTAs

---

### #2: Curiosity-Gap CTA (With Benefit Hint)

**Formula:** [CURIOSITY] + [VAGUE BENEFIT HINT]

**Why It Works:** Creates intrigue while maintaining clarity

**Example:**
- "See Your Personalized Plan"
- "Discover Your Results"
- "Find Out What's Possible for You"

**Conversion Boost:** +10-30% depending on audience

---

### #3: Number-Based CTA

**Formula:** [SPECIFIC NUMBER] + [BENEFIT]

**Why It Works:** Numbers are specific, credible, concrete

**Example:**
- "Get Your 7-Point Score"
- "See Your 5-Step Plan"
- "Access 10 Ready-To-Use Templates"

**Conversion Boost:** +8-20%

---

### #4: Action-Speed Based

**Formula:** [FAST ACTION] + [SPECIFIC TIME]

**Why It Works:** Reduces perceived friction

**Example:**
- "Get Started in 60 Seconds"
- "Create Your Plan in 5 Minutes"
- "Join in Less Than a Minute"

**Conversion Boost:** +12-22%

## CTA Button Text vs. Surrounding Copy

### The Button Should Say:

**[Primary Action]** in 2-4 words max

✓ "Get Instant Access"
✓ "Claim Your Spot"
✓ "Start Your Trial"
✓ "Register Now"
✗ "Click Here to Register for Our Event"
✗ "Fill Out This Form to Get Access to the Course"

### The Surrounding Copy Should Explain:

All the context, benefit, and urgency

**Example Structure:**

**Copy Above Button:**
"Ready to transform your copywriting in 30 days? This proven system has helped over 10,000 professionals generate 6-figures through direct response copy. Join the challenge risk-free."

**Button Text:**
"Start My 30-Day Challenge"

**Copy Below Button:**
"No credit card required. Money-back guarantee if you don't see results. Join now and get $500 in bonus templates."

## Implementation Checklist for Your CTAs

- [ ] **Benefit-Focused** — Does the button text communicate clear benefit? (Not just "Submit" or "Next")

- [ ] **Action-Oriented** — Starts with strong action verb? (Get, Claim, Start, Join, etc.)

- [ ] **Specific** — Names the specific benefit or item? (Not just "Get Access" but "Get Your Checklist")

- [ ] **Brief** — 2-5 words maximum on button text?

- [ ] **Contrast** — Visually stands out from page background?

- [ ] **Context** — Surrounding copy explains benefit, value, urgency?

- [ ] **Clarity of Outcome** — Visitor knows what happens after click? (Where they'll go, what they'll see)

- [ ] **Multiple CTAs** — Primary (hero), secondary (mid-page), final (before footer)?

- [ ] **Tested** — A/B tested different CTA copy?

- [ ] **Consistency** — All CTAs point to same action, or are secondary CTAs softer (alternative action)?

## Quick Testing Framework

**Test These CTA Variations:**

1. **Benefit-Based vs. Action-Based**
   - "Get Your Free Checklist" vs. "Download Now"

2. **Specific vs. Generic**
   - "Get Your Custom Copywriting Score" vs. "Get Started"

3. **Urgent vs. Relaxed**
   - "Claim Your Spot Before Friday" vs. "Get Started Anytime"

4. **Curiosity vs. Direct**
   - "Discover What's Possible" vs. "Get Instant Access"

5. **Single vs. Multiple Step**
   - "Start Trial" vs. "Start 7-Day Trial for $7"

**Track:** Click-through rate, conversion rate, time to conversion

**Winner:** Whichever drives highest conversion and lowest cost-per-acquisition
