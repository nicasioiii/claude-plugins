# The 7-Step Copy Structure: Quick Reference

Quick structure for when you need to write copy fast without losing effectiveness.

## STEP 1: HOOK (Headline + Opening)
**Length:** 1-3 sentences

**Purpose:** Get attention + establish relevance

**Formula:** Promise + Curiosity OR Benefit + Problem ID

**Examples:**
- "Cut Your Energy Bills in Half (Without Solar Panels)"
- "The Weird Trick Your Utility Company Doesn't Want You to Know"
- "Ready to Stop Wasting $300/Month on Overpriced Energy?"

---

## STEP 2: PROBLEM AGITATION
**Length:** 3-5 sentences

**Purpose:** Make problem real, urgent, emotional

**What to cover:**
1. Name problem specifically
2. Show consequence of inaction
3. Add emotional layer
4. Create urgency

**Template:**
"Every month, you [problem action]. You feel [emotion]. It's costing you [specific amount]. And it's only getting [worse/more expensive]. The time to act is [now/limited]."

**Example:**
"Every month, you watch your energy bill arrive. You open it, and your stomach sinks. Another $300+ gone. And it keeps rising. The clock is ticking—prices go up every month."

---

## STEP 3: TRUST/CREDIBILITY BUILD
**Length:** 2-4 sentences

**Purpose:** Show you understand + prove you're qualified

**What to cover:**
- Personal experience (been there yourself) OR credentials (expert background) OR results (proof you can deliver)

**Template:**
"I [personal experience/credential]. I've [results/experience]. I've seen [what others do wrong]. And I found [what works]."

**Example:**
"I spent 15 years in the energy industry. I've helped 10,000+ homeowners cut their bills. I've seen every trick in the book. And I found the one thing that actually works."

---

## STEP 4: SOLUTION INTRODUCTION
**Length:** 2-3 sentences

**Purpose:** Make solution clear and simple

**What to cover:**
1. State solution simply
2. Use metaphor for understanding
3. Hint at results (don't reveal everything)

**Template:**
"The solution is [simple description]. Think of it like [metaphor]. And when you [simple action], [benefit hint]."

**Example:**
"The solution is a 3-step system. Think of it like rerouting water through a dam instead of down a cliff. And when you follow it exactly, your bills drop immediately."

---

## STEP 5: BENEFITS DETAIL (THE MEAT - 40-60% of copy)
**Length:** 40-60% of total copy

**Purpose:** Paint transformation; create desire

**What to cover (for EACH major benefit):**
1. Functional benefit (what mechanically happens)
2. Dimensionalized benefit (what it looks like in real life)
3. Emotional benefit (how it makes you feel)
4. Future vision (how life improves)

**Template:**
"You'll [benefit]. That means [specific real-world outcome]. Which is [emotional impact]. Imagine [future state painting]."

**Example:**
"You'll cut your energy bill in half. That's $150 instead of $300. Every month, when the bill arrives, you'll smile instead of groan. Over a year, that's $1,800 in your pocket for things that matter."

**Key:** Expand EVERY major benefit. This is where persuasion happens. Most copywriters under-invest here.

---

## STEP 6: OBJECTION HANDLING
**Length:** 10-20% of copy

**Purpose:** Address doubts before reader has them

**What to cover (top 3-5 objections):**

**Template for each objection:**
"You might be wondering [objection]. That's a fair question. Here's the truth: [answer]. Here's proof: [evidence/testimonial]."

**Example:**
"You might be wondering: 'Is this complicated?' That's fair. Here's the truth: 95% of users set it up in under 30 minutes. Here's proof: Sarah set it up in 20 minutes with no technical background."

**Order by frequency:**
1. Performance risk ("Will this work for me?")
2. Financial risk ("Can I afford this?")
3. Effort risk ("Is it complicated?")
4. Buyer's remorse risk ("What if I regret this?")
5. Comparison risk ("Why you vs. others?")

---

## STEP 7: OFFER + CTA
**Length:** 2-4 sentences

**Purpose:** State offer clearly + create urgency + tell what to do

**What to cover:**
1. State offer/price clearly
2. Create authentic scarcity/urgency
3. Give specific action steps
4. Remind of benefit

**Template:**
"Here's the offer: [price] for [what they get]. We're raising the price to [higher amount] [when]. [Scarcity/urgency signal]. Click below. [Action step]. [Action step]. You'll [benefit reminder]."

**Example:**
"Here's the deal: $297 for the complete system. We're raising the price to $397 next month. Only 10 spots available at this price. Click below. Add to cart. Check out. Start saving within 30 minutes."

---

## TEMPLATE OVERVIEW

**7-STEP COPY TEMPLATE**

[HOOK: 1-3 sentences]

[PROBLEM AGITATION: 3-5 sentences]

[TRUST/CREDIBILITY: 2-4 sentences]

[SOLUTION INTRO: 2-3 sentences]

[BENEFITS DETAIL: 40-60% of copy - multiple paragraphs]

[OBJECTION HANDLING: 10-20% of copy - multiple objections covered]

[OFFER + CTA: 2-4 sentences]

---

## WORD COUNT EXPECTATIONS

For 7-step structure:

- **Short version:** 300-500 words
- **Medium version:** 700-1,200 words
- **Long version:** 1,500-3,000 words

The structure stays the same; you just expand each section.

---

## QUICK CHECKLIST

Before finalizing any 7-step copy:

- [ ] Hook works (reader knows in 5 sec this is for them)
- [ ] Problem is REAL (reader feels the pain)
- [ ] Solution is SIMPLE (not overwhelming)
- [ ] Benefits are DETAILED (functional → dimensional → emotional)
- [ ] Objections ADDRESSED (top 3-5 pre-empted)
- [ ] Offer is CLEAR (price, what included, what happens)
- [ ] CTA is SPECIFIC (exact next step)

---

## EXAMPLE: COMPLETE 7-STEP COPY

**[HOOK]**
"Cut Your Energy Bills in Half Without Complicated Equipment or Solar Panels"

**[PROBLEM AGITATION]**
"Every month, your energy bill arrives. You watch it inch upward. $300, $320, $350. It feels unfair. It IS unfair. You're paying more than neighbors for the same service. The cost keeps rising. And you feel powerless to stop it."

**[TRUST/CREDIBILITY]**
"I spent 12 years in the energy industry. I've helped 8,000+ homeowners slash their bills. I've seen every trick companies use. And I've discovered the one thing that actually works—and costs almost nothing to implement."

**[SOLUTION INTRO]**
"The solution is a 3-step system I call the Energy Redirect Method. Think of it like rerouting water through a different channel. Same water, different path, way more efficient. When you follow these 3 steps, your consumption drops immediately."

**[BENEFITS DETAIL - FUNCTIONAL]**
"You'll reduce your energy consumption by an average of 40%. That's not theoretical—that's from tracking 8,000+ homes. If you're paying $300/month, you're now paying $180. That's $120 every single month. Or $1,440 every year. Money that stays in your pocket."

**[BENEFITS DETAIL - EMOTIONAL]**
"But here's what matters more: How you FEEL. Next month when the bill arrives, instead of that gut-sinking feeling, you'll smile. You'll actually WANT to open it because you know you're winning. You'll tell your spouse. You might even brag to neighbors. That's not just money saved—that's peace of mind."

**[BENEFITS DETAIL - FUTURE VISION]**
"Imagine this: Five years from now, while neighbors grumble about their $400+ bills, you're paying $180. That's $13,200 you've kept instead of giving away. Money for your kids' college fund. Money for a vacation. Money for security. All because you took 30 minutes to learn this system."

**[OBJECTION HANDLING #1]**
"You might be wondering: 'Is this complicated?' No. 95% of users complete it in 30 minutes. You follow a simple checklist. No special knowledge needed."

**[OBJECTION HANDLING #2]**
"You might think: 'Will this actually work for me?' Yes. It works in apartments, houses, condos, mobile homes. We've tested across 50 states. 99% see results within 30 days."

**[OBJECTION HANDLING #3]**
"You might worry: 'What if I don't like it?' Full refund. 60 days. We're that confident."

**[OFFER + CTA]**
"Here's the offer: $297 for the complete system (guide + implementation checklist + support). We're raising the price to $397 next month as demand increases. Only 10 more spots at this price. Click below. Add to cart. Checkout takes 2 minutes. You'll get instant access. Start implementing today."

---

## KEY INSIGHT

The 7-step structure is a framework, not a straitjacket. You can expand/compress sections based on your offer and audience. But the flow should stay the same: Hook → Problem → Trust → Solution → Benefits → Objections → Offer.

This structure has worked across every industry, every offer type, every medium. Learn it. Use it. Master it.
