# The 7-Email Welcome Sequence: Complete Template

Based on Adil Amarsi's "Encyclopedia of Copy" formula—converts 30-91.4% depending on market fit.

## EMAIL 1: WELCOME TO THE PARTY

**Send:** Day 1 (immediately after opt-in)

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "Welcome To The Party, Pal!"
- "[Name], Your [Lead Magnet] Is Ready"
- "Your [Benefit] Starts Today"

**Structure:**

### Opening
Greetings + Brief Credibility Signal (Who you are, 1-2 authority signals)

**Example:**
"Hi [Name],

Welcome! I'm [Your Name]. I help [target market] [achieve big benefit].

In the past [timeframe], I've [big result]. I've worked with [credible clients/companies]. And I've learned a few things about [topic]."

### Personal Touch (2-3 personality traits—humanize)

**Example:**
"Before I say anything else: I'm obsessed with coffee. I live in [city]. And I believe [authentic belief]."

### Set Expectations (What they'll receive, frequency)

**Example:**
"Here's what to expect from me:

You'll get emails [frequency] with [type of content: strategies, frameworks, lessons, wins, stories].

I'll never send you junk. Every email is written just for you and serves one purpose: to help you [desired outcome]."

### Key Instructions (Ensure deliverability + engagement)

**Example:**
"1. **Reply to this email.** Just say 'hi' or ask a question. This helps me know you're there AND keeps me out of your spam folder.

2. **Create a folder for my emails.** Call it '[Your Name] Strategies' or whatever you want. This keeps my emails organized.

3. **Set up an automatic rule.** In Gmail/Outlook, create a filter to move my emails to that folder automatically. [Insert video link if helpful]

4. **Let me know what you're working on.** Reply and tell me: What's your biggest challenge right now? I'll take your answers seriously."

### Transparency on Selling (Be honest about future sales)

**Example:**
"Here's one thing I need to be transparent about: I'm going to sell you stuff over the course of our relationship.

Not today. Not tomorrow. But eventually, I'll present offers that can help you [achieve benefit].

Here's why: My consulting rates are $[high amount] per hour. I can't afford to give everything away for free. But I also genuinely want to help you. So I'll offer products at [discount/fraction] of my consulting rates.

Think of it as a way to work with me even if you can't afford one-on-one consulting."

### Reinforce Lead Magnet (Remind what they got)

**Example:**
"Your [Lead Magnet Name] is attached [in email/download link]. It contains [specific benefit it provides]."

### Call to Action (Next step)

**Example:**
"Download it now. Spend 15 minutes with it. Then reply to this email and tell me what you think."

### Personality Sign-Off

**Example:**
"Looking forward to hearing from you.

[Your Name]
P.S. Seriously, reply. I read every email."

## EMAIL 2: WINE & DINE (VALUE DELIVERY)

**Send:** Day 3

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "The #1 Mistake [Target Market] Makes"
- "What I Wish I'd Known About [Topic]"
- "[Name], Here's Something For You"

**Structure:**

### Reference Email 1 (Continuity + Personal)

**Example:**
"Hey [Name],

Thanks for replying to my last email. I loved hearing about [their response]. It's exactly what I see most [target market] struggling with.

Which is why I want to share something today."

### Deliver Real Value (Insight, framework, tip they can use immediately)

**Example:**
"Most [target market] think [common belief].

But here's what I've learned: [corrected belief].

Why? Because [explanation].

Here's how you use it: [3-5 step framework they can implement today]."

### Tell a Story or Share Lesson (Personal touch, not generic)

**Example:**
"I learned this the hard way.

Three years ago, I was [situation]. I thought [belief]. So I [action].

Result? [consequence].

That's when I realized [insight]. And everything changed."

### Build Authority Subtly (Results, experience, credentials)

**Example:**
"Since I figured this out, I've [result]. I've watched my clients [result]. And it's been the single biggest [transformation] in my business."

### Soft CTA (Light action, not "buy this")

**Example:**
"Try this one thing and let me know what happens. Reply to this email or leave a comment."

## EMAIL 3: ON-TWO SETUP (CREATE CURIOSITY)

**Send:** Day 5

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "Quick Question For You"
- "About What I Mentioned Yesterday..."
- "[Name], Something's Coming Tomorrow"

**Structure:**

### Reference Previous Email (Show continuity)

**Example:**
"Did you try that framework I shared?

Someone replied saying [result]. Another said [result].

But here's the thing I didn't mention yesterday..."

### Create Open Loop (Hint at something coming)

**Example:**
"There's one more element that makes it all work.

And tomorrow, I'm going to show you exactly what it is."

### Tease Benefit (Don't fully explain yet)

**Example:**
"Once you add this piece, conversions jump.

I'm talking 2-3x improvement.

But it's not what you'd expect."

### Build Anticipation (Foreshadow next email)

**Example:**
"Tomorrow, I'll break down how [big result happens].

You'll see exactly how to [implementation].

And I'll show you proof from [testimonial/results]."

### Soft CTA

**Example:**
"Hit reply and let me know: Are you ready for this?"

## EMAIL 4: CALL TO ARMS (EMOTIONAL ACTIVATION)

**Send:** Day 7

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "Here's The Truth"
- "This Changes Everything"
- "[Name], Are You Ready?"

**Structure:**

### Identify Problem (Paint the pain)

**Example:**
"Every day, [target market] struggle with [problem].

They [action] trying to solve it.

Nothing works.

So they [consequence]."

### Make Emotional (Appeal to feelings, not just facts)

**Example:**
"And they feel [emotion].

They feel like [emotion].

Like they're [emotion].

Like no matter what they do, [emotion]."

### Show Stakes (What's at risk if nothing changes)

**Example:**
"If nothing changes in the next 90 days:

You'll still be [painful situation].

You'll have spent another $[amount] on [failed attempts].

Your [relationship/health/business] will be [worse state].

And you'll be another year older wondering 'why didn't I fix this?'"

### Introduce Solution (Your offer as answer)

**Example:**
"But here's the good news:

It doesn't have to be this way.

There's a system that [solution description].

It works because [mechanism].

And when you use it, [big result]."

### Stronger CTA (More action-oriented)

**Example:**
"I'm opening [limited spots/limited time] to work with people on this.

Click below to see if you qualify."

## EMAIL 5: TESTIMONIAL EMAIL (SOCIAL PROOF)

**Send:** Day 9

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "What [Customer Name] Said"
- "This Is Why People Pay Me"
- "Real Results From Real People"

**Structure:**

### Lead with Compelling Testimonial (Quote or transcript)

**Example:**
"Here's what [Customer Name], [brief credential], said after [timeframe]:

'[Full testimonial quote describing transformation]

I was [before]. Now I'm [after]. This changed my [life/business/health].'

— [Name], [title/company]"

### Explain What They Experienced (Context + result)

**Example:**
"[Name] came to me struggling with [problem].

She'd already tried [attempted solutions].

Nothing worked.

So here's what we did: [brief explanation].

Within [timeframe], she had [specific result]."

### Show Transformation (Before → After)

**Example:**
"Before: [specific before-state: metric, feeling, situation]

After: [specific after-state: metric, feeling, situation]

That's a [x]% improvement in [what matters to them]."

### Include Multiple Testimonials (Show pattern)

**Example:**
"Here's another one:

'[Second testimonial from different person]'

— [Name], [credential]"

### CTA to Offer

**Example:**
"Results like these are why people work with me.

Want to see if you can get similar results?

Click below to learn how."

## EMAIL 6: CASE STUDIES (DEEP PROOF)

**Send:** Day 11

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "Here's How [Customer] Did It"
- "Case Study: $[X] in Results"
- "[Customer] Went From [Before] to [After]"

**Structure:**

### Story Setup (Who, situation, context)

**Example:**
"[Name] was a [description].

They had [situation].

They wanted [goal].

But [obstacle] was in the way."

### Their Challenge (What they were struggling with)

**Example:**
"They'd been [struggling] for [timeframe].

They'd tried [attempt 1], [attempt 2], [attempt 3].

Nothing worked.

They were ready to give up."

### What Changed (What they did differently)

**Example:**
"Then they discovered [your solution].

The first thing that happened: [result 1].

Then [result 2].

By [timeframe], [major transformation]."

### Specific Results (Numbers, metrics, transformation)

**Example:**
"Specific numbers:

- Revenue went from $[X] to $[Y] ([percentage]% increase)
- They went from working [before schedule] to [after schedule]
- Their [metric] improved by [percentage]%"

### Why It Worked (The mechanism)

**Example:**
"Why did it work?

Because [mechanism name] forces [result].

Once they understood [principle], everything became clear.

And once you understand it too, your situation will transform similarly."

### CTA

**Example:**
"You have the same opportunity they did.

Click below to see how."

## EMAIL 7: SALES EMAIL (DIRECT OFFER)

**Send:** Day 13

**Subject Line Ideas:**
- "Here's My Offer"
- "[Name], Final Email Before We Close Doors"
- "What's Next"

**Structure:**

### Reference Relationship Built (After 6 emails together)

**Example:**
"Over the past two weeks, I've shared:

- The framework that changed my business
- The principle that 85% of people miss
- Real case studies from people like you

You've seen the proof. You know it works."

### Remind of Transformation Possible (What's possible for them)

**Example:**
"Imagine where you could be in 90 days.

Not working [stressful situation].

Actually [desired situation].

Your [metric] improving by [percentage].

Your [life area] transforming."

### Present Offer Clearly (What they get)

**Example:**
"Here's what I'm offering:

[Product/Service Name]

It includes:

- [Component 1]: [What it does]
- [Component 2]: [What it does]
- [Component 3]: [What it does]
- [Bonus 1]: [Value]
- [Bonus 2]: [Value]

Normally, this costs $[value].

Today, I'm offering it at $[discounted price]."

### Guarantee/Risk Reversal (Remove fear)

**Example:**
"Here's my guarantee:

If you're not completely satisfied in 60 days, I'll refund 100% of your investment.

No questions. No hassle. We'll even part as friends.

I'm that confident this will work for you."

### Strong CTA (Clear, urgent)

**Example:**
"Click below. Enter your information. Complete checkout.

You'll get instant access.

Start implementing today.

Your transformation is 30 days away."

### P.S. (Urgency + offer sweetener)

**Example:**
"P.S. I'm only accepting [X] people this month.

We're at [number] so far.

First come, first served.

This offer closes [date].

Don't wait."

## EMAIL SEND SCHEDULE

**Recommended spacing:**

- Email 1: Day 1 (immediately)
- Email 2: Day 3 (+2 days)
- Email 3: Day 5 (+2 days)
- Email 4: Day 7 (+2 days)
- Email 5: Day 9 (+2 days)
- Email 6: Day 11 (+2 days)
- Email 7: Day 13 (+2 days)

**Alternative schedule (faster):**
- Email 1: Day 1
- Email 2: Day 2
- Email 3: Day 3
- Email 4: Day 4
- Email 5: Day 5
- Email 6: Day 6
- Email 7: Day 7

Choose spacing based on your market: cold lists need more spacing; warm lists can go faster.

---

## CONVERSION EXPECTATIONS

- **Cold list (brand new audience):** 10-20% conversion
- **Warm list (some familiarity):** 30-50% conversion
- **Hot list (existing audience):** 50-91.4% conversion

The better your lead magnet matches your offer, the higher the conversion.

---

## KEY ELEMENTS ACROSS ALL EMAILS

- **Conversational tone** throughout (like friend to friend)
- **Short paragraphs** (2-3 lines max)
- **Specific details** (not vague claims)
- **Personality** (not corporate)
- **Multiple CTAs** in longer emails (top, middle, bottom)
- **P.S. sections** (reinforce key benefit)
- **Sensory language** (help reader picture/feel)
