# Mind Reader Shortcuts -- Complete Guide

These 22 deeply held beliefs shape your One Reader at a level deeper than surface-level benefits or Cialdini's principles. They are universal human assumptions that color how people interpret every message.

**Source:** Copy Hackers (Joanna Wiebe) -- 10x Landing Pages

## How to Use This Reference

For each shortcut, there are three strategies:
1. **Roll with it** -- Tap into the belief and write the page around it
2. **Change it** -- Present counter-evidence to shift the belief
3. **State the opposite** -- Intrigue them by contradicting what they believe

Choose ONE shortcut as the foundation for your page. Do not try to leverage all 22.

---

## The 22 Mind Reader Shortcuts

### 1. The World Is Against Me
**The belief:** External forces are conspiring to keep me down. The system is rigged.

**Roll with it:** "You've done everything right. You've followed the rules. And yet the results aren't there. That's because the game is rigged against people like you -- unless you know the workaround."

**Change it:** "Here's the truth nobody wants to hear: the world isn't against you. But it IS indifferent. That indifference is actually your greatest opportunity, because most people won't bother to..."

**State the opposite:** "What if the world isn't against you? What if you've just been playing a different game than the one that rewards people like you?"

**Best for:** Underdog positioning, challenger brands, alternative solutions

---

### 2. It's Us Against Them
**The belief:** There is an in-group (us) and a hostile out-group (them). Community through shared opposition.

**Roll with it:** "While the agencies charge $15K/month for what you could do yourself, you and your fellow [community] members are taking control of your own results."

**Change it:** "The 'them' you think you're fighting? They're dealing with the same problems you are. The real enemy isn't another company -- it's the outdated approach everyone is using."

**State the opposite:** "What if there's no 'them'? What if the people you think are your competition are actually your best source of growth?"

**Best for:** Community-driven products, membership programs, movement brands

---

### 3. It's Not My Fault / There Must Be Someone to Blame
**The belief:** My current situation is caused by external factors, not my choices.

**Roll with it:** "It's not your fault your ads aren't converting. The platforms changed the rules. But now there's a way to win under the new rules..."

**Change it:** "Here's the uncomfortable truth: your results are a direct reflection of your system. Not your effort, not your talent -- your system. Change the system, change the results."

**State the opposite:** "Take responsibility for this one thing, and everything else gets easier."

**Best for:** Coaching programs, skill-building products, system-based solutions

---

### 4. They're Lying to Us
**The belief:** Authorities, companies, and institutions are not telling the truth.

**Roll with it:** "The industry has been telling you [common advice] for years. But the data tells a completely different story. Here's what they're not showing you..."

**Change it:** "Not everyone is lying to you. But most advice-givers have a blind spot: they can't see past what worked for THEM."

**State the opposite:** "What if the advice you've been ignoring is the advice that actually works?"

**Best for:** Contrarian positioning, myth-busting content, disruption narratives

---

### 5. It's Too Late
**The belief:** The best opportunities have passed. I'm too far behind. The market is saturated.

**Roll with it:** "If you've tried everything and nothing has worked, it's not too late -- it just means you haven't found the right approach yet."

**Change it:** "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now. Here's what's different about starting today..."

**State the opposite:** "It's not too late. In fact, the timing has never been better, because [specific market shift that creates new opportunity]."

**Best for:** Opportunity-based offers, comeback narratives, market-timing products

---

### 6. I'm Not in Control
**The belief:** Debt, family obligations, circumstances, or other people control my outcomes.

**Roll with it:** "When everything feels out of your control -- the algorithms, the economy, your boss's decisions -- the one thing you CAN control is [specific actionable thing]."

**Change it:** "You have more control than you think. The problem isn't control -- it's visibility. When you can see exactly what's happening in your [area], you can steer it."

**State the opposite:** "You already control the most important lever. You just don't know it yet."

**Best for:** Dashboard/analytics tools, personal development, automation products

---

### 7. I'm Not Worthy
**The belief:** I don't deserve the good outcome. It's for other people, not someone like me.

**Roll with it:** "You don't need to be special. You don't need a degree. You don't need permission. You just need the right system."

**Change it:** "Stop waiting to feel worthy. Worthiness is a feeling, not a prerequisite. The people who succeed aren't more worthy -- they simply started."

**State the opposite:** "You're already worthy. The only thing missing is the tool."

**Best for:** Democratized products, beginner-friendly solutions, inclusive brands

---

### 8. Others Know a Secret That I Don't
**The belief:** Successful people have insider knowledge that gives them an unfair advantage.

**Roll with it:** "There IS a secret. And it's been hiding in plain sight. The top 1% of [profession] all use the same approach, and today you're getting access to it."

**Change it:** "There's no secret. There's a system. And systems can be learned by anyone willing to follow the steps."

**State the opposite:** "The 'secret' everyone's looking for? It's not a secret at all. It's the boring fundamentals done consistently."

**Best for:** Course/training products, insider access offers, premium memberships

---

### 9. If I Could Just Get/Do/Have X, Life Would Get Better
**The belief:** One specific thing is the key to everything improving.

**Roll with it:** "You've been right all along. The one thing standing between you and [desired outcome] is [your product/solution]."

**Change it:** "No single thing will fix everything. But there IS one thing that makes everything else easier. And it's not what you think."

**State the opposite:** "What if you already have everything you need -- and the only thing missing is [unexpected insight]?"

**Best for:** Single-solution products, flagship offers, transformational programs

---

### 10. If They Only Knew (My Hidden Talent/Ability)
**The belief:** I have untapped potential that the world doesn't see.

**Roll with it:** "You know you're capable of more. Your skills, your ideas, your vision -- they deserve a bigger stage. Here's how to get there."

**Change it:** "Your talent isn't hidden. It's just not packaged right. When you position what you already know in the right way..."

**State the opposite:** "What if your hidden talent isn't what you think it is? What if your real superpower is something you take for granted?"

**Best for:** Personal branding, creator tools, portfolio/showcase platforms

---

### 11. There Must Be an Easier Way
**The belief:** The current approach is too hard. Someone must have found a shortcut.

**Roll with it:** "There IS an easier way. Not a magic bullet -- a smarter system that eliminates 80% of the work while keeping 100% of the results."

**Change it:** "The 'easier way' isn't about less work. It's about the RIGHT work. When you focus on [specific high-leverage activity], everything else becomes optional."

**State the opposite:** "The hard way IS the easy way. The people who skip the fundamentals spend 10x more time fixing mistakes."

**Best for:** Automation tools, done-for-you services, efficiency products

---

### 12. Life Is Dangerous / Different Is Dangerous
**The belief:** Change is risky. The unknown is threatening. Safety is in the familiar.

**Roll with it:** "We know change feels risky. That's why we built [specific safety feature]: a guarantee that protects you completely while you try something new."

**Change it:** "The most dangerous thing you can do is nothing. Standing still in a moving market means falling behind."

**State the opposite:** "Different isn't dangerous. Different is where the opportunity is. And here's exactly how to capture it safely."

**Best for:** Products requiring behavior change, switching costs, risk-averse audiences

---

### 13. A Fool and His Money Are Soon Parted
**The belief:** Spending money is risky. Smart people don't get tricked.

**Roll with it:** "You're smart to be skeptical. Most offers aren't worth the pixels they're printed on. Here's exactly why this one is different -- with the data to prove it."

**Change it:** "The real foolishness isn't spending money. It's spending TIME on approaches that don't work while the solution sits right in front of you."

**State the opposite:** "Smart money isn't cautious money. It's informed money. Here's the information you need to make a confident decision."

**Best for:** Premium/high-ticket offers, ROI-driven products, analytical audiences

---

### 14. Others Have Had All the Breaks
**The belief:** Success is about luck and privilege, not effort or strategy.

**Roll with it:** "You weren't born with connections. You didn't inherit a trust fund. But what if the playing field could be leveled by [specific equalizer]?"

**Change it:** "Every 'lucky break' you admire started with a decision. Here are the decisions that actually create luck."

**State the opposite:** "What if YOU'RE the one with the advantage -- and you just can't see it yet?"

**Best for:** Opportunity products, networking tools, skill-based offers

---

### 15. I Don't Deserve Success/Love/Wealth
**The belief:** Deep unworthiness. Success is for other people.

**Roll with it:** "This isn't about deserving. It's about deciding. You decided to look for a solution, and that decision alone separates you from 95% of people."

**Change it:** "Worthiness isn't earned. It's claimed. Claim your spot."

**State the opposite:** "You don't need to deserve it. You just need to show up."

**Best for:** Transformational programs, self-improvement, premium lifestyle brands

---

### 16. There's Only So Much to Go Around
**The belief:** Scarcity mindset. Someone else's success means less for me.

**Roll with it:** "There ARE limited spots. We cap enrollment at [number] to ensure quality. When they're gone, they're gone."

**Change it:** "This isn't a zero-sum game. When your [metric] goes up, it doesn't take from anyone else. In fact, rising tides lift all boats."

**State the opposite:** "There's more than enough. The problem was never supply -- it was knowing where to look."

**Best for:** Limited-enrollment programs, abundance-themed content, collaborative products

---

### 17. I'll Never Be Good Enough
**The belief:** Perfectionism and chronic self-doubt.

**Roll with it:** "You don't need to be 'good enough.' You need the right tools to close the gap between where you are and where you want to be."

**Change it:** "'Good enough' is a moving target. Stop chasing it. Start shipping instead."

**State the opposite:** "You're already good enough. Here's the proof: [data point about people like them who succeeded]."

**Best for:** Beginner-friendly tools, coaching programs, skill-development products

---

### 18. I'm a Fraud / Imposter Syndrome
**The belief:** I'm pretending to know what I'm doing. Someone will find out.

**Roll with it:** "That voice telling you 'you're not ready'? Everyone hears it. The difference is what you do next."

**Change it:** "Imposter syndrome isn't a sign of weakness. It's a sign you're growing. Here's how to use it as fuel instead of friction."

**State the opposite:** "You're not a fraud. You're a beginner. And beginners are supposed to not know everything. Here's the roadmap."

**Best for:** Professional development, certification programs, authority-building tools

---

### 19. I Need Permission
**The belief:** Someone needs to validate my idea, my career change, my investment.

**Roll with it:** "Here's your permission slip. You don't need anyone's approval to [desired action]. Start today."

**Change it:** "You already have permission. You just forgot. When was the last time someone told you 'no' versus the last time you told yourself 'not yet'?"

**State the opposite:** "Permission is overrated. The people you admire didn't wait for permission. They gave themselves the go-ahead."

**Best for:** Entrepreneurial products, career-change programs, creative tools

---

### 20. There's Always Tomorrow
**The belief:** Procrastination justified. Urgency doesn't apply to me.

**Roll with it:** "Tomorrow, this will still be here. But tomorrow, [specific thing that changes] won't be. Here's what you lose by waiting."

**Change it:** "There's always tomorrow -- until there isn't. The cost of waiting isn't $0. It's [specific cost of delay]."

**State the opposite:** "There IS always tomorrow. But tomorrow-you will wish today-you had started."

**Best for:** Urgency-based offers, time-sensitive opportunities, health products

---

### 21. I'm the Exception (This Won't Work for Me)
**The belief:** Other people's success stories don't apply to my unique situation.

**Roll with it:** "If you're thinking 'this won't work for me because [common reason],' here's a case study of someone who thought the exact same thing: [testimonial from similar person]."

**Change it:** "You're not the exception. And here's why that's actually great news: it means the system works regardless of [common variable]."

**State the opposite:** "You ARE the exception -- and that's exactly why this works for you. Here's why [their unique trait] is actually an advantage."

**Best for:** Results-based products, case-study-heavy offers, coaching programs

---

### 22. More Is Better
**The belief:** Quantity over quality. The bigger the package, the better the deal.

**Roll with it:** "Here's everything you get: [extensive value stack with dollar values for each item]."

**Change it:** "More isn't better. Better is better. That's why we stripped away everything that doesn't directly produce [desired result]."

**State the opposite:** "Less is more. One system that works beats ten that don't."

**Best for:** Minimalist brands, premium products, curation-based offers
