# Offer Scorecard Template

## How to Use This Scorecard

Score your offer on each dimension from 1-10. A score of 70+ out of 90 (Attractive Solution) or 56+ out of 70 (Irresistible Offer) indicates a strong offer. Below those thresholds, rework the offer before writing copy.

---

## PART 1: ATTRACTIVE SOLUTION SCORECARD (9 Dimensions)

Rate each 1-10 where 1 = terrible and 10 = exceptional.

### 1. Solves a Significant Problem
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Solves a mild inconvenience; market can easily live without it |
| 4-6 | Solves a real problem but not an urgent or painful one |
| 7-8 | Solves a pressing problem the market actively spends money on |
| 9-10 | Solves an urgent, painful problem that keeps the market up at night |

**Question to ask:** Would your prospect describe this problem as "keeping them up at night" or more like "mildly annoying"?

### 2. Produces a Big, Desirable Result
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Incremental improvement; "nice to have" |
| 4-6 | Meaningful improvement that is somewhat exciting |
| 7-8 | Dramatic improvement that the market deeply wants |
| 9-10 | Life-changing transformation the market dreams about |

**Question to ask:** If you told a prospect the exact result they would get, would their eyes light up?

### 3. New, Not Simply Better (Replacement > Improvement)
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Slight tweak to what already exists; feels like "more of the same" |
| 4-6 | Noticeable improvement but same fundamental approach |
| 7-8 | Significantly different approach from what the market has tried |
| 9-10 | Entirely new paradigm; replaces everything they have tried before |

**Question to ask:** Would your prospect say "Oh, another one of those" or "I have never seen this approach before"?

### 4. Viewed as Unique and Different
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Feels like a commodity; easily confused with competitors |
| 4-6 | Has some differentiators but shares most traits with competitors |
| 7-8 | Clearly distinct positioning; hard to find a direct substitute |
| 9-10 | One of a kind; no direct comparison exists in the market |

**Question to ask:** If someone Googled the generic solution category, would they immediately find 20 identical options?

### 5. Seen as Superior to Other Options
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | No clear advantage over alternatives |
| 4-6 | Better in some ways, worse in others |
| 7-8 | Clearly better on the dimensions that matter most to the market |
| 9-10 | Objectively superior on every dimension the market cares about |

**Question to ask:** Can you articulate 3 specific ways you are provably better than the top alternative?

### 6. Quick and Easy to Implement
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Complex setup; requires significant time, effort, or technical skill |
| 4-6 | Moderate effort to get started; learning curve present |
| 7-8 | Simple onboarding; value within hours or days |
| 9-10 | Plug and play; value within minutes |

**Question to ask:** How long from purchase to first value? Hours, days, or weeks?

### 7. Produces Fast Results
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Results take months to materialize |
| 4-6 | Some initial results in weeks, full results in months |
| 7-8 | Noticeable results within the first week |
| 9-10 | Visible results within 24-48 hours |

**Question to ask:** What is the earliest "win" a customer can experience?

### 8. At Least Partially Done For You (DFY)
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Entirely DIY; customer does all the work |
| 4-6 | Provides guidance but customer executes everything |
| 7-8 | Significant portions are done for them (templates, pre-built, automated) |
| 9-10 | Fully done for you; customer just reviews/approves |

**Question to ask:** What percentage of the work have you already done for the customer?

### 9. Gives the Prospect Success Confidence
**Score: ___ / 10**

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Prospect likely thinks "this probably will not work for me" |
| 4-6 | Prospect is cautiously optimistic |
| 7-8 | Strong proof that people like them succeed with this |
| 9-10 | Overwhelming evidence that success is nearly guaranteed for their situation |

**Question to ask:** Can you show a testimonial from someone in their exact situation who succeeded?

### ATTRACTIVE SOLUTION TOTAL: ___ / 90

| Score Range | Assessment | Action |
|------------|------------|--------|
| 80-90 | Exceptional | Write copy confidently; the offer sells itself |
| 70-79 | Strong | Minor offer tweaks, then write copy |
| 50-69 | Needs work | Improve the weakest 2-3 dimensions before investing in copy |
| Below 50 | Rework required | Do NOT write copy until the solution is redesigned |

## PART 2: IRRESISTIBLE OFFER SCORECARD (7 Dimensions)

### 1. Outcome Clarity
**Score: ___ / 10**

Can you state the specific, measurable result in one sentence? Does the customer know EXACTLY what they get?

### 2. Price Perception
**Score: ___ / 10**

Does the price feel like a fraction of the value delivered? Have you anchored against higher alternatives?

### 3. Time to Results
**Score: ___ / 10**

Is the timeline to first results clearly communicated? Is it faster than they expect?

### 4. Effort Required
**Score: ___ / 10**

Is the amount of work the customer needs to do clearly minimized? Have you provided shortcuts?

### 5. Perceived Likelihood of Success
**Score: ___ / 10**

Does proof exist that people like them succeed? Are objections preemptively addressed?

### 6. Risk Reversal
**Score: ___ / 10**

Is the guarantee strong enough to make the decision feel risk-free? Does it exceed market standards?

### 7. Urgency
**Score: ___ / 10**

Is there an authentic reason to act now? Is the deadline or scarcity real?

### IRRESISTIBLE OFFER TOTAL: ___ / 70

| Score Range | Assessment | Action |
|------------|------------|--------|
| 60-70 | Irresistible | Ship it |
| 50-59 | Strong | Strengthen weakest 1-2 dimensions |
| 35-49 | Needs work | Significant offer redesign needed |
| Below 35 | Not ready | Rebuild the proposition from scratch |

## PART 3: IMPROVEMENT PRIORITIZATION

List your 3 lowest-scoring dimensions from both scorecards:

1. **Weakest: _______________** (Score: ___) -- Improvement plan: _______________
2. **Second weakest: _______________** (Score: ___) -- Improvement plan: _______________
3. **Third weakest: _______________** (Score: ___) -- Improvement plan: _______________

**Rule:** Improving your weakest dimension by 2-3 points has more impact on overall offer attractiveness than improving your strongest dimension by 5 points.

---

## PART 4: THE ONE-LINER TEST

Write your offer as a single sentence of 5-15 words:

**My offer in one line:** _______________

Does this one-liner pass these checks?
- [ ] It states a specific, desirable outcome
- [ ] It implies speed or ease
- [ ] It would make a prospect say "Tell me more"
- [ ] It could NOT be confused with a competitor's offer

If the one-liner is weak, the offer needs more work regardless of scorecard totals.
