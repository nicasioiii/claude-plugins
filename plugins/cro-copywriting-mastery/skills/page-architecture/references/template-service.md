# Service Landing Page Template

**Source:** $1M Landing Page Blueprint (OnePeak Creative)
**Best for:** Agencies, consultants, professional services, coaches, freelancers
**Traffic:** Cold to warm paid traffic
**Goal:** Convert cold traffic into warm, ready-to-book leads

---

## Complete 6-Section Structure (Top to Bottom)

### Section 1: Strong Headline
**Purpose:** Promise results in a clear, benefit-driven headline

**Rules:**
- Lead with the result, not the service
- Bad: "Expert Marketing Consulting Services" (what you do)
- Good: "Get 3x More Qualified Leads Without Hiring a Marketing Team" (what they get)
- Include a subheadline that expands on the promise with specificity
- Use numbers and timeframes when possible
- Pass the 7-Second Test: visitor knows what you do and why they should care

**Template options:**
```
[Result they want] [Without obstacle they fear]
Subheadline: [How you deliver it] + [proof point]

OR

[Specific outcome] for [specific avatar]
Subheadline: [Brief explanation of method] + [credibility marker]
```

**Examples:**
- "Scale Your Revenue to $1M Without Burning Out Your Sales Team"
  - "Our proven outbound system has generated $47M in pipeline for 200+ B2B companies"
- "Get Your Weekends Back While Your Business Grows on Autopilot"
  - "We build and manage the marketing systems so you can focus on what you do best"

---

### Section 2: Lead Capture Form
**Purpose:** Collect quality leads without scaring them off

**Rules:**
- Ask for minimum viable information: Name + Email + Phone (or just Name + Email)
- Can include 1-2 pre-qualifying questions to improve lead quality
- Pre-qualifying examples: "Monthly revenue range" dropdown, "Team size" dropdown
- Keep the form visible above the fold on desktop
- Button copy should promise the next step: "Get Your Free Strategy Call" not "Submit"
- Add urgency or bonus: "Limited to 10 calls per month" or "Includes free audit"

**Form layout:**
```
[Headline restating the offer: "Get Your Free 30-Minute Strategy Session"]

Name: [____________]
Email: [____________]
Phone: [____________] (optional)
Monthly Revenue: [Dropdown: Under $50K / $50K-$250K / $250K-$1M / $1M+]

[  BOOK MY FREE STRATEGY CALL  ]

[Reassurance text: "No obligation. No hard sell. Just clarity on your next move."]
```

**Pre-qualifying strategy:**
- Adding qualification questions REDUCES lead volume but INCREASES lead quality
- If your sales team is overwhelmed with unqualified leads, add pre-qualifiers
- If you need more leads, remove everything except name and email
- Test both approaches and measure close rate, not just lead volume

---

### Section 3: How It Works
**Purpose:** Clear step-by-step section building confidence in the process

**Rules:**
- Exactly 3 steps (the rule of three)
- Each step: Number + Short title + 1-2 sentence description
- Make it feel simple and achievable
- Use action verbs: "Book," "Build," "Launch" not "Getting started," "Implementation"
- Include a small icon or illustration per step

**Template:**
```
How It Works

Step 1: [Action Verb] -- [Title]
[1-2 sentences describing what happens and what the client gets]

Step 2: [Action Verb] -- [Title]
[1-2 sentences describing what happens and what the client gets]

Step 3: [Action Verb] -- [Title]
[1-2 sentences describing the result they achieve]
```

**Example:**
```
How It Works

Step 1: Book Your Free Strategy Call
We'll review your current marketing, identify the biggest gaps, and map out
a plan to hit your revenue goal.

Step 2: We Build Your Growth Engine
Our team creates and launches your custom lead generation system -- ads,
funnels, email sequences, and tracking -- all done-for-you.

Step 3: Watch Qualified Leads Roll In
Within 30 days, you'll have a predictable pipeline of qualified prospects
booking calls with your sales team.
```

### Section 4: Social Proof
**Purpose:** Build trust fast with results and testimonials

**Rules:**
- Lead with the most impressive results
- Include specific numbers: revenue generated, leads produced, time saved
- 3-5 testimonials minimum
- Video testimonials are most powerful for services
- Include client logos if working with recognizable brands
- "As featured in" media mentions if applicable
- Case study snippets: "[Client] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]"

**Format options:**
- Results bar: "500+ clients served | $47M pipeline generated | 4.9/5 satisfaction"
- Client logo grid
- Video testimonial carousel
- Before/after case study cards

---

### Section 5: Short Brand Story
**Purpose:** Create personal connection and differentiation

**Rules:**
- 150-300 words maximum -- keep it tight
- First person from the founder
- Structure: Relatable struggle > Discovery > Method > Results for clients > Why you care
- Include a founder photo (professional but approachable)
- End with a bridge to the CTA: "That's why we built [service] -- and why I'd love to show you how it works for your business."

**Template:**
```
[Founder photo]

I started [Company] because [relatable problem you experienced].

After [years/struggle], I discovered [key insight or method]. Since then,
we've helped [X] businesses [specific result].

What makes us different: [1-2 sentences about your mechanism/approach].

[Transition to CTA: "Ready to see what this could look like for your business?"]
```

---

### Section 6: CTA
**Purpose:** Clear, action-focused call to action

**Rules:**
- Restate the offer (free call, free audit, free consultation)
- Repeat the form OR link back to the top of the page
- Include urgency: "Limited availability this month" or "Only accepting 5 new clients"
- Add a final reassurance: "No obligation, no credit card, no hard sell"
- Consider adding a phone number for prospects who prefer to call

**Template:**
```
Ready to [Desired Outcome]?

[  BOOK YOUR FREE STRATEGY CALL  ]

[Reassurance: "No obligation. We'll show you exactly how to [result] --
even if we're not the right fit."]

Prefer to talk? Call us at [phone number]
```

## Service LP Specific Tactics

**Pre-qualify leads through the form** -- Adding revenue range or team size dropdowns improves lead quality dramatically. Your sales team will thank you.

**Use urgency or bonuses** -- "Book this week and get a free competitive analysis" or "Limited to 10 calls per month" creates genuine urgency.

**Reference the greatest sales letter:** The Wall Street Journal "Two Young Men" letter is widely considered the greatest sales letter ever written. It told a story of two men who attended the same college but ended up in vastly different places. The letter sold $2 billion in subscriptions. Service pages that tell a similar "two paths" story convert exceptionally well.

**Trust signals for services:**
- Client logos
- Case study results with specific numbers
- Years in business
- Team size or capacity
- Industry certifications or awards
- Media mentions
- "As seen on" badges

**What service pages get wrong:**
- Talking about the process instead of the results
- Using jargon the client doesn't understand
- Asking for too much information in the form
- No social proof or only generic testimonials
- Missing a clear CTA (just a "Contact Us" link buried in the footer)
- Describing what you do instead of what the client gets
