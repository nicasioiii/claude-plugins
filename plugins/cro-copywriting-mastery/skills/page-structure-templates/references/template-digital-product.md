# Digital Product Landing Page Template (Complete)

## Full Structure with Copy Sections

This is a complete template for selling digital products (courses, software, memberships, guides, templates).

---

## SECTION 1: HERO (Above Fold)

### Component 1A: Headline (5-15 Words)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll. Make benefit instantly obvious.

**Formula:**
- "[Benefit] for [Avatar]"
- OR "Get [Desired Outcome] From [What They Already Have]"
- OR "The Only [Solution] That [Key Differentiator]"

**Examples:**
- "Build a 7-Figure Business Without Hiring a Team"
- "Get Full Control of Your Email in 30 Days"
- "Write Copy That Converts, Even If You've Never Written Before"

**Key:** Benefit first, not your product name.

### Component 1B: Subheadline (1-2 Sentences)

**Purpose:** Expand on headline. Build on the promise.

**Should address:**
- What problem does this solve?
- What makes this different?
- Who is this for?

**Example:**
"Headline: Build a 7-Figure Business Without Hiring a Team
Subheadline: Our course covers the exact systems we used to hit 7-figures with just 3 people. No hustle culture, no burnout, just leverage and strategy."

### Component 1C: Video or Image

**Purpose:** Show transformation or product in action.

**Options:**
- Video Sales Letter (VSL) showing founder/testimonials
- Screenshot of product in action
- Before/After transformation image
- Community/results image

**Key:** Reinforce the promise, don't distract.

### Component 1D: Primary CTA Button

**Purpose:** Enable immediate action.

**Button text:**
- "Get Started Now"
- "Enroll Now"
- "Get Access Today"
- "Start Learning Now"

**Design:** High contrast, prominent size, whitespace around it.

## SECTION 3: PROBLEM + ORIGIN STORY

### Opening Story (100-300 words)

**Purpose:** Build relatability and credibility. THIS is where conversions happen.

**Structure:**

1. **The Problem (Relatable)**
   "I used to spend 12 hours weekly just managing emails. I was drowning. Every meeting left me further behind. My team was frustrated. I was frustrated."

2. **The Struggle (Specific)**
   "I tried Outlook features. Didn't work. I tried two different email clients. Nothing. For a year, I accepted that email would always own my day."

3. **The Turning Point (Aha Moment)**
   "One day, I realized: I'm fighting the tool. What if I designed my workflow around the tool instead? What if I worked WITH email instead of against it?"

4. **The Solution (Simple)**
   "I developed a system. A workflow. A methodology. And it worked."

5. **The Transformation (Vivid)**
   "Within 30 days, email went from 12 hours to 3 hours. My stress dropped. My team noticed. They asked how I did it."

6. **The Offer (Natural)**
   "I turned that system into a course. Because if I can get this, anyone can."

**Key:** Make them see themselves in your story. Write for THEM, not about you.

### Why This Works

Storytelling converts better than facts because:
- Stories are memorable (people remember stories, not stats)
- Stories build trust (you've been there)
- Stories create emotional connection (they see themselves)
- Stories make the offer feel earned (you deserve this solution)

**Purpose:** Show what's possible. Paint the after state.

"Sarah came in overwhelmed. She had a digital product but no audience.
After going through the course, she built a list of 50,000 subscribers.
Within 6 months, she generated $150K in revenue.
Now she spends 10 hours/week managing her business instead of 50."

**Format 2: Results List**

"Students have achieved:"
- Built audiences of 50K+ people
- Generated $100K+ in revenue
- Launched courses successfully
- Worked with premium clients
- Reduced their work hours by 50%

BEFORE: "Invisible online, unsure of positioning, no consistent income"

**Key:** Show transformation, not just results. Who did they BECOME, not just what did they ACHIEVE.

## SECTION 7: THE OFFER / PRICING

### Offer Presentation

**Structure:**

Headline: "Here's What You Get"

Description of what's included:
- The complete course (X modules, Y videos)
- All worksheets and templates
- Private community access
- Bonus materials
- Lifetime access to updates

**Pricing:**

Present one main option clearly:

"**One-Time Investment: $297**
Full access to all modules, templates, worksheets, community, and future updates."

Or with payment plans:

"**Option 1: One Payment - $297** (Save $50)
**Option 2: 3 Payments - $119** (Pay over 3 months)"

**Money-Back Guarantee:**

"We're confident this will change how you approach [topic]. If you're not satisfied within 60 days, just email us for a full refund. No questions asked."

**Why:** The guarantee signals confidence. It removes the final barrier to purchase.

## SECTION 8: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### Common Questions to Address

**Q: Who is this for?**
A: Anyone who wants to [desired outcome]. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, this course will [specific benefit]."

**Q: How long does this take?**
A: Most students complete it in [timeframe]. You can move at your own pace."

**Q: What if I already know some of this?**
A: You can skip ahead. The modules build on each other, but you can jump to what you need."

**Q: Do I need any special tools or software?**
A: No. Just [basic requirement]. Everything else we cover is free or included."

**Q: What if it doesn't work for me?**
A: We offer a 60-day money-back guarantee. If you don't feel like it was worth your investment, you get your money back."

**Q: How long do I have access?**
A: Forever. Once you buy, you have lifetime access to all materials and future updates."

**Q: Is there a community?**
A: Yes, a private community where you can ask questions and connect with other students."

**Q: Can I get a refund if I change my mind?**
A: Yes, within 60 days of purchase. Just email support."

**Key:** Address top objections preemptively. Remove barriers to purchase.

## Design Principles for Digital Product Pages

1. **White space** > Cramped content
2. **Clear hierarchy** > Same-size text
3. **Text-heavy** > Design-heavy (proven to convert better)
4. **Simple fonts** > Decorative fonts
5. **Clear CTAs** > Subtle buttons

---

## Testing Priority for Digital Product Pages

Test in this order (highest impact first):

1. **Headline** (Can move conversion 20-30%)
2. **Story section** (Can move conversion 15-25%)
3. **Social proof placement/quantity** (Can move conversion 10-20%)
4. **CTA placement/copy** (Can move conversion 5-15%)
5. **Design/colors** (Can move conversion 1-5%)

---

## Page Length

- **Short:** Hero + Story + CTA + FAQ = 1-2 pages
- **Medium:** All sections above = 3-5 pages
- **Long:** All sections + video testimonials + detailed benefits = 5+ pages

**Rule:** Long pages often convert better (more content = more proof). Test both.

---

## Mobile Considerations

- Make CTAs larger on mobile
- Stack content vertically
- Ensure testimonials are readable
- Keep video embeds mobile-friendly
- Test on actual mobile device

---
