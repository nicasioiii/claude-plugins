# Arman Assadi's 10-Step Copywriting Framework

Each step builds on the previous. When stacked together, they form a complete sales letter that can be repurposed for any medium: sales page, VSL, webinar, email sequence, or pitch deck.

Used across 11 different 7-figure launches with clients including Neil Patel, Lewis Howes, Timothy Sykes, and companies like IBM, T-Mobile, and PayPal.

---

## STEP 1: GRAB THEIR ATTENTION (Headline + Subhead)

**Purpose:** Get attention. Nothing more.

### The Three Subconscious Questions
Every visitor immediately asks:
1. What is this?
2. Who is it for?
3. What will it do for me?

### Headline Rules
- Call out your audience with ultra-specificity
- Use "you" and "your" — NEVER write in "I"
- Nobody cares about you. They are there for THEM.
- Use geo-targeting when possible as a growth hack

### Subhead Rules
- Show you understand their problem
- Infuse wants, desires, and fears
- Show how you solve their problem
- Lead with the benefit

### The 20-25 Headline Iteration Process
1. Write at least 20-25 headlines minimum
2. Each slightly better than the last
3. Improve one word, one adjective, try a different power word
4. Magic happens around headline 20-25 where you exhaust the obvious
5. Test final candidates with 3-5 people
6. Always A/B test — control vs. challenger

### Writing Level
Write at a middle school reading level for mass audiences. Only go complex for highly educated specialist audiences.

## STEP 2: PROBLEM SETUP + PROVIDE SOLUTION

**Show empathy through storytelling.** This is the most effective way to demonstrate understanding.

### Story Approach Questions
- Did you face this issue yourself? (Scratch your own itch)
- Were you helping a friend with this pain?
- Were you inspired by something you saw?
- Did you go on a hero's journey to discover the solution?

### Problem Identification
- Pick the ONE most important problem
- Show you have been in their shoes
- Use the language of their pain: expensive, time-consuming, difficult, keeps you up at night

### Solution Setup
- Show ease of use through memorable metaphors ("as simple as brushing your teeth")
- People remember stories, metaphors, and examples

### Prove Three Things (Bonus Points)
1. **Right person:** Your journey accumulated unique knowledge
2. **Right time:** Tools and knowledge converged at the perfect time
3. **Only one:** You are the ONLY one who can provide this (like Neo in the Matrix)

### Anti-Pattern
Stay away from gimmicky pharmaceutical-commercial speak ("Do you wake up in the middle of the night and experience heartburn?"). Speak from authenticity.

---

## STEP 3: SHOW RESULTS & DETAIL THE BENEFITS

### Show Speed to Results
"If you do this, you'll see X result in no time..."

### Future Cast
"Imagine where you'll be... [vivid description]"
"In 5 years, imagine where you'll be..."

### Detail the Benefits
- Refer to the F.A.B. chart (Features, Advantages, Benefits)
- This section often becomes the MEATIEST part of the sales letter
- Keep audience's visions aligned with your products
- Use powerful emotional language
- Sell the TRANSFORMATION, not just features

---

## STEP 4: CREDENTIALS & SOCIAL PROOF

### Three Categories
1. **Credentials:** Affiliations, awards, licenses, influencers who use it
2. **Credibility:** Studies, research, backers, team, stats
3. **Social Proof:** Testimonials, case studies, reviews

### Most Important in Testimonials
Show the TRANSFORMATION: "What was your life like before?" → "How's your life now?"

### Format
- Transformation/end result
- Customer photo
- First and last name
- Associated company

### Pro Tip
Twist data to work in your favor: "See why people from 170 countries read armanassadi.com" (pulled from Google Analytics).

---

## STEP 5: MAKE YOUR OFFER

### The Takeaway Sale (Reverse Psychology)

Instead of hard-selling, present the offer with "take it or leave it" confidence:

- Come from such belief that you are doing them a FAVOR
- "If it's not right for you, that's okay"
- "This is only for people who are serious"
- Disqualifying people shows you do not NEED them — they need you
- People think: "Wait, why would you take this away from me?"
- Almost always outperforms hard-sell in testing

### Key
Be CLEAR about the ONE core offer. Not upsells. Not cross-sells. The ONE thing.

---

## STEP 6: ADD BONUSES + STACK THE VALUE

### The 5-10x Value Rule
Perceived value of everything received should be 5-10x what they pay.
$100 offer → show $500-$1,000 in total value.

### Bonus Requirements
1. RELEVANT to the core offer
2. Best bonuses ACCELERATE RESULTS
3. Examples: phone call, special PDF, accelerated training

### Value Stacking Process
1. Break the core offer into sub-components (each has independent value)
2. Assign dollar values to each
3. Add bonuses, assign dollar values
4. Stack everything and show total
5. Show total value next to actual price

---

## STEP 7: REVEAL YOUR PRICE

### Price Reveal Methods
- Sales page: Show stacked value, cross it out, reveal actual price
- Video/live: Tell the story of why this price is special

### Pricing Stories
- "This is a way to thank my community"
- "As a special offer for beta testers"
- Create feelings of EXCLUSIVITY

### Rules
- Start with ONE price point
- Be conservative initially, then test
- Show round numbers with confidence: "$1,000 — that's my offer"

---

## STEP 8: INJECT SCARCITY / URGENCY / WARNING

### Authentic Only
Fake urgency DESTROYS trust permanently. Play the long game.

### Authentic Scarcity Examples
- "I can only take on 5 clients because I choose quality over quantity"
- Setting a deadline and ACTUALLY sticking to it
- Beta pricing that genuinely increases later

### The Warning Element
Before closing: "Warning: the cost of not doing this program..."
Make the cost of inaction obvious and direct.

---

## STEP 9: GIVE A GUARANTEE

Remove ANY risk, objection, or fear from the customer's mind.

### Template
"If for any reason you're not satisfied after [period], send us an email and we'll refund you 100%."

### Positioning
1. Call out specific fears they have at this stage
2. Position the guarantee to address those specific fears

---

## STEP 10: FINAL CALL-TO-ACTION / CLOSE

### Three-Part Closing Process

**Part 1 — Re-Summarize:**
- Key benefits
- Price
- Guarantee
- Scarcity/urgency
- Future vision
- How they get results

**Part 2 — The Closing Formula:**
"If you want to do [DESIRED OUTCOME], and achieve [BENEFITS], then you need to [ACTION] before it runs out."

**Part 3 — The CTA Button (First Person):**
Switch to THEIR voice. They read it as if speaking:

**DO use:** "Enroll me" / "Sign me up" / "I want this now" / "Start my journey" / "I want to invest"

**DO NOT use:** "Purchase" / "Subscribe" / "Submit" / "Buy now"

## PATTERN INTERRUPTS ON SALES PAGES

Long copy benefits from strategic interruptions:
- **Myth-busting sections:** "Myth: You need X. Reality: ..."
- **Who this is for / Who this is NOT for** (disqualification at top)
- **Callout boxes** with surprising data
- **Tone shifts** (humor after serious content)
- **Question interrupts:** "Still with me? Good. Because this next part is the key to everything."

---

## THE ANTI-GIMMICK PHILOSOPHY

Everything gimmicky, boring, salesy, and sleazy is rejected. Speak from authenticity. The moment you put your customer through something inauthentic, you lose their trust. You may earn the short-term sale with manipulation, but you destroy the long-term relationship.

**Art + Science + Authenticity = World-Class Copy**
- Science = 80% (templates, frameworks, formulas)
- Art = 20% (your creative spin, voice, authenticity)
