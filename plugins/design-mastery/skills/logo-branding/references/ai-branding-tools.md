# AI Tools for Rapid Brand Exploration

## The Mood Board Method (ChatGPT)

### Step 1: Brand Vibe Report

- Describe business, audience, positioning, and brand personality in prose
- ChatGPT analyzes and writes comprehensive "brand vibe report"
- This becomes the foundation for all visual work

### Step 2: Mood Board Generation

- Use Brand Vibe Report to generate visual mood board images
- Explore multiple directions (minimal, playful, premium, bold)
- Compares different aesthetics for brand

### Step 3: Visual Brand Guidelines

- Feed mood board image + vibe report into AI
- AI writes comprehensive visual brand guidelines
- Documents color palette, typography, imagery style, patterns

### When to Use This

- Quick exploration before formal design process
- Client needs direction options fast
- Testing multiple brand personalities
- Building mood boards for stakeholder approval

### Limitations

- AI may not capture exact brand essence
- Requires human refinement
- Not substitute for professional logo design
- Better for brand direction than final logo

## Branded Product Mockups (Google Gemini/Nano Banana)

### Using AI for Context

- Upload logo design → AI creates product mockups
- See how logo looks on business cards, t-shirts, websites
- Rapid visualization of application context

### Prompt Formula

1. "I want [product mockups] showing [your logo design]"
2. "Preserve every color, proportion, and detail exactly"
3. "Create various contexts: [business card, website header, app icon]"
4. "Style should reflect [brand mood]"

### Advantages

- Fast presentation context
- Client sees logo application
- No mockup templates needed
- Can iterate quickly
