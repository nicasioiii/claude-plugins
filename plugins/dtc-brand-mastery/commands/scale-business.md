# Scaling Strategy Planner

## Step 1: Gather Business Context

Ask the user these questions before proceeding. Do not skip any.

### Required Inputs
1. **Current monthly revenue**: Approximate total across all channels?
2. **Active channels**: Which are you currently selling on? (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, wholesale, subscription, other)
3. **Revenue split**: Approximate % from each channel?
4. **Team size**: How many people work on the business? (including you, freelancers, agencies)
5. **Biggest bottleneck**: What is the single biggest thing limiting growth right now?
6. **SOPs documented?**: Do you have documented processes/SOPs? (none, some, comprehensive)
7. **Current tools**: What project management and operations tools are you using?
8. **Goal**: What revenue target are you aiming for, and in what timeframe?

---

## Step 2: Revenue Level Assessment (Load: 14-operations-scaling)

Based on monthly revenue, determine the stage:

### Under $50K/month -- Foundation Stage
- Focus: product-market fit, unit economics, single-channel mastery
- Team: freelancers only (no full-time hires)
- Priority: nail operations manually before automating
- Next channel: whichever matches your strongest skill (organic social? paid ads? Amazon?)

### $50K-$100K/month -- Traction Stage
- Focus: systematize core operations, test second channel
- Team: freelancers + agencies, spend 60% on hire-out jobs, 40% on growth
- Priority: begin Escala systemization (process maps, basic SOPs)
- Next channel: add the channel you are NOT on (Amazon if DTC-first, Shopify if Amazon-first)

### $100K-$200K/month -- Scale Stage
- Focus: build systems, transition to full-time team
- Team: begin hiring full-time, starting with Zone of Incompetence tasks
- Priority: full Escala implementation (Miro Masterboard, ClickUp Wiki + Workflows)
- Next channel: third channel + email/SMS infrastructure

### $200K+/month -- Growth Stage
- Focus: omnichannel optimization, leadership hiring, subscription model
- Team: 2-3 mid-level employees (~$20K payroll) covering four pillars
- Priority: 90-day quarterly review process, technology audit, AI agents
- Next channel: wholesale, subscription, international

## Step 3: Channel Expansion Roadmap (Load: 14-operations-scaling)

Using the DTC Model Wheel (Matt Clark), map out the recommended channel sequence:

1. Identify which channels are active (from user input)
2. Identify next logical channel based on sequence: Owned > Email > Paid > Organic > Influencer > Affiliate > Amazon > Wholesale > Subscription
3. For each recommended next channel, provide:
   - Expected revenue contribution at maturity
   - Key resources needed to launch
   - Timeline estimate
   - Integration points with existing channels

Reference the revenue distribution targets:
- Shopify: 40-50% | Amazon: 20-30% | Email/SMS: 10-15% | Wholesale: 5-10% | Subscription: 5-10%

## Step 4: Three Levers Analysis (Load: 14-operations-scaling, 12-pricing-finance)

For each active channel, assess the three revenue levers:
1. **Traffic**: current sources, growth potential, cost
2. **Conversion Rate**: current CR, optimization opportunities
3. **AOV**: current AOV, bundle/upsell opportunities

Identify which lever has the most room for improvement (usually AOV -- no new customers required).

---

## Step 5: Hiring & Team Plan (Load: 14-operations-scaling)

Based on revenue level and Four Zones assessment:
1. Ask user to list their top 5-10 weekly time-consuming tasks
2. Categorize each into Zone of Incompetence, Competence, Excellence, or Genius
3. Recommend delegation order (incompetence first)
4. Recommend hire type (freelancer, agency, full-time) based on revenue level
5. Estimate true cost (1.5x base salary for full-time)

## Step 6: Systemization Priority List (Load: 14-operations-scaling)

Based on SOP status and team size:

### If no SOPs exist:
1. Start with Escala Phase 1 (Assessment): document current state
2. Identify top 3 processes that consume most time
3. Sprint-document those 3 processes first
4. Recommend ClickUp setup (Wiki + Workflows)

### If some SOPs exist:
1. Audit existing documentation against Escala 5-level hierarchy
2. Identify gaps at L3-L5 (tasks, subtasks, instructions)
3. Prioritize sprint documentation for highest-impact gaps
4. Implement technology audit (Start/Stop/Continue)

### If comprehensive SOPs exist:
1. Implement 90-day quarterly review process
2. Assess delegation/automation index
3. Evaluate AI agent opportunities (brand bot, internal chatbot)
4. Optimize onboarding workflow for new hires

---

## Step 7: Generate Scaling Roadmap

Output a structured 90-day plan with:
- **Month 1**: Quick wins (highest-impact, lowest-effort changes)
- **Month 2**: System building (SOPs, tools, processes)
- **Month 3**: Growth execution (new channel launch, hiring, optimization)

Include specific metrics to track progress and decision points for course correction.
