# Validation Tools & Research Methods

## HELIUM 10 TOOL SUITE

### X-Ray (Chrome Extension)
- Install Chrome extension, then visit any Amazon search results page
- Click the X-Ray icon to see estimated monthly revenue, sales, price, BSR, and review count for every listing on the page
- Key filters: revenue minimum ($5,000/month), review count maximum (<200 for opportunity), price range
- Use to quickly scan category viability without manual calculation

### Cerebro (Reverse ASIN Lookup)
- Enter a competitor's ASIN to find ALL keywords they rank for
- Key data: search volume, organic rank, sponsored rank, competing products
- Filter by minimum 1,000 searches/month for viable keywords
- Use "Opportunity" filter to find keywords where few competitors rank on page 1
- Export keyword list for listing optimization and PPC campaigns

### Magnet (Keyword Research)
- Enter seed keyword to find related keywords with search volume data
- Filter by minimum search volume (2,000+ for main keyword)
- Look for 3-5 relevant keywords totaling 5,000+ combined monthly searches
- Identifies long-tail opportunities competitors miss

### Black Box (Product Discovery)
- Filter by: category, revenue range, review count, price range, sales count
- Recommended starting filters: revenue >$5,000/month, reviews <200, price $15-$50
- Browse results for product ideas that match PRIME or Four Criteria frameworks
- Export prospects to research spreadsheet

### Frankenstein (Keyword Processor)
- Paste large keyword lists, remove duplicates, compress to fit backend search term limits
- Essential for Amazon backend optimization (250 byte limit)

## BSR INTERPRETATION GUIDE

### How BSR Works
- BSR (Best Seller Rank) is relative to sales within the last hour in that category
- Lower number = higher rank = more sales
- BSR is reported at parent level; use Helium 10 or Product Opportunity Explorer for child-level estimates
- A BSR of 10,000 or less in a main category indicates solid demand (Matt Clark's rule)

### Top Amazon Categories by Seller Participation
1. Home & Kitchen: 40% of sellers
2. Sports & Outdoors: 21%
3. Toys & Games: 19%
4. Beauty & Personal Care: 19%
5. Health, Household & Baby Care: 18%
6. Kitchen & Dining: 16%
7. Office Products: 15%
8. Garden & Outdoor: 14%
9. Tools & Home Improvement: 14%
10. Pet Supplies: 13%

### BSR Quick Validation
- Find at least 2+ products in your target category with BSR of 10,000 or less
- If top sellers have BSR under 1,000, the market has strong proven demand
- If no products in the subcategory break BSR 20,000, demand may be too low

## GOOGLE TRENDS USAGE GUIDE

### Setup
- Go to trends.google.com
- Set timeframe to "Past 5 years" for trajectory analysis
- Set geography to "Worldwide" (or target market for specificity)
- Compare your product term against a known stable category

### Interpretation
- **Steady or rising line:** Good -- sustained or growing demand
- **Declining line:** Red flag -- market is shrinking
- **Seasonal spikes:** Caution -- plan inventory around peaks, but consider if off-season sales justify year-round operations
- **Flat near zero:** Market may be too small or search behavior differs (people may browse rather than search)

### Advanced Techniques
- Compare related product terms to find which variation has more demand
- Check "Related queries" section for rising search terms
- Use "Rising" filter to spot emerging trends before they peak
- Cross-reference with Amazon search volume data from Helium 10

## FACEBOOK AD LIBRARY RESEARCH

### Process
- Visit facebook.com/ads/library
- Search for competitor brand names or product categories
- Filter by country, platform, and active status
- Look for ads that have been running for 30+ days (indicates profitability)
- Note: ad creative style, hook format, landing page URLs, offer structure

### What Long-Running Ads Tell You
- If an ad has been active for 60+ days, the advertiser is almost certainly profitable on it
- Multiple active ads from the same brand = validated product with proven demand
- Study their hook (first 3 seconds of video), offer, and CTA

## TIKTOK SEARCH VALIDATION

### Manual Validation Process
1. Search product keywords directly on TikTok
2. Check if similar products are being sold (look for the shopping bag icon)
3. Analyze number of sales shown on product listings
4. Check engagement (views, comments, shares) on product videos
5. Note creator count -- many creators promoting = validated demand

### Software Validation
- Use TikTok analytics or third-party tools (Kalodata, Fastmoss) to track competitor performance
- Identify revenue driven by similar products on the platform
- Check trending products in TikTok Shop seller center

## COMPETITOR REVENUE ESTIMATION

### Order Number Subtraction Method
- Place an order on competitor's store on day 1 of the month
- Note the order number (e.g., #1247)
- Place another order on day 30 (e.g., #1589)
- Subtract: 1589 - 1247 = 342 orders in 30 days
- Multiply by estimated AOV for monthly revenue estimate

### Commerce Inspector Tool
- Shows competitor best sellers, monthly revenue estimates, traffic sources
- Useful for DTC/Shopify competitor analysis
- Reveals which products drive the most revenue

### Amazon-Specific Methods
- Helium 10 X-Ray: shows estimated monthly revenue per listing
- Product Opportunity Explorer (Amazon Seller Central): enter ASIN to see related products with 360-day click data
- Search Query Performance Report (Brand Analytics): shows impression share and conversion data
