---

## Creator Outreach Workflow (Michelle)

### Daily Volume Targets
- **Standard outreach:** 30-50 creators saved/invited per day via Target Collaborations
- **Mass outreach:** 1,000-2,500 creators per day using affiliate outreach bots
- **Expected conversion rate:** ~10% (outreach to completed content)
- If you want 100 pieces of content, reach out to ~1,000 creators

### Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Find and Save:** Search creators by keywords, hashtags, interests relevant to brand/products. Click heart to save.
2. **Create Target Collaboration:** Create daily with 30-50 saved creators
3. **Share via IM:** After inviting, copy/paste invite message and share Target Collaboration card via direct message
4. **Follow up:** Continue messaging invited creators who haven't responded

### What Creators Look For
- Lifestyle fit with their existing content
- Commission structure (clear, competitive)
- Ease of product demonstration
- Clear communication about product value and benefits
- Note: Free shipping does NOT apply to creators (they receive samples free)

### Daily KPIs to Track
- Number of creators reached out to
- Number of sample requests received
- Number of samples shipped
- Content pending count
- Completed content count

## Sample Request Management Workflow (Michelle)

### Automated Tab System (Built into TikTok Shop)
1. **Review tab** -- new sample requests arrive here
2. **Ready to Ship tab** -- approved requests move here
3. **Shipped tab** -- after shipping, requests move here
4. **Content Pending tab** -- after delivery confirmation, creators move here automatically
5. **Completed Content tab** -- finished content appears here

### Sample Approval Criteria
Evaluate each creator on:
- **Sales performance** (if they have history)
- **Fulfillment percentage** (do they actually create content?)
- **Brand relevance** based on their content

### Post-Approval Workflow
1. Approve sample request
2. Open messages to "Ready to Ship" affiliates
3. Send **templated message** with product features and creation instructions
4. Once in "Content Pending" tab: send **second templated message** repeating instructions and requesting link/spark code

### Automation Note
This was formerly a manual spreadsheet process. TikTok now automates sample tracking. When orders sync with Amazon MCF or Shopify, sample requests ship automatically and tracking updates move creators through tabs.

## Review Importing Pipeline (Amazon -> Shopify -> TikTok Shop)

### Overview
Amazon Reviews -> Shopify/Website (via Helium 10 export) -> TikTok Shop (via Yotpo)

**A website (Shopify) is REQUIRED as intermediary -- cannot import directly from Amazon.**

### Step 1: Export Amazon Reviews
1. Use Helium 10 Review Insights Chrome extension
2. Filter for 4 and 5 star reviews (can include 3 stars if desired)
3. Click "Export Table" to download CSV

### Step 2: Import into Shopify via Yotpo
**App:** Yotpo Product Reviews & UGC (NOT other Yotpo services)

**CSV Template Requirements:**
| Field | Format Notes |
|---|---|
| Date | **YYYY-MM-DD** (year-month-day with leading zeros) |
| Shopify product handle | Found at end of Shopify product page URL |
| Images | ONE image URL per review line only |
| Public review | Must type **TRUE** (capital letters) |
| Review emails | Use generated fake emails (Amazon reviews have none) |
| Comment content/date | Optional fields |

**Multiple Images Workaround:** If one review has multiple images, paste extra image URLs into reviews that have no images.

**Common Import Failures:**
- Date formatting errors (most common)
- Missing "TRUE" in public review field
- Incorrect Shopify product handle URL

### Step 3: Import from Shopify to TikTok via Yotpo
1. Access reviews.yotpo.com or via Shopify app
2. **Must upgrade to PAID Yotpo plan** (free version doesn't work)
   - Select monthly billing
   - Lower order estimate to reduce cost if small import
3. Contact Yotpo support: request importing Shopify reviews as **verified** to TikTok Shop
4. Yotpo processes reviews (~4 hours)
5. Reviews appear on TikTok Shop in **up to 3 days**

### Critical SKU/Barcode Matching
- Yotpo matches products by verifying **SKU and barcode** (UPC or GTIN)
- These often don't match between platforms
- Even if you need to make up a SKU: ensure it matches between Shopify and TikTok Shop
- Check: TikTok Shop listing -> Sales Information -> Product Identifier Code and Seller SKU
- **Both must match Shopify** for reviews to link correctly

### Post-Import
Return to Yotpo billing settings and cancel subscription if only needed for one-time import.

## GMV Max Optimization -- Detailed (Michelle)

### Key Metrics to Monitor
- **Cost** (how much you're spending)
- **ROI** (return on ad investment)

### Optimization Example Walkthrough
1. Campaign started at ROI target ~4.6 -- not getting traction (too restrictive)
2. Lowered ROI target from 4.6 to 2 -- orders increased but cost also increased
3. Achieved actual ROI of ~3 -- want closer to 5
4. Raised ROI target back up -- limits budget but improves profitability
5. Iterate until finding sweet spot between volume and profitability

### Creative Analysis
Two tiers of video performance:
1. **Conversion videos** -- high orders and gross revenue -> scale these
2. **Awareness/top-of-funnel videos** -- high product impressions and clicks, lower conversion
   - Don't discount these; they drive discovery
   - High product click rate is valuable even without direct sales

### Keyword Expansion
- Available for both Video Shopping Ads AND Product Shopping Ads
- Recommended for seasonal product campaigns
- Helps optimize for seasonal keywords and improve organic visibility

## Video Shopping Ads vs GMV Max Decision (Michelle)

| Criteria | Video Shopping Ads | GMV Max |
|---|---|---|
| Content volume | Low (limited content) | High (proven converting content needed) |
| Content proven? | Not yet | Yes, conversion data exists |
| Control level | More manual control | More automated |
| When to use | Starting out; building content library | After establishing converting content |

**Rule:** Start with Video Shopping Ads. Graduate to GMV Max once you have converting content proven through organic performance or VSA data.

## Customer Marketing Campaigns (Michelle)

### Overview
- Target recent and potential customers
- Audiences are **dynamic and update weekly**
- Review and set up **weekly**

### Two Campaign Types
1. **Recent Customers** (e.g., "Lapsed Customers" audience)
2. **Potential Customers** (people who browsed but didn't buy)

### Setup Process
1. Navigate to campaign setup
2. Choose ONE audience per plan
3. Name campaign identifying target audience
4. Choose timing: Send Now or Send Later
5. Choose theme: New Arrivals, Best Sellers, or Active Discounts/Deals
6. Select **up to 4 products** to feature
7. Optionally add coupon
8. Preview -> Submit

### Optimization
- Assess performance weekly
- Adjust product selections, focus, and timing based on data
- Update for inventory changes, new arrivals, and seasonal shifts
