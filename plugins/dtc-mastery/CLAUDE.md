# DTC Mastery v2.0.0 -- Routing & Operations

## Plugin Overview

Complete DTC brand building knowledge engine covering the full product-to-exit lifecycle across Shopify, Amazon, and TikTok Shop. Built from 6 extraction files covering Gretta van Riel (Start & Scale), Jason Wong (Building Blocks), Matt Clark (Omnichannel Machine), Kevin King Summit speakers (3 Billion Dollar Seller Summits), Amazon FBA courses (ASM14, Infinite Income, Product Development Blueprint, Escala Academy, Amazon Advertising Academy), and TikTok Shop Mastery System.

## Workflow Layers (Sequential Dependencies)

```
Layer 1: product-ideation -> brand-strategy -> product-development -> sourcing-suppliers -> quality-inspection

Layer 2 (parallel after Layer 1):
  shopify-store-setup (DTC primary -- "sell on Shopify first, expand to Amazon")
  amazon-listing-optimization -> amazon-ppc-advertising (Amazon channel)
  tiktok-shop-setup (TikTok channel -- requires existing inventory)

Layer 3 (after platforms live):
  content-creator-strategy (fuels all channels)
  launch-strategy (coordinates multi-platform go-live)
  pricing-unit-economics (checked continuously)
  external-traffic-list-building (perpetual sales flywheel)

Layer 4 (at scale):
  operations-systems -> exit-valuation
```

## Instructor Disagreements (Quick Reference)

| # | Topic | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Markup multiplier (2.5x vs 4x vs one-third) | 4x landed cost for DTC Shopify; 2.5x floor for low-CAC products; one-third rule for Amazon |
| 2 | Audience first vs. product first | Both valid; choose based on strengths (social-native vs. analytical) |
| 3 | Patent strategy (skip vs. file early) | Skip for first product with limited capital; file early if planning exit in 3-5 years |
| 4 | Initial inventory (MOQ vs. 300-500 vs. 1,000+) | 300-500 for DTC-first; MOQ for ultra-lean; 1,000+ for Amazon ranking |
| 5 | Landing page vs. product page | Landing pages for paid cold traffic; product pages for organic/direct |
| 6 | TikTok pricing vs. Amazon pricing | TikTok can be HIGHER -- Amazon bots can't see TikTok prices |
| 7 | Payment terms (50/50 vs. 30/70) | 30/70 is industry standard; 100% upfront is a red flag |
