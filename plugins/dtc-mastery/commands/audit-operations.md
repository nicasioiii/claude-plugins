# Operations Efficiency Audit

## Step 1: Gather Business Context

Ask the user:
1. **Business stage:** How long have you been selling? Monthly revenue range?
2. **Team size:** How many people (including yourself) work on the business?
3. **Current tools:** What software/platforms do you use? (Shopify, Amazon Seller Central, project management, etc.)
4. **Pain points:** What takes the most time? What breaks most often?
5. **SOPs:** Do you have any documented processes? How many?
6. **Outsourcing:** Do you use any VAs, freelancers, or agencies?

## Step 2: Process Maturity Assessment

Load `skills/operations-systems/SKILL.md` and `skills/operations-systems/references/operations-systems-ref.md`.

Score the business across the 5-level process hierarchy:
1. **Level 1 -- Undefined:** No documentation, founder does everything
2. **Level 2 -- Documented:** Key processes written down but not standardized
3. **Level 3 -- Standardized:** SOPs with checklists, anyone could follow them
4. **Level 4 -- Measured:** KPIs tracked, performance benchmarked
5. **Level 5 -- Optimized:** Continuous improvement, automated where possible

Rate each operational area: fulfillment, customer service, marketing, finance, product development.

## Step 3: Technology Audit

Evaluate current tool stack against recommended architecture. Identify gaps, redundancies, and integration opportunities.

## Step 4: Output Operations Report

Present:
- Overall maturity score per area
- SOP priority list (top 5 processes to document first)
- Technology recommendations with estimated cost/benefit
- Outsourcing readiness assessment with role recommendations
- Automation opportunities ranked by impact and ease
- 30/60/90 day operations improvement roadmap
- Next steps: if mature enough, route to exit-valuation
