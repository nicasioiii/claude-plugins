# External Traffic & List Building Planner

## Step 1: Gather Channel Details

Ask the user:
1. **Primary platform:** Where do you sell today? (Amazon, Shopify, both?)
2. **Product category:** What do you sell?
3. **Current traffic sources:** Where does your traffic come from now?
4. **Email list:** Do you have one? How many subscribers?
5. **Content assets:** Do you have a blog, YouTube channel, or social presence?
6. **Budget:** Monthly budget available for external traffic efforts?

## Step 2: Build Funnel Strategy

Load `skills/external-traffic-list-building/SKILL.md` and `skills/external-traffic-list-building/references/external-traffic-list-building-ref.md`.

Generate:
- Blog-to-Amazon funnel architecture (if Amazon seller)
- Google Business Profile optimization checklist
- Attribution link setup (Amazon Attribution, Shopify UTMs)
- Landing page strategy for list capture

## Step 3: List Building Plan

Provide:
- Email list building tactics prioritized by effort/impact
- Lead magnet recommendations for the product category
- Opt-in placement strategy (site, social, packaging inserts)
- Email flow recommendations (hand off to email-marketing-mastery for execution)

## Step 4: Output External Traffic Plan

Present:
- Perpetual sales machine blueprint customized to their channels
- Traffic source priority matrix (organic, paid, referral)
- Attribution tracking setup guide
- Content calendar framework for traffic generation
- KPI targets: email subscribers, attribution revenue, organic traffic
- Next steps: route to operations-systems for scaling
