# Full DTC Business Audit

## Step 1: Gather Business Overview

Ask the user:
1. **Business name and product(s):** What do you sell?
2. **Time in business:** How long have you been operating?
3. **Revenue:** Approximate monthly revenue range?
4. **Channels:** Which platforms are you selling on? (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, other)
5. **Team:** How many people work on the business?
6. **Biggest challenge:** What is your #1 bottleneck right now?
7. **Goal:** What are you trying to achieve in the next 6-12 months?

## Step 2: Score All 15 Skill Areas

For each skill, ask 2-3 targeted diagnostic questions and score 1-5:

### Layer 1: Foundation
- **Product Ideation** (1-5): Is the product validated with data? Clear PSSP chain?
- **Brand Strategy** (1-5): Is brand identity defined? Consistent voice and positioning?
- **Product Development** (1-5): Are specs documented? Sampling process followed?
- **Sourcing & Suppliers** (1-5): Reliable supplier? Good terms? Backup sourcing?
- **Quality Inspection** (1-5): Pre-shipment inspections? AQL standards applied?

### Layer 2: Platform Setup
- **Shopify Store** (1-5): Store live and optimized? RPV tracked? Upsells active?
- **Amazon Listing** (1-5): SEO optimized? A+ content? Review velocity healthy?
- **Amazon PPC** (1-5): Campaigns structured? ACOS within target? SQP analyzed?
- **TikTok Shop** (1-5): Shop live? Affiliates active? GMV Max running?

### Layer 3: Growth
- **Creator Strategy** (1-5): Active creator pipeline? Spark codes? Content repurposing?
- **Launch Strategy** (1-5): Launch playbook used? Pre-launch audience built?
- **Pricing & Unit Economics** (1-5): Margins healthy? Break-even ROAS known? RPV tracked?
- **External Traffic** (1-5): Attribution set up? Blog funnels? Email list growing?

### Layer 4: Scale & Exit
- **Operations** (1-5): SOPs documented? Tools integrated? Team scalable?
- **Exit Readiness** (1-5): IP protected? Financials clean? Valuation estimated?

## Step 3: Output Audit Report

Present:
- Scorecard table with all 15 scores and color coding (1-2 red, 3 yellow, 4-5 green)
- Overall business health score (weighted average)
- Top 3 weakest areas with specific improvement actions
- Top 3 strongest areas to leverage
- Recommended next command to run (route to the weakest skill's command)
- 30/60/90 day priority roadmap
