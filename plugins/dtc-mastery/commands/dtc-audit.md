# Full DTC Business Audit

## Overview
This is a comprehensive audit that evaluates your DTC/e-commerce business across all major functional areas. It produces a scored report card with category-by-category assessment and a prioritized action plan.

---

## Step 1: Gather Business Profile

Ask the user these questions before proceeding. Do not skip any.

### Required Inputs
1. **Business name and product category**: What do you sell?
2. **Monthly revenue**: Approximate total across all channels?
3. **Active channels**: Which platforms? (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, wholesale, subscription)
4. **Revenue split**: Approximate % from each channel?
5. **Business age**: How long have you been selling?
6. **Team size**: How many people? (founder, employees, freelancers, agencies)
7. **Current gross margin**: Approximate % after COGS?
8. **Biggest challenge**: What is the single biggest issue right now?
9. **Goal**: What are you trying to achieve in the next 6-12 months?

## Step 4: Prioritized Action Plan

### Critical Issues (Score 0-3) -- Fix Immediately
List specific actions for each critical category. These are business risks.

### Improvement Opportunities (Score 4-5) -- Fix Within 30 Days
List specific actions that would move each category to "Adequate" or better.

### Optimization Opportunities (Score 6-7) -- Improve Within 90 Days
List specific actions to move from good to great.

### Strengths to Maintain (Score 8-10)
Acknowledge what is working well. Suggest how to protect and leverage these strengths.
