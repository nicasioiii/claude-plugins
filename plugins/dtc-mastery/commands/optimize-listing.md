# Amazon Listing Optimization Audit

## Step 1: Gather Listing Details

Ask the user:
1. **ASIN or listing URL:** What product are you optimizing?
2. **Main keywords:** What are your top 3-5 target keywords?
3. **Current performance:** Approximate daily sessions, conversion rate, BSR?
4. **Reviews:** How many reviews? What star rating?
5. **A+ content:** Do you have A+ / Brand Story content live?
6. **Competitors:** Who are your top 3 competitors? (ASINs or URLs)
7. **Brand Registry:** Are you enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry?

## Step 2: Audit Current Listing

Load `skills/amazon-listing-optimization/SKILL.md` and `skills/amazon-listing-optimization/references/amazon-listing-optimization-ref.md`.

Evaluate:
- **Title** (0-10): Keyword placement, readability, character count, Rufus/Cosmo optimization
- **Bullets** (0-10): Benefit-led, keyword-rich, formatted for scan-ability
- **Backend keywords** (0-10): 250-byte utilization, no duplication, Spanish/misspelling coverage
- **Images** (0-10): Main image CTR potential, lifestyle shots, infographics, video
- **A+ Content** (0-10): Module selection, brand story, comparison charts

## Step 3: Generate Optimized Listing

Provide:
- Rewritten title following SEO formula
- Rewritten bullet points with benefit-feature-proof structure
- Backend keyword recommendations
- Image shot list with priority order
- A+ content module layout recommendation

## Step 4: Review Acquisition Plan

Output:
- Review velocity targets based on category benchmarks
- Vine enrollment recommendation
- Insert card strategy (compliant)
- Follow-up email sequence guidance

## Step 5: Next Steps

Direct to /setup-amazon-ppc once listing is optimized and has 10+ reviews.
