# Creator Outreach Campaign Planner

## Step 1: Gather Campaign Details

Ask the user:
1. **Product:** What product are you promoting?
2. **Platform focus:** TikTok Shop affiliates, Instagram influencers, YouTube, or multi-platform?
3. **Budget:** Monthly budget for creator partnerships? (product seeding vs. paid)
4. **Current creator relationships:** Do you have any existing partnerships?
5. **Content style:** What type of content performs best for your product? (demo, unboxing, tutorial, lifestyle)
6. **Target audience:** Who is your ideal customer? (demographics, interests)

## Step 2: Build Outreach Strategy

Load `skills/content-creator-strategy/SKILL.md` and `skills/content-creator-strategy/references/content-creator-strategy-ref.md`.

Generate:
- Creator persona profiles (micro, mid-tier, macro targets)
- Daily outreach targets and volume goals
- Outreach message templates (initial contact, follow-up, negotiation)
- Creator evaluation criteria and scoring rubric

## Step 3: Sample & Content Management

Provide:
- Sample seeding workflow and tracking system
- Content brief template customized to the product
- Spark code setup and tracking process (TikTok Shop)
- Usage rights and content licensing framework

## Step 4: Repurposing Workflow

Output:
- Content repurposing matrix (organic, paid, listing, social)
- FEMSA framework application for creator content
- Platform-specific reformatting guidelines
- Performance tracking KPIs per creator

## Step 5: Output Campaign Plan

Present:
- 30-day outreach calendar with daily targets
- Creator pipeline tracker template
- Budget allocation across tiers (micro/mid/macro)
- Expected timeline from outreach to live content
- Next steps: feed content into launch-strategy or tiktok-shop-setup
