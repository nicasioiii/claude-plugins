# Multi-Platform Launch Planner

## Step 1: Gather Launch Details

Ask the user:
1. **Product:** What are you launching?
2. **Platforms:** Which channels? (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, or combination)
3. **Launch date:** When do you want to go live? (or flexible?)
4. **Inventory:** How many units available for launch?
5. **Pre-launch assets:** Do you have an email list, social following, or existing audience?
6. **Budget:** Launch marketing budget available?
7. **Creator content:** Do you have creator/UGC content ready? (If not, route to /plan-creator-outreach)

## Step 2: Pre-Launch Strategy

Load `skills/launch-strategy/SKILL.md` and `skills/launch-strategy/references/launch-strategy-ref.md`.

Generate:
- Pre-launch audience building plan (waitlist, social, email)
- Giveaway mechanics if applicable (viral loop structure)
- Content calendar for pre-launch hype period
- Inventory allocation across channels

## Step 3: Launch Sequence

Build platform-specific launch timelines:
- **Shopify:** Soft launch, friends & family, public launch sequence
- **Amazon:** Ranking strategy, initial review plan, PPC launch structure
- **TikTok Shop:** 33-step sequence timing, affiliate activation, GMV Max ramp

## Step 4: Traffic Testing Framework

Provide:
- Time-release traffic testing approach
- Budget allocation across channels for launch week
- KPI benchmarks for each platform (conversion rate, ACOS, RPV)
- Kill criteria -- when to pivot or pause

## Step 5: Output Launch Plan

Present:
- Unified multi-platform launch timeline (day-by-day)
- Channel-specific action items with owners
- Budget breakdown by channel and tactic
- Success metrics and review cadence
- Post-launch transition plan (route to external-traffic-list-building and pricing-unit-economics)
