# Exit Preparation & Valuation Checklist

## Step 1: Gather Business Details

Ask the user:
1. **Business age:** How long have you been operating?
2. **Revenue:** Last 12 months revenue and net profit?
3. **Channels:** Where do you sell? Revenue split by channel?
4. **Growth trend:** Is revenue growing, flat, or declining? YoY change?
5. **Owner involvement:** How many hours per week do you work on the business?
6. **IP portfolio:** Any trademarks, patents, or proprietary processes?
7. **Exit timeline:** When would you ideally like to sell? (6 months, 1 year, 2+ years?)

## Step 2: Valuation Estimate

Load `skills/exit-valuation/SKILL.md` and `skills/exit-valuation/references/exit-valuation-ref.md`.

Calculate:
- SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings) from provided financials
- Applicable multiple range based on business profile
- DTC premium factors (brand strength, multi-channel, IP)
- Estimated valuation range (low, mid, high)

## Step 3: IP Audit

Evaluate:
- Trademark status and portfolio completeness
- Patent opportunities and timing considerations
- Brand Registry and platform protections
- Trade secrets and proprietary processes documentation

## Step 4: Exit Readiness Scorecard

Score across key buyer criteria:
- Financial documentation quality (1-5)
- Owner dependency / transferability (1-5)
- Growth trajectory and defensibility (1-5)
- IP and brand asset strength (1-5)
- Operations documentation and SOPs (1-5)
- Supply chain stability (1-5)

## Step 5: Output Exit Plan

Present:
- Valuation estimate with supporting rationale
- Exit readiness scorecard with overall grade
- Value-add action plan (top 5 moves to increase valuation)
- IP filing timeline and recommendations
- Marketplace recommendations (aggregators, Empire Flippers, brokers)
- 6-month exit preparation roadmap
- If operations score is low, route to /audit-operations first
