# Shopify Store Setup Checklist

## Step 1: Gather Store Details

Ask the user:
1. **Product(s):** What are you selling?
2. **Brand:** Do you have brand assets ready? (logo, colors, fonts) (If not, route to /build-brand)
3. **Shopify plan:** Which plan are you on or planning? (Basic, Shopify, Advanced)
4. **Fulfillment:** Self-fulfillment, 3PL, or dropship?
5. **Existing store:** Starting fresh or optimizing an existing store?
6. **Payment:** Payment processors set up? (Shopify Payments, PayPal, etc.)
7. **Target RPV:** Do you know your Revenue Per Visitor target?

## Step 2: 14-Step Store Checklist

Load `skills/shopify-store-setup/SKILL.md` and `skills/shopify-store-setup/references/shopify-store-setup-ref.md`.

Walk through each step:
1. Domain and DNS configuration
2. Theme selection and customization
3. Product page optimization
4. Collection and navigation structure
5. Payment and checkout setup
6. Shipping rates and zones
7. Tax configuration
8. Legal pages (privacy, terms, returns)
9. Email notification customization
10. Analytics and tracking setup
11. App stack recommendations
12. Speed optimization
13. Mobile optimization review
14. Pre-launch testing checklist

## Step 3: RPV Maximization

Generate:
- Upsell funnel architecture (pre-purchase, in-cart, post-purchase)
- Bundle strategy recommendations
- Free shipping threshold calculation
- Subscription setup guidance (if applicable)
- AOV optimization tactics

## Step 4: Fulfillment Guide

Provide:
- Fulfillment option comparison for their situation
- 3PL evaluation criteria (if applicable)
- Shipping strategy (free shipping threshold, flat rate, calculated)
- Returns process setup

## Step 5: Output Store Launch Plan

Present:
- Completed 14-step checklist with status per item
- RPV optimization plan with projected impact
- App stack recommendations with costs
- Pre-launch QA checklist
- Next steps: route to launch-strategy and pricing-unit-economics
