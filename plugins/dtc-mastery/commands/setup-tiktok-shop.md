# TikTok Shop Setup Wizard

## Step 1: Gather Shop Details

Ask the user:
1. **Product(s):** What are you selling on TikTok Shop?
2. **Existing channels:** Are you already selling on Shopify or Amazon?
3. **Inventory:** Do you have inventory ready? Where is it stored?
4. **TikTok presence:** Do you have a TikTok account? Follower count?
5. **Creator network:** Do you have relationships with TikTok creators?
6. **Budget:** Monthly budget for TikTok Shop (ads + creator seeding)?
7. **Geo-locking needs:** Do you need to restrict pricing visibility from Amazon bots?

## Step 2: 33-Step Launch Sequence

Load `skills/tiktok-shop-setup/SKILL.md` and `skills/tiktok-shop-setup/references/tiktok-shop-setup-ref.md`.

Walk through the complete launch sequence organized by phase:
- **Phase 1 -- Account Setup:** Seller Center registration, business verification, payment setup
- **Phase 2 -- Product Setup:** Listing creation, pricing strategy, fulfillment integration
- **Phase 3 -- Affiliate Program:** Commission structure, creator outreach, sample seeding
- **Phase 4 -- Ads Setup:** GMV Max campaign creation, budget allocation, targeting
- **Phase 5 -- Operations:** Customer service, review management, order processing

## Step 3: Fulfillment Integration

Provide:
- Fulfillment option comparison (self-ship, FBT, 3PL)
- Inventory sync setup if multi-channel
- Shipping template configuration
- Returns policy setup

## Step 4: Team Structure

Output:
- Recommended team roles for TikTok Shop operations
- Daily task checklists per role
- Creator management workflow
- Escalation procedures for common issues

## Step 5: Output TikTok Shop Launch Plan

Present:
- Customized 33-step checklist with timeline
- Pricing strategy (geo-locking recommendations if needed)
- Affiliate program setup with commission tiers
- GMV Max campaign structure and budget plan
- Week 1-4 launch calendar
- Next steps: route to plan-creator-outreach for affiliate pipeline
