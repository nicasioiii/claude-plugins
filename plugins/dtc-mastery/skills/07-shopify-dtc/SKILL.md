## Platform Selection Rationale (Gretta Van Riel)

| Platform | Stores | Price | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shopify | 800,000+ active | $29-$299/mo | Best app ecosystem; recommended default |
| BigCommerce | ~60,000 | $29.95-$250/mo | Brick-and-mortar transitioning online |
| Magento | ~25% market share | $0-$20,000/yr | Enterprise-level; NOT for beginners |

**Recommendation:** Always start on Shopify with a free theme. Healthish (successful brand) runs on a free Shopify theme. You would never know.

## 14-Step Store Build Process (Matt Clark)

**Timeline: Weeks 2-5 of 12-week launch**

1. **Create Shopify store** -- start free trial; store name/URL cannot be changed after creation (Gretta)
2. **Add first product** -- title, description, SKU, price, photos from multiple angles, variants
3. **Choose theme** -- simple, clean, mobile-responsive, fast-loading; free themes are fine initially
4. **Update company/contact info** -- required for compliance
5. **Activate Shopify Payments** -- no setup fees; 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction; Stripe-powered
6. **Create legal pages** -- privacy policy, terms of service, refund policy (Shopify provides templates)
7. **Create shipping & returns page**
8. **Create contact page**
9. **Create navigation menu** -- Home, Shop, About, Contact
10. **Add custom domain** -- $10-$20/year via Shopify or GoDaddy; can use subdomain (e.g., merch.founder.com)
11. **Add remaining products** -- start with 1 hero product, expand to 3-5
12. **Set up fulfillment** -- ship yourself, Amazon FBA, or 3PL
13. **Configure sales tax collection**
14. **Activate store** -- go live

### Additional Setup (Gretta Van Riel)
- Set store currency, unit metric system, weight units in Settings
- Connect sales channels: eBay, Point of Sale, Buy Button (embed on existing site), Messenger, Facebook Shop, Instagram Shopping
- Newsletter signup in footer
- Always add PayPal alongside Shopify Payments ("every time you put PayPal on a website, it increases conversions")

## P.A.S.T.O.R. Framework for Product Pages (Matt Clark)

| Letter | Element | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **P** | Problem | Identify the customer's pain point |
| **A** | Amplify | Emphasize what happens if they DON'T solve the problem |
| **S** | Solution | Introduce your product as the answer |
| **T** | Transformation/Testimonial | Show results they can expect (use real reviews) |
| **O** | Offer | Present clear offer with price/discount |
| **R** | Response | Ask them to buy (CTA) |

### Live PASTOR Example -- Anti-Fatigue Kitchen Mat (Matt Clark)
- **P:** "Want a comfortable mat to stand on while washing dishes, preparing food? Most mats are not comfortable and look like they belong in a warehouse."
- **A:** "Hours on your feet in your house makes doing chores a chore. There's a better way."
- **S:** "Our new, extra comfortable, easy-to-clean, thick kitchen mat is custom designed to look like an heirloom rug. Simply wipe it clean any time a spill happens."
- **T:** Review quote showing transformation and delight
- **O:** "Our premium, long-lasting mat. Normally retails for $99. Right now you can save..."
- **R:** "Order your mat today to make everything you do at home more enjoyable and beautiful."

Implementation time: under 1 hour per product page.

## Conversion Optimization Checklist (Matt Clark, Gretta)

- [ ] Clear value proposition above the fold
- [ ] Professional product photography (first image drives 40%+ of engagement)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design (60%+ of traffic is mobile)
- [ ] Easy checkout (3-click minimum)
- [ ] Trust signals: reviews, guarantee, contact info visible
- [ ] Fast page load time (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] Clear CTA buttons
- [ ] 3+ reviews on product page (use Judge.me app)
- [ ] 30-day money-back guarantee
- [ ] Promo bar (sticky bar with discount/free shipping message)
- [ ] Popup to capture leads (10% off for email)
- [ ] Do a live test purchase before any customer acquisition

### Key Store Stats
- Home page is traffic triage, not a sales tool -- product pages are 5x more important
- Target conversion rate: 2-5% (visitors to buyers)
- Mobile conversion typically 20% lower than desktop
- Cart abandonment rate: 60-80% is normal
- Well-optimized store: 2-3% conversion with $60+ AOV before scaling ads

## Landing Page Structure (Matt Clark)

### 10 Elements (in order)
1. Clear, benefit-driven headline (not brand-focused)
2. Hero image/video showing product in use
3. Problem-solution framework
4. 3-5 testimonials with photos/videos
5. Objection-handling section
6. Trust badges (guarantees, certifications)
7. Single clear CTA above the fold
8. Multiple CTAs throughout page
9. Fast loading (<3 seconds), mobile-responsive
10. Minimal navigation (no header menu distracting from offer)

### Build Process
1. Pick hero product (highest demand, best margins)
2. Research 3-5 competitor landing pages; swipe successful elements
3. Plan content: Hook -> Problem -> Solution -> Social Proof -> Offer -> CTA -> Trust
4. Build with Zipify Pages (Shopify-native), LeadPages, or Unbounce
5. Create 5 different hooks and test with small ad spend
6. Scale with winning hook

## 5-Hook Testing Method (Matt Clark)

**Why:** First 3 seconds of video ads determine success. Mobile users scroll instantly; algorithm measures early dropoff.

### Three Hook Elements
1. **Visual Hook (Second 1):** Bright colors, movement, pattern interrupts, unexpected imagery
2. **Curiosity Hook (Seconds 1-2):** Incomplete thought/cliffhanger ("This small change saved me $10,000...")
3. **Story Hook (Seconds 2-3):** Begin narrative arc, relatable situation

### Create 5 Variations
1. Visual surprise (bright colors, movement)
2. Curiosity question ("Did you know...")
3. Problem statement ("Tired of...")
4. Customer testimonial ("I was skeptical too...")
5. Benefit visualization (transformation)

Run each as separate ad, same landing page/offer/audience. Track 3-second dropoff rate, completion rate, CTR, conversion. Scale winner. Keep testing new hooks -- creative fatigues after 2-3 weeks.

## AOV Optimization -- 8 Strategies (Jason Wong)

**AOV Formula:** Total Revenue / Total Orders. Increasing AOV from $30 to $40 = 33% revenue boost with same customer count.

1. **Increase price** -- test at 2,500 visitors per price point
2. **Price anchoring** -- display premium option to make others attractive (Doe Lashes: $100 twin pack as anchor)
3. **BNPL / Shop Pay** -- 50% higher AOV, 30% faster checkout, 28% less abandonment
4. **Free shipping threshold** -- set at 20-30% above current AOV (e.g., free over $75)
5. **Volume discounts** -- buy 2 get 3rd at discount; quantity pricing
6. **Product bundling** -- analyze Shopify order data for items bought together
7. **Cross-selling** -- recommend complementary products at cart/checkout
8. **Upselling** -- offer higher-tier version of same category

### 9-Step Revenue Maximization System (Matt Clark)
1. Add irresistible guarantee (30-60 day money-back)
2. Display promo bar (sticky bar)
3. Add popup to capture leads (10% off for email)
4. Add abandoned cart email flow (recovers 5-15% of abandoned carts)
5. Add upsells to all products (+$10-30/order typical)
6. Add product bundles
7. Add super bundle (buy 3, significant discount)
8. Execute first customer reactivation campaign
9. Plan one promotion for each of next 4 weeks

## Abandoned Cart Flow (Matt Clark -- Klaviyo)

- Triggers when someone enters email at checkout but doesn't buy
- **Email 1:** Wait 4 hours, send "Looks like you left something behind" with dynamically populated product image
- **Email 2:** Wait additional time, send "Your cart is about to expire"
- Use Klaviyo defaults -- they work fine for starting
- **TEST IT YOURSELF:** Go to your store, enter your email, start checkout, abandon, verify emails arrive
- Recovery rate: 5-15% of abandoned carts

## Upsell Funnel Architecture (Matt Clark -- Zipify)

Matt's result: over $1 million in upsell sales alone ("basically free money").

### 4 Offer Types
1. **Pre-Purchase Upsell:** Pop-up BEFORE checkout -- same product at 20% discount
2. **Post-Purchase Upsell 1:** After credit card entered -- same/different product at 25% off
3. **Post-Purchase Downsell:** If they DECLINE Offer 1 -- steeper discount (30%) or different product
4. **Post-Purchase Upsell 2:** If they ACCEPT Offer 1 -- 2-3 pack or complementary product

### Two-Funnel Strategy
1. **Main Product Funnel:** Triggers only for hero/best-selling product (specific, tailored offers)
2. **Catch-All Default Funnel:** Triggers on ANY product purchase (set priority below main funnel)

After setup: do a REAL test purchase with your actual credit card to verify upsells appear.

### Bundle Methods (3 Options)
1. **Manual Bundle:** Create new Shopify product, handle fulfillment manually
2. **App-Based Bundle (Bundle Products Upsell by Thematic):** Define discount tiers in app; auto-adds to cart
3. **Combo Bundle (Advanced):** Same app but requires adding code to page

## Anti-Patterns
- Spending money on a premium theme before having products and traffic
- Skipping the live test purchase before marketing
- Running ads to home page instead of product page or landing page
- Scattering ad budget across channels too early (master one first)
- Not setting up abandoned cart flow before driving paid traffic
- Using only one hook for all ads (test 5 minimum)
- Ignoring mobile experience (60%+ of traffic is mobile)
- Not installing upsells before scaling -- leaving money on table
