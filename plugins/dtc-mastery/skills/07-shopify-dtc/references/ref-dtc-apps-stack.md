## First $1K on Shopify Checklist (Matt Clark -- ASM14)

### Part 1: Store Setup
- Create Shopify account and add first product
- Choose clean theme (free is fine)
- Add legal pages, shipping policy, contact page

### Part 2: Optimize for Sales
- Apply PASTOR framework to product page
- Add guarantee (30-60 day money-back)
- Add promo bar with offer
- Add popup (10% off for email signup)
- Install Judge.me for reviews
- Add abandoned cart email flow (Klaviyo)
- Do live test purchase

### Part 3: Build Your List
- Set up email capture popup
- Create welcome email sequence (3-5 emails)
- Build pre-launch waitlist if not yet launched

### Part 4: Get Traffic Flowing
- Post on brand social media with professional photos
- Boost best organic post with Facebook ad ($100-200)
- Create Google Search ad for brand name ($100-200)
- Post on personal social media
- Create launch discount code (e.g., LAUNCH20 for 20%)
- Ask for reviews 3-5 days after purchase

## Off-Amazon Sales Machine -- 6-Week System (Matt Clark -- ASM14)

### Week 1: Maximize Revenue Per Visitor
- Install upsell app (Zipify OneClick Upsell)
- Set up pre-purchase and post-purchase upsell funnels
- Add product bundles and volume discounts
- Goal: increase AOV by 30-50%

### Week 2: Create Best Landing Page
- Build dedicated landing page for hero product
- Apply 10-element structure (headline, hero image, problem-solution, testimonials, CTA)
- Create 5 hook variations for testing
- Set up as ad destination (not home page)

### Week 3: Launch and Test Ads
- Facebook ads: Conversions objective, $5-10/day, interest-based audiences
- Run all 5 hook variations simultaneously
- Let run 3-7 days before evaluating
- Track: CTR, CPC, conversion rate, ROAS

### Week 4: Scale One Traffic Source
- Double down on winning hook + audience combination
- Increase budget gradually (max 50% per week increase)
- Kill underperformers (below 1.5x ROAS)
- Target: 3:1 ROAS or better

### Week 5: Multiple Traffic Sources
- Add Google Search ads ($10-20/day)
- Add TikTok or Pinterest (niche-dependent)
- Build organic content calendar

### Week 6: Repeat & Subscription Sales
- Launch email reactivation campaign for 30-60 day dormant customers
- Plan 4 weekly promotions (holiday, referral, UGC contest, flash sale)
- Evaluate subscription model for consumable products

## Email & SMS Coupon Delivery Templates (Matt Clark -- ASM14)

### Email Template
Subject: Your exclusive discount inside
Body structure:
- Thank the customer for joining
- Present the offer clearly (discount code, amount, expiration)
- Include product image + benefit reminder
- Clear CTA button ("Shop Now and Save X%")
- Urgency element (48-hour expiration)

### SMS Template
"{Brand}: Thanks for signing up! Use code {CODE} for {X}% off your first order. Shop now: {link} Reply STOP to unsubscribe"

## Free Traffic Daily System (Matt Clark -- ASM14)

Daily activities for organic traffic (10-15 minutes each):
1. Post one piece of content on brand Instagram
2. Post one piece of content on brand TikTok
3. Engage with 10 accounts in your niche (genuine comments)
4. Share product in 2-3 relevant Facebook groups (add value, don't spam)
5. Answer 1-2 questions on Quora related to your product category
6. Repost best customer UGC with permission
7. Update Instagram Stories (behind-the-scenes, polls, product demos)
8. Pin best-performing content on Pinterest
9. Respond to all comments and DMs within 2 hours
10. Track which free channels drive most traffic (UTM parameters)

## DTC Model Wheel -- Omnichannel Expansion (Matt Clark)

Recommended channel launch sequence:
1. **Shopify store** (foundation)
2. **Facebook/Instagram ads** (first paid channel, master in 6-8 weeks)
3. **Email marketing** (owned audience, start from day 1)
4. **Google Search ads** (intent-based, add at $5K/month)
5. **Amazon FBA** (marketplace volume)
6. **TikTok** (organic + paid, niche-dependent)
7. **Google Shopping** (product feeds)
8. **Pinterest** (visual products, long content lifespan)
9. **Retail / wholesale** (once brand is established)

### Revenue Distribution at Maturity
- Shopify direct: 40-50%
- Amazon: 20-30%
- Email: 10-15%
- Other channels: 10-20%

### Three Key Revenue Levers
1. **Traffic** -- more visitors
2. **Conversion rate** -- better percentage of buyers
3. **AOV** -- more revenue per order

Optimize in reverse order: AOV first (cheapest), then conversion, then traffic (most expensive).
