## TIKTOK SHOP TARGET COLLABORATION WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Find and Save Creators
- Go to TikTok Shop Seller Center -> Affiliate Center -> Find Creators
- Search by keywords, hashtags, and interests relevant to your brand
- Filter by follower count, engagement rate, content niche
- Save 30-50 creators daily to your list

### Step 2: Create Target Collaboration
- Navigate to Affiliate Center -> Target Collaboration
- Create new collaboration with saved creators
- Set commission rate (competitive with category norms)
- Include product details and shipping information
- Set collaboration duration

### Step 3: Share via Direct Message
After creating Target Collaboration, send personalized IM to each creator:

```
Hi [Creator Name]! I just sent you a Target Collaboration invite for
[Product Name]. It's [1-sentence product description].

Commission: [X]% per sale
Free sample included

I think this would be a great fit for your [content type] content.
Check your collaborations tab to accept!

Let me know if you have any questions.
```

### Step 4: Follow Up
- Follow up after 48 hours if no response
- Keep messages brief and friendly
- Do not spam -- 2-3 follow-ups maximum

## INFLUENCER BRIEF TEMPLATE

### Campaign Brief Document Structure
1. **Brand overview:** 2-3 sentences about brand, mission, target audience
2. **Product details:** What it is, key features, unique selling points
3. **Campaign objective:** Awareness, sales, content generation, or combination
4. **Content requirements:**
   - Platform(s): Instagram Reel, TikTok, YouTube Short, Story, etc.
   - Format: Video (length), photo, carousel
   - Number of deliverables: e.g., 1 Reel + 3 Stories
   - Key messages to include (2-3 maximum)
   - Hashtags to use: #[BrandHashtag] + relevant niche tags
5. **Do's and Don'ts:**
   - Do: show product in use, mention key benefit, be authentic
   - Don't: mention competitors, make medical/health claims, use copyrighted music
6. **Timeline:** Content submission date, revision window, publish date
7. **Compensation:** Payment terms, commission structure, product gifting
8. **Tracking:** Unique promo code, affiliate link, or UTM parameters
9. **Usage rights:** Can brand reuse content? Duration of rights? Platforms?

## UGC CONTENT GUIDELINES

### Best-Performing UGC Format
**Problem > Product > Solution (under 30 seconds)**
1. Hook: state the problem in first 3 seconds
2. Introduce the product (show it, unbox it, hold it up)
3. Demonstrate the solution (show it working, before/after, transformation)
4. Call to action (link in bio, shop now, use code)

### Content Multiplier Strategy
One piece of UGC can become 3-10 variations:
- Different hooks (first 3 seconds swapped)
- Different text overlays
- Different music/sound
- Shortened version
- Extended version with more detail
- Reaction/duet format

### Content Rules for TikTok Shop
- Do NOT repost creator content as TikTok Shop tagged video on brand account (violation for unoriginal content)
- Use spark codes to promote creator content as ads
- Put ad spend behind whatever performs best organically

## CREATOR PERFORMANCE EVALUATION

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | How to Measure | Good Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Content completion rate | Completed / Samples sent | 30%+ |
| Orders generated | TikTok Shop affiliate dashboard | Varies by product |
| Revenue per creator | Total attributed revenue | Track monthly |
| Cost per piece of content | (Product cost + shipping) / Completed pieces | Lower is better |
| Spark code availability | % of converting videos with active spark codes | 80%+ |
| Content quality | Subjective review of production value | Meets brand standards |

## INSTAGRAM BUSINESS PROFILE SETUP

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] Convert to business profile (not personal or creator)
- [ ] Complete bio with brand description + value proposition
- [ ] Add website link (or LinkTree for multiple destinations)
- [ ] Set up contact information (email, phone)
- [ ] Pin 3 videos to profile grid:
  1. Brand story / mission video
  2. Product demo or commercial
  3. Customer testimonial / UGC
- [ ] Create highlight categories (Products, Reviews, Behind the Scenes, FAQ)
- [ ] Enable Instagram Shopping (if selling DTC)

### Ongoing Instagram Practices
- Repost every customer story tag (builds loyalty + exposes to their audience)
- Use LinkTree for: Amazon listing, website, YouTube, newsletter signup
- QR code on packaging: "Join our community" -> Instagram -> landing page
- Post consistently (daily minimum)
- Use Insights to identify Top Performing Content

## BRAND HASHTAG STRATEGY

### Hashtag Categories (Use Mix of All Three)
1. **Brand hashtag:** unique to your brand (#[BrandName], #[BrandCommunity])
2. **Niche hashtags:** relevant to your product category (5-10 per post)
3. **Broad hashtags:** high-volume, wider discovery (3-5 per post)

### Hashtag Best Practices
- Total: 10-15 hashtags per post (Instagram)
- Mix sizes: some with millions of posts, some with thousands
- Create a branded hashtag for customer UGC tracking
- Track which hashtag combinations drive most engagement
- Update hashtag sets monthly based on performance

## CAPCUT EDITING TIPS
- Free tool made by ByteDance (TikTok's parent company)
- Templates available for quick edits
- Text overlay for TikTok SEO (add keywords as small text)
- Split and trim for creating multiple versions from one clip
- Speed adjustment for dramatic effect
- Export directly to TikTok
