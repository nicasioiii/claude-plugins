# Amazon Listing Optimization -- Complete Reference

## SEO-Optimized Title Formula (Melisa Vong)

**Structure:** Brand Name + Main Keyword + Secondary Keywords + Key Benefit

**Rules:**
- Include highest-volume keywords naturally
- <200 characters including spaces (varies by category)
- Put main keyword early in title
- Main keyword at BEGINNING of title = strongest relevancy signal (Brandon Young)

---

## Bullet Points (Melisa Vong)

- Use all 5 bullet points
- Total <1,000 characters across all 5
- Lead each bullet with a CAPS benefit header
- Sprinkle keywords throughout
- Communicate benefits, not just features
- Two selling modes: **Emotion vs Logic** -- tailor copy to customer avatar

---

## Backend Keywords (Melisa Vong)

- Include additional keywords not in visible listing
- Use Helium 10 Scribbles tool to track keyword usage across all listing fields
- Use Cerebro to find keywords competitors are indexed for but you are not

---

## Keyword Research Process (Vong)

1. Use Helium 10 Magnet with seed keyword
2. Filter by search volume (highest first)
3. Verify relevance by clicking through -- do similar products show up?
4. Compile master keyword list with monthly search volumes
5. Prioritize high-volume, high-relevance keywords for title
6. Distribute remaining across bullets, description, backend

**Long-tail targeting:** Search for 3-6 word keywords with >10,000 searches/month for lower competition opportunities.

## Image Strategy (Melisa Vong + Matt Kostan)

**Hero image (Vong):**
- 4:5 aspect ratio (2000x2500 pixels)
- Renders > photography for cost efficiency

**Secondary images (Kostan):**
- 1-2 highlights per image maximum (cognitive load reduction)
- Tell shoppers WHY to buy, not just what the product is
- Anker model: dead-simple images that communicate one benefit each

**Comparison charts (Vong):**
- Never name competitors on Amazon (infringement risk)
- Focus on YOUR features vs generic alternatives

**Before/after photos:** Real customer results convert better than stock imagery

**UGC platforms:** Billow.app, UGC Factory, InSensePro for influencer content

**Design outsourcing:** NLC.com (No Limit Creatives) -- unlimited designs per month

**Amazon AI tool:** imagecheck.com -- see how Amazon's algorithm interprets your images (demographics, objects, text detected)

## Review Benchmarks (Vong)

- Younger shoppers expect 150-200 reviews per product
- Older shoppers may buy with <100 reviews
- **Minimum 10 reviews before increasing PPC budget** -- otherwise "throwing money into a fire pit"
- Products with <50 reviews can still do $5,000+/month if positioned well

---

## Review Acquisition Strategies (Vong)

**Compliant methods (ranked by priority):**
1. **Amazon Vine Program** -- Brand Registry required; free enrollment; uncontrollable review quality
2. **"Request a Review" button** in Seller Central -- 100% TOS-safe; automate with Helium 10
3. **Product inserts** directing to review funnel (careful wording; never incentivize)
4. **High-quality products** = natural positive reviews
5. **VideoPeel** for video testimonials published to listing

## Unlimited Vine Reviews via Color Variations (Tomer Rabinovich)

- Launch 10 "black" colors with different shade names (obsidian, charcoal, midnight, etc.)
- Each gets enrolled in Vine separately (30 units each)
- Merge all under one parent listing after reviews come in
- Merging: open case > description "merge listings" > select leading ASIN
- Result: 150+ Vine review units from what is essentially one product color

## Six-Star Vine Review Strategy (John Miranda)

- Ship Vine reviewers over-the-top packaging (gift box, premium unboxing)
- Include bonus complimentary product they did not expect (extra t-shirt, book, stickers)
- Insert card with tips/instructional video (no review solicitation)
- Result: Vine reviewers feel compelled to give exceptional reviews

## Negative Review Management (Vong)

- Brand Registry unlocks ability to contact customers with 3-star or lower ratings
- Offer courtesy refund to unhappy customers
- Monitor seller feedback continuously

**SOP for removing negative seller feedback:**
1. Locate the negative feedback
2. Request removal through Seller Central
3. Contact Amazon support if auto-removal fails
4. Open case if still unresolved

## Variation-Level Analysis (Tomer Rabinovich)

[CONTRARIAN] All existing tools (Helium 10, Jungle Scout) are WRONG on variation-level sales data.

**Correct method:**
- Reviews / Days Live = Reviews Per Day ratio
- Distribute total parent sales proportionally by review velocity
- Use Keepa variations tab for accurate ratings AND reviews per variation

**Case study:** Brand went from $25K/month to $125K/month with ONE product by launching the right variations identified through this analysis.

## Listing Preservation Tactics (Kevin King)

### Out-of-Stock Preservation
- Do NOT close listing when out of stock (zero daily sales destroy ranking)
- Set min/max price bounds (e.g., $5.95 min, $29.95 max)
- When out of stock: raise price to $60 on a DIFFERENT settings page (not the min/max page)
- Triggers "out of bounds" temporary suspension that preserves rankings better than closing

### Listing Hack: Forbidden Keywords via Q&A
- Banned words (CBD, etc.) can be indexed through customer Q&A answers
- Example: answering "CBD is not allowed on this site" in Q&A indexes the listing for CBD keywords
- Once indexed, PPC campaigns can target those keywords

## Spanish Keyword Opportunity (Kevin King)

USA = 2nd largest Spanish-speaking population globally. Many Latin American buyers purchase on Amazon.com.

**Process:**
1. Load top 20 competing ASINs into Cerebro
2. Download all keywords
3. Add Google Sheets formulas: DETECTLANGUAGE, GOOGLETRANSLATE
4. Sort by language code "ES"
5. Find Spanish keywords with search volume
6. Add to backend keywords and PPC campaigns

## A+ Premium with One Product (Kevin King)

Normally requires 15 approved A+ content submissions for Premium eligibility.

**Hack:** Create copy of existing A+ content > toggle to keep same ASIN > submit for approval > repeat 15 times. Unlocks Premium A+ with a single product.

## Style Variation Hack (Elena)

To create variation types not available in your category's flat file:
1. Download inventory file from a different category that HAS the variation type you want
2. Copy the correct variation theme naming
3. Paste into your category's flat file
4. Upload -- works for variation types and other missing fields

- Remove "augmenter" on listing fields via support ticket (use exact word "augmenter")

- Call seller support on Saturdays to reach Costa Rica team (more advanced)
- Get past seller support bot: translate query to Simplified Chinese via Google Translate, paste into "describe your issue"
