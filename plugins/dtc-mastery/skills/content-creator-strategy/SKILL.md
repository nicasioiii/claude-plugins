## INSTRUCTOR SYNTHESIS

This skill synthesizes four distinct creator strategy philosophies:

### Michelle (E06) -- TikTok Shop Affiliate Engine
- Volume-based outreach: 30-50 creators/day minimum, ~10% content conversion rate
- Pipeline management through Target Collaborations with daily KPI tracking
- Spark code collection and management as the link between organic and paid amplification
- Outsourceable to trained VAs once workflows are documented

### Tim Jordan (E04) -- UGC Flywheel & Platform Integration
- Three platform types: commission-based (Levanta), paid matching, UGC production (JoinBrands)
- Flywheel model: one UGC video feeds Facebook ads, DSP retargeting, TikTok Shop, Amazon listings
- Capture non-converting traffic from creator links as segmented audiences for DSP retargeting
- Free sample platforms for micro-creator content at zero cost

### Gretta van Riel (E01) -- Million-Dollar Influencer Blueprint
- Product-for-post model as primary growth driver across all four brands
- Focus on micro-influencers (10K-100K) for better engagement rates and ROI
- 3-step partnership framework: brand awareness post, sales promotion post, retargeting post
- Attribution via post-purchase survey preferred over coupon codes

### Jason Wong (E02) -- UGC Content Production
- FEMSA framework: Frameworks, Elements, Segments, Structure, Auditing
- Element mutation: take winning ad elements and reproduce as UGC at low cost
- 4-screen UGC mashup (Shackelford): four simultaneous UGC videos with text overlay
- Modular content approach: $1K video splits into 10 variations at $100/piece

## CREATOR OUTREACH DECISION TREE

**Step 1: Which platform are you primarily selling on?**

### TikTok Shop Sellers
- Use Michelle's Target Collaboration workflow (30-50 creators/day)
- Prioritize creators whose content fits your product's TikTokability score
- Collect spark codes from every creator for GMV Max amplification
- -> See reference Section 1 for full 6-step workflow

### Amazon / Shopify Sellers
- Use Tim Jordan's three-platform approach:
  - Commission-based (Levanta, Amazon Associates) for ongoing traffic
  - UGC production (JoinBrands, Billo) for ad creative and listing content
  - Paid matching for top-tier creators with large audiences
- -> See reference Section 3 for UGC flywheel setup

### Pre-Launch / New Brands
- Start with Gretta's product-for-post model at micro-influencer level
- Send 50 units to 50 creators before launch to seed content
- Focus on Instagram first for visual brands; TikTok for demonstrable products
- -> See reference Section 2 for tier selection and partnership framework
