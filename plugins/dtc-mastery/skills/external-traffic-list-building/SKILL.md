## INSTRUCTOR SYNTHESIS

### Travis Zigler (E04) -- Perpetual Sales Machine
- Focus on PROBLEM-based keywords (not product keywords) for dramatically lower CPC
- Blog-to-Amazon funnel: Google ad spend drives blog traffic + email leads + Amazon sales simultaneously
- Case study: $400/week Google spend = 2,000 blog clicks + 350 email leads + $1,200 Amazon sales (3x ROAS)
- Built 15,000 email list in under 2 months; previous brand reached 150,000 before multimillion-dollar exit
- Perpetual cycle: Google > blog > emails > blog > Amazon > repeat

### Norm Farrar (E04) -- Google Business Profile
- Online retailers CAN register for Google Business Profile
- Case study: 0 to 273,000 impressions driving traffic to Amazon listing
- Product listings in GBP are SHOPPABLE -- link directly to Amazon, Shopify, Walmart
- 10 minutes/day of updates yielded 273K impressions

### Kevin King (E04) -- Build, Own, Engage Audience
- Three most valuable assets: email address > mobile phone > physical mailing address
- Email list > all other assets (brand equity, IP) -- can always pivot and monetize
- Smart marketer formula: get attention where easiest (social/Amazon) > transfer to owned channel
- Attribution link keyword hack for Amazon ranking boost

### Gretta van Riel (E01) -- Community & Content
- 3 C's of community: Content, Collaboration, Consistency
- Content curation vs creation for efficient audience building
- Email ROI benchmarks: $44 per $1 spent (4,400% ROI)
- Content territories as pillars for consistent organic output

### Matt Clark (E03) -- Branded Search Spillover
- Shopify/social presence creates branded search on Amazon
- Customer reactivation campaigns re-engage past buyers
- Initial traffic generation before paid ads (9 organic steps)

## EXTERNAL TRAFFIC DECISION TREE

**Step 1: Where are you selling?**

### Amazon Sellers
1. Set up blog around the PROBLEM your product solves (Travis Zigler)
2. Run Google ads to problem-based keywords (much lower CPC than product keywords)
3. Blog includes lead magnet + Amazon attribution links
4. Welcome email sequence drives back to blog and Amazon cyclically
5. Set up Google Business Profile with shoppable product links
6. Use attribution link keyword hack for ranking boost
- -> See reference Sections 1, 2, 3

### Shopify Sellers
1. Build email list via popup/quiz (minimum 15% off incentive)
2. Content marketing via blog/social for organic traffic
3. Customer referral program on thank-you page + autoresponders
4. Affiliate program with best possible commission without losing money
5. Branded search spillover drives Amazon sales as side benefit
- -> See reference Sections 3, 4

### Both Platforms
- Email list is the #1 asset -- always prioritize list building
- Instagram as brand landing page (70% check IG before visiting website)
- Every customer touchpoint should include email capture opportunity
