## INSTRUCTOR DISAGREEMENT: MARKUP MULTIPLIER

| Instructor | Rule | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Gretta van Riel (E01) | 2.5x COGS minimum | Floor for products with very low CAC (organic/influencer-only) |
| Matt Clark (E03) | 4x landed cost minimum | Accounts for ad spend, returns, platform fees; DTC Shopify default |
| Melisa Vong (E05) | One-third rule (33% margin) | Amazon-specific where fees and ad costs are predictable |

**Resolution:** Use **4x landed cost** as the planning target for DTC Shopify products (Clark). Use **2.5x** as the absolute floor only for products with very low CAC. The **one-third rule** applies specifically to Amazon where fee structures are fixed. Present all three with context when advising.

## PRICING DECISION FRAMEWORK

**Step 1: Calculate Landed Cost**
- Product price + import duty + freight cost = landed cost
- Use Golzari's calculator formula (see reference Section 2)
- Verify HTS codes with tariffterminator.com before launch

**Step 2: Apply Markup Rule**

| Channel | Minimum Markup | Target Markup |
|---|---|---|
| Shopify DTC | 4x landed cost | 5x+ landed cost |
| Amazon FBA | 3x landed cost (one-third rule) | 4x landed cost |
| TikTok Shop | 4x+ landed cost | Build in affiliate commissions + discounts |
| Wholesale | 2.5x COGS | Must leave room for retailer margin |

**Step 3: Validate with RPV Math (Shopify)**
- RPV = AOV x Conversion Rate
- If RPV > CPC, you are profitable on paid traffic
- Case study: member went from $100K/mo to $800K/mo by focusing solely on maximizing RPV

**Step 4: Validate with ACOS/TACOS (Amazon)**
- Contribution Profit = Revenue - COGS - freight - referral fees - FBA fees - placement fees
- Contribution Profit = your break-even ACOS ceiling
- Net Profit = Contribution Profit - PPC spend

## MARGIN RESCUE TACTICS

When margin is too low (Gretta -- E01):
1. **Cut production costs** -- count every penny, negotiate, different manufacturer
2. **Bundle low-margin with high-margin items** -- themed packs, upsells, cross-sells
3. **Raise the price** -- "when in doubt, raise the price"; can always bring it down later
