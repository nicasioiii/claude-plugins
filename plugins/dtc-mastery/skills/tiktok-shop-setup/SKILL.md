## Geo-Locking Rules (Michelle -- CRITICAL)

- TikTok social accounts are GEO-LOCKED to the country of the IP address when created
- Must create account on a US phone or phone with US SIM card
- If ANY team member accesses the account without a US VPN, the account switches to that country -- **permanently**
- Account created in a different country will not work -- must create a new one
- Desktop-only setup is insufficient; TikTok requires mobile device to finish setup

**Permanent rule:** All team members must ALWAYS log in with a US VPN. No exceptions.

## TikTok Shop Conversion Optimization -- 3 Keys (Michelle Barnum Smith)

1. **Free shipping** -- 80% of shoppers will not buy without it; 16% conversion lift
2. **Flash deals** -- creates scarcity + visibility in TikTok's deal sections
3. **Coupons/promo codes** -- layer on top of deals for maximum perceived value

Combine all three simultaneously for maximum conversion.

## TikTok Shop Pricing Strategy (Michelle Barnum Smith)

[CONTRARIAN] TikTok Shop price can be HIGHER than Amazon price. Amazon's pricing bots do not monitor TikTok Shop prices.

**Build into TikTok retail price:**
- Referral fees
- Fulfillment costs (Amazon MCF is more expensive than FBA)
- Discounts and flash deals
- Coupons
- Affiliate commissions
- Ad costs (GMV Max)

**Result:** Set retail price high enough that after all deductions, margin remains healthy.

## GMV Max Ads (Michelle)

### Prerequisites
- Must have a good amount of content with shoppable links
- Content must be converting BEFORE turning on GMV Max
- If insufficient content: use Video Shopping Ads first as interim

### Setup Process
1. Marketing > Shop Ads > Create GMV Max Ads
2. Select ONE product at a time (individual product campaigns give more control)
3. Optimization goal: Gross Revenue
4. ROI target: start with TikTok's suggested target
5. Budget: TikTok suggests amount (ceiling, not guaranteed spend)
6. Schedule: run continuously
7. Videos: Auto-select (automatically includes new affiliate videos as posted)
8. Verify linked account videos AND affiliate videos are available
9. Publish

### ROI-Budget Teeter-Totter Rule
- Higher ROI target = less budget spent (stricter efficiency limits reach)
- Lower ROI target = more budget spent (looser efficiency allows more reach)
- Target ROI of ~5x is the goal; ROI of ~3x needs optimization upward

### Optimization Cadence
- Review after campaigns have run for 2+ weeks
- Analyze each product campaign separately (composite graph is misleading)
- If ROI too low + cost too high: increase ROI target
- If not getting traction/spend: decrease ROI target

### Spark Code Management
- "Unauthorized" videos in creative tab = shoppable posts without spark codes or with expired codes
- If an organic video is generating orders without ad spend, contact that creator IMMEDIATELY to get/extend the spark code
- This is money being left on the table

## The Complete 33-Step Launch Sequence (Michelle)

**Phase 1: Validate** (Steps 1-5)
Score product on TikTokability, validate via TikTok search, check prohibited/restricted lists.

**Phase 2: Register & Set Up** (Steps 6-11)
SS4 letter, business registration, US social account, identity verification, link accounts, connect ads.

**Phase 3: Configure Operations** (Steps 12-17)
Customer support, shipping, fulfillment integration, syncing rules, test order, team members.

**Phase 4: Build Social Proof** (Steps 18-21)
Export Amazon reviews, import via Yotpo pipeline, verify SKU matching.

**Phase 5: Drive Traffic** (Steps 22-25)
Set up 3 always-on promotions, daily creator outreach, process samples, collect spark codes.

**Phase 6: Accelerate with Ads** (Steps 26-30)
Video Shopping Ads first if needed, then GMV Max per product, optimize after 2 weeks.

**Phase 7: Scale** (Steps 31-33)
Weekly customer marketing campaigns, outsource affiliate management, install Asana workflow templates.

Full step-by-step detail is in the reference file.
