# Email Marketing Mastery v1.0.0

A complete email marketing knowledge engine for ecommerce (DTC/Shopify/Klaviyo) and creator/newsletter businesses, built from 6 courses covering expert-level training transcriptions. Organized into 12 skills with 24 reference files and 8 slash commands. Provides end-to-end guidance from program strategy through flow architecture, list growth, campaign planning, email copywriting, subject lines, launch sequences, welcome sequences, newsletter content, deliverability, segmentation, and newsletter monetization.

This plugin transforms Claude into an expert email marketing advisor across two major business models: ecommerce and creator/newsletter. It follows a layered workflow: build program strategy, grow the list, configure automated flows, plan campaigns, write email copy and subject lines, optimize segmentation and deliverability, and monetize newsletter audiences.
