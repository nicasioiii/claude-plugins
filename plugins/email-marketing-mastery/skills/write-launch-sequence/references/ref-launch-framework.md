# Reference: Launch Sequence -- Complete Email-by-Email Framework

## PRE-LAUNCH EMAIL TEMPLATES (Belgray -- E04)

### Email Template 1: Sneak Peek
**Subject line direction:** Curiosity, personal, behind-the-scenes
**Structure:**
- What I'm obsessed with right now: creating/writing/dreaming up bonuses for [thing]
- Include a pic of work in progress (desktop screenshot, messy desk, prototype)
- Here's a little about it -- one tantalizing nugget of info
- CTA: Click here to be first to know when it's available (interest list link)
- PS: "If you're one of the people who's asked me 'How do you [objective]?' you're going to love this. Stay tuned!"

### Email Template 2: Enviable Lifestyle / Results
**Subject line direction:** Self-interest, aspiration
**Structure:**
- Friends always ask me "How do you [enviable result]?"
- Paint a picture of how good you have it because of [concept product teaches]
- "It's all because I know how to [concept]"
- Introduce the creator/product
- Bullet points of what they'll learn (benefits, not features)
- Hopeful message + CTA to interest list or free content
- PS: Link + what it is for skimmers (always include a TL;DR in the PS)

### Email Template 3: Education / "Catch the Wave"
**Subject line direction:** Timeliness, FOMO, trend-awareness
**Structure:**
- You may have been hearing about [trend]
- Stats on why now is the moment
- Why you don't have to worry about having waited too long
- Why it's not just for [type of person they think they're not]
- Examples of all different ways people apply this
- CTA to free content or interest list
- Urgent close: "now's the time or be left in the dust"

### Email Template 4: Personal Journey
**Subject line direction:** Emotional, transformation, vulnerability
**Structure:**
- I used to have this problem. It looked like this, felt like this.
- Here's what my life looks like now.
- It changed because of one simple thing: [concept product teaches]
- If you can relate to me before, and want the transformation I've had...
- CTA to free content or interest list

### Email Template 5: Contrast
**Subject line direction:** Tension, us-vs-them, exclusivity
**Structure:**
- I was talking to someone who has [common problem]
- Sad thing is, that's most people
- They don't know what I've learned: that [concept] can solve this
- Bulleted list of awesome ways this knowledge makes your life enviable
- CTA to free content or product

### Email Template 6: Webinar / Masterclass Promotion
**Subject line direction:** Event urgency, FOMO, scarcity
**Structure:**
- Heads up -- if you're interested in [topic], this is a MUST
- Name of webinar + bulleted highlights
- Why attend live (bonuses, giveaways, no replay, etc.)
- CTA: Grab your seat
- PS: "This class only happens once a [time frame] and fills up fast"
- PPS: Soft opt-out link for those who don't want promo emails

## CART PRE-OPEN EMAIL TEMPLATE

**Subject line direction:** Urgency, preparation, excitement
**Structure:**
- It's almost here... I've been talking about [thing]
- Describe what it is and why it's exciting
- Announce your bonuses (full description -- this is the ONE email where you lay it all out)
- Explain affiliate relationship transparently (if applicable)
- Instructions on how to claim bonuses (step-by-step)
- Fast Action Bonus description + deadline
- "Keep an eye on your inbox. I'll be here at [hour] to tell you it's Go Time."

## CART-OPEN EMAIL TEMPLATES

### Cart Open Announcement
**Subject line direction:** Direct, urgent, action-oriented (e.g., "RUN! [Product] -- now open!")
**Structure:**
- "The doors are finally open!"
- Link near the TOP for people ready to pounce (do not make them scroll)
- Why you want in + the big mistake most people make
- Bulleted benefits + problems it eliminates
- Bonus package description (abbreviated -- link to full details page)
- Fast Action Bonus with time frame / limited spots
- Instructions to claim bonuses
- PS: TL;DR for skimmers -- one sentence, one link

### Fast Action Bonus Reminder
**Subject line direction:** Scarcity, countdown, social proof
**Structure:**
- Reminder: doors are open, people are already in
- Fast Action Bonus deadline (hours remaining)
- CTA to join
- Screenshots of social proof (IG stories, DMs, emails from early buyers)
- PS: "Post your stories and tag me so I can celebrate with you!"

## MID-CART OBJECTION BUSTER TEMPLATES

### Objection 1: "It won't work for me"
**Subject line direction:** Self-deprecating, relatable
**Template:**
- Tell a story showing your flaw, limitation, or seeming liability
- "I'm not the 'type' for this either. I'm [funny self-deprecation]."
- "And yet, here I am with [result]."
- "If I can do it, you absolutely can too."
- Case study or testimonial from someone who also thought it would not work for them
- CTA + PS with link

### Objection 2: "This is for suckers"
**Subject line direction:** Contrarian, honest, skeptic-friendly
**Template:**
- Validate their skepticism: "I get it. The internet is full of people selling 'magic pills.'"
- Agree that a lot of marketers are full of crap
- "Here's why this is different:" + stats, quotes, real reviews
- Social proof: results from real people, not cherry-picked testimonials
- End with: "Still skeptical? Write me back, let's discuss."

### Objection 3: "Can't afford it"
**Subject line direction:** Empathetic, investment-framing
**Template:**
- Share your own scary investment story (the first course or product YOU bought that felt too expensive)
- "What if it doesn't work?" vs. "What if it does?"
- Paint picture of possible ROI / results
- Case study of someone who thought they couldn't afford it and the return they got
- Payment plan mention if available
- CTA

### Objection 4: "I'm not ready"
**Subject line direction:** Motivational, "no one's ever ready"
**Template:**
- Story of something you did before you were ready
- "You know what happens to people who wait until they're ready? They wait forever."
- Most people who succeed were not ready when they started
- The product/program itself makes you ready -- that is the point
- CTA

### Objection 5: "I've tried everything"
**Subject line direction:** "This time is different" (but earned, not cliched)
**Template:**
- Share what you tried that didn't work
- "Here's what was different about this approach"
- It's not their fault those other things failed -- those things were missing [key element]
- This approach includes [key element] which is why the results are different
- CTA

### Future Pacing Email
**Subject line direction:** Aspirational, vivid imagery
**Template:**
- "Close your eyes. It's [3 months/6 months/1 year] from now."
- Paint vivid picture: where they are, what they've accomplished, how friends are reacting
- "Do you like that picture? Or would you rather 'future you' look just like 'current you'?"
- CTA

### FAQ Email
**Subject line direction:** Curiosity -- "Were you wondering this?" (do not reveal the question)
**Template:**
- Questions and answers, visually broken up (bold Q, regular A)
- Mix practical questions (price, refund policy, time commitment) with emotional ones
- "Please let me know if you have questions I didn't answer."
- Alternative (E06): Sprinkle FAQs through PS sections of other emails instead of one big FAQ email

### Testimonial / Social Proof Email
**Version 1:** Feature someone's amazing result with quote + screenshot. "This person was skeptical because [reason]..."
**Version 2:** Screenshot of real subscriber reply. Elaborate on the angle that converted them.

### Catch-Up / "What Even Is This?" Email
- Nutshell description of what you're promoting
- For people who missed earlier emails or skimmed and didn't absorb
- Full benefit list + bonus list + CTA
- "If you've been wondering what all my emails have been about..."

## CART-CLOSE EMAIL TEMPLATES

### Closing Tomorrow
**Subject line direction:** Countdown, emotional, story-based
**Template:**
- Story about your journey or someone else's transformation
- "Don't wait another [timeframe] to do yourself this favor."
- Full CTA + bonus reminder

### Closing Today (Morning)
**Subject line direction:** Direct deadline -- "Last day -- you have till [time]"
**Template:**
- Empowering message on why they can do it
- Full bonus details one more time
- "I can't wait to see you [achieve result]!"
- CTA

### One More Hour (Schedule 70 minutes before close)
**Subject line direction:** Maximum urgency -- "Going, going, gone"
**Template:**
- Short. Empowering. Urgent.
- "Join us here before it disappears."
- PS: "[Product] plus all my bonuses are available for one more hour via this link."

### Aspirational Close (Throssell -- E06)
- On final day, include aspirational or inspirational element
- Use quotes, relatable lessons, the "two paths" close
- Pull rather than push: "Come on, you know this quote. Here is your opportunity to live it."

---

## LAST CALL EMAIL (Throssell -- E06)
- Short last-call email 1-3 hours before sale ends
- Put deadline info IN the subject line so people do not have to open to get the reminder
- Example: "Last chance to get 33% off [product name]"
