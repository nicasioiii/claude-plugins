# /design-funnel -- Complete Funnel Design

## INTAKE QUESTIONS (Ask All Before Designing)

### Question 1: What You Sell
**What is your product or service?**
- Describe what you sell
- What is the price point (or intended price)?
- Is it digital, physical, service, or SaaS?

### Question 2: Target Audience
**Who is your ideal customer?**
- Demographics (age, gender, location, income)
- What problem are you solving for them?
- How aware are they of the problem and solutions? (If unsure, say so -- we'll assess)

### Question 3: Traffic Source
**How will you drive traffic to this funnel?**
- A) Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads
- B) Google ads
- C) YouTube ads / content
- D) Organic social media
- E) Email list
- F) Not sure yet

### Question 4: Business Stage
**Where are you in your business?**
- A) Brand new (pre-revenue)
- B) Some revenue but no funnel yet
- C) Have a funnel, building a new one
- D) Scaling ($10K+/month), need additional funnel

### Question 5: Budget & Resources
**What's your monthly ad budget and team size?**

## DESIGN PROCESS

### Step 1: Market Assessment (Load market-research)
- Assess awareness level using Schwartz 5 levels
- Identify which segment of the market to target first
- Determine copy length and education requirements

### Step 2: Funnel Type Selection (Load funnel-architecture)
- Score with 15-factor system if needed
- Match to funnel type based on product, price, and awareness
- Apply platform congruency rules for traffic source

### Step 3: Offer Stack Design (Load offer-stack-design)
- Design front-end offer (attraction or direct)
- Map order bump
- Design upsell sequence (1-3 upsells based on business model)
- Plan backend sequence
- Consider continuity if applicable

### Step 4: Economics Validation (Load funnel-economics)
- Model expected CPA, AOV, COGS, GP
- Check LTGP:CAC ratio
- Validate CFA level target

### Step 5: Output
Deliver a complete funnel map:
1. **Funnel Type** -- Selected type with rationale
2. **Page Map** -- Every page in sequence with purpose
3. **Offer Stack** -- All offers with pricing and positioning
4. **Copy Brief** -- What each page needs to accomplish (which skill to load for writing)
5. **Economics Projection** -- Expected metrics and profitability
6. **Build Order** -- What to build first, second, third (sequential layering)
