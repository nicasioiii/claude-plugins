## Money Model Definition (Hormozi)

A **money model** is a series of offers. A **good money model** is a series of offers structured to minimize CAC, maximize GP, collected in the first 30 days.

**Build sequentially -- one layer at a time** like layers of paint. Do not launch with a full money model. Start with one offer, then add layers as cash flow allows. This is echoed by Katie: start with one bump + one upsell.

---

## The Four-Pronged Money Model (Hormozi)

| Offer Type | Objective | When |
|---|---|---|
| **Attraction Offers** | Liquidate CAC (break even on acquisition) | First contact |
| **Upsell Offers** | Maximize profit | Immediately to 30 days |
| **Downsell Offers** | Maximize conversion (turn no's into yes's) | After any "no" |
| **Continuity Offers** | Stabilize cash / maximize LTV | After initial offers |

## 6 Attraction Offer Types (Hormozi)

1. **Win Your Money Back** -- Set a goal; if they hit it, they get money back (Hormozi's #1 money-maker ever, 78% success rate)
2. **Free Giveaways** -- Chance to win big prize for contact info; offer discounted version to non-winners
3. **Decoy Offers** -- Advertise free/cheap thing; present two options: stripped decoy vs. premium (target 70-80% premium uptake)
4. **Buy X Get Y Free** -- Free bonus items increase main product purchase rate
5. **Pay Less Now or Pay More Later** -- Ascending price creates urgency
6. **Free With Consumption** -- Massive free value before paid offer; the more expensive the eventual offer, the more free stuff first

See references/attraction-offers.md for full details on each type.

## Upsell Offer Categories (Georgi)

What you can sell as upsells:
- **Better Results** -- premium version
- **Faster Results** -- accelerator
- **Easier Results** -- done-for-you, automated
- **Expanded Results** -- solving a new or complementary problem
- **Savings** -- save more today (and get better results)
- **Holism** -- solve the ENTIRE problem, not just part
- **Improved Version** -- upgrade what they just bought

## Downsell Cardinal Rule (Hormozi)

**You can offer something DIFFERENT for less. You CANNOT offer the SAME THING for less.**

Dropping your price is not downselling. It's discounting. And it destroys trust permanently.

## The 3T Offer Triangle (Gusten)

| Stage | Elements | Questions |
|---|---|---|
| **Transformation** | WHO + OUTCOME + PROBLEM | Who do I help? What do they want? What's stopping them? |
| **Transportation** | VEHICLE + SOURCE + TIME | How do I get them there? Why trust me? How fast? |
| **Transaction** | FEE + RISK + PRIORITY | How much? What if it fails? Why now? |

**Between stages:**
- Transformation + Transportation = **Compulsion** (compelling desire)
- Transportation + Transaction = **Convenience** (ease of implementation)
- Transaction + Transformation = **Confidence** (trust it will work)

## Value Ladder / Ascension Model (Gusten)

| Level | Price Range | Type | Delivery | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Ebook, checklist, challenge, free trial | Automated | Get leads; prove competence |
| **Low Ticket** | $1-$100 | Templates, workshops, bundles | Automated | Create impulse buyer |
| **Automated Offer** | $100-$2,000 | Course, membership, software | Automated | Passive income; build base |
| **Backend** | $3,000-$25,000+ | Coaching, consulting, DFY, mastermind | Application/call | Highest revenue; direct access |

## Pricing Psychology Quick Rules

1. **Free or full price, never moderate discounts** (Hormozi). 10-20% off doesn't change behavior. Either go free to change behavior or charge full price to maintain value.
2. **Minimum effective discount: 50%+** on something people already know the value of.
3. **Anchor pricing:** Show the 5-10x price first to make the real price feel like a relief.
4. **Monthly price vs. total commitment:** People think about monthly cash flow, not total cost. $200/month for 12 months feels cheaper than $2,400 one-time even though it's identical.
5. **"Minimum" package naming:** Calling the base option "minimum" instead of "basic" or "economy" creates ~90% take rate (Hormozi).

See references/pricing-psychology.md for the complete framework.

## Sequential Build Order

**For new businesses (Katie's advice):**
1. Main product (front-end)
2. One order bump ($27-$37)
3. One upsell ($67-$197)
4. Start selling; generate cash flow
5. Add additional upsells over time
6. Add backend email sequences
7. Add continuity

**For businesses at $10K+/month (Hormozi's framework):**
1. Get customers reliably
2. Make them pay for themselves within 30 days (CFA Level 2)
3. Make them pay for OTHER customers (CFA Level 3)
4. Maximize each customer's long-term value
5. Add continuity and prepayment

## Instructor Disagreement: Number of Upsells

**Katie:** Start with 1 bump + 1 upsell. Cap at 4 upsells total. Offer fatigue kicks in at upsell 4.

**Gusten:** 1-2 upsells max. 3+ hurts completion rate.

**Hormozi:** Money model can have 5+ stages including supplements, meals, continuity, prepayment.

**Resolution:** Katie and Gusten align for digital product funnels (info products, courses, templates). Hormozi's expanded model works for in-person/hybrid service businesses with live sales and physical products. Route by fulfillment model: digital = 1 bump + 2-3 upsells max; physical/service with live sales = can go deeper.
