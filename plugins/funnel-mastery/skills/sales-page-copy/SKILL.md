## INSTRUCTOR SYNTHESIS NOTES

This skill synthesizes Gusten Sun (CONVERSIONS framework, 10-step page) and Stefan Georgi (Brief 2.0, evidence types, research-driven copy) with Katie/Floyd (high-ticket page structure).

### Key Agreement: Research Before Copy
Both Gusten and Georgi insist on deep market understanding before writing. Gusten uses 9 Pre-Funnel Questions; Georgi uses the 25-Point Brief 2.0. Both are valid starting points -- Georgi's is more comprehensive for DTC/supplement; Gusten's is faster for info products/coaching.

### Key Agreement: Sell the Idea Before the Offer
Gusten calls it the "Domino Statement." Georgi calls it "selling the mechanism." Both agree: if the prospect believes in the process/method, the product becomes the obvious vehicle. Never lead with "buy my thing" -- lead with "here is why everything you tried before failed, and here is the real answer."

## THE C.O.N.V.E.R.S.I.O.N.S. FRAMEWORK

The master architecture for every sales page. Each letter = a section principle:

| Letter | Element | Purpose |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **C** | Clear Outcome | Make them interested to stay on the page |
| **O** | Opportunity Selling | Visualize their future via a NEW opportunity |
| **N** | New Method | Show a better way (your vehicle/mechanism) |
| **V** | Value Proposition | Describe benefits, not features |
| **E** | Empathy | Make them feel deeply understood |
| **R** | Results | Let proof do the talking |
| **S** | Source-Approved | Show others loving it (external proof) |
| **I** | Irresistible Offer | Make it hard not to buy |
| **O** | Objection Handling | Calm their fears |
| **N** | Next Steps | Invite with clear CTA |
| **S** | Scarcity | Give reason to act NOW |

### Opportunity Selling vs. Improvement Selling

This is the single most important distinction in sales page copy:

- **WRONG (improvement):** "Better funnel builder" / "Write better copy" / "Get stronger"
- **RIGHT (new opportunity):** "AI system that writes for you" / "Data-driven supplements from YOUR blood test"

New opportunities bypass skepticism because the person has not failed at THIS specific approach before. Improvement selling triggers memories of past failures with similar promises.

## THE 10-STEP PERFECT SALES PAGE

### Step 1: Big Promise / Desired Outcome
- Pre-headline: Who is it for? ("Attention business owners" / "Free training starting [date]")
- Main headline: Specific outcome + timeframe + "without" objection removal
- Include above the fold: logo bar, ratings, small testimonial, CTA button, guarantee icons

### Step 2: Empathy (Understanding Their Pain)
- Stakes: "Here's why you should care"
- Connection: "I've been there"
- Pain points list: "You're likely feeling..."

### Step 3: Opportunity / The Only Solution
- "You've tried [old method]. Here's why it didn't work."
- Introduce the new vehicle/mechanism
- Before/after the gap and bridge

### Step 4: USP / Comparison / Positioning
- Comparison table (us vs. competitors / old way vs. new way)
- Why this is different from everything they've tried

### Step 5: Benefits (Feature -> Benefit -> Impact)
- Feature: "Weekly coaching calls"
- Benefit: "So you learn the skills faster"
- Impact: "So you feel confident closing clients"

### Step 6: Introduce the Offer
- Visual mockups of what they get
- Offer stack: list everything with individual values
- Price anchoring: "Normally $X separately, now $Y"

### Step 7: Social Proof
- Sprinkle throughout page (not just one section)
- SPECIFIC testimonials matching your claims
- Visual testimonials: photo + headline quote + video

### Step 8: Risk Reversal
- Money-back guarantee / Payment plans / Free trial
- "I've taken most of the risk for you"

### Step 9: Authority (About Section)
- Tell transformation story, NOT personal hobbies
- Epiphany moment: "I almost quit, then discovered this one thing..."

### Step 10: Urgency / Scarcity
- Price, time, FOMO, spot limits
- Close with FAQ for clarity
- What/When/Why close section

## HIGH-TICKET SALES PAGE (Application Funnel)

For $5K-$25K+ offers, use a different structure (Katie/Floyd):

1. **Promo banner** at top (current offer/deadline)
2. **Tagline answering 4 questions:** How long? What is it? What benefit? How is it different?
3. **Heart section** -- mission, story, genuine belief
4. **Who it's perfect for** -- state the avatar clearly
5. **Authority proof** -- specific results you've achieved
6. **What's inside** -- focus on WHY the support helps, not just information

**Critical rule:** You cannot sell high-ticket like information. You must sell it as transformation via coaching. Customers already buy courses from you -- the high-ticket must clearly be about coaching/mentorship access, not more content.

## COPY PRINCIPLES (Quick Reference)

**Domino Statement:** Sell the IDEA before the OFFER. If someone believes the bridge works, the offer is just the boat to cross it faster.

**"When" Not "If":** "When you land your first client..." (assumes it will happen) beats "If you implement this..." (implies doubt).

**Persona Triggers:**
- Positive: "Imagine waking up to Stripe notifications every morning"
- Negative: "The shame you feel when you say you should spend more time with family but you're stressed at work"

**Evidence-Based Selling:**
- Internal direct evidence: your own claims, results, certifications
- External indirect evidence: testimonials, case studies, data, PR mentions
- External evidence is stronger because the reader perceives it as uncontrollable (more trustworthy)
