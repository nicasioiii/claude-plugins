# AI Ad Script Templates & Frameworks

## THE 60-SECOND AD MACHINE (CUSTOM CHATGPT)

A custom GPT that asks a series of questions and generates a complete ad script:

### Question Sequence
1. **Describe your role/business** -- "I'm a travel agent"
2. **What's your product?** -- Specific offering
3. **What results does your product provide?** -- Benefits and outcomes
4. **Name great features** -- Why audience should be excited
5. **How fast can someone get results?** -- Speed of delivery/outcome
6. **Name 3 challenges/pain points** -- Customer frustrations
7. **What's your call to action?** -- "Click the link to learn more" performs best
8. **Confirm ideal audience** -- Quick recap for verification
9. **Script generated** -- Complete ready-to-use ad script

### Script Styles Available
- "Create an ad script for your AI clone" (includes AI humor/self-awareness)
- "Create a funny script" (humor-based)
- "Create a classic ad script" (serious/professional)

## AI CLONE SCRIPT TEMPLATE

### Opening Formula
```
Scene: [Subject in professional setting, looking at camera]
"Hey, I'm not really [Name], your [role]. I'm just her AI clone."
"The real [Name] is [doing impressive work] right now."
```

### Structure
```
[HOOK -- 0-3 sec]
AI introduces itself with humor or self-awareness

[PAIN POINT -- 3-10 sec]
"Are you [specific frustration]? [Expand on the problem]."

[SOLUTION -- 10-18 sec]
"[Name/Brand] can [specific solution]. [Key differentiator]."

[BENEFITS -- 18-22 sec]
"You'll get [benefit 1], [benefit 2], and [benefit 3]."

[CTA -- 22-25 sec]
"Click the link to [specific action]. [Urgency if applicable]."

[AI HUMOR CLOSE -- optional]
"I'll still be here working because AI doesn't get vacation days."
```

## SHORT-FORM CONTENT SCRIPT TEMPLATE

### Step 1: Fill Out Questionnaire
- "I am a [WHO] that helps [WHO] achieve [WHAT]"
- "The product I am selling is [WHAT]"
- "My customer's pain points are [3 PAIN POINTS]"
- "The desired results my customers want are [3 DESIRED END RESULTS]"
- Ask ChatGPT for 30 ideas for informational reels

### Step 2: Pick Topic and Prompt ChatGPT
**For short-form (30 sec):**
"I liked the topic on [TOPIC]. Write a 30-second Instagram Reel script that I could use to post as shortform content. No scene changes needed."

**For long-form (2 min):**
"I liked the topic on [TOPIC]. Write me a 2 minute script for a long form youtube video. No scene changes needed."

## UGC-STYLE AI AD TEMPLATE

### Prompt
```
"I am a [WHO] that teaches/sells [WHAT] to [WHO]"
"Results that my clients want are [3 DESIRED RESULTS]"
"My call to action is [WHAT]"
"I am going to get AI to create a UGC style ad with a custom avatar"
"I am going to have a [describe avatar] talking to the camera"
"Can you give me a sample 30 second script"
"I don't want it to be a testimonial because this represents someone
 who is not a client since they're AI generated. I just want them to
 talk about my product or service, benefits, pain points it solves."
"There should be no scene changes. Just one simple scene tiktok style."
"This is done with HeyGen so we can't do cute laughs or anything.
 Will just be a straight forward script"
```

### UGC Style Characteristics
- Single scene, TikTok-style framing
- Person talking directly into selfie camera
- Casual, conversational tone
- Settings: inside car, walking, at home
- No fancy production -- authentic feel

## SCENE-BY-SCENE SCRIPT FORMAT

After writing a basic script, ask ChatGPT:
"Would you like me to break this into a scene by scene table with visuals and AI prompts?"

### Output Format
| Scene | Dialogue | Visual Direction | Whisk Prompt |
|-------|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 (0-8 sec) | "Hey, I'm not really Jessica..." | Subject in office, casual, looking at camera | "A professional woman in her 30s sitting at a modern desk, looking directly at camera, warm lighting" |
| 2 (8-16 sec) | "The real Jessica is busy booking..." | Subject in travel-themed setting | [Refine from Scene 1]: "Now in front of a travel agency backdrop with world maps" |
| 3 (16-24 sec) | "She can help you find..." | Subject walking through destination | [Refine]: "Walking through a beautiful Italian street, smiling" |

## SCRIPT QUALITY CHECKLIST

1. Script broken into 8-second segments (Google Flow limit)
2. Each segment has clear visual direction for Whisk
3. Dialogue is conversational, not corporate
4. Hook is in the first 3 seconds
5. CTA is clear and specific
6. Total length under 60 seconds for social platforms
7. Scene changes every 6-10 seconds for visual variety
8. No words that are commonly mispronounced by AI
9. Humor or personality element included (not generic)
10. Script addresses pain points before introducing solution
