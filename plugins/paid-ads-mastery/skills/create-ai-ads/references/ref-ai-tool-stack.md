# AI Tool Stack, Setup & CapCut Editing Workflow

## REQUIRED TOOLS -- SETUP GUIDE

### 1. Google Ultra Account ($124.99/month)
- **Sign up:** Google Gemini > Subscriptions > Google Ultra
- Sometimes requires signing up for a lower tier first, then upgrading
- Provides: Unlimited Veo 3 Fast generations in Google Flow
- Without Ultra: ~5 generations per day (insufficient for ad production)
- **Important:** Gemini is only used for signup. All work happens in Google Flow.

### 2. ChatGPT (Free or $20/month Plus)
- Used for: Script writing via 60-Second Ad Machine custom GPT
- Plus gives faster responses and access to custom GPTs
- Can use free tier if budget-constrained

### 3. Google Flow (labs.google/flow/about)
- **No separate account needed** -- uses Google Ultra subscription
- AI video generation platform
- Three modes: Text to Video, Frames to Video, Ingredients to Video
- Settings: Veo 3 Fast, 4 outputs per prompt, Portrait for social

### 4. Google Whisk (labs.google/fx/tools/whisk)
- **Free to use** -- no subscription required
- AI image generation for creating scenes before animating in Flow
- Critical "Refine" feature for maintaining character consistency
- Used BEFORE Google Flow in the workflow

### 5. CapCut (Desktop or Online)
- Free version sufficient for most needs, Pro recommended for advanced features
- Available as desktop app or web version
- Used for: Final assembly, music, SFX, captions, trimming

## OPTIONAL TOOLS

### HeyGen ($29/month)
- Creates digital twin avatar speaking any script up to 30 minutes
- Clones both appearance AND voice
- Best for: YouTube videos, long-form social, course content
- Free version available but does NOT clone voice

### SubMagic (submagic.co)
- Auto-adds captions/subtitles and B-roll footage to videos
- Reduces the "noticeably AI" factor
- Paid faster alternative to doing captions manually in CapCut

### ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.com)
- Voice cloning from 5-10 minutes of clear audio
- Free tier available with limited features
- Multi-language support
- Clone once, use across unlimited scripts

### Descript
- Edit video like editing a document
- Auto-removes filler words and background noise
- Voice cloning for corrections without reshooting
- Saves ~70% of editing time vs. traditional editors

### Opus Clip
- Turns long-form video into 20+ social clips
- AI virality scoring (focus on clips scoring 70+)
- Auto captions, hooks, platform-specific formatting

### Midjourney
- Professional AI image generation
- Higher quality than Whisk for certain styles
- Requires Discord access
- Use when Whisk quality is insufficient

## CAPCUT EDITING WORKFLOW

### Core Editing Tasks (In Order)
1. **Import clips** from Google Flow downloads
2. **Arrange** clips in script order on timeline
3. **Trim dead space** -- remove all pauses before/after dialogue
4. **Remove AI artifacts** -- cut weird pauses, breathing, accent issues
5. **Add music** -- background music from CapCut library
6. **Add sound effects** -- enhance transitions and key moments
7. **Add captions** -- for accessibility and engagement
8. **Review pacing** -- ensure quick scene-to-scene movement
9. **Export** -- MP4, resolution matching platform requirements

### Essential Hotkeys
- **B** = Blade tool (cuts/splits clips at playhead)
- **A** = Arrow/selection tool (for moving clips)

### Audio Replacement Trick
When AI mispronounces a single word:
1. Record yourself saying the correct word(s) on your phone
2. Send audio file to computer
3. Import into CapCut timeline
4. Cut original audio at the error point
5. Lower original audio volume in that section
6. Layer your recorded correction over it
7. May need extra words around the error for natural flow

### Music Strategy in CapCut
- Use CapCut's built-in music library (copyright-cleared)
- Music volume: 10-20% of dialogue volume (background, not competing)
- Build tension with music, pause at key moments
- Music pause + key statement = maximum impact

## RECOMMENDED STARTING WORKFLOW

### For Solo Creators / Small Businesses
1. Start with Google Ultra + free ChatGPT + free CapCut
2. Use 60-Second Ad Machine GPT for scripts
3. Generate images in Whisk, animate in Flow
4. Edit in CapCut with captions and music
5. Add HeyGen when you need long-form content

### For Agencies
1. Full stack from day one (efficiency matters at volume)
2. Standardize the 4-step workflow for all client work
3. Create template scripts by industry vertical
4. Build Whisk character libraries per client (save refined images)
5. Use Opus Clip to repurpose ads into organic content
