# PMax Asset Groups, Display/Discovery Ads & Approval Checklists

## PMAX ASSET GROUP SETUP

### Required Assets per Asset Group

| Asset Type | Requirements | Best Practices |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Headlines | Up to 5 (30 chars each) | Use the 7 headline types framework |
| Long headlines | Up to 5 (90 chars each) | Expand on headline with supporting benefit |
| Descriptions | Up to 5 (90 chars each) | Mix benefit, trust, and CTA-focused |
| Images | Up to 20 (1200x628 landscape, 960x960 square, 480x600 portrait) | Lifestyle + product shots |
| Logos | Up to 5 (1200x1200 square, 1200x300 landscape) | Clear on all backgrounds |
| Videos | Up to 5 (YouTube hosted) | 15-30 sec for PMax placements |
| Business name | 1 | Your brand name |
| Final URL | 1 | Most relevant landing page |

### Asset Quality Signals
- **Landscape images:** Show product in use (lifestyle context)
- **Square images:** Product-focused or product + key benefit text
- **Portrait images:** Mobile-optimized, vertical format
- **Videos:** Can be repurposed from YouTube ads (shorter cuts work)
- **Headlines:** Apply the same 7 headline types as RSAs

### Feed-Only vs Full-Asset PMax
- **Feed-only:** Only product feed, no creative assets. Google uses feed images + auto-generated text. Best for: Shopping-focused campaigns with strong feed.
- **Full-asset:** Product feed + all creative assets. Google shows across Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover. Best for: Brands wanting full-funnel reach.
- Test both approaches -- results vary by account.

## ASSET PERFORMANCE RATINGS

Google rates assets as: Low, Good, Best, or Learning. But these are CTR-driven, not conversion-driven.

### The Real Framework (Use CPI Instead)

| | High Impression Share | Low Impression Share |
|---|---|---|
| **High Conversions/Impression** | Winners -- keep and scale | Hidden Gems -- Google isn't showing enough |
| **Low Conversions/Impression** | Silent Killers -- remove immediately | Underperformers -- replace |

**Priority:** Remove Silent Killers first. They eat impressions without converting.

### Asset Group Reporting
- Use the asset group reporting view in Google Ads
- Third-party scripts (e.g., Mike Rhodes PMax script) can provide deeper asset-level data
- Check asset performance weekly for new campaigns, bi-weekly for mature ones

## DISPLAY / DISCOVERY RESPONSIVE AD SPECS

### Display Responsive Ads
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Headlines | Up to 5 (30 chars each) |
| Long headline | 1 (90 chars) |
| Descriptions | Up to 5 (90 chars each) |
| Business name | Required |
| Images | Landscape (1200x628), Square (1200x1200), optional portrait |
| Logo | Square (1200x1200), optional landscape |
| Videos | Optional (YouTube hosted) |

### Discovery Ads
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Headlines | Up to 5 (40 chars each -- slightly longer than Display) |
| Descriptions | Up to 5 (90 chars each) |
| Images | Landscape (1200x628), Square (1200x1200) |
| Logo | Required |
| Call to action | Auto-selected or manual |

### Image Best Practices for Display/Discovery
- Three categories of images needed:
  1. **Lifestyle images** -- Product in use, showing the end result/transformation
  2. **Product-focused images** -- Product shot (avoid plain white background -- add context)
  3. **Product + Results images** -- Product alongside the outcome it delivers
- High contrast and color to stand out in feeds
- People in images outperform product-only images
- Mobile-first design (majority of impressions are mobile)

## GOOGLE AD APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS

### Universal Rules (All Campaign Types)
- No misleading claims or exaggerations
- No prohibited content (weapons, drugs, adult content, counterfeit goods)
- Landing page must match ad promise
- Prices must match between ad and landing page
- No excessive capitalization (OK: "FREE" / Not OK: "BUY THIS NOW")
- No exclamation marks in headlines
- Proper grammar and spelling required

### Shopping-Specific Approval
- Product images must accurately represent the product
- No watermarks or promotional text on images
- Price must match website exactly
- Product must be in stock
- Shipping and return policy must be visible on website

### Video-Specific Approval
- No shocking or exploitative content
- No misleading thumbnails (clickbait)
- Audio must be clear and appropriate
- Subtitles recommended but not required

### PMax-Specific
- All assets (images, headlines, descriptions) must comply with Google Ads policies
- Assets are shown across multiple networks -- ensure they work in all contexts
- Logo must be clear at small sizes (it appears on mobile cards)

## CREATIVE-TO-CHANNEL TEMPERATURE MATCHING

Match creative messaging to the channel's audience temperature:

### Search (Hot Traffic -- High Intent)
- Direct, benefit-focused headlines
- Strong CTA
- Price/offer in description
- Match the exact search query

### Shopping (Warm-to-Hot Traffic)
- Product title = keywords that match search
- Image = visual differentiation from competitors
- Price = competitive and clearly visible

### PMax (Mixed Temperature)
- Provide assets for ALL awareness levels
- Google will match the right asset to the right audience
- Include both problem-aware headlines AND direct-offer headlines

### Display / Discovery (Cold-to-Warm Traffic)
- Lead with curiosity or pattern interrupt
- Lifestyle images (not product-only)
- Softer CTA ("Learn More" over "Buy Now")
- Education-first messaging

### YouTube (Variable Temperature)
- Remarketing: direct, offer-focused, address specific stage
- TOF: education-first, problem-aware hooks, longer format
- Match creative to audience signal type

## ASSET CREATION CHECKLIST (PRE-LAUNCH)

### For RSAs
- [ ] 6-8 headlines using the 7 types framework
- [ ] All 7 types represented
- [ ] 25-30 characters per headline
- [ ] 2-3 descriptions, 80-90 characters each
- [ ] Headlines work standalone AND together
- [ ] DKI configured for relevance anchor headline
- [ ] One RSA per ad group

### For PMax Asset Groups
- [ ] 5 headlines (30 chars) + 5 long headlines (90 chars)
- [ ] 5 descriptions (90 chars each)
- [ ] Landscape, square, AND portrait images
- [ ] At least 1 logo (square + landscape versions)
- [ ] At least 1 video (15-30 sec recommended)
- [ ] Final URL leads to most relevant page
- [ ] Business name set correctly

### For Display/Discovery
- [ ] 5 headlines, 5 descriptions
- [ ] Landscape + square images minimum
- [ ] Images feature people (not just products)
- [ ] Logo clear at small sizes
- [ ] CTA appropriate for channel temperature
