## AI PRODUCT IMAGE GENERATION: 4K WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Source Clear Product Image
- Find product photo on white background OR PNG (transparent)
- Quality must be: Clear, centered, professional
- File format: JPG or PNG

### Step 2: Upscale with Topus Gigapixel
- Software: Topus Gigapixel ($37-70/month subscription)
- Process: Right-click image → Open with Topus Gigapixel
- Upscale factor: 2x recommended
- Model: "Recommended" setting (auto-selects best)
- Time: 2-5 minutes per image
- Output: High-quality PNG/JPG

### Step 3: Find Inspiration References
- Platform: Pinterest (best for aesthetic discovery)
- Search: Product category + aesthetic (e.g., "ceramic mug minimal aesthetic")
- Collect: 5-10 reference images showing desired style
- Benefit: Pinterest shows 50+ similar images after clicking

### Step 4: Generate Lifestyle Image
- Tool choice:
  - Midjourney: More artistic control, reference support
  - Flux: Faster generation, simpler interface
- Generate: 4-8 variations, test different compositions
- Selection: Product centered, aesthetic matches brand, natural lighting, non-distracting background

### Step 5: Edit & Export
- Tool: Photoshop or CapCut for minor adjustments
- Edits: Color grading, crop, text overlay (optional)
- Export: 3000x3000px minimum, PNG with transparency preferred

**Quality standards:**
- Skin texture realistic (if humans present)
- Light reflections accurate
- Shadow placement natural
- Product proportions correct
- Overall aesthetic professional, not "AI looking"

**Timeline:** 30-45 minutes per product image
**Skill level required:** Low-moderate

## AI VOICEOVER: ElevenLabs WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Script Preparation
- Format: Written script with natural pacing
- Punctuation matters: Commas = short pause, periods = longer pause, "..." = dramatic pause
- Emphasis: Use ALL CAPS for emphasis, **bold** for stress
- Exclamation marks: Add energy (use strategically)
- Conversational: Sound like real speech, not robotic

**Example:**
```
Hey there. You know that feeling when... your neck just won't cooperate?
Yeah, SAME. I was in your position.
That's until I discovered THIS.
```

### Step 2: Voice Selection
- Voice choice: Default voice of chosen avatar (most natural)
- Brand consistency: Use same voice across all ads
- Testing: Some voices sound more authentic than others

### Step 3: Parameter Tuning
- Speed: 1.0 (default) OR 0.9-1.1 (slight variation)
- Pitch: 0.8-1.2 (adjust for gender/age variation)
- Stability: 0.5-0.7 (lower = more natural, slight variation)
- Clarity: 0.8-1.0 (higher = crisp, but can sound artificial)
- Similarity: 0.75-1.0 (how closely mimics original voice)

### Step 4: Testing Before Cost
- Preview: Listen before generation (preview = no cost)
- Adjust: Tweak parameters based on preview
- Regenerate: Use credits efficiently

### Step 5: Generation
- Cost per voiceover: 1-2 credits (ElevenLabs tier dependent)
- Pro tier: $11/month (recommended for consistent production)

**Timeline:** 5-10 minutes per voiceover
**Skill level required:** Low

## AI UGC VIDEOS: ARCADS WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Script with Hook System
- Three-hook structure: Test 3 different openings in ONE video
- Separation: Use `[break_time]` code between hooks
- Break_time = ~1.5 second pause for natural separation

**Example script:**
```
[Hook 1: Question approach]
Have you ever woken up with neck pain so bad you can't work?

[break_time]

[Hook 2: Statistic approach]
Did you know 67% of people experience neck pain from sleep position?

[break_time]

[Hook 3: Problem approach]
You know that feeling when you wake up stiff and sore...

[break_time]

[Main body script - continues from whichever hook resonates]
Here's what finally fixed it for me. This pillow changed everything...
```

**Efficiency:** One video generation = 3 hook variations tested

### Step 2: Avatar Selection
- Library: 20-50+ different AI avatars
- Selection criteria: Age match (teens vs professionals), ethnicity diversity, gender options, expression
- Preview: Watch avatar before committing
- Testing: Test different ethnicities, ages

### Step 3: Script Formatting for Authenticity
- Punctuation rules:
  - Commas: 0.3-0.5 second pause
  - Periods: 0.5-1 second pause
  - Ellipsis (...): 1-2 second pause (dramatic effect)
  - Exclamation marks: Add energy
- Capitalization: Normal sentence case (not ALL CAPS)
- Contractions: Use "it's", "that's", "I'm" (sounds natural)
- Filler words: "Um", "like", "you know" (authenticity)
- Pacing: Short + long + medium sentences (varied rhythm)

### Step 4: Voice Configuration
- Voice: Select appropriate (default usually best)
- Speed: 1.0 (default) to 1.2 (slightly faster, energetic)
- Pitch: Adjust if swapping gender of avatar
- Stability/Clarity/Similarity: Use ElevenLabs recommended settings

### Step 5: Preview & Generation
- Preview audio: Listen to voiceover before generation (free)
- Generation: Costs 1 credit (expensive, preview first!)
- Processing time: 10-15 minutes
- Quality: Choose HD if available

**Timeline:** 30-45 minutes per AI UGC video
**Skill level required:** Low-moderate
**Cost per video:** $1-2 (credit system)

**Platform alternatives:**
- Make UGC: Similar to Arcads, different avatar library
- CreatorIQ: Alternative AI UGC platform

## AI VOICEOVER + VIDEO INTEGRATION

**VSL workflow (voice + product footage):**

1. Generate voiceover with ElevenLabs
2. Create or source product footage (can be real footage, AI-generated, or stock)
3. Edit in CapCut:
   - Import voiceover
   - Align video footage to voiceover timing
   - Add subtitles/captions
   - Color grade if needed
   - Export

4. Quality check:
   - Audio/video sync (no lip-sync needed but timing matters)
   - Audio quality clear (no background noise)
   - Subtitles readable, accurate

**Timeline:** 1-2 hours for complete VSL workflow

## PROMPT ENGINEERING FOR AI IMAGES

**Prompt structure formula:**
[Subject] + [Action] + [Setting] + [Mood/Lighting] + [Camera/Lens] + [Quality/Style]

**Example breakdown:**
```
SUBJECT: "Ceramic travel mug"
ACTION: "with hot coffee being poured"
SETTING: "on white marble kitchen counter"
MOOD: "golden hour natural sunlight"
CAMERA: "shot on 35mm lens with shallow depth of field"
QUALITY: "professional product photography, ultra high quality, magazine cover style"

Full prompt:
"Close-up of ceramic travel mug with hot coffee being poured on white marble kitchen counter with golden hour natural sunlight, shot on 35mm lens with shallow depth of field, professional product photography, ultra high quality, magazine cover style"
```

**Advanced techniques:**
1. Reference images: Attach 1-3 images (Midjourney/Flux) to guide style
2. Negative prompts: Specify what NOT to generate ("no AI artifacts", "no distortion", "no hands")
3. Weight modifiers: Emphasize key aspects with `::weight` modifier
4. Style codes: Photography genre codes ("shot in style of National Geographic")
5. Lighting descriptions: Specific terms ("golden hour", "studio key light", "rim lighting")
6. Lens language: "Shot on 85mm", "24mm wide", "50mm portrait" impacts composition

**Common mistakes:**
- Over-specifying (confuses AI)
- Vague descriptions ("nice photo" vs "professional studio lighting")
- Requesting hands prominently (AI struggles)
- Requesting text in images (AI struggles)
- Conflicting moods ("dark and moody" + "bright and cheerful")

**Testing methodology:**
- Generate 4-8 variations per prompt
- Test different lens focal lengths (35mm vs 85mm)
- Test different lighting descriptions
- Test with and without reference images
- A/B test results with audiences (which style converts best?)

## QUALITY ASSURANCE FOR AI CREATIVES

**When AI falls short:**

**Issue: Hands look weird**
- Solution: Generate without hands visible, or use Photoshop GenFill to fix

**Issue: Product looks distorted**
- Solution: Try Flux instead of Midjourney (often better for products), or add "realistic proportions" to prompt

**Issue: Text in image is unreadable**
- Solution: Don't request text in AI generation, add text in Figma/Photoshop after

**Issue: Product disappears into background**
- Solution: Add "product clearly visible and centered" to prompt, use contrasting background color

**Issue: Looks obviously AI-generated**
- Solution: Add "natural, not AI generated, photorealistic" to negative prompt

**When to use hybrid approach (AI + manual):**
- AI generates foundation
- Manual tweaks (color, text overlay, small adjustments)
- Photoshop GenFill for problem areas
- Result: 80% AI speed, 100% polish

## ITERATION AT SCALE

**AI advantage: Test quantity**
- Traditional: 10-20 creative variations
- AI approach: 100+ creative variations

**Testing methodology:**

1. **Batch 1: Hook types** (5-10 ads testing different hooks)
   - Problem hook
   - Question hook
   - Statistic hook
   - Visual hook
   - Story hook

2. **Batch 2: Visuals** (5-10 ads testing different aesthetics)
   - Minimalist aesthetic
   - Lifestyle aesthetic
   - Product-focused
   - Aspirational
   - Authentic/raw

3. **Batch 3: Copy angles** (5-10 ads testing different promises)
   - Speed benefit
   - Quality benefit
   - Price/value
   - Social proof angle
   - Guarantee angle

4. **Scale winners**
   - Track which variations hit 2.0x+ ROAS
   - Produce 10-20 more of winning variation
   - Stop when new variations underperform

**Timeline:** 5-7 days to test 100 variations, identify winners
**Cost:** $200-300 to test 100 ads

## HYBRID WORKFLOW (AI + TRADITIONAL)

**When to combine:**
- Use AI for 80% of testing (cheap, fast)
- Use traditional for 20% of scaling winners (polish, authenticity)

**Example workflow:**
1. Create 50 AI UGC variations (test all hooks, visuals, copy)
2. Identify top 3-5 performers (2.0x+ ROAS)
3. Hire UGC creator to produce 5-10 higher-quality versions of winning concept
4. Run top-quality versions alongside AI versions

**Result:**
- Fast discovery phase (AI)
- Premium execution phase (traditional)
- Best of both worlds

## WHEN TO USE AI VS TRADITIONAL

**Use AI when:**
- Testing hypotheses (cheap, fast iteration)
- Need high volume creatives (100+ variations)
- Budget constraints (can't afford traditional production)
- Short timeline (need creatives in days, not weeks)
- Testing new markets/audiences (risk mitigation)

**Use traditional when:**
- Winner is identified (scale with polish)
- Brand requires high-end aesthetic (luxury positioning)
- Product needs authentic human element (personal testimonials)
- Customer sees product before buying (physical inspection)

**Use hybrid when:**
- Best of both (AI discovery + traditional polish)
- Budget allows ($300-500 per scaled winner)
- Timeline allows (1-2 weeks)
