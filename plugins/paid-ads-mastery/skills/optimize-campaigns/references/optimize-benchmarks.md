# Performance Benchmarks by Platform & Vertical

## Meta Ads Benchmarks

### E-commerce Product Sales (Catalog/DPA)

**ROAS Targets:** 1.8-2.5x (healthy buffer above breakeven)
**CPA Target:** 25-40% of AOV
**CTR:** 1.2-2.0%
**CPM:** $2.50-5.00
**Hook Rate:** 35%+
**Hold Rate (Video):** 10-15%
**Cost Per Add to Cart:** $3-8
**Cost Per Init Checkout:** $8-15

**Best Performing Format:**
- Video: 9x16 reels format (60-120 sec)
- Static: 1x1 square with lifestyle image + headline

## Google Ads Benchmarks

### Google Shopping (Standard)

**ROAS Targets:** 3.0-4.0x (minimum viable 3.0)
**ROAS by Product Price Tier:**
- Low ticket (<$30): 3.5-5.0x (high volume offsets thin margins)
- Mid ticket ($30-100): 3.0-4.0x
- High ticket (>$100): 2.5-3.5x (slower conversion)

**Quality Score Breakdown:**
- 10: 0.3% of keywords (exceptionally rare)
- 8-9: 20-30% of keywords (strong CTR + landing page)
- 6-7: 40-50% of keywords (healthy account)
- 4-5: 20-30% of keywords (needs attention)
- <3: >5% signals systemic problem

**Conversion Rate:** 2-5% (varies by traffic source)
**CPC:** $0.20-0.80 depending on competition
**Impression Share:** Target 70%+ on profitable keywords

---

### Google Search Ads (Branded + Non-Branded)

**ROAS Targets:** 2.5-3.5x
**CPC by Match Type:**
- Exact match: $0.50-1.50 (highest intent)
- Phrase match: $0.30-1.00 (good intent)
- Broad match: $0.20-0.60 (lower intent)

**Quality Score Drivers:**
- CTR: Account-level CTR >5% = strong
- Landing Page: Relevance to keyword critical
- Ad Relevance: Copy must match keyword

**Best Performing Scenarios:**
- Branded keywords (own brand): ROAS 4.0-6.0x
- Competitor keywords: ROAS 2.0-3.0x
- Informational keywords: ROAS 1.5-2.5x

---

### Google Performance Max

**ROAS Targets:** 2.5-3.5x (learning phase: 1.5-2.0x expected)
**Learning Phase Duration:** 7-14 days (expect volatility)
**Post-Learning Phase:** ROAS stabilizes around 2.5x+

**Asset Group Performance Variance:**
- Best performing asset group: Often 40-60% better ROAS than worst
- Implication: Test 2-3 asset groups simultaneously to identify winner

**Conversion Rate:** 1-3% (lower than Shopping, higher reach)

---

### YouTube Ads (Non-Skippable + Skippable)

**ROAS Targets:** 1.5-2.5x
**CPV (Cost Per View):** $0.05-0.25 (varies by audience)

## Red Flags vs. Green Lights

### Red Flags (Investigate Immediately)

- CTR 50%+ below platform benchmark
- Hook rate <15% (video creative issue)
- Quality score <4 on 20%+ of keywords
- ROAS variance ±0.5+ daily (data quality issue)
- CPA inflating 5%+ per week

### Green Lights (Safe to Scale)

- ROAS stable ±0.2 for 7+ days
- CTR within 10% of benchmark
- Quality scores 7+ on 80%+ of keywords
- Conversion rate stable week-over-week
- CPA within ±5% of target
