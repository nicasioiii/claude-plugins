# Google Full-Funnel Strategy - 4 Pillars Framework

## Overview
- **When to implement:** Budget $1,500+/day AND proven channels working
- **Structure:** 4 coordinated pillar campaigns: Awareness, Consideration, Capture, Retention
- **Timeline:** 8-12 weeks to optimize all 4 pillars together
- **Expected ROAS:** Varies by pillar (1.0-1.5 awareness; 2.0-3.0 capture)
- **Total portfolio ROAS:** 2.0-2.5 (blended across all pillars)

---

## THE 4-PILLAR FRAMEWORK

### Pillar 1: AWARENESS (Demand Generation) - 20% of budget
**Goal:** Build brand awareness among cold audience
**Channels:** Display, YouTube (cold audiences), GDN
**Audience:** Interests, In-market, Similar audiences (100M+ people)
**Budget allocation:** 20% ($300/day if $1,500/day total)
**Expected ROAS:** 0.8-1.2 (top funnel; break-even expected)
**Timeline to profit:** 6+ months (long-tail)

**Campaigns:**
- YouTube Discovery (in-market audiences)
- Display Network (interest targeting)
- Podcast ads (audio awareness)

**Success metric:** Brand lift; assisted conversions; top-funnel engagement

### Pillar 2: CONSIDERATION (Demand Generation) - 30% of budget
**Goal:** Educate interested prospects; build consideration
**Channels:** YouTube (consideration), Search (broad keywords), Display (remarketing start)
**Audience:** Website visitors, Past customers, Shopping audiences, Intent modifiers
**Budget allocation:** 30% ($450/day)
**Expected ROAS:** 1.5-2.5 (mid-funnel; some conversions)
**Timeline to profit:** 3-4 months

**Campaigns:**
- YouTube Retargeting (website visitors 0-30 days)
- Search Broad Keywords (awareness, problem-aware)
- Display Remarketing (past visitors, low intensity)

**Success metric:** Assisted conversions; repeat visits; time-on-site increase

### Pillar 3: CAPTURE (Demand Capture) - 35% of budget
**Goal:** Convert high-intent customers actively searching
**Channels:** Shopping, Search (high-intent), Performance Max
**Audience:** Branded keywords, Product searchers, Shopping audiences, Past high-intent visitors
**Budget allocation:** 35% ($525/day)
**Expected ROAS:** 2.5-4.0 (bottom funnel; high-intent)
**Timeline to profit:** 2-4 weeks

**Campaigns:**
- Google Shopping (high-bid and low-bid testing)
- Search (branded + high-intent keywords)
- Performance Max (all channels, proven products only)

**Success metric:** Direct conversions; ROAS; cost per conversion

### Pillar 4: RETENTION (Demand Capture + Loyalty) - 15% of budget
**Goal:** Re-engage customers, encourage repeat purchases, build loyalty
**Channels:** Email (owned), Remarketing, RLSA (Remarketing Lists for Search Ads)
**Audience:** Past purchasers, Email list, High-value segments
**Budget allocation:** 15% ($225/day)
**Expected ROAS:** 3.0-5.0 (warmest audience)
**Timeline to profit:** Immediate (repeat customers)

**Campaigns:**
- Dynamic Remarketing (show past customers their viewed products)
- RLSA (bid higher on search for past purchasers)
- Cross-sell campaigns (customers bought X → show Y)

**Success metric:** Repeat purchase rate; customer lifetime value; AOV increase

## MEASUREMENT & ATTRIBUTION

### Primary Metrics by Pillar

| Pillar | Primary Metric | Target | Secondary Metrics |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Brand lift / Assisted conversions | +15-30% site visits | Impressions, reach, frequency |
| **Consideration** | Assisted conversions | +30-50% add-to-carts | Return visitors, pages/session |
| **Capture** | Direct conversions / ROAS | 3.0+ ROAS | CPA, conversion rate |
| **Retention** | Repeat purchase rate | 20%+ customers buy again | Customer LTV, AOV |

### Data-Driven Integration
```
Awareness → Consideration → Capture → Retention
    ↓            ↓            ↓           ↓
Impressions   Clicks       Sales      Repeat Sales
    ↓            ↓            ↓           ↓
Brand Lift   Add-to-Cart   ROAS    Customer LTV
```

**Each pillar feeds the next:** Awareness customers move to Consideration, then to Capture, then to Retention.

## SYNERGY EFFECTS (Why All 4 Matter)

### Without Full Funnel (Just Capture - 35% budget)
```
- Spend: $525/day on Shopping/Search
- Conversions: 30/day (highly efficient)
- Cost per customer: $17.50
- ROAS: 3.5
- Monthly revenue: $90K
```

### With Full Funnel (All 4 pillars - $1,500/day)
```
- Awareness: Creates warm audience
- Consideration: Educates that audience
- Capture: Converts aware audience (higher conversion rate)
- Retention: Repeats purchase
- Spend: $1,500/day across all pillars
- Conversions: 150+/day (30 capture + 50 assisted from consider + 20+ repeat)
- Average cost per customer: $10 (lower due to awareness warm-up)
- ROAS: 2.5 blended (lower per pillar but higher aggregate)
- Monthly revenue: $450K+ (5x from Capture alone)
```

**Key insight:** Full funnel achieves lower cost per customer + higher customer lifetime value by warming up audiences before they hit Capture stage.

## ADVANCED: CHANNEL ATTRIBUTION MODEL

**Choose attribution model based on goal:**

| Model | Best For | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **First-click** | Top-funnel budgeting | Over-invests in awareness |
| **Last-click** | Bottom-funnel budgeting | Under-values awareness |
| **Time-decay** | Balanced view | Most complex setup |
| **Multi-touch** | Full-funnel (RECOMMENDED) | Requires GA4 + proper setup |

**Google's recommendation:** Use Multi-touch attribution to fairly credit all pillars.

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Don't start with full funnel** — Build Capture first, then add others
2. **20% Awareness / 30% Consideration / 35% Capture / 15% Retention** is optimal allocation
3. **Awareness pillar breaks even long-term** (months 6+); don't expect immediate ROI
4. **Consideration is the bridge** between cold (Awareness) and hot (Capture)
5. **Capture is workhorse** (35-45% budget); highest ROAS
6. **Retention is most profitable** (3.0-5.0 ROAS); often neglected
7. **Integration matters** — Full funnel achieves lower CAC than Capture alone
8. **Monthly reviews essential** — Rebalance budget based on pillar performance
9. **Customer LTV increases** when you run all 4 pillars (repeat rates improve)
10. **Synergy effect** — Full funnel 2-3x more efficient than Capture-only once optimized
