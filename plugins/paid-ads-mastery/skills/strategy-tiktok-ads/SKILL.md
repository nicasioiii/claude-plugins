## PLATFORM PHILOSOPHY

TikTok is an entertainment platform disguised as a social network. Sound-on environment; thumb primed for next dopamine hit.

**The Mantra:** Ads must entertain first and sell as a natural consequence.

When brands swap TV-style ads for TikTok-first videos: watch time nearly doubles, conversions jump 37%.

### What This Means for Ad Creation
- Ads cannot act like interruptions -- they must behave like the content audience came to consume.
- Low-fi beats polished. Real people over actors. Natural lighting. Trending sounds.
- Vertical 9x16, no UI overlap, keep text/elements in safe zone.

---

## THE ALGORITHM: THREE PILLARS

1. **User interactions** -- swipes, rewatches, comments, shares, profile taps.
2. **Video information** -- captions, on-screen tags, hashtags, sounds.
3. **Device and account settings** -- language, location, device type.

The power curve sits in the first 3 seconds. Opening frame drives the earliest watch-time signal that determines distribution.

## SIX CREATIVE PRINCIPLES (ACA)

### 1. TikTok First
- Low-fi, vertical, human, ideally funny.
- 9x16, no UI overlap.
- Real people over actors, natural lighting, trending sounds.

### 2. Lean Into Trends
- **Forces:** Big shifts (lasting months/years).
- **Moments:** Flash-wave memes lasting weeks.
- **Signals:** Editing styles, viral sounds.
- Process: Identify -> Isolate the mechanic -> Iterate 3 versions.

### 3. Ad Structure
- Hook -> Key Message -> CTA.
- Suspense/surprise/strong emotion in first 3 seconds increases watch time by 1.7x.

### 4. Stimulation
- Fast cuts, kinetic captions, emojis, camera movements, dramatic zooms.
- Each is a micro-reward that keeps attention firing.

### 5. Sound
- Music sets mood; faster BPM boosts memorability.
- Voiceovers clarify value props.
- ASMR and sound effects elevate creative.

### 6. Creative Refresh
- When CPA rises 20% above week-one average: rotate in fresh assets.
- When CTR drops 30%: rotate in fresh assets.

## THREE AD STRUCTURES

| Structure | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Talent-engagement forward** | Personality first, engaging demos, product woven through | Brands with strong creator relationships |
| **Problem/product forward** | Highlight pain point, reveal product, demonstrate, urgent CTA | Problem-solution products |
| **No visible talent** | Low-fi, hands-in-product only with voiceover | Products that demo well visually |

## PRODUCTION ESSENTIALS

### Sweet Spot Ad Length
20-35 seconds. Long enough for message, short enough for completion.

### Safe Zones
- Top 20% and bottom 20% are UI land -- keep text/key elements out.
- Design for the center frame.

### Minimum Creative Volume
5-7 unique creatives per conversion campaign, refreshed weekly.

### Casting & Diversity
Showcase diversity in casting. Show one product maximum (3 if they are a bundle).

### Price Disclosure
TikTok is price-sensitive. Disclose price or promotion early in the ad.

---

## THREE-WEEK CREATIVE ITERATION LOOP

- **Week 1:** Launch varied hooks (3-5 creatives per ad group). Test different approaches.
- **Week 2:** Double down on winners. Increase budget on top performers.
- **Week 3:** Remix sound, captions, and first 3 seconds of winners. Refresh for another cycle.

Repeat continuously.

## SIX-DAY TESTING ACTION PLAN

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | Pull audience insights, list 3 subcultures, write 3 scripts |
| Day 2 | Spin 5 variants in Symphony/CapCut, tag each with creative principle |
| Day 3 | Launch test budget ($50 per ad set, auto placement) |
| Day 4 | Check 3-second hold rate and CTR, kill underperformers |
| Day 5 | Remix best performers 2x (change hook or sound only) |
| Day 6 | Scale top ads, discard losers, document learnings, repeat |

## ANTI-PATTERNS

1. **TV-style ads on TikTok** -- overproduced, interruption-style content kills performance.
2. **Ignoring sound** -- TikTok is sound-on. Silence or generic stock music misses the platform's strength.
3. **No creative refresh** -- TikTok creative fatigues faster than any other platform. Weekly refresh is minimum.
4. **Too few creatives per campaign** -- less than 5-7 unique creatives starves the algorithm.
5. **Saving best content for end** -- viewers won't wait. First 3 seconds determine everything.
6. **Ignoring safe zones** -- logos, text, and CTAs getting covered by TikTok UI looks sloppy and hurts performance.
7. **Not disclosing price** -- TikTok audiences are price-sensitive. Hiding the price creates distrust.
8. **Static ads only** -- TikTok is a video-first platform. Static formats underperform dramatically.
