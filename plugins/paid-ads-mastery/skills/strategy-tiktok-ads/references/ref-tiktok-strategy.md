# TikTok Strategy & Creative Principles

## PLATFORM PHILOSOPHY

### Entertainment Disguised as Social
- TikTok is an entertainment platform, not a traditional social network.
- Sound-on environment by default -- users expect audio.
- Thumb primed for next dopamine hit -- your ad competes with the most entertaining content on the internet.
- Ads that act like interruptions fail. Ads that entertain succeed.

### The Performance Data
- When brands swap TV-style ads for TikTok-first videos: watch time nearly doubles.
- Conversions jump 37% with TikTok-native creative.
- The difference between "ad" and "content" on TikTok is the difference between success and failure.

---

## THE ALGORITHM: THREE PILLARS

### Pillar 1: User Interactions
- Swipes (negative signal), rewatches (strong positive), comments, shares, profile taps.
- Rewatch is one of the strongest signals -- if someone watches twice, the algorithm pushes hard.
- Shares = strongest signal of all (content worth spreading).

### Pillar 2: Video Information
- Captions (keywords the algorithm reads).
- On-screen tags and text.
- Hashtags (categorization, not reach).
- Sounds (trending sounds get distribution boost).

### Pillar 3: Device and Account Settings
- Language, location, device type.
- These are baseline filters, not primary distribution drivers.

### The Power Curve
The first 3 seconds drive the earliest watch-time signal. This signal determines whether the algorithm gives your ad broader distribution. Everything hinges on the opening frame.

## SIX CREATIVE PRINCIPLES (Detailed)

### Principle 1: TikTok First
What it means in practice:
- **Low-fi production:** iPhone-shot, not studio. Natural lighting, not ring lights.
- **Vertical 9x16:** No horizontal crops. Native vertical.
- **Human faces:** Real people over actors. Authenticity reads immediately.
- **Humor when possible:** Funny ads outperform serious ads on TikTok consistently.
- **No UI overlap:** Keep all important elements in the center safe zone.
- **Trending sounds:** Use platform-native audio, not stock music.

### Principle 2: Lean Into Trends
Three categories of trends:

| Category | Duration | Example | How to Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Forces** | Months to years | Sustainability, AI, wellness | Align brand message with cultural shift |
| **Moments** | Days to weeks | Flash memes, viral challenges | Rapid response: identify, isolate mechanic, iterate 3 versions |
| **Signals** | Weeks to months | Editing styles, caption formats, viral sounds | Adopt the format, apply your message |

**Process:** Identify trend -> Isolate the core mechanic that makes it work -> Iterate 3 versions with your product/message.

### Principle 3: Ad Structure
**Hook -> Key Message -> CTA.**

- Suspense, surprise, or strong emotion in first 3 seconds increases watch time by 1.7x.
- The hook must signal relevance AND generate curiosity simultaneously.
- Key message should be clear and singular -- one benefit, one reason to care.
- CTA should feel natural, not forced (part of the content, not bolted on).

### Principle 4: Stimulation
Every 1-3 seconds, something must change to maintain attention:
- Fast cuts between angles.
- Kinetic captions (moving, appearing, disappearing).
- Emojis and visual reactions.
- Camera movements (zooms, pans, reveals).
- Dramatic zooms for emphasis.

Each visual change is a micro-reward that keeps the viewer's attention firing. Think of it as feeding small dopamine hits throughout the ad.

### Principle 5: Sound
- **Music sets mood:** Faster BPM boosts memorability and energy.
- **Voiceovers clarify:** Use voiceover to explain value prop while visuals demonstrate.
- **ASMR and SFX:** Sound effects elevate creative impact (satisfying sounds, product sounds).
- **Trending sounds:** Give distribution boost. Monitor TikTok Creative Center for current trends.
- **Strategic silence:** Occasional pause for emphasis (before key reveal).

### Principle 6: Creative Refresh
Fatigue triggers (rotate in fresh assets when either occurs):
- CPA rises 20% above week-one average.
- CTR drops 30% from peak.

TikTok creative fatigues faster than Meta or Google. Weekly refresh cadence is the minimum standard for active campaigns.

## THREE AD STRUCTURES (Detailed)

### Structure 1: Talent-Engagement Forward
- **Opening:** Creator personality hooks viewer (humor, energy, relatability).
- **Middle:** Product woven into engaging content/demonstration.
- **Close:** Natural CTA from the creator.
- **Best for:** Brands with strong creator relationships, products that benefit from personality.
- **Example pattern:** Creator doing their routine -> discovers/uses product -> genuine reaction -> recommendation.

### Structure 2: Problem/Product Forward
- **Opening:** Highlight specific pain point (bold text + visual).
- **Middle:** Reveal product as solution, demonstrate in action.
- **Close:** Urgent CTA with specific offer.
- **Best for:** Problem-solution products, health/wellness, productivity tools.
- **Example pattern:** "Tired of [problem]?" -> show product -> demo results -> "Get yours now, link in bio."

### Structure 3: No Visible Talent
- **Opening:** Product shot or hands-in-product with voiceover.
- **Middle:** Close-up demonstration, satisfying visuals, ASMR elements.
- **Close:** Product shot with text CTA.
- **Best for:** Products that demo well visually (gadgets, food, beauty products).
- **Example pattern:** Close-up of product -> voiceover explains benefits -> demonstration -> result shot -> CTA text.

## KEY METRICS & BENCHMARKS

### Primary Performance Metrics
| Metric | Healthy Benchmark | What It Tells You |
|---|---|---|
| 3-second hold rate | Above 70% | Hook quality -- are people staying past the first frame? |
| Average watch time | At least 1.4x video length | Content quality -- are people rewatching? |
| Completion rate | 18-25% for conversion ads | Full message delivery -- is the story compelling enough? |
| Engagement rate | 4-8% for consumer brands | Community resonance -- does it spark interaction? |
| CTR | Above 2% for e-commerce | Purchase intent -- are people clicking through? |

### Fatigue Indicators
| Signal | Threshold | Action |
|---|---|---|
| CPA rising | 20% above week-one average | Rotate in fresh creative |
| CTR dropping | 30% from peak | Rotate in fresh creative |
| Frequency climbing | Above 3x in a week | Expand audience or refresh creative |

## TIKTOK vs META vs GOOGLE: KEY DIFFERENCES

| Dimension | TikTok | Meta | Google |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary mode | Entertainment | Social/Discovery | Intent/Search |
| Audio | Sound-on default | Sound-off default | Varies by format |
| Creative lifespan | Days to weeks | Weeks to months | Months to years (YouTube) |
| Production style | Raw, authentic, low-fi | Mix of polished and UGC | Informational, demo-focused |
| Hook window | 1-3 seconds | 3 seconds | 5 seconds (YouTube skip) |
| Refresh cadence | Weekly minimum | Bi-weekly to monthly | Monthly to quarterly |
