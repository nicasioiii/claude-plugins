Help me prepare for, run, or debrief a discovery call. Build question sequences using Problems First or Goals First syntax (Gordon), qualify prospects against the Belief Ladder, amplify pain with the three-layer technique (Alexander), apply the Funnel Technique for multi-stakeholder deals (Serhant), and weave in calibrated questions and labeling from tactical empathy (Voss). Diagnose whether to close, nurture, or disqualify based on the four qualification criteria.
