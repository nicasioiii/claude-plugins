# DM & Phone Setting Scripts

## DM Setting Scripts (B2C -- Alexander)

### Standard DM Flow (Instagram/Facebook/Twitter)

**Opening (after they respond to CTA):**
```
"Hey! Thanks for reaching out. Just have a few quick questions to
see if we can help. You came from the [story/post/ad] -- looks like
you want to learn [topic]. Sound right?"
```

**Current Situation:**
```
"Awesome. So what do you do for work right now?"

[They respond]

"Got it. And what's the main reason you're looking to get into
[topic]? What's driving that?"
```

**Goal Quantification:**
```
"Makes sense. So if this worked out, what would you need to be
making monthly to feel like it was worth it?"

[Get a specific number]

"Nice. And is that to replace your job income, or on top of it?"
```

**Time Gap + Urgency:**
```
"How long has [goal] been a goal of yours?"

[They respond]

"What have you tried so far?"

[They respond]

"If this has been the goal for [X time], what makes it important
to figure this out NOW rather than waiting?"
```

**Financial Qualification:**
```
"Got it. So obviously getting started with something like this
requires some capital. If this program was exactly what you needed
to get to [goal], do you have $[range] available to invest in
yourself?"
```

**Handling Financial Objection in DMs:**
- If they say too much: "Is that what you're HOPING to invest, or is that ALL you have?"
- If truly insufficient: "No worries -- we actually have a [lower-ticket option] that might be a better starting point. Want me to share details?"
- If they have financing: "We have a buy-now-pay-later option. Do you have a rough idea of your credit score?"

**Booking:**
```
"Perfect -- you sound like a great fit. Let me get you on a longer
Zoom call with one of our coaches for a deeper dive. They'll walk
you through exactly how this works for your situation.

Here's the link: [calendar link]

Choose a time you are 100% good for. And I mean 100% -- pick a
time where nothing will come up."
```

**Confirmation:**
```
"Drop me a confirmation whenever you book and what time you chose."

[They confirm]

"Awesome. You're going to be speaking with [Coach Name]. They're
really good -- helped a lot of people in your situation. Show up
ready with questions. Talk soon!"
```

## Phone Setting Scripts (Alexander -- Updated May 2025)

### Cold Lead Opening (VSL Opt-In)
```
"Hey, is this [Name]?"

[Yes]

"[Name], this is [You] from [Company]. I saw you filled out an
application about [outcome] just like 2 minutes ago. Does that
ring a bell?"

[If confused]: "Yeah so [Influencer name] -- you signed up with
[their email]. You answered some questions about wanting to
[their stated goal]."

"Got a few minutes to chat quick? Just 3-5 minutes to see if
we can actually help."
```

### Discovery Block (3-5 Minutes)
```
"So what caught your attention? What made you want to fill out
the application?"

[Listen]

"Cool. And what do you do for work right now?"

[Listen]

"What's the long-term goal with this -- get out of the job and
do it full-time, or more of a side hustle?"

[Listen -- if surface-level answer:]

"Everyone says 'extra cash' -- that's easy to say. What
specifically would change if you had an extra [amount] coming
in every month?"
```

### Financial Qualification
```
"Obviously you're starting something here, and starting anything
requires capital. How much would you say you have set aside to
invest so you can [their desired outcome]?"
```

**If enough:** Proceed to booking.

**If borderline:**
```
"Is that what you're HOPING to invest, or is that ALL you have
available? I just want to make sure I point you in the right
direction."
```

**If insufficient for high-ticket:**
```
"I appreciate you being honest. The main program is at a higher
price point, but we do have a [lower-ticket option] that could
be a great starting point. Want me to tell you about it?"
```

**If financing is available:**
```
"We have a buy-now-pay-later option that a lot of people use.
Do you have a rough idea of your credit score? Just ballpark
is fine."
```

### Booking
```
"Perfect. Let me get you on a call with one of our coaches.
They're going to walk you through exactly how this works for
your specific situation.

When works best for you -- morning or afternoon?"

[They choose]

"Great. I have [time] available on [date]. Let me send you
the calendar link right now."
```

### Locking Down the Show-Up
```
"Quick thing -- nothing is going to come up that prevents you
from showing up to that call, right?"

[If uncertain language]: "Is that a yes or no? I just want to
make sure nothing pops up. These slots fill up fast and I want
to make sure you get the time."

[If yes]: "Perfect. You'll get a confirmation email and I'll
send you a text reminder 30 minutes before. Show up ready
with questions."
```

## Triage Scripts (Gordon)

### Inbound Triage (Already-Booked Call)

**Opening:**
```
"Hey [Name], this is [You] from [Company]. Just calling to
make sure you're all set for your call on [date/time].

Quick question -- what's the number one thing you're hoping
to get out of the conversation?"
```

**Mini Discovery:**
```
"Got it. And just so I can make sure [Coach] is prepared --
what's your current situation with [topic]? Where are you at
right now?"

[Listen]

"And ultimately, where do you want to be?"
```

**Sell the Consult:**
```
"Perfect. [Coach] has helped a ton of people in your exact
situation. They're really going to dig into your specifics
and show you a clear path forward.

The call will be about 30-45 minutes. Come prepared with
questions -- the more specific, the more they can help."
```

### Messenger Scripts (Gordon)

**2-Step Indirect:**
1. Share a piece of content from the brand
2. Follow up:
```
"What'd you think of that? Pretty eye-opening right?"

[They respond]

"By the way -- are you still working on [goal]? We have
a couple spots opening up for coaching calls this week.
Want me to grab you one?"
```

**Direct:**
```
"Hey [Name] -- saw you [applied/signed up/expressed interest].
I'm [You] from [Company]. Got 2 minutes? Just want to see
if we can help and if it's worth booking a deeper call."
```

**Cold Lead (VSL opt-in, did not book):**
```
"Hey [Name], this is [You] from [Company]. I noticed you
signed up to watch our training but didn't get a chance to
book a call yet.

No pressure at all -- just wanted to see if you had any
questions or if it's even worth your time to chat further.
What caught your eye about it?"
```

## Voicemail Scripts

### Speed-to-Lead Voicemail
```
"Hey [Name], it's [You] from [Company]. You just filled out
an application about [outcome] -- just wanted to connect real
quick. Shooting you a text right now with some info. Talk soon!"
```
Keep under 15 seconds. Create curiosity, not a sales pitch.

### Follow-Up Voicemail (Missed First Call)
```
"Hey [Name], it's [You] again from [Company]. Tried to catch
you earlier. I have something I think you'll find interesting
based on what you shared in your application. Texting you now."
```

### No-Show Voicemail
```
"Hey [Name], it's [You] from [Company]. I had you on the
calendar for [time] today -- looks like we missed each other.
No worries at all. Let me know when a better time works and
we'll get you rescheduled."
```

## Turning Uncertain Language into Certain Language

**Source:** Alexander. One of the most important setter skills.

### The Principle
If you let uncertain language slide at any point, the entire interaction becomes uncertain and ends in a no-show or DQ.

### Common Conversions

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "It should work" | "Is that a yes or no? I just want to make sure nothing pops up." |
| "I'll try to be there" | "I need you there 100%. Can you commit to showing up?" |
| "Yeah, probably" | "You sound a little uncertain. Is there something that might come up?" |
| "I think so" | "What would make it a definite yes?" |
| "Hopefully" | "I want to make sure this time works. Is there a better slot?" |
| "I feel like it could help" | "You sound a little unsure. What specifically makes you feel it CAN get you to [goal]?" |

### Closing-Level Conversion
When a setter hears uncertain language on commitment:
```
"Hey -- I want to make sure I'm setting you up right. If you
show up and this turns out to be exactly what you need, are
you ready to take action? Or is this more of a research call?"
```

This pre-qualifies commitment AND sets expectations for the closer.
