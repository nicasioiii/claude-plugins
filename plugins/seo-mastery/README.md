# SEO Mastery Plugin v1.0

**Comprehensive SEO Knowledge Base for Organic Search Success**

---

## Overview

The **seo-mastery** plugin consolidates 42,000+ lines of SEO expertise into 9 deep, actionable skills. Designed for entrepreneurs and marketing teams running B2B ecommerce, SaaS, local service, and content businesses.

**Core principle:** Deep expertise beats shallow coverage. Each skill is a complete workflow with frameworks, templates, and checklists.

---

## The 9 Skills

### SKILL 1: Keyword Research & Intent Alignment
**When to use:** "What keywords should I target?"

Find rankable keywords aligned with buyer intent. Includes:
- Three research methodologies (volume-based, intent-based, topical clusters)
- Intent classification framework (informational vs. commercial vs. transactional)
- Vertical keyword playbooks (ecommerce, SaaS, local, affiliate)
- Long-tail mining techniques

**Reference files:** 5 comprehensive guides covering research tools, keyword difficulty analysis, vertical strategies.

---

### SKILL 2: Competitive Landscape & Gap Analysis
**When to use:** "Who's ranking for this keyword? What gaps exist?"

Understand market dynamics, find opportunities. Includes:
- SERP analysis framework
- Domain authority assessment
- Content gap identification
- Backlink analysis for link opportunities
- Market saturation checklist

**Reference files:** 5 guides for SERP analysis, DA deep-dives, competitor backlink research, market sizing.

---

### SKILL 3: Content Cluster & Topical Authority
**When to use:** "How do I organize my content for authority?"

Design topical authority architecture. Includes:
- Content cluster definition & sizing
- Pillar-satellite strategy
- Internal linking architecture
- Publishing cadence (all-at-once vs. staggered)
- Scaling from 10 → 100+ articles

**Reference files:** 5 guides with cluster templates, pillar page architecture, internal linking playbook, publishing strategies.

---

### SKILL 4: On-Page SEO & Technical Optimization
**When to use:** "What makes a page rank?"

Optimize meta tags, headers, schema, images. Includes:
- Meta title & description formulas
- H1-H6 hierarchy best practices
- Schema markup implementation (Article, FAQ, Product, BreadcrumbList)
- Image optimization workflows
- Internal linking best practices

**Reference files:** 6 guides covering meta optimization, heading structure, schema, image optimization, URL strategy.

---

### SKILL 5: Content Production & AI Workflows
**When to use:** "How do I write SEO content at scale?"

AI-powered content creation with quality control. Includes:
- AI content workflow (Perplexity → Claude → editing → publishing)
- Content type frameworks (8 types: reviews, roundups, how-tos, comparisons, etc.)
- E-E-A-T demonstration strategies
- Quality control checklists
- Fact-checking and original research integration

**Reference files:** 7 guides with AI workflows, content type templates, writing checklists, E-E-A-T best practices, quality standards.

---

### SKILL 6: Technical SEO & Site Audit
**When to use:** "Why isn't my site ranking? Core Web Vitals issues?"

Fix crawlability, indexation, and performance. Includes:
- Technical hierarchy (crawlability > indexation > mobile > CWV > on-page)
- Crawlability audit workflow (robots.txt, sitemaps, internal linking)
- Indexation troubleshooting (Search Console coverage issues)
- Core Web Vitals optimization (LCP, FID, CLS)
- Site speed optimization (images, caching, server response)
- JavaScript SEO guide (client-side rendering)
- Structured data validation

**Reference files:** 8 guides covering crawl audits, CWV optimization, indexation fixes, mobile-first, site speed, schema validation, JavaScript SEO.

---

### SKILL 7: Link Authority & Outreach Strategy
**When to use:** "How do I get backlinks?"

White-hat, gray-hat, and strategic link acquisition. Includes:
- Link quality framework (DR, traffic, relevance, anchor text)
- HARO workflow (daily process, response templates)
- Guest posting strategy (pitch templates, editor relationships)
- Skyscraper outreach campaign (find high-backlink content, create better version, outreach)
- PBN strategy (risks, domain sourcing, linking strategies)
- Outreach email templates
- Press release SEO distribution
- Link velocity and natural linking pacing

**Reference files:** 8 guides with HARO setup, guest posting process, skyscraper methodology, PBN playbook, email templates, press release distribution.

---

### SKILL 8: Local SEO, Ecommerce SEO & Programmatic Scale
**When to use:** "How do I scale with multiple locations? Product pages? Automate content?"

Consolidates local, ecommerce, and programmatic strategies. Includes:

**Local SEO:**
- NAP consistency foundation
- Google Business Profile optimization (posts, Q&A, attributes, photos)
- Citation strategy (Tier 1-3 directories, indexation)
- Location page templates (pillar vs. individual pages)

**Ecommerce SEO:**
- Product page optimization
- Product schema implementation (rich results)
- Category page strategy (faceted navigation, pagination)
- Review schema and aggregated ratings

**Programmatic SEO:**
- 12 playbooks (template, curation, comparison, list, example, location, persona, integration, glossary, translation, directory, profile)
- AI content at scale (Tier 1/2/3 quality levels, prompt engineering)
- Thin content vs. unique value (quality gates)
- Scaling workflow (pilot → batch test → scale)

**Parasite SEO:**
- High-authority platforms (Google Sites, LinkedIn, Reddit, YouTube, Medium)
- Content distribution strategy
- Zero-risk approach (Google properties)
- Platform-specific best practices

**Reference files:** 8 guides covering GBP mastery, citations, location page templates, ecommerce schema, category strategy, 12 programmatic playbooks, AI content scaling, parasite SEO strategies.

---

### SKILL 9: SEO Analytics & Reporting
**When to use:** "How do I prove SEO ROI? What are my KPIs?"

Measurement infrastructure, reporting, and ROI calculation. Includes:

**Setup:**
- GA4 configuration (events, custom dimensions, key events)
- Google Search Console integration
- Tag Manager setup

**Measurement:**
- SEO KPI definitions (executive, strategic, tactical)
- Attribution models (last-click, first-click, linear, time-decay, position-based)
- Organic revenue calculation (3 methods)
- ROAS calculation

**Reporting:**
- Monthly SEO review checklist (30-60 minutes)
- Looker Studio dashboard templates (executive, keywords, pages, technical, attribution, competitive, AI visibility)
- Monthly reporting cadence

**Advanced:**
- Rank tracking strategies (choosing tools, monitoring, SERP analysis)
- AI search visibility monitoring (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI citations)

**Reference files:** 9 guides covering GA4 setup, GSC mastery, KPI definitions, attribution models, ROI calculation, Looker Studio dashboards, monthly checklists, rank tracking, AI visibility monitoring.
