# /link-outreach -- Link Building Outreach Campaign

## Command Purpose

Create a complete link building outreach campaign with prospect lists, personalized templates, tracking spreadsheet structure, and follow-up sequences.

## Invocation

```
/link-outreach [strategy] [niche/topic] [target-count]
```

**Examples:**
```
/link-outreach haro "SaaS marketing" 20
/link-outreach skyscraper "email marketing guide" 50
/link-outreach guest-posting "technical SEO" 30
/link-outreach niche-edits "project management software" 15
/link-outreach link-swaps "dog parks directory" 10
/link-outreach badges "best plumbers" 20
```

## Supported Strategies

### 1. HARO / Featured.com
- Daily workflow for monitoring journalist queries
- Response formula (credentials -> quotable answer -> example -> bio)
- Target: 20% response-to-placement success rate
- Unnotified link audit process

### 2. Skyscraper
- Find high-linked content in niche (sort by backlinks in Ahrefs Content Explorer)
- Create significantly better version
- Contact everyone who linked to original
- Pitch improved version as replacement

### 3. Guest Posting
- Prospecting via search operators and competitor backlink analysis
- Topic pitch with 2-3 ideas and brief outlines
- Negotiation for dofollow links (body + author bio)
- Post-placement optimization (internal link OTHER pages to your guest post)

### 4. Niche Edits
- Identify target pages with existing traffic and referring domains
- Request content updates alongside link insertion (freshness requirement)
- Verify topical relevance and page quality before purchasing
- Budget: $60-100 per quality niche edit

### 5. Link Swaps (Directory/Authority Sites)
- Find competitor backlinks from small publishers in Ahrefs
- Assess quality in 15 seconds (DR, traffic, relevance, linked domains)
- Personalized outreach with screenshot of competitor link
- Offer: link swap or payment ($50-100)

### 6. Badge/Award Strategy (Directories)
- Create "Top 5/10 Best X in [City]" page
- Design embeddable badge in Canva
- Generate embed code with backlink
- Email businesses with award notification

## Campaign Output Template

### For Each Strategy, Generate:

**1. Prospect Research Brief**
- Target criteria (minimum DR, traffic, relevance requirements)
- Search operators or Ahrefs filters to use
- Red flags to avoid (link farms, no-traffic sites, spam signals)

**2. Prospect List Structure**
| # | Domain | DR | Monthly Traffic | Page Traffic | Contact | Relevance Score | Status |
|---|--------|----|----|----|----|----|----|
| 1 | example.com | 45 | 12K | 800 | editor@... | 8/10 | To Contact |

**3. Outreach Email Templates**

**Initial outreach** (personalized per strategy):
- Subject line (2-3 variations)
- Body copy (specific to strategy type)
- Clear value proposition for the recipient
- Call to action

**Follow-up sequence:**
- Follow-up 1 (Day 5): Brief re-statement of value
- Follow-up 2 (Day 12): Additional value offer (social share, testimonial)
- Follow-up 3 (Day 21): Final touch, leave door open

**4. Tracking Spreadsheet Columns**
| Target | Strategy | DR | Contact | Sent Date | Follow-up 1 | Follow-up 2 | Response | Link Status | Anchor Text | Link URL | Notes |
|--------|----------|----|----|----|----|----|----|----|----|----|----|

**5. Campaign Velocity Plan**
- Weekly sending targets (ramp from 10-15/day to 50-75/day per account)
- Email warm-up timeline (3+ weeks before full volume)
- Anchor text distribution plan for acquired links
- Link velocity safety check (page-by-page, not site-wide)

## Pre-Campaign Checklist

Before launching any outreach campaign:

1. **Domain health check:** Is the outreach domain warmed up? SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured?
2. **Link profile audit:** What's the current anchor text distribution? What ratios should new links follow?
3. **Velocity assessment:** How many links/month is safe given current profile? (Page-by-page analysis)
4. **Content readiness:** Is the target page content-worthy? Would YOU link to it? If not, improve first.
5. **Competitor benchmark:** What links do top 5 competitors have? Match their quality tier.

## Post-Campaign Actions

1. **Index new links:** Submit to indexing services (Omega Index, Google Search Console URL submission)
2. **Monitor rankings:** Track target keyword positions weekly for 60 days
3. **Audit link quality:** Verify all placed links are live, dofollow (if agreed), and properly anchored
4. **Social signals:** Build social signals to high-value new backlinks to activate hint directive
5. **Tier 2 building:** For high-value nofollow placements, build tier 2 links to the linking page

## Strategy Selection Guide

| Situation | Best Strategy | Why |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| New site, no authority | HARO + Guest Posting | Builds authority from scratch with editorial links |
| Established site, plateau | Niche Edits + Skyscraper | Targeted power boost to specific pages |
| Directory site | Link Swaps + Badges | Directory-specific tactics with highest ROI |
| Competitive niche | All strategies combined | Need volume and diversity |
| Low budget | HARO + Badge Strategy | Both free (time-only investment) |
| Quick results needed | Niche Edits | Fastest link acquisition method |
