# Content Promotion & Lifecycle Management

## Automated Promotion System (Nat Eliason)

### Zapier RSS Automation
- Trigger: RSS feed detects new WordPress post
- Actions: auto-tweet on Twitter, auto-post to Facebook page, create Trello tasks for VA
- Connect to: Pinterest, LinkedIn, Buffer, and other platforms
- Instagram blocks web access -- cannot be automated this way

### Re-Promotion on Autopilot
- Tools: MeetEdgar or Post Planner
- Cycles through content library, fills scheduled time slots with articles not recently shared
- For each article, create a few text variations (avoid repetitive look)
- Keep equal variation count per article (no disproportionate sharing)

## Republishing Strategy (Nat Eliason)

### Medium
- Use Medium's "Import a Story" feature (Profile > Stories > Import a Story)
- Paste your URL -- copies article with links intact
- Medium auto-adds canonical tag back to your site (no SEO cannibalization)
- Publish to Medium publications (Better Humans, The Mission, Hacker Noon) for more exposure
- Results: typical 3K-5K views/month, spikes to 13K-20K

### LinkedIn
- Copy article and publish as LinkedIn article
- Takes minutes, no SEO cost
- LinkedIn articles do not outrank originals -- no canonical needed
- Can drive significant traffic sporadically

### Timing Rule
Wait 2+ days before republishing anywhere to guarantee Google indexes your original site first.

### Canonical Tags
HTML tag telling Google which version is original. Medium handles automatically. Set via Yoast or similar for other platforms.

## External Publication Strategy

### Process
1. Find target publication editors' contact info
2. Pitch an ORIGINAL article first (not republished). Give them something great
3. After first article performs well, ask about accepting republished content
4. Once approved, send new articles when relevant
5. Build list of all publications your target market reads

### Key Rule
Crush it on your first article. After one successful piece, publications typically accept everything going forward.

### Aggregator Sites
- Submit tips to sites like Lifehacker, Rockstar Finance
- Build editor relationships for ongoing coverage

## Reddit Promotion (Nat Eliason)

### Strategy
- Post articles to relevant subreddits
- 5-10% success rate, but when it works: 40K-50K views in 24-48 hours
- Find subreddits by searching your topic
- Check if they allow link posts (not just text/self posts)

### Risks
- Getting downvoted, ignored, or banned
- Some subreddits have strict self-promotion rules
- Build genuine participation history before promotional posts

## Influencer Outreach Post-Publication (Nat Eliason)

### Do This
- Tag influencers/brands mentioned in your article when you tweet about it
- Include them naturally in tweet body: "How [Book] by @Author can change your life [link]"
- Makes it easy to simply retweet (much easier than creating new content)

### Do NOT Do This
- Email them saying "I mentioned you, check it out" -- that is annoying
- Ask for a share or link directly
- Be pushy or transactional

## Facebook Boosting (Nat Eliason)

- Boost published articles with $100-250+ for extra exposure
- Target: people who liked your page, custom audiences from Mailchimp, interest targeting
- Set budget and let run for ~1 week
- More traffic = better ranking signals for new content

## Content Lifecycle Management (Vahe Arabian)

### Content Re-Usage Strategies
- **Reformat** content into different media (video, image SEO, web stories)
- **Repurpose** for different platforms (Flipboard, social, syndication)
- **Improve** existing content to boost rankings and extend lifecycle

### Evergreen vs News Philosophy
- Editorial teams default to "write, publish, forget" due to volume -- this is working harder, not smarter
- Reuse content to extend its lifecycle
- Grow traffic baseline by maintaining and updating evergreen content
- Content gap analysis should feed cluster strategy continuously

### CTR Optimization (Ongoing)
- Google changes search result layouts constantly
- Continuously check whether search intent aligns with what people search for
- Optimize tactical content placement across promotional and informational content
- Look at banners and affiliate link placement tactically

### Internal/External Link Optimization
- Use Link Whisperer to auto-detect mentions for new content internal links
- Run quarterly sprints to review and optimize linking across old and new content
- External link optimization: digital PR, content assets for link earning

Publishers must optimize for multiple surfaces:
- Google Discover
- Top Stories
- Live Video
- news.google.com
- Web Stories
- Standard organic search results

All are distribution levers for content SEO efforts.

## Quarterly Campaign Sprints (Vahe Arabian)

- Segment content production into quarterly campaigns run as sprints
- Ensure promotional resources behind tactical content
- Complement with social, paid, outreach
- Measure and evaluate efforts quarterly, then iterate
- Especially important for new publishers trying to get off the ground

## Expansion Strategies (Vahe Arabian)

- Expand into new countries (international), languages (multilingual), local areas
- Progress from local to regional to national coverage
- Experiment with new surfaces: web stories, Google Discover, video, image SEO
- Even without hard ROI data, find ways to experiment -- stay ahead of competitors
