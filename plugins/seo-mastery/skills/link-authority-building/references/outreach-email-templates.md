[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
Website: [Your URL]
[LinkedIn or social profile link]

## Key Principles for All Outreach Emails

1. **Personalize**: Use their name; reference specific article
2. **Short**: Keep to 3-4 short paragraphs
3. **Lead with value**: "Why is this good for THEM?"
4. **Specific**: Don't say "great blog"; say "loved your article on X"
5. **Easy**: Include link; don't make them hunt
6. **Professional**: Correct spelling, proper formatting
7. **Contact info**: Name, title, website, email, phone
8. **Timing**: Send early in day (8am-12pm best open rate)
9. **Follow up**: 2 follow-ups after initial email
10. **Give up**: After 3 emails with no response, move to next target

---

## Subject Line Formulas (Highest Open Rates)

**Formula 1**: [Specific Value] for [Their Audience]
Example: "6 Link Building Strategies for B2B SaaS Companies"

**Formula 2**: Question That Interests Them
Example: "Are You Updating Your Link Building Guide for 2024?"

**Formula 3**: Specific Article Reference
Example: "Following Up on Your Article: Link Building Trends"

**Formula 4**: Collaboration/Partnership
Example: "Partnership Idea: Content Collaboration"

**Formula 5**: Problem/Solution
Example: "Guest Post: Fixing the #1 Link Building Mistake"

## Best Practices by Channel

### Gmail/Email
- Personalize subject line
- Keep opening paragraph short (hook within first sentence)
- Include clear CTA
- Response time: 24-48 hours typical

### LinkedIn InMail
- Shorter message (LinkedIn users scan)
- Profile link credibility
- Response time: Similar to email

### Twitter/X DM
- Very short intro
- Link to article
- Direct to email for details
- Response time: Faster (more casual)

---

## Don't Do This (Disqualifiers)

❌ Generic mass emails (obvious copy-paste)
❌ All caps or excessive exclamation marks!!!
❌ Spelling/grammar errors
❌ "I noticed you have a website about..."
❌ Asking for link directly in first email
❌ Boasting about yourself
❌ Not proofreading before sending
❌ Sending multiple emails same day
❌ Unclear call-to-action
❌ No signature/contact info

## Final Checklist

Before hitting send:
- [ ] Spelled their name correctly
- [ ] Referenced specific article/post
- [ ] Clear why this matters to THEM
- [ ] Link/resource included
- [ ] Professional tone
- [ ] Signature with contact info
- [ ] No spelling errors
- [ ] Not overly salesy
- [ ] Clear next step/CTA
