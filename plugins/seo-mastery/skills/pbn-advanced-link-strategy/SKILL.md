# PBN & Advanced Link Strategies

## Core Philosophy

PBNs and advanced link strategies occupy the gray-to-black hat spectrum of SEO. They are powerful, controllable, and carry inherent risk. The goal is to minimize detection footprints while maximizing link juice transfer. PBNs are "the closer" -- used to push from page 2 to page 1 after foundational links are set.

**Risk Management Principle:** Never use PBNs as your ONLY link type. Post-May 2020 update, diversified link profiles are mandatory. Balance PBN links 1:1 with other link types.

## PBN Fundamentals

Power lives in the DOMAIN, not the website. When a website goes down, 99% of inbound links remain because nobody monitors outbound links.

**PBN homepage links are dramatically more powerful than inner page links.** A DA 20 PBN homepage link can outperform a DA 80 inner page link (Morrison). Always ensure your money link appears on the PBN homepage.

**Success metrics (Floate):** Hit rate (deindex rate) with proper due diligence: ~1.7% per 100 PBNs built. PBNs built 5+ years ago are still undetected and operational.

**Most common way Google finds PBNs:** NOT footprints or domain registration -- it's reverse-engineering from the sites being ranked. Risk is ~10x higher when BUYING PBN links vs. building your own.

## When to Use PBNs

- **Never on brand new domains** (Morrison). First build foundation: social profiles, citations, guest posts, branded anchor links. Let those age 3-6 months. Then fire PBN links.
- On aged/auction domains used as money sites: can fire PBNs immediately (domain already has authority).
- For inner pages on authority domains: can fire PBN immediately, though padding with 1-2 branded links first is safer.
- PBNs are "the closer" -- push from page 2 to page 1 after foundations are set.

## Domain Acquisition

### INSTRUCTOR DISAGREEMENT: Auction vs. Expired

**Morrison (contrarian):** Never buy expired domains. Always buy auction/aged domains. Expired domains have had their WHOIS age reset; the best domains get picked up at auction. Expired = leftovers.

**Floate:** Uses both but acknowledges auction domains maintain their original registration date.

**Resolution:** Auction domains are superior for preserving WHOIS age. Expired domains are cheaper but carry age-reset risk.

### Where to Find Domains
**Crawlers:** ExpiredDomains.net (free), Register Compass ($37/month -- Morrison's top pick), SpamZilla ($37/month)
**Auctions:** GoDaddy Auctions, NameCheap Marketplace, NameSilo, NameJet, Flippa, SEDO
**Brokers:** Odys Global (premium), TB Solutions (large inventory), SEO.Domains

### Domain Pricing
- Typical range: $25-$100 per domain
- Bidding tactic: avoid round numbers ($101.13 beats $100). Wait until deadline approaches.
- Price tiers by DR/TF/DA for budget discipline

### Domain Analysis & Filtering (Morrison)
- TLD: .com, .net, .org only. Skip .info.
- Trust Flow: minimum 7-8. Domain Authority: minimum 11-13.
- Pro tip: filter for domains with at least 1 .gov or .edu link (indicates quality backlink profile)
- Domain name should logically match its category
- DA and PA should be within ~12-14 of each other

### Backlink Analysis (Ahrefs)
1. Check titles/URLs of referring domains for common theme/topic (no theme = skip)
2. Verify DR AND traffic alignment (DR 60-70 should have 1,000+ domain traffic)
3. Geographic relevance bonuses for local-relevant links
4. Moz Spam Score: limit to 30% max

## PBN Setup & Anti-Footprint

**Never use SEO hosting or "PBN hosting" services** (except LaunchCDN). They are easy targets for Google.

### Hosting Diversification
- Each domain: different hosting provider, different account, different identity, different email
- Use well-known cheap hosts: SiteGround, HawkHost, Hostinger, GreenGeeks, NameCheap Hosting
- Each domain needs unique IP address

### CDN Diversification
- Use Cloudflare (free) -- each domain needs its OWN Cloudflare account (separate email)
- Also use Amazon CloudFront for diversity
- 50-site split example: 10 Cloudflare, 10 CloudFront, 30 premium shared hosting
- LaunchCDN: recommended PBN-specific host ($49/month for 20 sites)

### Anti-Footprint Checklist (abbreviated)
- Multiple registrars, different contact info, different emails, spaced registration timing
- Different hosting companies, unique IPs, servers in different locations
- Different analytics accounts, different SEO plugins, different themes, different authors
- DO let crawlers scan your sites (blocking is MORE suspicious than allowing)
- Never interlink your PBNs to each other

## Parasite SEO

### Social Media Parasite System (26-Post Campaign Per Keyword)
Post across 8 mandatory platforms (X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, YouTube, Reddit, Medium) plus 3 optional (Pinterest, Blue Sky, Tumblr).

**Campaign structure:** 2 X posts, 2 Instagram posts, 2 Threads posts, 2 LinkedIn posts, 2 Facebook posts, 1 Reddit post, 1 Pinterest pin, 1 Blue Sky post, 1 Tumblr post, 1 YouTube post, 1 YouTube video, 1 X long-form article, 1 Medium article, plus short video posts across platforms.

### AI Parasite Pages
Use AI website builders (Manus AI, Lovable, Bolt.new) to create free landing pages. Primarily for backlink/citation building rather than direct ranking.

### Press Release Parasites
- **PR Log (prlog.com):** Free, 2 clickable links, indexes in 2-4 hours
- **OpenPR (openpr.com):** 1 free/30 days, up to 5 links, indexes in 20-25 minutes

### Medium as Parasite Property
Key discovery: Medium articles require 10+ real followers to index. With fewer followers, articles will not index regardless of indexing service.

### Fast Indexing Services
1. Rocket Indexer -- indexes in 30-60 seconds ($39/1,200 guarantees)
2. IndexMeNow -- high rate, only charges for indexed URLs
3. Ralphie Indexer -- backup/complementary

## 301 Redirect Strategy

- Find aged domains with clean links from relevant/related content in your niche
- Do 301 through .htaccess file, NOT registrar forwarding (preserves inner page juice)
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks to see impact
- Reversible: remove the 301 code and everything returns to normal within 1-2 weeks
- Danger: wrong niche 301 causes topical confusion

**Canonical tags as alternative:** Add 150-250 words of fresh content to a non-relevant page, wait 3-6 weeks for freshness update, then canonicalize to target page.
