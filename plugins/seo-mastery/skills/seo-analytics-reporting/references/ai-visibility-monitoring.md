## AI Visibility KPIs

### KPI 1: Citation Count
**Definition:** Number of times your content cited in AI responses
**Metric:** Citations per month across all AI models
**Tool:** Promptwatch, Profound, custom tracking
**Target:** Grow 15-20% monthly
**Example:** Month 1: 500 citations → Month 2: 575 citations (+15%)

### KPI 2: AI Search Rankings
**Definition:** Position of your content in AI-generated answers
**Metric:** # of keywords where you're in top 3 citations
**Tool:** Manual tracking (search your target keywords)
**Target:** Move from 10% → 30% of keywords in top 3
**Example:** 100 target keywords → 30 appear in top 3 AI citations

### KPI 3: Visibility Score
**Definition:** Weighted score based on platform + position
**Metric:** ChatGPT #1 = 10 points, Perplexity #2 = 6 points, etc.
**Tool:** Promptwatch dashboard
**Target:** Grow 20% monthly
**Example:** Month 1: 1,000 points → Month 2: 1,200 points (+20%)

### KPI 4: Citation Sentiment
**Definition:** How you're mentioned (positive, neutral, negative)
**Metric:** % of citations mentioning you positively
**Tool:** Profound AI analysis
**Target:** 80%+ positive
**Example:** 100 citations → 82 positive, 15 neutral, 3 negative

## Measurement Tools (2026 Landscape)

### Promptwatch (Primary)

**What it does:**
- Tracks 2.5M+ AI prompt responses
- Shows which queries cite your content
- Tracks position in response
- Reports on sentiment

**Setup:**
1. promptwatch.io → Create account
2. Add domain
3. Dashboard auto-populates with citation data

**Cost:** ~$500/month for enterprise

### Profound (Alternative)

**What it does:**
- Analyzes 250M+ AI responses
- Shows ranking across 8+ AI models
- Tracks LTV impact of AI visibility

**Setup:**
1. profound.ai → Connect domain
2. Authorize server access
3. Dashboard shows visibility trends

**Cost:** ~$500-1000/month

### Manual Tracking (Free)

**Workflow:**
1. Create list of 50 target keywords
2. Weekly: Search each keyword in ChatGPT + Perplexity
3. Record: Does AI cite you? What position? Positive mention?
4. Track in spreadsheet

**Effort:** 30 minutes/week

**Example spreadsheet:**
```
Keyword | ChatGPT Cited? | Position | Perplexity Cited? | Sentiment |
"best running shoes" | Yes | #2 | Yes | #1 | Positive |
"marathon training" | No | - | Yes | #3 | Neutral |
```

## Content Strategy for AI Visibility

### What AI Models Prefer

✅ **Ranked lists** ("Top 10 X", "Best Y")
✅ **Statistics & research** (AI loves data points)
✅ **Comparison tables** (structured data)
✅ **Original insights** (not aggregation)
✅ **Fresh content** (recent updates signal)
✅ **Unique methodology** (how you picked top 10)

❌ **Thin content** (AI will cite authority instead)
❌ **Outdated information** (from 2020, AI wants 2025+)
❌ **Unstructured content** (pure prose without headers)
❌ **Affiliate-heavy** (AI deprioritizes ad-driven content)

### Content Audit for AI Visibility

1. **Identify top 20 keywords** (where you want AI citations)
2. **Search in ChatGPT/Perplexity**
3. **Note:** Does your content appear? In what position?
4. **For missing keywords:** Create content with:
   - Clear ranking methodology
   - Statistics/research
   - Comparison table
   - Unique angle
5. **For weak positions:** Strengthen with:
   - Original research
   - Updated data (cite 2025/2026 sources)
   - More detailed comparisons

## Optimization Workflow

### Weekly (5 minutes)

1. Check Promptwatch dashboard
2. Note any new keywords appearing in AI responses
3. Identify top-cited pages (replicate format)

### Monthly (30 minutes)

1. Export citation report from Promptwatch/Profound
2. Segment: Which AI models cite you most?
3. Which keywords getting citations?
4. Which getting zero citations? (content gap)
5. Plan content to fill gaps

### Quarterly (1 hour)

1. Full AI visibility audit
2. Search 50 target keywords manually
3. Document: # cited, position, sentiment
4. Compare quarter-over-quarter (is visibility growing?)
5. Adjust content strategy based on patterns

## Integration with SEO Strategy

**2026 SEO must optimize for both:**

1. **Google traditional SERP** (still 60-70% of queries)
2. **AI Search** (now 30-40% of queries)

**Content approach:**
- Write for both simultaneously
- Structured data helps both (AI + traditional)
- Original research helps both
- Fresh updates help both
- Rankings and AI citations correlated (usually)

### Content Template for AI + SERP

```
H1: [Keyword] - Complete Guide 2026

Intro (200 words):
- What is [keyword]
- Why it matters
- How we chose/researched this

Ranking Methodology (300 words):
- Our evaluation criteria (makes AI notice)
- How we weighted factors
- Comparison methodology

[Ranked list or comparisons]

Research/Data (500+ words):
- Original statistics or survey
- Benchmark data
- Unique insights

Conclusion (200 words):
- Summary ranking
- Final recommendation
- CTA
```

**This format works for:**
- Google #1-3 ranking (strong content)
- AI top 3 citation (clear methodology + data)
