# Organic ROI Calculation: Proving SEO Value

**Length:** ~200 lines

## The Formula

```
Organic Revenue = Organic Sessions × Conversion Rate × Average Order Value
Organic Profit = Organic Revenue - SEO Budget
Organic ROAS = Organic Revenue ÷ SEO Budget (or Profit ÷ Budget for pure ROAS)
Payback Period = SEO Budget ÷ Monthly Organic Profit
```

## Real-World Example (SaaS Company)

### Scenario

**Monthly metrics:**
- Organic sessions: 10,000
- Conversion rate: 2% (visitors → free trial)
- Average customer value: $5,000/year ($417/month MRR)
- SEO budget: $15,000/month (2.5 FTE + tools)

### Calculation

```
Monthly Organic-Attributed Revenue:
10,000 sessions × 2% CR × $417 monthly customer value = $83,400

Monthly Organic Profit:
$83,400 - $15,000 = $68,400

Annual Organic Revenue:
$83,400 × 12 = $1,000,800

Annual Organic Profit:
$68,400 × 12 = $820,800

Organic ROAS:
$83,400 ÷ $15,000 = 5.56x (or 556% ROI)

Payback Period:
$15,000 ÷ $68,400 = 0.22 months (= 1 week)

Break-even Analysis:
- SEO investment breaks even in 1 week
- Every additional week of operation = $68,400 profit
- 52 weeks/year = $820,800 annual profit from organic
```

## Local Service Example (Plumbing)

### Scenario

```

Monthly Profit:
$32,000 - $5,000 = $27,000

ROAS:
$32,000 ÷ $5,000 = 6.4x

Payback Period:
$5,000 ÷ $27,000 = 0.19 months (= 6 days)

Annual Profit:
$27,000 × 12 = $324,000
```

## Handling Variable Conversion Rates

**Problem:** CR varies by traffic quality, season, page type.

**Solution:** Segment and calculate separately:

```
Blog readers: 20K sessions × 1% CR × $150 = $30K
Product pages: 15K sessions × 3% CR × $200 = $9K
Service pages: 10K sessions × 5% CR × $300 = $15K

Total: $54K revenue
```

## Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Don't:** Include brand awareness value in ROI (speculative)
✅ **Do:** Only count measurable revenue from organic

❌ **Don't:** Use last-click attribution (undervalues organic)
✅ **Do:** Use linear or position-based attribution

❌ **Don't:** Ignore CAC (customer acquisition cost)
✅ **Do:** Compare organic CAC to other channels

❌ **Don't:** Forecast growth beyond 2-3 years (unreliable)
✅ **Do:** Update quarterly with actual data
