# SEO KPI Definitions & Calculation Framework

**Length:** ~200 lines

## Executive KPIs (What Your Boss Cares About)

### Organic Revenue
**Definition:** Total revenue attributed to organic search traffic
**Calculation:** Organic sessions × Conversion rate × Average order value
**Target:** Grows 20-30% annually
**Example:** 10,000 sessions × 3% conversion × $150 AOV = $45,000/month

### Organic ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
**Definition:** Revenue generated per dollar spent on SEO
**Calculation:** Organic revenue / SEO budget
**Target:** 5x or higher (500% ROI)
**Example:** $45,000 revenue ÷ $10,000 SEO budget = 4.5x ROAS

### Organic Cost Per Conversion
**Definition:** How much SEO spend generates one conversion
**Calculation:** Total SEO budget / Total organic conversions
**Target:** Decreasing month-over-month (more efficient over time)
**Example:** $10,000 SEO budget ÷ 300 conversions = $33 CPC

### Organic Profit Margin
**Definition:** Net profit from organic after costs
**Calculation:** (Organic revenue - Customer acquisition cost) / Organic revenue
**Target:** 40-60% (varies by industry)
**Example:** ($45,000 - $15,000) / $45,000 = 67% profit margin

## Strategic KPIs (What Your Marketing Manager Cares About)

### Organic Traffic Growth
**Definition:** Month-over-month organic session growth rate
**Calculation:** (This month - Last month) / Last month × 100
**Target:** 10-15% monthly growth for scaling sites
**Example:** (45,000 - 41,000) / 41,000 = 9.8% growth

### Organic Conversion Rate
**Definition:** % of organic sessions that result in conversion
**Calculation:** Organic conversions / Organic sessions × 100
**Target:** 2-5% (varies by industry; SaaS higher, ecommerce lower)
**Example:** 1,350 conversions / 45,000 sessions = 3% CR

### Top 10 Rankings
**Definition:** Number of keywords in positions 1-10 SERP
**Calculation:** Manual count or rank tracking tool
**Target:** Grow by 5-10 new top-10 rankings monthly
**Example:** Month 1: 150 top-10 keywords → Month 2: 165 (+15)

### Average Ranking Position
**Definition:** Mean SERP position across tracked keywords
**Calculation:** Sum of all positions / Number of keywords
**Target:** Improve from position 15 → position 8 over 6 months
**Example:** 500 keywords averaging position 12.5

## Tactical KPIs (What Your SEO Team Tracks)

### Organic Search Impressions
**Definition:** Number of times your pages appeared in search results
**Calculation:** GSC → Performance → Impressions
**Target:** Grow 15-20% monthly (signal of expansion)
**Example:** 500,000 monthly impressions

### Click-Through Rate from Search
**Definition:** % of search impressions that result in click
**Calculation:** Clicks / Impressions × 100
**Target:** 2-3% (improve via title/description optimization)
**Example:** 10,000 clicks / 500,000 impressions = 2% CTR

### Pages Indexed
**Definition:** % of your site's pages in Google's index
**Calculation:** Indexed pages / Published pages × 100
**Target:** 90%+ indexation rate
**Example:** 900 indexed pages / 1,000 published = 90%

### Organic Traffic by Page Type
**Definition:** Distribution of traffic across content types
**Calculation:** Sessions by page type (GA4 segment)
**Target:** Varies by business (track % change month-over-month)
**Example:** Blog: 40% | Product: 35% | Service: 25%

### Backlink Growth
**Definition:** New referring domains per month
**Calculation:** Ahrefs/SEMrush → Backlinks → New domains
**Target:** 5-10 new referring domains monthly
**Example:** Added 8 new domain links this month

## Industry-Specific KPIs

### For Ecommerce Sites

**Product Page Engagement:**
- Avg session duration on product pages (target: >90 seconds)
- % of sessions with "view_item" event (target: >30%)
- AOV for organic customers (target: equal to paid)

### For SaaS Companies

**Demo/Trial Signups:**
- Organic sessions → Demo requests (target: 5-10%)
- % of demos from organic (target: 30-40% of total demos)
- Free trial to paid conversion (target: 20-30%)

### For Local Service Businesses

**Call Submissions:**
- Organic sessions → Calls made (target: 5-15%)
- Cost per call (target: <$30)
- Call-to-booking rate (target: 25-40%)

### For Content/Publisher Sites

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Pages per session (target: >2 pages)
- Avg session duration (target: >3 minutes)
- Scroll depth (target: 50%+ of page)
- Newsletter signups from organic (target: 2-5% of sessions)

## Reporting Cadence

### Daily Monitoring
- Real-time organic traffic
- Key conversion events (are they flowing?)
- Page speed/Core Web Vitals issues

### Weekly Reporting
- Organic traffic trend
- Top-performing pages
- Ranking changes (+/- movements)
- Traffic by keyword

### Monthly Reporting
- Revenue attribution
- ROAS calculation
- Growth rate analysis
- Problem areas to fix

### Quarterly Strategy Review
- Year-over-year growth comparison
- Attribution model analysis
- Goal progress vs. targets
- Budget allocation adjustments

## When KPIs Are Going Wrong

| Metric | Issue | Likely Cause | Fix |
|--------|-------|--------------|-----|
| Traffic down 20% | Major drop | Algorithm update, indexation issue, competitor | Check GSC coverage, monitoring rankings |
| Conversions flat | Despite traffic ↑ | Lower quality traffic, page issues | Review traffic quality, improve conversion funnel |
| CPA increasing | Cost efficiency down | Less relevant traffic, conversion rate drop | Check keyword quality, improve pages |
| CTR declining | Lower SERP visibility | Title/desc needs refresh, losing positions | A/B test new titles, improve snippets |
| Ranking dropping | Loss of authority | Backlink losses, technical issues, outdated content | Build links, audit technical, update content |
