# /build-brand

## Skills Activated
- `brand-foundation`

## Workflow

### Step 1: Niche Selection

Ask the user about their expertise, interests, and goals. Then guide them through the 6 ways to niche down:

1. By Sub-Industry
2. By Demographic
3. By Experience Level
4. By Company Size/Revenue
5. By Problem/Outcome
6. By Location/Market

Use the Niche Formula (Skills + Profit - Passion) if they are undecided. Help them validate demand on Udemy/Skillshare/Amazon.

Present the Professional Constellation model to show that niching down EXPANDS opportunities, not limits them.

### Step 2: Content Pillars

Help define 3-5 content pillars that:
- Are broad enough to explore for months
- Are focused enough for instant relevance
- Together paint a complete picture of brand value
- Represent core competencies, not surface-level topics

### Step 3: Ideal Viewer Avatar

Guide the user through avatar construction:
- Demographics (age, gender, income, location, profession)
- Psychographics (values, beliefs, pain points, desires)
- Transformation statement: "I want to go from X to Y so that I can Z"
- Name the avatar

If the user prefers the simpler approach: use the Younger-Self Method (avatar = yourself 1-2 years ago).

### Step 4: Brand DNA

Guide through:
- Brand Positioning Formula: Audience needs + What makes you unique - What competitors do = Your positioning
- Big Promise: "I help [WHO] go from [PAIN] to [OUTCOME] through [METHOD] without [OBJECTION]"
- 3A Strategy (Ask, Analyze, Amplify)
- Brand Pyramid (Core > Personality > Visuals)
- Brand Spectrum positioning (formal/casual, serious/humorous, etc.)

### Step 5: Mission Statement

Help craft a mission statement that passes the Fluff Test. If you can add "and make the world a better place" and it still sounds coherent, it's too vague.

### Step 6: Visual Identity

Guide through Rule of Twos (beginners) or Rule of Threes (intermediate):
- Colors (1 primary + 1-2 accent)
- Fonts (1 header + 1 body)
- Imagery style

Suggest creating a Brand Mothership Document as a single source of truth.

### Output

Deliver a complete brand foundation summary with:
- Niche statement
- 3-5 content pillars
- Avatar profile with transformation statement
- Big Promise statement
- Brand positioning formula
- Visual identity guidelines
- Recommended next step: run `/content-plan`
