# /build-brand -- Interactive Brand Foundation Builder

## INTAKE QUESTIONS (Ask All Before Building)

### Question 1: Current State
**Where are you in your creator journey?**

| Choice | Context |
|--------|---------|
| A) Starting from scratch (no content yet) | Full brand-foundation process, start with niche selection |
| B) Creating content but no clear brand | Focus on positioning, pillars, and avatar refinement |
| C) Established creator wanting to rebrand | Audit existing brand, identify gaps, rebuild DNA |
| D) Business owner adding personal brand | Align personal brand with existing business positioning |

### Question 2: Platform Focus
**Which platform(s) are you focusing on?**
- A) Instagram / TikTok (visual, short-form)
- B) LinkedIn (text, professional)
- C) YouTube (long-form, evergreen)
- D) Multi-platform
- E) Not sure yet

### Question 3: Business Model
**How do you plan to monetize (or already monetize)?**
- A) Coaching / consulting / services
- B) Digital products (courses, templates, ebooks)
- C) Brand partnerships / sponsorships
- D) Community / membership
- E) Not sure yet -- just want to grow first

### Question 4: Expertise & Experience
**Tell me about your background:**
- What industry/field are you in?
- What unique experiences or skills do you have?
- What problems can you solve better than most people?
- What have people paid you for (or asked you to help with)?

### Question 5: Creator-CEO Spectrum
**Which resonates more with you?**
- A) I want to be deeply involved in creation (writing, filming, editing)
- B) I want to build systems and delegate production
- C) Somewhere in between

## GENERATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Niche Selection
1. Load `brand-foundation` SKILL.md
2. Load `ref-brand-positioning.md`
3. Apply the platform-appropriate niche strategy:
   - LinkedIn -> narrow/relevance-first (E02 approach)
   - Instagram/TikTok -> can go broader (E04a approach)
   - YouTube -> narrow on topic, broad on audience (E03a approach)
4. Run the Information Advantage discovery if user is unsure
5. Apply the 6 Ways to Niche Down
6. Draft positioning formula: "I help [audience] solve [problem] so they can [result]"

### Step 2: Content Pillars
1. Define 3-5 pillars based on niche and expertise
2. Test each pillar: broad enough for months of content? Focused enough to understand instantly? Removing it weakens brand?

### Step 3: Ideal Viewer Avatar
1. Guide through IVP Deep-Dive Exercise (5 questions, 5 answers each)
2. Build 3 audience segments with Identity-Emotion-Action
3. Document the target avatar profile

### Step 4: Mission Statement
1. Draft using formula: "My mission is to [VALUE as verb] [AUDIENCE IDENTITY]"
2. Apply fluff test (unique? singular focus? applies to all segments?)

### Step 5: Brand DNA
1. Define macro branding elements (positioning, content type, associations)
2. Define micro branding elements (colors, fonts, visual style)
3. Document brand guidelines

### Step 6: Visual Identity
1. Select 3 brand colors
2. Select font pair
3. Define photography/video style
4. Define mobile-first design principles

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Deliver a complete Brand Foundation Document containing:

1. **Positioning Statement**: "I help [audience] solve [problem] so they can [result]"
2. **Niche Definition**: Industry + outcome + platform strategy
3. **Content Pillars**: 3-5 pillars with descriptions
4. **Ideal Viewer Avatar**: IVP profile with key fears, dreams, beliefs
5. **Audience Segments**: 3 segments with Identity-Emotion-Action
6. **Mission Statement**: One sentence, fluff-tested
7. **Brand DNA**: Macro + micro elements documented
8. **Visual Identity**: Colors, fonts, style guidelines
9. **Creator-CEO Position**: Where they sit on the spectrum
10. **Competitive Advantage**: 3-5 bullets on differentiation
11. **Next Step**: "Proceed to /content-plan to build your content calendar"
