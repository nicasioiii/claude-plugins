# /create-lead-magnet -- Lead Magnet Designer

## INTAKE QUESTIONS (Ask All Before Designing)

### Question 1: Monetization Context
**What is the paid offer this lead magnet will lead into?**

| Choice | Lead Magnet Strategy |
|--------|---------------------|
| A) Coaching or consulting ($1K+) | Lead magnet as trust-builder, qualify leads for calls |
| B) Digital course or product ($50-$500) | Lead magnet as sample of course value, direct upsell |
| C) Community or membership ($29-$69/month) | Lead magnet as taste of community value, low-friction join |
| D) No paid offer yet -- just building email list | Lead magnet as audience-builder, optimize for downloads |
| E) Not sure yet | Design lead magnet that works for multiple future paths |

### Question 2: Audience & Problem
**Who is this for and what problem does it solve?**
- Describe your target audience in one sentence
- What is the BIG problem your paid offer solves?
- What is a MICRO-problem (a smaller, earlier problem) you can solve for free?
- What does your audience currently search for or ask about most?

### Question 3: Expertise Proof
**What makes you credible on this topic?**
- A) Personal results (you did it yourself)
- B) Client results (you helped others do it)
- C) Professional experience (you worked in this field)
- D) Research and synthesis (you have studied this deeply)
- E) Multiple of the above

### Question 4: Format Preference
**What format works best for your audience?**
- A) PDF guide or ebook (1-10 pages)
- B) Checklist or cheat sheet (1-2 pages)
- C) Template or spreadsheet (fill-in-the-blank tool)
- D) Mini video training (15-30 minute recording)
- E) Free audit or assessment (personalized result)
- F) Not sure -- help me choose

### Question 5: Distribution Platform
**Where will you promote this lead magnet?**
- A) Instagram (bio link, story CTA, reel CTA)
- B) LinkedIn (featured section, post CTA, autoplug)
- C) YouTube (description link, end screen, pinned comment)
- D) Multi-platform
- E) Email list only (growing existing list)

## GENERATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Micro-Problem Selection
1. Load `monetize-content` SKILL.md
2. Load `ref-lead-magnets.md`
3. Apply the Micro-Problem Strategy:
   - List the big problem your paid offer solves
   - Break it into 10-15 micro-problems
   - Identify the "early narrow" problem: the one that, when solved, unlocks the rest of the journey
4. Select the micro-problem with highest urgency and lowest complexity

### Step 2: Solution Type Selection
1. Choose from the 3 solution approaches:
   - **Reveal**: Make them aware of a problem they did not know existed (diagnosis)
   - **Sample**: Free trial or taste with limited access
   - **Step**: Free step 1 of X -- give away first step, charge for the rest
2. Match solution type to their paid offer:
   - Coaching -> Reveal (free audit/assessment) or Step (first step framework)
   - Course -> Sample (first module free) or Step (one technique from the course)
   - Community -> Sample (free event or challenge) or Reveal (diagnostic quiz)

### Step 3: Delivery Mechanism Selection
1. Choose from the 4 delivery mechanisms:
   - **Software**: Spreadsheet, dashboard, tool
   - **Information**: Course, lesson, presentation, tips
   - **Service**: Free audit, free first session, done-for-you sample
   - **Product**: Assessment chart, short ebook, supplement sample
2. Match to their format preference and audience expectations

### Step 4: Naming
1. Apply the Naming Formula:
   - "The [Target Audience] [Desired Outcome] [Without Pain] [Even If Obstacle]"
   - Structure: [NUMBER] [WHAT] [HOW] for [WHO] to [OUTCOME] [TIME FRAME]
2. Generate 5 title options
3. Numbers 3-7 work best in titles
4. Test each name: Is the value immediately obvious? Does it create urgency?

### Step 5: 13-Component Structure
1. Build the complete lead magnet outline using all 13 components:
   - Title and Subtitle
   - Authority-Building Hook (select from 5 types)
   - Clear Value Promise
   - Quick Implementation Guide
   - Core Framework/System (give it a memorable name, 3-7 components)
   - Step-by-Step Action Plan
   - Strategic Connection to Main Offer
   - Non-Pushy Next Steps
   - Credibility Bio Section
   - Social Proof Elements
   - Strategic CTAs throughout
   - Resource Links and Contact Info
   - Natural Progression Guide
2. Write detailed outline content for each component

### Step 6: Distribution Plan
1. Design the opt-in and delivery flow:
   - Landing page structure: headline, subhead, 3-5 bullet outcomes, form, thank-you page
   - Email delivery: immediate delivery email + 3-email nurture sequence
2. Plan promotion touchpoints:
   - Bio link placement
   - Featured section (first card on LinkedIn)
   - Pinned post with CTA
   - Mention in 1-2 relevant posts per month
   - Comment keyword trigger strategy

### Step 7: Design Direction
1. Format specifications: 2-5 page PDF recommended
2. Design principles: clean, legible, large headings, short bullets, one font pair, brand colors
3. Tool recommendations: Canva, Notion (export to PDF), Google Slides

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Deliver a complete Lead Magnet Blueprint containing:

1. **Micro-Problem Identified**: The specific problem this solves and why it was chosen
2. **Solution Type**: Reveal, Sample, or Step -- and why
3. **Delivery Mechanism**: Software, Information, Service, or Product
4. **Title Options**: 5 name variations with recommended pick
5. **13-Component Outline**: Full outline with detailed content for each component
6. **Core Framework**: Named framework with 3-7 steps, ready to flesh out
7. **Landing Page Copy**: Headline, subhead, 5 bullet outcomes, CTA button text
8. **Email Sequence**: 3-email nurture sequence outlines (subject lines + key points)
9. **Distribution Checklist**: Where to place opt-in links, how often to promote
10. **Design Brief**: Page count, layout recommendations, tool suggestions
11. **Connection to Paid Offer**: How the lead magnet naturally leads to the upsell
12. **Next Steps**: "Create the lead magnet content" and "Use /write-post to promote it"
