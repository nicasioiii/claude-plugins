# /edit-video -- Guided Video Editing Workflow

| Choice | Editing Approach |
|--------|-----------------|
| A) Instagram Reel (< 90 sec) | Fast pacing, retention focus, 9:16, 30fps, captions mandatory |
| B) TikTok video (< 60 sec) | Fast pacing, consider native editor, 9:16, 30fps |
| C) YouTube Short (< 60 sec) | Fast pacing, 9:16, 30fps, caption-friendly |

### Question 2: Editing Style Preference

## OUTPUT FORMAT

After collecting answers, deliver a customized editing guide:

### Section 1: Editing Workflow (Step by Step)

Adapt the 7-step process to their specific situation:

**Step 1: Organize**
- File naming convention for their footage
- Folder structure recommendation
- Which takes to keep vs. discard

**Step 2: Build Timeline**
- Import order based on their content structure
- Rough arrangement: Hook > Body > Conclusion
- Identify gaps that need B-roll or graphics

**Step 3: Rough Cut**
- Dead space removal (0.2-0.3 sec gaps between sentences)
- Remove stumbles, repeated takes
- Target duration for their platform

**Step 4: Pacing & Flow**
- Apply 2-second rule if retention-based style
- Or thoughtful pacing guidance if story-based style
- Identify re-hook points if video body runs long

**Step 5: Enhancement**
- Caption/subtitle addition (platform-specific rules)
- Music selection and volume mixing (music at ~15-25% of voice level)
- Sound effects placement (if retention style)
- B-roll insertion points
- Text overlay and graphics

**Step 6: Polish**
- Color grading guidance (basic: white balance, exposure, contrast, saturation)
- Audio cleanup (noise removal, volume normalization)
- Transition check (most cuts should be hard cuts; transitions for emphasis only)
- Brand elements (watermark, handle, end card)

**Step 7: Quality Check**
- [ ] Mute test: video communicates without audio?
- [ ] UI overlay test: nothing blocked by platform buttons?
- [ ] Phone test: looks good on phone screen, not just desktop?
- [ ] Caption accuracy: all auto-captions proofread?
- [ ] Hook strength: first 1-3 seconds grab attention?
- [ ] Ending: cuts immediately after final value (no lingering)?

## CONDITIONAL GUIDANCE

### If Beginner (Experience Level A or B)
- Provide more detailed step-by-step instructions
- Recommend starting with CapCut or iMovie
- Focus on the basics: clean cuts, captions, simple music
- Reassure that authentic style is valid and growing in popularity
- Skip advanced VFX and transitions

### If Retention-Based Style Selected
- Emphasize the 2-second rule with specific examples
- Provide sound effect placement guidance
- Zoom/reframe technique for single-camera footage
- Text pop-up timing aligned with key words

### If Multi-Platform Edit
- Recommend creating a master edit at highest quality
- Then reformat: 16:9 > 9:16 (reframe), adjust captions, trim length for each platform
- Export settings table for each platform

### If They Have No B-Roll
- Recommend B-roll alternatives: stock footage, screen recordings, text-on-screen, zoomed crops of A-roll
- Suggest a B-roll capture session for future videos
- Reference film-video skill for B-roll planning

## RULES FOR THIS COMMAND

- Always ask about their editing software before giving step-by-step instructions
- Tailor complexity to their experience level
- Provide exact export settings, not vague guidance
- If they describe a specific editing problem, diagnose it before giving the full workflow
- Emphasize the mute test and UI overlay test as non-negotiable quality checks
- Never assume they have B-roll -- ask and adapt
- For short-form: captions are mandatory, not optional
- Recommend the combined editing style when they are unsure
