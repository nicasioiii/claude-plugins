# /grow-audience -- Platform-Specific Growth Plan Generator

## INTAKE QUESTIONS (Ask All Before Building Plan)

### Question 1: Platform Selection
**Which platform are you building on?**

| Choice | Skill Activated |
|--------|----------------|
| A) Instagram | `instagram-growth` |
| B) LinkedIn | `linkedin-growth` |
| C) TikTok | `tiktok-growth` |
| D) Twitter/X | `twitter-growth` |
| E) YouTube | `youtube-growth` |
| F) Multiple (specify) | Primary platform skill + cross-reference |

### Question 2: Current Status
**Where are you right now?**
- How many followers/subscribers do you have?
- How long have you been posting?
- How often are you currently posting?
- What types of content are you creating?

### Question 3: Growth Goals
**What's your goal?**
- A) First 1,000 followers (starter phase)
- B) 1K to 10K followers (momentum phase)
- C) 10K to 100K followers (scaling phase)
- D) 100K+ (authority/celebrity phase)
- E) Specific number or timeline

### Question 4: Time & Resources
**How much time can you dedicate weekly to content creation?**
- A) 2-5 hours/week (side project)
- B) 5-15 hours/week (serious side project)
- C) 15-30 hours/week (part-time creator)
- D) 30+ hours/week (full-time creator)

### Question 5: Content Type
**What type of content do you create or want to create?**
- A) Educational / How-to
- B) Entertainment / Humor
- C) Personal brand / Thought leadership
- D) Business / Company account
- E) Mix (specify)

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

After gathering intake answers, produce a growth plan with:

### Section 1: Platform Algorithm Strategy
- How the algorithm works for their specific platform
- Key metrics to optimize for
- Content types that perform best on this platform
- What the algorithm rewards vs. penalizes

### Section 2: Content Strategy
- Recommended content mix (by percentage)
- Content types to focus on for their growth stage
- Posting frequency recommendation
- Batch creation system

### Section 3: Engagement Strategy
- Platform-specific engagement tactics
- Comment strategy
- Community building tactics
- Collaboration/networking opportunities

### Section 4: Profile Optimization
- Bio/headline recommendations
- Profile picture and visual branding
- Pinned/featured content strategy
- Link and CTA placement

### Section 5: 30/60/90-Day Milestone Plan

| Timeframe | Actions | Target Metrics |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Days 1-30 | Foundation phase | Posting consistency, baseline metrics |
| Days 31-60 | Optimization phase | Engagement improvement, content refinement |
| Days 61-90 | Scale phase | Growth acceleration, audience building |

### Section 6: Key Metrics to Track
- Platform-specific benchmarks from the skill file
- Weekly tracking cadence
- When to pivot vs. when to persist
