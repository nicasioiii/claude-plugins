# Partnerships & Pricing Quick Reference

## Pitch Deck Page-by-Page Template

### Page 1: Mission Statement
**Template:** "My mission is to [value verb] [audience identity]"

**Fluff Test Checklist:**
- [ ] Is it unique to you? (Could another creator say the exact same thing?)
- [ ] Is there a singular focus? (Not trying to do 5 things)
- [ ] Does it apply to all audience segments? (Universal enough)
- [ ] Have you shared it out loud? (Sounds natural, not corporate)

**Examples:**
- "To educate and empower the next generation of creators"
- "To help everyday people build wealth through smart real estate investing"
- "To make healthy cooking accessible for busy professionals"

### Page 2: Creator Overview
**Write 3-5 bullets answering:**
- What makes you different from other creators in your niche?
- What is your unique qualification or background?
- What have you accomplished that proves credibility?
- What do brands get by working with you specifically?

### Page 3: Three Audience Segments
**Template per segment:**

| Segment | Name | Description | Primary Platform |
|---------|------|------------|-----------------|
| 1 | [Label] | [Who they are, what they want] | [Where they engage] |
| 2 | [Label] | [Who they are, what they want] | [Where they engage] |
| 3 | [Label] | [Who they are, what they want] | [Where they engage] |

**Rule:** Hyper-specific + hyper-engaged > large + general. Specific audience = irreplaceable. General audience = replaceable by anyone bigger.

### Page 4: Social Proof
**Organize by segment:**

| Segment | Proof Type | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Aspiring [niche] | DM screenshot | "Your video changed my approach to..." |
| Professional [niche] | Industry endorsement | Notable figure tweet/comment |
| Industry | Press mention | Featured in [publication] |

### Pages 5-6: The Menu
**Integration Template:**

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|

| Monthly growth rate | [%] month-over-month |

| Publishing frequency | [X]/week or [X]/month |

| Past sponsors | [Brand logos or names] |

### Page 7: Pricing & Packaging
**Package Template:**

| Package | Contents | Price |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Single integration | 1 x 60-90 sec integration + description link | $X |
| Starter (3-pack) | 3 integrations over 3 months | $X (10% savings) |

| Annual partner | 12 integrations + dedicated content | Custom pricing |

## Floor Price Calculator Worksheet

### Step 1: Monthly Expenses
| Category | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Rent/mortgage | $____ |
| Production costs (software, hosting) | $____ |
| Freelancers (editor, designer) | $____ |
| Equipment amortized | $____ |
| Living expenses (food, transport, health) | $____ |
| Insurance | $____ |
| Miscellaneous | $____ |
| **Total monthly expenses** | **$____** |

### Step 2: Add Margin
- Total expenses x 1.4 = Minimum monthly revenue
- Example: $10,000 x 1.4 = **$14,000 minimum**

### Step 3: Determine Inventory
- Available brand deal slots per month: ____
- Floor price = Minimum revenue / Slots
- Example: $14,000 / 4 = **$3,500 floor**

### Step 4: Apply Fill Rate
- Annual slots: ____ x 12 = ____
- Realistic fill rate: 50%
- Actually sold: ____ x 0.5 = ____

### Step 5: Factor Commission
- Agent commission (10%): $____
- Lawyer commission (5%): $____
- Net per deal: Floor price x 0.85 = $____

### Go-to-Market Price
- Floor price is your MINIMUM (never go below)
- List price: 1.5-2x floor price
- Example: $3,500 floor --> List at $5,000-$6,500
- Leave negotiation room between list and floor

## Inbound Response Template

**For specific, personalized inbound inquiries:**

Subject: Re: Partnership Opportunity with [Your Name]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out! I'd love to explore a partnership with [Brand].

I've attached our pitch deck which covers our audience, content formats, and partnership options. A few quick highlights:

- [X] subscribers/followers across [platforms]
- [Key audience segment] makes up [%] of our audience
- We've partnered with [notable brand names] in the past

I'd love to hop on a quick call to discuss how we can create something great for [Brand]. Are you available [suggest 2-3 times]?

Best,
[Your Name]

**For generic, mass-blast inquiries ("Hey influencer"):**
Archive. Do not respond. These are rarely legitimate opportunities.

## Negotiation Leverage Points

| Lever | How to Use | Impact |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| **Deliverables** | Add more content pieces for higher total price | Increases deal size |
| **Usage rights** | Organic-only is standard. Paid media boost = premium charge | 25-100% price increase |
| **Exclusivity** | Competing brands excluded for time period | 20-50% premium |
| **Name/image/likeness** | Brand uses your face in their ads | Significant premium |
| **Term/duration** | Longer deals = lower per-unit but higher total | Predictable revenue |
| **Whitelisting** | Brand runs ads from your account | 25-50% premium |

### Pricing Philosophy Options
- **Option A:** Show price in pitch deck (saves time, easy yes/no)
- **Option B:** Show menu without price, get on a call (more time but potentially higher deals)
- Early career: Option A (efficiency). Established: Option B (maximize value)

## Influencer Marketing Pricing (Nathan Chan)

### Pricing by Platform and Tier

| Platform | Nano (1-10K) | Micro (10-50K) | Mid (50-250K) | Macro (250K-1M) |
|----------|-------------|----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Instagram Post | $50-$250 | $250-$1,000 | $1,000-$5,000 | $5,000-$25,000 |
| Instagram Reel | $100-$500 | $500-$2,000 | $2,000-$10,000 | $10,000-$50,000 |
| YouTube Integration | $200-$1,000 | $1,000-$5,000 | $5,000-$20,000 | $20,000-$100,000 |
| TikTok | $50-$300 | $300-$1,500 | $1,500-$8,000 | $8,000-$40,000 |

### Engagement Rate Pricing Formula
- Calculate engagement rate: (Likes + Comments) / Followers x 100
- Higher engagement rate = higher per-follower value
- Industry benchmark: 1-3% engagement = standard; 3-6% = premium pricing; 6%+ = ultra premium

### UGC Pricing for Smaller Creators

|-------------|----------|-------------|

| UGC + usage rights | $300-$600 | $1,000-$2,000 |
| UGC + whitelisting | $500-$1,000 | $1,500-$3,000 |

## Brand Partnership Red Flags

| Red Flag | Why | Action |
|----------|-----|--------|
| "We'll pay you in exposure" | Not a business arrangement | Decline |
| Brand misalignment with audience | Damages trust with followers | Decline |
| Requests to hide sponsorship disclosure | Illegal (FTC/ASA requirements) | Decline |
| Excessive creative control | Defeats purpose of creator partnership | Negotiate or decline |
| No written agreement | No protection for either party | Request contract |
| Payment after 90+ days | Cash flow risk | Negotiate 50% upfront |
| Asking below floor price | Unsustainable | Counter at floor minimum |
