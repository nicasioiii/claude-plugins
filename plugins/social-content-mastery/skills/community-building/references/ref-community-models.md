## DM Sales Scripts

### Script 1: Comment-to-DM (After Lead Magnet Delivery)
```
Day 0 (auto): "Hey [Name]! Here's the [lead magnet] you requested: [link]. Let me know if you have any questions!"

Day 1 (auto): "Did you get a chance to check out the [lead magnet]? The section on [specific tip] is what most people find most useful."

Day 3 (manual): "Hey [Name], curious -- what's your biggest challenge with [topic] right now?"

[Wait for response, then personalize]

Day 5+ (manual): "Based on what you shared, I think you'd get a lot from [offer]. Want me to share the details?"
```

### Script 2: Warm DM for Networking
```
"Hey [Name]! Your post about [specific thing] really resonated with me. I've been working on something similar with [your experience]. Would love to connect and swap notes."
```

### Script 3: High-Ticket DM Close
```
After qualifying conversation:
"From everything you've shared, it sounds like you're dealing with [specific problem] and want [specific outcome]. I work with [type of client] on exactly this through my [program name]. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to see if it's a fit?"
```

### DM Rules
- Never send unsolicited pitch DMs (destroys trust and brand)
- Only DM people who have engaged with your content
- Start every DM conversation with genuine interest, never with an offer
- Ask questions before presenting solutions
- Maximum 2 follow-ups before moving on

## Building in Public Checklist

### Phase 1: Problem Agitation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Post 5 pieces of content highlighting the problem your product solves
- [ ] Use numbers: "Where they are vs. where they could be"
- [ ] Share personal stories of experiencing this problem
- [ ] Ask audience if they relate (engagement validates demand)

### Phase 2: Proof Building (Week 2-3)
- [ ] Share proof that your method works (screenshots, results, numbers)
- [ ] Show the unique mechanism behind your approach
- [ ] "Leak" early version to 3-5 people for testimonials
- [ ] Post before/after transformations from early access users

### Phase 3: Offer Construction (Week 3-4)
- [ ] Promise something bold about your product
- [ ] Show what qualifies you (unique selling point)
- [ ] Present irresistible offer (add value, do NOT discount)
- [ ] Share massive thread/carousel on what's included
- [ ] Open waitlist with early-bird pricing

### Phase 4: Launch (Week 4-5)
- [ ] Prime email list 3 days and 1 day before
- [ ] Launch day: announce price + deadline for early-bird
- [ ] Day +1: share testimonial screenshots
- [ ] Day +3: remind of price increase deadline
- [ ] Day +5: final close, doors closing

## Community Engagement Systems

### Daily Engagement Loop
| Time | Action | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Morning | Post value content (tip, question, resource) | Start conversations |
| Midday | Reply to all comments from morning | Show presence |
| Afternoon | Welcome new members personally | Build connection |
| Evening | Share relevant resource or member spotlight | Add value |

### Weekly Engagement Rituals
| Day | Ritual | Format |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Monday | "What are you working on this week?" | Discussion post |
| Tuesday | Teaching/tutorial post | Educational content |
| Wednesday | Member spotlight | Celebration post |
| Thursday | AMA or live Q&A | Interactive event |
| Friday | "Win of the week" sharing | Celebration post |
| Weekend | Light/fun/personal content | Humanizing content |

### Monthly Engagement Events
- Monthly challenge (30-day content challenge, accountability challenge)
- Guest expert session (invite someone your community admires)
- Community survey (what do they want more/less of?)
- Member-of-the-month recognition
- Annual: community meetup or virtual summit

## Community Retention Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Monthly churn rate | Under 5% | 5-10% | Over 10% |
| Daily active users | 20%+ of members | 10-20% | Under 10% |
| Posts per member/month | 2+ | 1 | Under 1 |
| Response time to questions | Under 4 hours | 4-24 hours | Over 24 hours |
| NPS score | 50+ | 30-50 | Under 30 |

### Retention Levers (In Order of Impact)
1. **Accountability structures** -- Progress tracking, goal-setting, check-ins
2. **Member-to-member connections** -- Buddy systems, small cohorts, introductions
3. **Exclusive live access** -- Weekly calls, AMAs, guest experts
4. **Tangible results** -- Members achieving outcomes they joined for
5. **Identity and belonging** -- Shared language, community name, inside references
6. **Sunk cost** -- Annual pricing, multi-month commitments, earned status/points
