# Content Planning Reference

## CONTENT MATRIX -- COMPLETE (Frabrand, E01)

### Goal Ratios by Strategy Phase

| Phase | Attraction | Nurture | Conversion | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Growth (default) | 80% | 20% | 0% | 80% of year |
| Launch | 40% | 30% | 30% | 2-3 week windows |
| Balanced | 60% | 30% | 10% | Transition periods |
| Community | 30% | 60% | 10% | Established accounts |

### Content Type Definitions

**Attraction content** -- Designed to reach new audiences
- Educational reels/shorts
- Trending topic takes
- Shareable tips and frameworks
- Broad-appeal hooks with specific value

**Nurture content** -- Designed to deepen trust with existing audience
- Personal stories and behind-the-scenes
- Detailed tutorials and case studies
- Community Q&A and responses
- Value-dense carousels and threads

**Conversion content** -- Designed to drive specific actions
- Product/service spotlights
- Testimonial shares
- Launch announcements
- Lead magnet promotions
- Direct CTAs

## LEAN CONTENT MULTIPLICATION -- COMPLETE SYSTEM (Dickie Bush, E02)

### Variable 1: Content Pillars

Example for a "Real Estate Investing" creator:
- Pillar 1: Finding deals
- Pillar 2: Financing strategies
- Pillar 3: Property management
- Pillar 4: Market analysis
- Pillar 5: Investor mindset

### Variable 2: Content Buckets

| Bucket | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Futurism | Position as thought leader | "The rental market is shifting. Here's what smart investors are doing in 2025" |
| Your Story | Build connection and trust | "How I lost $50K on my first rental -- and what I learned" |
| Actionable Advice | Deliver immediate value | "3 steps to analyze any rental property in under 10 minutes" |

### Variable 3: Magical Multipliers

Take ONE topic (e.g., "Buying First Rental Property") and multiply across 10 angles:

| Multiplier | Post Title |
|---|---|
| Tips | "7 tips for buying your first rental property" |
| Stats | "The average first rental property makes $X/month" |
| Steps | "Step-by-step: How to buy your first rental in 90 days" |
| Lessons | "5 lessons from buying my first rental at age 25" |
| Benefits | "Why owning rental property beats stocks" |
| Reasons | "3 reasons you should buy rental property before age 30" |
| Mistakes | "The 5 biggest mistakes first-time landlords make" |
| Examples | "I analyzed 10 rental properties. Here's what I found" |
| Questions | "Should you buy a rental property in 2025?" |
| Personal Stories | "I bought my first rental with $5K down. Here's how" |

### Variable 4: Audience Archetypes

Same topic, different audience:
- Young professional with W-2 income
- Single parent looking for side income
- Corporate executive diversifying assets
- Retiree seeking passive income
- Couple saving for financial freedom

### Variable 5: Sub-Topic Expansion

"How to buy rental property" variations:
- Short-term vs. long-term rentals
- Beach properties vs. city apartments
- No money down strategies
- Hard money loan approach
- Partnership structures
- Maximum cash flow optimization
- Emerging markets
- Seller financing deals

### Total Output

5 Pillars x 3 Buckets x 10 Multipliers x 5 Archetypes x 10 Sub-topics = 7,500 potential content pieces. Even using 1% = 75 unique posts.

### Monthly Planning Session

1. Review last month's analytics (30 min)
2. Identify top 5 performing posts -- note topics, formats, hooks
3. Identify bottom 5 -- note what failed
4. Generate 30+ ideas using Endless Idea Generator
5. Assign ideas to calendar slots
6. Pre-write hooks for first week
7. Schedule batch creation days

## FORMAT TESTING PROTOCOL -- 100-DAY METHOD (Frabrand, E01)

### Phase 1: Equal Testing (Days 1-100)

- 33% carousels (or text-based format)
- 33% single images (or static visual)
- 33% reels/video (or dynamic format)
- Track performance of each format separately

### Phase 2: Analysis (Day 100)

Evaluate each format on:
- Average reach
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, saves, shares)
- Follower growth contribution
- Time investment vs. return
- Enjoyment factor (sustainability)

### Phase 3: Optimization (Day 101+)

- 60% best-performing format
- 20% second-best format
- 20% experimental/new format
- Reassess quarterly

## REPURPOSING STRATEGY -- 3 TIERS (Frabrand, E01)

### Tier 1: Basic (Same content, minor changes)

- Same content reposted every 6 months
- Change graphics slightly
- Update copy for freshness
- Works because audiences forget and new followers haven't seen it

### Tier 2: Advanced (Same content, different format)

| Original Format | Repurpose To |
|---|---|
| Carousel | Reel (narrate the carousel slides) |
| Reel | Carousel (turn key points into slides) |
| Blog post | Twitter thread + LinkedIn post + IG carousel |
| Podcast episode | 5-10 short-form clips + quote graphics |
| Newsletter | 3-5 social posts |
| Notebook screenshots | Carousel format |

### Tier 3: Pro (Same template, different content)

Create reusable templates:
- "30-step plan" template used monthly with different topics
- "Bad vs. Good" comparison template applied to different subjects
- "Weekly roundup" format with rotating content
- "Myth vs. Fact" template for various niche myths

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC REPURPOSING TIPS (Frabrand, E01)

| Platform | Key Adjustment |
|---|---|
| TikTok | Test multiple hooks for same video; TikTok doesn't penalize volume; use TikTok search engine with trend filters |
| Pinterest | Post everything in one day per month; algorithm distributes for months; optimize headlines for search |
| YouTube Shorts | Choose thumbnail via mobile; keep headlines under 50 characters; link Shorts to at least one full-length horizontal video |
| LinkedIn | Convert carousels to document format; caption cutoff is 2 lines (vs. 1 on IG); don't repeat hook in caption |
| Threads | Add context/hook text above carousel; saves don't matter -- comments and shares do |
| General | Hashtags only matter on Instagram; captions only matter on Instagram |

## BATCH CREATION SYSTEMS

### Daily Creation System (First 100 Days)

Create one post from scratch every day. Fastest learning curve. No batch efficiency yet -- pure skill-building.

### Batch System (After 100 Days) -- Frabrand

| Day | Task | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Idea generation + research | 2-3 hours |
| Tuesday | Copywriting (all posts for week) | 3-4 hours |
| Wednesday | Copy review + revision | 1-2 hours |
| Thursday | Design/filming | 3-4 hours |
| Friday | Scheduling + distribution setup | 1-2 hours |
| Weekend | REST (scheduled, non-negotiable) | -- |

### YouTube Batch Recording (Sunny, E08)

- Block same 2 days every month for filming
- Day 1: B-roll (screencasts, visuals)
- Day 2: A-roll (on-camera content)
- Shoot 4-8 videos over those 2 days
- Only change your TOP between videos (that's all viewers see)

### Content Repurposing Chain (Dickie Bush, E07)

1. Book chapter/reading notes > write down thoughts
2. Thoughts > newsletter or email
3. Newsletter > 3-5 social posts
4. Best-performing post > thread or series
5. Thread > course module or guide
6. Guide > upsell or product

| Platform | Recommended Cadence | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| YouTube | 1x/week | Never need more; consistency matters more than volume |
| Instagram | 1x/day (reels or carousel) | Stories daily in addition |
| TikTok | 1-3x/day | Only if quality maintained; 1x/day minimum |
| LinkedIn | 3-5x/week | More posting = faster skill development |
| Twitter/X | 3-5x/day (tweets) + 1x/week (thread) | High volume platform |
| Pinterest | Monthly batch (all at once) | Slow algorithm, long distribution |
| YouTube Shorts | 3-5x/week | Supplement long-form, not replace |

## THE CONTENT CYCLE (Frabrand, E01)

From 1,500+ posts, only about 100 ideas are truly solid. The rest are repurposed variations. Only 15 posts went truly viral (1%). The creators who win are the ones who persist through the 99% that don't go viral.

### Supply and Demand Cycles

1. High supply + stable demand > engagement drops
2. Engagement drops > creators quit > supply decreases
3. Supply decreases + demand stable > engagement rises > cycle restarts

These cycles happen 2-3 times per year. If you are in it for 3,000 days (not 30 or 300), short-term dips are irrelevant.
