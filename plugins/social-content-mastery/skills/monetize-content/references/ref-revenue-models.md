## Revenue Stream Hierarchy

### Tier 1: Owned Products (Highest Priority)
- **Digital courses** -- Build once, sell forever. Highest margin.
- **Ebooks / templates** -- Low creation cost, high perceived value with good positioning
- **Coaching programs** -- High-ticket, recurring. Group > 1:1 for scalability

### Tier 2: Recurring Passive
- **Affiliate marketing** -- Target 30-40% commission on recurring SaaS
- **Membership / community** -- Monthly recurring revenue (see community-building)
- **YouTube AdSense** -- Passive but requires 100K+ views/month for meaningful income

### Tier 3: Active Income
- **Brand partnerships** -- (see brand-partnerships skill)
- **Speaking engagements** -- High per-event, limited frequency
- **Consulting / freelance** -- Time-for-money (transition to productized service)

### Tier 4: Platform Bonuses
- **Instagram Reels Bonus** -- Variable, unreliable. Example: $100 for 20K views
- **TikTok Creator Fund** -- Low CPM, not a business model
- **YouTube Shorts Fund** -- Supplementary only

## Coaching Offer Design Framework

### The 5 Elements of an Irresistible Offer

1. **Specific transformation** -- "Go from 0 to 10K followers in 90 days" (not "grow on social media")
2. **Clear mechanism** -- Name your method/framework (e.g., "The Content Flywheel System")
3. **Time-bound promise** -- "In 90 days" or "In 6 weeks"
4. **Risk reversal** -- Money-back guarantee, pay-for-results, or free trial
5. **Proof** -- Testimonials, case studies, your own results

### Offer Stack Structure

| Component | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Core training (video/written) | Deliver the method |
| Community access | Peer support + accountability |
| Weekly group call | Direct coaching + Q&A |
| Templates/tools | Speed up implementation |
| DM/email support | Remove friction between calls |
| Bonus: 1:1 call | Premium differentiator |

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Self-paced | $297-$997 | Training + community | Scale |
| Group coaching | $1,000-$5,000 | Above + weekly calls | Revenue + results |
| 1:1 premium | $3,000-$10,000/mo | Everything + personal attention | Maximum transformation |

## Pricing Psychology Playbook

### Price Anchoring (Nicolas Cole)

**The principle:** Context determines perceived value.

**Execution:**
1. Calculate client's potential upside from working with you
2. Present that number first in your pitch
3. Then present your fee
4. The fee feels small relative to the anchored upside

**Example conversation:**
- "Your email list of 50,000 subscribers, at industry-standard 1% conversion, with a $500 product, could generate $250K in revenue"
- "My fee to write your weekly newsletter is $3K/month"
- "That's 1.2% of the potential revenue I'm helping you capture"

### Prepayment Discount

- Average deal length: 6 months
- Common churn point: month 3-4
- Strategy: Offer 10-15% discount for full upfront payment
- Example: $3K/mo x 6 = $18K --> Offer $15K-$16K upfront
- Benefit to you: eliminates churn risk, improves cash flow
- Benefit to client: saves money, demonstrates commitment

### The Advil Metaphor (Nicolas Cole)
- Saying yes to any paying client = taking Advil for the pain
- Short-term relief, long-term damage to positioning
- Must say NO to some paying clients to make space for RIGHT clients
- Right clients: match your niche, appreciate your value, refer more like themselves

## Launch Selling Timeline (Twitter/X Growth System)

### Pre-Launch Phase (14 days)
| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| -14 | Start agitating the problem in daily content |
| -10 | Share proof that your method works (numbers, results) |
| -7 | Promise something bold about upcoming product |
| -5 | Show unique mechanism behind your promise |
| -4 | Post 5 tweets pointing out the problem |
| -3 | Leak product to a few people for before/after results |
| -2 | Write thread on lessons from the product with waitlist CTA |
| -1 | Final prime: email list + post screenshots of early results |

### Launch Phase (5 days)
| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Launch | Announce price + early-bird deadline |
| +1 | Share testimonial screenshots + remind of price increase |
| +2 | Post results/wins from early buyers |
| +3 | Address remaining objections with content |
| +5 | Final close: "doors closing" or "price increases tomorrow" |

### Key Principle
"Dissolve objections with your content, collect payments with your website."
- Identify FAQ objections before launch
- Create content addressing each one
- By launch day, audience should have zero remaining objections

## Funnel Architecture by Traffic Temperature

### Cold Traffic (Paid Ads, New Visitors)
```
Ad --> Landing Page --> Free Webinar/Training --> Email Sequence (5-7 emails) --> Sales Page --> Purchase
```
- Long nurture needed (trust not yet built)
- Webinars convert 5-15% of attendees
- Email sequence builds relationship over 7-14 days

### Warm Traffic (Your Social Media Followers)
```
Content --> Comment/DM Trigger --> Lead Magnet --> Short Email Sequence (2-3 emails) --> Sales Page --> Purchase
```
- Shorter funnel = higher conversion (trust already built)
- Most sales come from direct/short funnels for warm audiences
- Your followers already know/like/trust you -- long funnels add friction

### Hot Traffic (Email List, Past Customers)
```
Email Announcement --> Sales Page --> Purchase
```
- Simplest funnel for warmest audience
- Past customers convert at 5-10x rate of new prospects
- Upsell existing customers before acquiring new ones

## YouTube Monetization Hierarchy (Sunny Lenarduzzi)

### Revenue Streams Ranked for YouTube Creators
1. **Own products** (courses, coaching) -- Highest per-viewer value
2. **Affiliate marketing** -- Passive, compounds over time
3. **Brand partnerships** -- Premium pricing with engaged audience
4. **YouTube AdSense** -- Passive but low CPM for most niches
5. **Super Chats / Memberships** -- Supplementary for live streamers
6. **Speaking engagements** -- Leverage YouTube authority

### YouTube-Specific Settings
- Enable mid-roll ads on videos 8+ minutes
- Enable post-roll ads on all videos
- Monetize all eligible content (even old videos)
