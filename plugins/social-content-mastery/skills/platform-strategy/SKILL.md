## INSTRUCTOR DISAGREEMENT NOTICES

### Disagreement 1: Niche Width by Platform

| Position | Advocate | Core Argument |
|---|---|---|
| **Niche to the outcome** | Gusten Sun (E03a) | Niche down to the outcome you deliver, not the topic you discuss. Specificity creates authority. |
| **Expand your niche** | Justas/Instapreneur (E04a) | Broader content = more views = more of your target audience via math. 1M views x 50% target demo > 100K views x 100% target. |
| **Optimize for relevance** | Dickie Bush & Nicholas Cole (E02) | LinkedIn rewards relevance over reach. 200 CFOs with budget > 10K views on generic marketing tips. |

**Resolution:** Platform determines the right approach. LinkedIn rewards narrow relevance (go specific). Instagram/TikTok reward reach and can go broader for volume. YouTube rewards search specificity (narrow on topic, broad on audience). Start narrow to build authority, expand strategically as audience grows.

### Disagreement 2: Hashtag Impact

| Position | Advocate | Core Argument |
|---|---|---|
| **Hashtags help categorization** | General advice | Hashtags help the algorithm categorize your content for the right audience. |
| **Hashtags have minimal impact** | Justas (E04a) | Tested across multiple accounts -- no significant difference between using and not using hashtags. |

**Resolution:** Use hashtags only for new accounts under 5K followers to help the algorithm learn your niche. After that, hashtags add negligible value. If unsure which to use, skip them entirely -- wrong hashtags do more harm than good.

### Disagreement 3: Ideal Short-Form Length

| Position | Advocate | Core Argument |
|---|---|---|
| **7-15 seconds optimal** | Justas (E04a) | Shorter = higher completion rate = better signal to algorithm. |
| **Up to 60 seconds with strong storytelling** | Mino Lee (E01), Nicklas Christl (E05b) | Story-driven content builds deeper trust and enables coaching-funnel conversions. |

**Resolution:** Shorter for algorithm tricks, broad reach, and top-of-funnel growth. Longer for trust-building, mid/bottom-funnel, and coaching/high-ticket content. Match length to content purpose, not a universal rule.

## PLATFORM SELECTION DECISION TREE

**Step 1: Pick ONE main platform (E03d Anton Mehto). Optimize specifically for it, then repost to others.**

### Decision Framework

```
Are you B2B or targeting professionals?
  YES -> LinkedIn (primary), repurpose to YouTube + Instagram
  NO -> Continue

Are you building a long-form educational brand or course business?
  YES -> YouTube (primary), repurpose shorts to Instagram + TikTok
  NO -> Continue

Do you want the best all-in-one platform (awareness + community + monetization)?
  YES -> Instagram (recommended default), repurpose to TikTok + YouTube Shorts
  NO -> Continue

Is your target audience primarily young (16-25)?
  YES -> TikTok for discovery, Instagram for monetization
  NO -> Continue

Is your target audience primarily women?
  YES -> Consider Pinterest (500M+ MAU, 76% women) alongside Instagram
  NO -> Instagram (default)
```

### Platform Assessment Summary

| Platform | Best For | Algorithm Priority | Content Style | Monetization |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Instagram Reels** | All-in-one (recommended default) | Watch time, shares, saves, comments | Polished, visual | DMs, stories, email funnels, brand deals |
| **YouTube** | Evergreen compound machine | Search + watch time | Educational, story-driven | Own products > sponsorships > AdSense > affiliate |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, professionals, thought leadership | Relevance, dwell time, value filter | Text-first, tactical, specific | Lead gen, coaching, consulting, B2B services |
| **TikTok** | Young audience, hook testing, raw content | Watch time, shares | Raw, authentic, trend-driven | Brand deals (not creator fund) |
| **Facebook Reels** | 35+ demographic, 600M monthly Reels users | Engagement, watch time | Native uploads (not cross-posted from IG) | Community + ads |
| **Pinterest** | Women-focused audiences, evergreen discovery | Saves, click-throughs | Visual, educational pins | Traffic to external sites |
| **Threads/X** | Top-of-funnel, idea testing | Replies, reposts | Text-first, conversational | Hard to monetize directly |

### Social Media Funnel Positioning (E04b)

| Funnel Stage | Platforms |
|---|---|
| **Top (See you)** | Instagram, TikTok, Threads, X |
| **Middle (Trust you)** | Instagram, YouTube, Facebook Groups |
| **Bottom (Buy from you)** | LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube |

Only Instagram and YouTube effectively serve all three funnel stages.

## PLATFORM ALGORITHM MECHANICS

### Instagram Algorithm (E04a, E04b)

**Core signals (ranked):**
1. Watch time / retention rate (primary metric across ALL platforms)
2. Shares and sends (strongest viral signal)
3. Saves (high-value engagement)
4. Comments (dwell time proxy)
5. Likes (weakest signal)
6. Profile follows from content

**How it works:**
- Algorithm scans content, eliminates low quality/spam, categorizes by niche, measures signals, ranks content (1-500 in each user's feed)
- Multiple algorithms per platform -- stories, explore, feed, hashtags each have their own
- Good signals minus bad signals = content rank
- Bad signals: bounce rate, unfollow, hide, report, "not interested," closing app
- Instagram needs minimum ~30 posts to understand your niche and target audience
- Shadow banning exists -- check Account Status in settings

**Cross-reference:** For detailed Instagram algorithm mechanics, hashtag strategy, trending audio, posting times, and story algorithm -> load `ref-instagram-algorithm.md`

### LinkedIn Algorithm (E02)

**The LinkedIn Value Filter** -- before content hits any feed, LinkedIn checks:
1. **Authority Check:** Does this person consistently create content in their stated expertise area?
2. **Utility Assessment:** Will this help someone solve a real problem?
3. **Audience Matching:** Who needs this content most? (job titles, interests, connection patterns)
4. **Quality Gatekeeping:** Spam tagging, excessive hashtags, off-platform links get deprioritized

**Master metric: Dwell time.**
- Dwell time hierarchy: Longer read time > long comment > short comment > like
- A comment = 2-3 min of dwell time (read, process, formulate, type)
- The Bookmark Test: Can you create content so valuable someone wants to SAVE it?
- Algorithm logic: Holds attention longer -> platform thinks valuable -> distributes to more people -> flywheel

**Your niche IS your network:**
- Content first distributed to small test group of connections + topic-interested non-connections
- If it performs well in test group, serves to larger group. Repeat.
- Spam connecting with random people = untargeted test group = poor performance = algorithm buries content
- Build network deliberately with niche-relevant connections

**Cross-reference:** For complete LinkedIn profile optimization -> load `ref-linkedin-profile.md`

### TikTok Algorithm (E01, E03d)

- Measures watch time, shares, and comments
- Tests on base batch (100-10,000+ depending on account authority)
- If base test group shows good metrics, pushes to next larger batch the following day
- Account authority builds over time -- consistent bangers lead to larger base test audiences
- Algorithm prioritizes reach over followers -- raw/authentic content preferred
- Young, highly engaged demographic; trends move fast
- WARNING (E03d): TikTok threatened with US ban. Do NOT make it your primary platform, but always repost to it.

### YouTube Algorithm (E03a)

- YouTube is the ONLY platform with true long-term search-driven discovery
- Videos from 1 year ago with good title/thumbnail still rank in search and bring revenue
- Instagram/TikTok content dies within days; YouTube is the evergreen compound machine
- Shorts vs long-form: debate ongoing whether Shorts drives long-form traffic
- Use short-form and other platforms to guide attention TO YouTube

**Cross-reference:** For YouTube strategy, monetization hierarchy, Shorts debate -> load `ref-youtube-strategy.md`

## PROFILE OPTIMIZATION

### Universal Bio Formula (E03a, E04a)

**3-line structure:**
- **Line 1:** What you do / who you serve / what they get. "Helping [target audience] to [desired outcome]"
- **Line 2:** Credibility/results. Client results, personal wins, big names. No achievements yet? Make a promise: "You can be my next success story"
- **Line 3:** Call to action. "Get my free [lead magnet] here" / "Join my [community]"

**Profile picture:** Must be you (not logo, not NFT). Clean headshot, good lighting, face ~60-70% of frame, eye contact, neutral background.

**Username:** Use real name (not business name). People follow people. Avoid numbers. Minimal underscores/dots.

### Instagram-Specific (E03a, E04a)

- **Story Highlights:** Organize strategically to complement bio: about you, products/services, testimonials. Not random stories.
- **Landing page:** Use Beacons (free) for link-in-bio + Gumroad for products and email collection
- Optimized bio can 2x to 4x follower conversions from the same view count (E04a)

### LinkedIn-Specific (E02)

LinkedIn profile is a complete optimization system with 7 headline variations, 8 banner templates, 7 about section frameworks, skills strategy across 4 buckets, and featured section strategy.

**Cross-reference:** For the full LinkedIn profile system -> load `ref-linkedin-profile.md`

### YouTube-Specific (E03a, E03b)

- Channel = your evergreen content library
- Thumbnail packaging rules: subtle tension, max 3 storytelling elements, don't repeat title in thumbnail, pass squint test, consistent branding element (E03b)
- Title + thumbnail are two different levers -- they should work off each other, not tell the same story twice

## CROSS-PLATFORM REPURPOSING STRATEGY (E04b)

### 4 Repurposable Elements Per Post
1. **Video footage** -> Facebook/Meta, Pinterest, YouTube Shorts, TikTok
2. **Carousel graphics** -> Twitter threads, Threads posts, LinkedIn documents, Pinterest pins
3. **Single images** -> nearly all platforms
4. **Captions/copy** -> Twitter, Threads, LinkedIn posts, YouTube community, newsletter

### Repurposing Rules
- Upload NATIVELY to each platform (do not cross-post from Instagram to Facebook -- people cannot follow/engage properly)
- Caption-driven reels ("read the caption") do NOT repurpose well -- captions do not work the same across platforms
- TikTok hook testing: post same video with different hooks on TikTok first (no frequency penalty), then take winning hook to Instagram

### On-Platform Repurposing (3 Levels, E04b)
1. **Basic:** Same content with minor changes, posted 6+ months later. Change graphics and copy slightly (algorithm tracks duplicates)
2. **Advanced:** Same content, different format (notebook photos as carousel vs. original reel with B-roll of notebook)
3. **Pro:** Same format template, different content each time (reusable template weekly with different topic values)

## POSTING TIME & CADENCE GUIDANCE

| Platform | Recommended Cadence | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 1 reel/day + 1-3 stories/day (E04a) or 3-5x/week (E04b) | 30 min BEFORE follower peak activity time |
| LinkedIn | 3-5x per week (E02) | Maintain predictable cadence/windows each week |
| TikTok | 1-3x/day during learning phase | Test different times; algorithm handles distribution |
| YouTube | 1-2x/week long-form; daily Shorts optional | Consistency matters more than specific time |
