# Growth Tactics Reference

## INSTAGRAM GROWTH STRATEGIES (DETAILED)

### Strategy 1: Focus on Reels (E04a)
- Reels are the primary growth driver for Instagram
- Posts + stories = retention of existing followers, NOT discovery
- Recommended content split: 70% Reels, 25% Stories, 5% regular posts
- Posting cadence: 1 reel/day, 1+ story/day, 1+ regular post/month
- Reels have a separate Explore section = higher visibility to non-followers than any other format

### Strategy 2: Daily Consistency (E04a)
- Posting daily = 7x more likely to go viral than posting once/week (pure math: 7x more chances)
- Algorithms love consistency -- more data points = better audience targeting
- More content = more experimentation = faster learning curve
- Never stop posting once you start. Taking a week off can drop reach by 10x, and recovery takes months
- At minimum, stay active with stories during any planned break
- Batch-create content before vacations or planned absences

### Strategy 3: Anomaly Method (E04a)
**Step-by-step process:**
1. Find 10-20 creators in your niche
2. Use Sort Feed Chrome extension (sortfeed.com) to sort their Reels by views
3. Filter for last 200 videos (proxy for ~12 months)
4. Identify anomalies: reels with significantly higher views than that creator's average
5. Open top videos in new tabs
6. Analyze: hook structure, audio choice, hashtags, value delivery, visual approach
7. Save all anomalies to a collection
8. Recreate with your own touch -- aim to make it at least 10% better

**Case study:** Instructor recreated a 700K-view reel with small changes (hook rewrite, better visuals) and got 8 million views.

**Run this process every 2-4 weeks** to assess what is working now that was not before (E05a Kallaway hook research process aligns with this).

### Strategy 4: Double Down on Winners (E04a)
- When YOUR reel goes viral, recreate it within 1 week
- Change: visuals, hook wording, angle -- keep: same core idea/topic
- Most people who saw original will NOT see the new one -- algorithm distributes to different segments
- Works 97% of the time
- More than half the time, the second reel outperforms the first
- Case studies:
  - 1.3M view reel recreated 3 days later = another 1.3M views
  - 3M view reel recreated with more controversy = 9M views
- "Squeeze the lemon" -- keep recreating until the idea is fully exhausted
- This is the single easiest growth hack: the algorithm already validated the topic

### Strategy 5: Read the Caption Strategy (E04a)
- Super short reel (4-6 seconds total)
- Structure: strong hook (3-5 sec) + CTA to read caption (1-3 sec)
- Skip the middle part entirely -- all value goes in the description/caption
- Algorithm hack: every second reading description counts as reel watch time
- This effectively doubles average watch time vs actual reel length
- Do not overuse -- audience gets tired of always being told to read captions

### Strategy 6: "Why Is It So Fast" Strategy (E04a)
- Structure: Strong hook + rapid-scrolling value showcase impossible to read at normal speed
- Viewers MUST replay and pause to read all the value = massively increased watch time
- Example hook: "Disappear for 60 days and set yourself 10 years ahead" then rapid step-by-step guide
- Must have genuine value behind it -- if content is empty after replay, viewers skip you permanently
- Use sparingly (max 2-3x/week)
- "Lie a little bit" about speed of desired outcome, but never about the outcome itself

### Strategy 7: Comment Magnet — BEST STRATEGY (E04a)
**Setup:**
1. Create a free digital product relevant to your niche (1-2 page ebook is fine if valuable)
2. Quality test: would you pay $5 for this product?
3. Host on Gumroad (free) to collect emails
4. Set up ManyChat automation to auto-send product link via DM when keyword commented

**Execution:**
1. Create reel with CTA: "Comment [keyword] and I'll send it to you"
2. ManyChat detects keyword comment -> auto-DMs product link
3. Viewer goes to Gumroad -> enters email -> gets free product

**Benefits (triple):**
1. Massively increased engagement (comments are strongest signal)
2. Email list building (every commenter becomes a lead)
3. Viewer gets genuine value (builds trust)

**Rules:**
- Free product must be relevant to niche AND to reel topic
- Create ONE product that works across multiple reels (aligned with main niche)
- Comment magnet should preview what you will eventually sell
- Message template: greeting + product name + link + instructions ("choose price, type 0 for free") + request for review

### Strategy 8: Visual Hook Pattern Recognition (E04a)
- Track which visual elements correlate with higher performance
- Examples: specific shots, fonts, color grading styles, angles
- When pattern identified, incorporate systematically into future content
- Builds recognizable branded visual identity over time
- Examples: sitting in front of computer = high engagement, dark aesthetic shots = more views

## 5x STORY VIEWS STRATEGY (E04a)

### How Story Algorithm Works
- Story algorithm ranks by engagement history
- Most interacted-with creators show first in story bar
- Replies and sticker interactions = highest-value engagement signals

### Tactics
- Post engagement stickers (questions, polls, quizzes) to drive interaction
- Story reply magnets: offer something free if they reply with keyword X
- Keep stories SHORT (3-5 seconds). Photo stories default to 5 seconds -- trick: convert to 3-second video in CapCut, upload as video story
- Ideal story count: 3 per day. Maximum 5 -- more = more skips = bad data for algorithm
- Post stories during follower peak activity times
- Before posting promotional story, post engagement story couple hours earlier
- If story views drastically drop: stop posting stories for 1-3 days. Instagram gives additional push on return

## LINKEDIN ENGAGEMENT SYSTEM (E02)

### New Connection Welcome System
- Template: "Hey [name], thanks for connecting! If you ever need help with [your expertise], let me know. Also, here are a few posts I think you'd find helpful: [link 1] [link 2] [link 3]"
- Creates traffic engine: drives new connections to best posts -> engagement -> algorithm distribution
- Automate with VA ($2-3/hour) not third-party software (LinkedIn restricts automation tools)
- At scale: 50-200+ connection requests per day = massive daily DM opportunity

### Strategic Connection Requests
- LinkedIn allows 200 connection requests per week
- Send 5-10 personalized requests per day
- Reference a post or specific challenge in the request
- Two target lists:
  - 25 creators/accounts similar to you (serve same audience, different offers)
  - 25 big influencers your buyers follow (their comment sections = where your buyers hang out)

### Daily Commenting (3-5 Posts/Day)
- Comment on posts in your niche that your target audience sees
- Add 1 concrete example, a number, or a clarifying question
- Never write "Great post" or one-word comments
- 2-3 sentences, conversational, first-person, unique twist
- LinkedIn ranks comments by insightfulness -- valuable comments rise to top of comment section

### Profile Viewer Follow-Up
- Check "Who Viewed Your Profile" daily
- These are warm leads -- connect, engage, or follow up

### Comment Reply Strategy
- Reply to every comment on your posts within 24 hours
- Ask a follow-up question to continue the thread
- Each reply extends dwell time on your post

## AUTOPLUGS & CTA SYSTEM (E02)

### Autoplug Rotation
| CTA Type | Template |
|---|---|
| **Direct** | "Full breakdown is in my free guide -- grab it here" |
| **Benefits** | "If activation is stuck, this 5-step PDF will unstick it" |
| **Social proof** | "This process added +18% in 21 days -- details in the guide" |
| **Conversation** | "Comment 'guide' and I'll DM it" |

### Rules
- One CTA per post maximum
- Keep it soft, specific, and relevant to the post topic
- Post native content; put external links in first comment or profile/Featured section
- End with engagement trigger: question, request for examples, or keyword-comment CTA

### Pitch In Public (Monthly, E02)
- Publish one dedicated offer post per month
- Structure: problem -> stakes -> your method -> proof -> invite
- Write core offer in one sentence: who it's for, outcome, timeline, price range
- This is transparent, authentic selling

### Warm DM Script (After Value Exchange, E02)
1. Acknowledge context: "Saw your comment on [X]"
2. Ask goal/problem: "What's the target you're aiming for?"
3. Offer help: "I've got a 5-step checklist -- want it?"
4. If they engage, offer 15-minute audit (clear agenda and outcome, not a pitch call)

## CROSS-PLATFORM REPURPOSING STRATEGY (E04b)

### 4 Repurposable Elements Per Post
| Element | Repurpose To |
|---|---|
| Video footage | Facebook/Meta, Pinterest, YouTube Shorts, TikTok |
| Carousel graphics | Twitter threads, Threads posts, LinkedIn documents, Pinterest pins |
| Single images | Nearly all platforms |
| Captions/copy | Twitter, Threads, LinkedIn posts, YouTube community, newsletter |

### Repurposing Rules
- Upload NATIVELY to each platform (never cross-post from Instagram to Facebook)
- Caption-driven reels ("read the caption") do NOT repurpose well
- TikTok hook testing: post same video with different hooks on TikTok first (no frequency penalty), then take winning hook to Instagram

### On-Platform Repurposing (3 Levels, E04b)
1. **Basic:** Same content with minor changes, posted 6+ months later. Change graphics and copy slightly (algorithm tracks duplicates)
2. **Advanced:** Same content, different format (notebook photos as carousel vs original reel with B-roll)
3. **Pro:** Same format template, different content each time (reusable structure, weekly with different topic)

## EARLY ENGAGEMENT TACTICS (E04a)

### Pre-Posting Engagement
- 10 minutes before posting, comment on other creators' posts in your niche
- They often reciprocate, creating early engagement signal on your new post
- Do not comment on accounts with 100K+ followers (they do not need engagement exchange and will not reciprocate)
- Target mid-tier creators (5K-50K) for mutual engagement

### ManyChat Automation Setup
1. Create free Gumroad product page
2. Connect ManyChat to Instagram account
3. Set keyword trigger (e.g., "GUIDE")
4. Create automation flow: detect keyword comment -> send DM with product link
5. Test the flow before going live
6. Include in DM: greeting, product name, link, instructions, request for review

### Gumroad Email Collection Hack (E04a)
- Set price to $0 but suggested price at $10 (still free but some people pay voluntarily)
- Need $100 net profit on Gumroad to send emails to 100+ people
- Gumroad hack: buy your own product to cross $100 threshold (lose ~10% to fees, one-time cost)

## WEEKLY AUTHORITY ASSET CURATION (E02)

One of the most underrated tactics for demonstrating expertise:
- Pick something your niche finds valuable and curate examples every week
- Could be: subject lines, email templates, landing page examples, social hooks, website copy
- When audience sees you are the go-to source for something they value, they keep coming back

### Good Authority Asset Criteria
- Small and digestible (consumed in under 2 minutes)
- Practical utility (solves a real, recurring problem)
- Pattern recognition (identifiable elements you can analyze and teach)
- Accessibility (can find 10-12 examples per week)
- Scalability (compounds into larger resources -- "101 Cold Email Openers" after 6 months)
- Demonstration value (shows both taste/curation and expertise/analysis)

## GROWTH FAILURE DIAGNOSIS

### Why Creators Fail (E04b)
1. **Counting only on consistency:** Consistency is table stakes. Adaptability matters more
2. **Blaming the algorithm:** Multiple algorithms exist per platform. If it fails on IG and YouTube, it is the content
3. **Gatekeeping value:** Free content should be better than competitors' paid content. People pay for proximity/access, not information
4. **Motivated by money:** Audiences sense desperation. Prioritize giving value
5. **Analysis paralysis:** Consuming courses feels productive but creates nothing. Act > fail > learn > adapt

### Supply & Demand Reality (E04b)
- Social media is a zero-sum game -- limited attention, your gain = someone else's loss
- Supply of content only increasing (especially with AI). Demand decreasing as platforms multiply
- 3-phase cycle: engagement drops -> creators quit -> engagement rises for survivors. Repeat.
- Long-term players (3,000+ days, not 300) always win through these cycles
- Engagement dips happen 2-3x per year on Instagram -- normal, not reason to quit
