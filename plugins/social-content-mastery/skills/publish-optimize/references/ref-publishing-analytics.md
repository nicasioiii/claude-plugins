# Publishing Analytics Quick Reference

## Engagement Ratio Benchmarks (Frabrand / Content Creation Dojo)

### Instagram Minimum Engagement Ratios for Distribution

| Metric | Target Ratio | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Likes-to-Reach | 1:10 | 100 likes per 1,000 reach |
| Saves-to-Likes | 1:1 | 100 likes should equal ~100 saves |
| Shares-to-Likes | 1:10 | 100 likes should equal ~10 shares |

### Content Type Primary Metrics

| Content Type | Optimize For | Why |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Educational | Saves | People bookmark to reference later |
| Inspirational | Shares | People forward to friends who need it |
| Entertainment | Likes | Quick dopamine hit = quick engagement |
| Promotional | Profile visits + DMs | Conversion-focused content |

### Reach Distribution Levels

| Level | Follower:Non-Follower | Meaning |
|-------|----------------------|---------|
| Good | 1:1 | Content resonating beyond existing audience |
| Phenomenal | 1:3 | Strong algorithm distribution |
| Viral | 1:10 | Massive non-follower reach |

## Retention Graph Diagnosis Patterns

### Pattern 1: Hook Drop (0-3 seconds)
- **Graph shape:** Sharp cliff at start, then stabilizes
- **Root cause:** Hook failure -- not compelling enough or targeting wrong audience
- **Fixes:**
  - Rewrite hook using different archetype (question, controversy, story, stat)
  - Improve first-frame visual (movement, text, face close-up)
  - Remove any "hey guys" or warm-up filler
  - Test spoken hook vs. text-on-screen hook

### Pattern 2: End Drop
- **Graph shape:** Steady retention then sharp fall near end
- **Root cause:** Outro too long, value already delivered, viewer got what they needed
- **Fixes:**
  - Cut everything after final value delivery
  - End with CTA immediately after key insight
  - Use looping technique (connect ending to beginning)

### Pattern 3: Middle Staircase
- **Graph shape:** Wiggly declining staircase throughout body
- **Root cause:** Pacing too slow, dead space, repetitive content
- **Fixes:**
  - Add visual change every 2-3 seconds (B-roll, angle change, text)
  - Increase rate of revelation (new info every 3-5 seconds)
  - Cut any section that doesn't advance the core promise
  - Add open loops before each new section

### Pattern 4: Flat/Rising
- **Graph shape:** Retention holds steady or actually increases
- **Root cause:** Content is working extremely well
- **Action:** Document everything about this video and replicate the formula

## Platform Publishing Specifications

### Instagram Reels
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Max length:** 90 seconds (sweet spot: 15-30s for growth, 60-90s for depth)
- **Captions:** Auto-generated + manual review. Classic font, positioned 30% up from bottom
- **Cover image:** Custom thumbnail, NOT a random frame
- **Audio:** Original audio OR trending audio (check early bird checklist)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant, mix of niche-specific and broader category
- **Caption:** Hook in first line, value or story in body, CTA at end
- **Posting:** Share to Feed AND Stories for maximum distribution

### TikTok
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Max length:** 10 minutes (sweet spot: 30-60 seconds)
- **Two-hook system:** Visual hook (first frame) + verbal hook (first sentence)
- **Context captions:** 1-2 lines of text that add context to spoken content
- **Hashtags:** Optional; use for geo-targeting or skip entirely
- **Export:** 30fps, high quality

### YouTube Long-Form
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (1920x1080px minimum, 4K preferred)
- **Thumbnail:** Custom, passes squint test, max 3 elements, subtle tension
- **Title:** Does NOT repeat thumbnail text
- **Description:** SEO-optimized first 2 lines, timestamps, links below fold
- **Tags:** 5-10 relevant tags
- **End screen:** 20-second end screen with next video suggestion
- **Cards:** Link to related content at relevant moments

### YouTube Shorts
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Max length:** 60 seconds
- **Looping:** End connects to beginning for replay
- **No end screen or cards available

### LinkedIn
- **Text posts:** First 200 characters = hook (before "See more" fold)
- **Character limit:** ~3,000 characters
- **Formatting:** Use numbered items, slashes, colons as visual headers
- **Video:** Native upload, square (1:1) or vertical (9:16)
- **Posting:** 3-5x/week, weekday mornings

### Twitter/X
- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet, threads for longer content
- **Images:** 1200x675px or 1:1 ratio
- **Video:** Up to 2:20 (regular) or 10 min (premium)
- **Threads:** Open with strongest tweet, each tweet must stand alone
- **Engagement window:** First 30 minutes critical for distribution

## Weekly Analytics Review Template

### Step 1: Data Collection (10 minutes)
- Screenshot analytics dashboard for each platform
- Note: total reach, engagement rate, follower change, top post, worst post

### Step 2: Winner Analysis (10 minutes)
- Best performer this week: ___
- Topic: ___ | Hook style: ___ | Format: ___ | Length: ___

### Step 3: Loser Analysis (5 minutes)
- Worst performer this week: ___

- Hook problem? Pacing problem? Topic problem?

### Step 5: Next Week Plan (10 minutes)
- Double down on: ___ (from winner analysis)
- Test this week: ___ (one A/B test)
- Avoid: ___ (from loser analysis)

## Posting Frequency Guidelines by Platform

### Consensus Recommendations

| Platform | First 100 Days | Established | Maximum Value |
|----------|---------------|------------|---------------|
| Instagram | 1x/day | 5-7x/week | Daily + Stories |
| TikTok | 1-3x/day | 1x/day minimum | 3x/day if quality holds |
| YouTube | 1x/week | 1x/week | 2x/week max |
| LinkedIn | 3-5x/week | 3-5x/week | Daily max |
| Twitter/X | 1x/day minimum | 3-5x/day | Unlimited (text is cheap) |
| Facebook Reels | Repost from IG | 3-5x/week | Daily |

### The Quality-Quantity Decision

**Default:** Post daily for first 100 days (skill-building + algorithm data)

**After 100 days:** Reduce to sustainable cadence while maintaining quality

**Contrarian exception (Jimmy Farley):** Every other day is valid IF you maintain B-to-A quality per video. Proven at 1M+ followers. Only applies to creators who have demonstrated consistent quality.

**Platform-specific note:** YouTube is the exception -- 1x/week from day one. Long-form requires more production time and the algorithm rewards consistency over frequency.

## The 30-Day Content Challenge Rules

1. Post every day for 30 days straight (no exceptions)
2. Track all metrics in a spreadsheet
3. Try at least 3 different content formats
4. Study your analytics every Sunday
5. At day 30: identify your top 3 performers and your bottom 3
6. Next 30 days: create content modeled after your top 3
7. Repeat the cycle

## Algorithm Universal Principles (Cross-Platform)

Every social platform algorithm follows the same 5-step process:

1. **Scanning** -- Algorithm scans content for signals (text, audio, visuals, metadata)
2. **Elimination** -- Removes content violating policies or low-quality signals
3. **Organization** -- Categorizes content by topic, format, audience segment
4. **Signal collection** -- Tests with small audience batch, measures engagement signals
5. **Ranking** -- Based on signals, decides distribution breadth

**Key insight:** Your job is NOT to "game the algorithm." Your job is to master the psychology of capturing and retaining attention. The algorithm simply measures how well you do that.
