# Hook Templates Vault

## 7 Spoken Hook Archetypes (E05a Kallaway)

Each creates a curiosity loop differently. Ordered from easiest to hardest:

### 1. Fortuneteller
**Mechanism**: Compare existing present to a new future
- "This [tool/product] is going to change how people [activity] in the future"
- "In 5 years, [common practice] will be completely obsolete. Here's what's replacing it"
- "The way you [activity] today? It won't exist in 2 years"

### 2. Experimentation
**Mechanism**: Frame pain point solved through live example/experiment
- "You can [achieve outcome] if you just do this..."
- "I tested [approach] for 30 days -- here's what happened"
- "Watch what happens when I [surprising action]"

### 3. Educational / Tutorial
**Mechanism**: Frame pain point solved through method/tool you teach
- "If you want to [outcome], use the [method name]"
- "Here's exactly how to [outcome] in [timeframe]"
- "The [number]-step system for [desired result]"

### 4. Secret Reveal
**Mechanism**: Reveal a secret, hidden truth, or finding
- "There's a [resource] nobody knows about but all [authority figures] are using"
- "The truth about [topic] that nobody talks about"
- "[Industry insiders] don't want you to know this about [topic]"

### 5. Contrarian / Negative
**Mechanism**: State a non-obvious belief in the first line
- "People spend way too much time obsessing over [common focus]"
- "Stop [popular advice]. It's actually hurting your [outcome]"
- "[Common practice] is the worst thing you can do for [result]"

### 6. Comparison
**Mechanism**: Compare many versions of something
- "These are the top [number] [options]...but which is actually best for [specific use]?"
- "I tried every [product category] so you don't have to"
- "[Option A] vs [Option B] -- the results were shocking"

### 7. Question
**Mechanism**: Post a question they want answered
- "Why are so many people [unexpected behavior] when they [activity]?"
- "What would happen if you [surprising action] for [timeframe]?"
- "Have you ever wondered why [phenomenon]?"

### Combo Multiplier: Raw Shock
Can be combined with any archetype. Hit viewer with instant scroll-stop word/action/sound.
- "See this..." (combined with striking visual)
- "[Shocking statement]..." (pause, then archetype)

## 8 Instagram Hook Types (E04a Justas)

1. **Information gap**: "Here is how all [authority figures] [achieve surprising result] in just [timeframe]"
2. **Negative hook**: "If you're stuck trying to [goal], that's probably because your [approach] sucks"
3. **Question hook**: "Do you know the laziest way to [outcome]?"
4. **Story hook**: "I wanted to [goal]. Before, [obstacle] seemed impossible..."
5. **Challenge hook**: "If you're tired of [problem], then listen to what I'm about to say"
6. **How-to hook**: "Here's how you can [outcome] in the shortest period possible without [pain]"
7. **Social proof hook**: "Here's why all [authority figures] are talking about [topic]"
8. **Controversial-to-desired**: "Stop [popular practice] if you want to [outcome]. Instead start doing [alternative]"

### Instagram Hook Rules
- Be dramatic. Use strong words: "best," "top," "secret"
- Strong comparisons ("Replace a 4-year college degree with these 8 videos")
- Write in simple English (7th grade reading level)
- Clear about what video is about + create curiosity

## 16 LinkedIn Hook Formats (E02 Dickie Bush & Cole)

1. **"If...Then" Algorithm**: {X} "If, then" algorithms for {Topic}
2. **Brutal But True Story**: The first {Time} of my {Activity} were brutal. {Neg1}, {Neg2}, {Neg3}
3. **Average Person Is Wrong**: The average person thinks {Belief}. They're wrong:
4. **Curated Opinion**: The problem with {Behavior} in your {Area}: - {Consequence}
5. **One Habit, Huge Outcome**: The one habit that {Outcome}: {Habit}. But most people waste years trying to {Action}
6. **"How To" Post**: How to get "{Outcome}" on {Platform} this {Timeframe}
7. **What Is It Really About?**: {Topic} isn't about {Misconceptions}. It's how people feel on a {Moment}
8. **Belief Changer**: At {Age}, I was: [negatives]. My life was {Adjective}. But {Action} changed everything
9. **Here's How I Do It**: Here's what I {Action} every day to {Outcome} while {Benefit}
10. **Stop/Start**: Stop {Undesirable} and start {Desired}, here's how
11. **Investment In Myself**: I paid {Experts} $X,XXX to attend their {Event}. Here are {X} takeaways
12. **Truth Nobody Accepts**: The truth about {Subject} -- they're great for {Benefit}, but that's about it
13. **The Shortcut**: The shortcut to {Outcome} isn't: [accepted practices]. It's {Simple Practice}
14. **Powerful Assets**: {Number} powerful {Content Type} to change your life. If you're in your {Age Range}, read this
15. **Misconception Reframe**: {Outcome} isn't a destination. It's a daily {Action}
16. **Ready To Give Up**: After {X} {Timeframe} {habit}, I had {Neg1}, {Neg2}, and I was ready to give up

### LinkedIn Hook Rules
- Length: 2 short punchy sentences with line break OR 2-3 lines under 140 characters
- Last sentence can end with colon ":" if appropriate
- No formatting or headers in hooks. No hashtags
- Preview before publishing (tools: Clio, AuthoredUp)

## 10 Contrast Hook Patterns (E02)

Hooks that create contrast between two ideas produce the strongest curiosity gaps:

1. **Before/After**: "I used to [old state]. Now I [new state]. Here's what changed"
2. **Common Belief vs Truth**: "Everyone says [belief]. The truth is [reality]"
3. **What Most Do vs What Works**: "Most people [common approach]. Top performers [different approach]"
4. **Expectation vs Reality**: "I expected [outcome]. What actually happened was [surprise]"
5. **Easy Way vs Hard Way**: "There's a hard way to [task]. And then there's this"
6. **Old vs New**: "[Old method] worked in [year]. In [current year], you need [new approach]"
7. **Myth vs Fact**: "Myth: [common claim]. Fact: [surprising truth]"
8. **What I Thought vs What I Learned**: "I thought [assumption] for [time]. I was wrong"
9. **Small Change, Big Result**: "One tiny change to [process] 3x'd my [metric]"
10. **Cost of Inaction**: "Not [action] is costing you [specific amount] every [timeframe]"

## Gusten Sun Hook Categories (E03a)

### Intriguing Questions
- "What if I told you that you didn't need to [unpleasant thing] to get [desired result]?"
- "Ever wonder why [problem] keeps happening? Here's the truth"
- "Are you making this one mistake that's costing you [big result]?"

### Controversial Opinions
- "Everything you know about [topic] is wrong"
- "This is why [common advice] doesn't actually work"
- "This [tool/resource] feels illegal to know"

### How-to
- "How I went from [starting point] to [desired result] in just [timeframe]"
- "The exact system I use to [achieve result] without [pain point]"

### Mistakes
- "Stop doing this if you want to [achieve goal]"
- "The [number] biggest red flags when [choosing/doing something]"

### Personal Story
- "It took me [X years] to realize this one thing"
- "I went from [X to Y] in [timeframe] -- here's how you can too"

### List
- "Here are 3 ways to [achieve goal] in the next [timeframe]"
- "The [number]-step formula I used to [achieve specific goal]"

## Visual Hook Structure Database (E05a)

### Visual Selection Options
- Creator A-roll (talking to camera)
- Creator in B-roll (doing something silently)
- Subject in B-roll (someone else doing something)
- Non-subject B-roll (not a person)
- Examples/graphics (screenshots, photos, other videos)
- Arrow/highlights directing attention

### Visual Movement Options
- **A-Roll**: None | Slow zoom | Quick zoom | Transition
- **B-Roll**: None | Quick cuts | Swipe transitions | Text overlays | Slow zooms | 3D effect

### Layout Structures
- Full screen A-roll (in studio or in world)
- Full screen A-roll with partial graphic overlay
- Full screen B-roll
- Full screen B-roll with A-roll overlaid (greenscreen/circle)
- Split screen A-roll + B-roll

## Text Hook Structure Database (E05a)

### Word Structures
- Base captions (repeat of spoken words)
- [Subject] does [thing]
- Future of [thing]
- [Shocking statement]

### Visual Layout
- Plain text | Text with shadow | Text on colored background | Text on physical prop

### Rules
- Fewest words possible. Font as big as possible without overwhelming
- Easy to read when overlaid on visuals (white/colored background behind text = cheat code)
- Keep text near eye level of subject. Do not block overlays

## Audio Hook Structures (E05a)

Four options:
1. **No audio**: Zero music, zero SFX (creates intimacy, forces focus on spoken words)
2. **Music only**: Backing track, no SFX
3. **SFX only**: No music, with sound effects for emphasis
4. **Both**: Music + SFX layered

Match audio choice to video emotion and hook style. Cutting music entirely makes viewer pay MORE attention to what you say next (use sparingly for important moments).
